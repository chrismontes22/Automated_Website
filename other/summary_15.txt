Introducing DoorDash Tasks
============================================================

**DoorDash launches "Tasks" to let delivery drivers earn money by completing small, non-delivery jobs.**

**Key points**

*   DoorDash is expanding beyond food delivery by offering "Tasks," which are short, on-the-ground activities for businesses.
*   Examples of tasks include taking photos of restaurant menus, verifying delivery drop-off locations, or helping autonomous vehicles navigate.
*   A new standalone app is being piloted for tasks that help train AI and robotics, such as recording speech or filming everyday activities.
*   Dashers can choose to complete these tasks between deliveries or on their own time, with upfront pay based on the effort required.
*   The program is currently available in select U.S. markets and is already being used by companies in retail, insurance, and technology.

**Why it matters**
This move allows DoorDash to monetize its massive network of drivers for data collection and physical-world verification, providing businesses with real-time insights while offering drivers more flexible ways to earn income.
