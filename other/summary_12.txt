Apple To schedule Gemini-Powered Siri Debut For iOS 26.5 Beta
============================================================

**Apple plans to supercharge Siri with Google’s Gemini AI in an upcoming software update.**

**Key points**

*   Apple is expected to release the iOS 26.5 beta in late March 2026, which will introduce deep integration with Google’s Gemini AI.
*   The partnership aims to turn Siri into a more capable assistant that can perform complex, cross-app tasks.
*   The update fulfills Apple’s promise to deliver advanced "Personal Intelligence" features by the end of the first quarter of 2026.
*   Apple will continue to prioritize user privacy by combining Gemini’s power with its own secure "Private Cloud Compute" and local device processing.
*   This release serves as a major stability update before Apple unveils its next generation of software at the Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) in June.

**Why it matters**
This integration marks a pivotal shift for Apple, transforming Siri from a basic voice tool into a competitive AI assistant capable of handling sophisticated automation. It represents a strategic move to close the gap with rival AI platforms while maintaining Apple's strict standards for user privacy.
