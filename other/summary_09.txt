Apple iPhone 20: All We Know About the Buttonless, All-Glass Redesign Coming in 2027
============================================================

**Apple is reportedly planning a major redesign for the iPhone’s 20th anniversary in 2027, potentially featuring a buttonless, all-glass aesthetic.**

**Key points**

*   **Radical Design:** The device may eliminate all physical buttons, replacing them with haptic-feedback touch zones integrated into the frame.
*   **Display Upgrades:** Apple is exploring an "all-screen" design that hides cameras and sensors under the display, though experts are divided on whether this technology will be ready by 2027.
*   **Advanced Hardware:** The phone is expected to feature a highly efficient 2nm processor and new silicon anode battery technology, which allows for larger battery capacity in a smaller space.
*   **Premium Positioning:** Similar to the 2017 iPhone X, this model is expected to be an ultra-premium, "era-defining" device that sits above the standard Pro lineup in both features and price.
*   **Release Timing:** While no official date is set, the phone is expected to launch in September 2027 to commemorate two decades of the iPhone.

**Why it matters**
As Apple faces stiff competition from AI-focused rivals, this anniversary model represents a critical opportunity to refresh the iPhone’s identity and set the standard for the next decade of mobile technology.
