Xiaomi Returns to Laptops After Four Years with a MacBook Air Rival That Outclasses It on Paper for $1,275
============================================================

**Xiaomi’s new Book Pro 14 laptop aims to challenge the MacBook Air by offering superior hardware specs and better connectivity.**

**Key points**

*   **High-end display:** The laptop features a 14.6-inch OLED touchscreen with a 120Hz refresh rate and 3.1K resolution, outperforming the standard MacBook Air screen.
*   **Superior connectivity:** Unlike the minimalist MacBook Air, the Book Pro 14 includes a wide variety of ports, including HDMI 2.1, USB-A, and Thunderbolt 4, eliminating the need for dongles.
*   **Lightweight performance:** Despite being lighter than the MacBook Air, the device uses an advanced cooling system that allows it to sustain higher performance levels without overheating.
*   **Competitive battery:** Xiaomi claims nearly 20 hours of battery life and fast-charging capabilities that can restore 50% power in under 30 minutes.
*   **Limited availability:** The laptop is currently exclusive to the Chinese market, with no confirmed timeline for a global release.

**Why it matters**
Xiaomi has designed a machine that directly addresses common user frustrations with Apple’s minimalist design, such as the lack of ports and thermal throttling. If it launches globally, it could become one of the most serious Windows-based competitors to the MacBook Air in years.
