Elon Musk’s Latest Outlandish Plan Is a Giant Chip Factory In Texas
============================================================

**Elon Musk announces "Terafab," an ambitious plan to build a massive, custom chip factory in Austin, Texas.**

**Key points**

*   The new facility, dubbed "Terafab," aims to produce specialized chips for Tesla’s autonomous vehicles, Optimus robots, SpaceX, and xAI.
*   Musk claims current chip suppliers like TSMC and Samsung cannot keep up with the rapid production speed he requires for his AI and space goals.
*   The project is designed to meet massive power demands for AI, with Musk aiming for a "terawatt output" per year.
*   The plan is extremely expensive, with some analysts estimating costs could reach $300 billion.
*   Industry experts warn the project faces significant hurdles, including the extreme complexity of chip manufacturing and a lack of specialized domestic expertise.

**Why it matters**
This project represents a high-stakes gamble by Musk to gain total control over his supply chain, though its success is uncertain given the immense financial cost and the technical difficulty of building advanced chip factories in the U.S.
