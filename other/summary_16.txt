Huawei crowdfunds world’s first ‘Mesh Crystal Antenna’ Wi-Fi 7 router — stunning glowing ornament also has a ‘shark fin’ heat exhaust, but is currently a Japan market exclusive
============================================================

**Huawei has launched a uniquely designed, glowing Wi-Fi 7 router in Japan that aims to turn home networking equipment into a decorative centerpiece.**

**Key points**

*   **Bold Design:** The "WiFi Mesh X3 Pro" features a glowing, crystal-like mountain aesthetic and a "shark fin" cooling system, moving away from the traditional hidden router design.
*   **High-Performance Tech:** Despite its ornamental look, the device supports the latest Wi-Fi 7 standards, including MLO and 4K-QAM, and includes two 2.5 Gbps Ethernet ports.
*   **Smart Features:** The router includes customizable lighting that can change based on the time of day and is managed via a dedicated mobile app.
*   **Crowdfunding Success:** Currently exclusive to the Japanese market, the product has seen massive interest, exceeding its crowdfunding goal by over 8,600%.
*   **Practical Benefits:** By encouraging users to place the router in a central, visible location rather than hiding it in a corner, the design may actually improve Wi-Fi signal coverage throughout the home.

**Why it matters**
This product represents a shift in consumer tech design, attempting to transform functional but unsightly networking hardware into an attractive piece of home decor. If successful, it could set a new trend for manufacturers to prioritize aesthetics alongside technical performance.
