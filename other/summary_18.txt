Last Chance – Final 24 hours for Best Buy’s giant Tech Fest sale: MacBook Neo, Copilot+ PCs, TVs, smart home, more
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**

Today is the final day of Best Buy’s "Tech Fest" sale, offering significant discounts on MacBooks, gaming gear, and smart home devices.

**Key points**

*   **Last Chance:** The sale ends tonight, marking the final opportunity to secure current promotional pricing and bonus rewards.
*   **MacBook Incentives:** Best Buy Plus and Total members can receive a $50 bonus store credit with the purchase of new MacBook Neo, M5 MacBook Air, or M5 Pro/Max MacBook Pro models.
*   **Broad Tech Discounts:** Beyond Apple products, the sale includes deals on gaming laptops, monitors, smart home devices, and video games.
*   **Member Perks:** Additional rewards are available for members on select TV purchases of $499 or more.
*   **Competitive Pricing:** The event serves as an early spring sale, featuring deep discounts on various PC brands, including ASUS, HP, and Dell.

**Why it matters**

This sale represents a final window for shoppers to access specific price drops and member-exclusive rewards before the event concludes. It also serves as a precursor to broader spring retail sales expected from competitors like Amazon later this month.
