### Why it Matters
The convergence of geopolitical conflict, supply chain bottlenecks, and labor unrest is creating a volatile environment for global markets and corporate stability. These developments signal a period of heightened economic uncertainty that could force businesses to reevaluate their reliance on fragile international logistics and emerging technologies.
