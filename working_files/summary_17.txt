South Korean chip startup FuriosaAI is expanding its global footprint by deploying its RNGD accelerators into Equinix data centers located in Lisbon, Portugal.

### Key Points
- FuriosaAI is targeting the European market to compete in the growing artificial intelligence infrastructure sector.
- The deployment utilizes the company's specialized RNGD hardware, designed to optimize AI inference workloads.
- Equinix provides the physical data center infrastructure necessary for FuriosaAI to scale its operations across Europe.
- This move marks a strategic shift for the startup as it moves beyond its domestic South Korean market.

### Why it Matters
This expansion highlights the increasing competition among hardware manufacturers to provide cost-effective, high-performance alternatives for AI inference tasks. By securing space in major global data centers, FuriosaAI aims to challenge established chip giants and offer enterprises more specialized options for managing AI workloads.
