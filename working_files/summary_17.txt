The complete guide to building a website with AI: From prompt to publish
============================================================

AI website builders allow users to create professional, functional websites in minutes by using generative prompts to handle layout, content, and design without requiring any traditional coding knowledge.

### Key Points
- AI platforms generate complete website drafts by interpreting user-provided details about business goals, target audiences, and design preferences.
- The global market for AI-powered website builders is projected to reach between $14.78 billion and $17.4 billion by 2035.
- Tools like the Bluehost AI Website Builder integrate domain registration, managed WordPress hosting, and visual editing into a single, streamlined workflow.
- Users can refine AI-generated results using visual editors to swap images, adjust color palettes, and personalize content to match their specific brand identity.
- Best practices for success include providing detailed context in prompts, verifying AI-generated information, and maintaining a focus on accessibility and SEO.

### Why it Matters
These tools significantly lower the barrier to entry for entrepreneurs and small businesses by eliminating the need for expensive developers or complex coding skills. By accelerating the development process, AI builders allow creators to focus on brand strategy and content rather than technical infrastructure.
