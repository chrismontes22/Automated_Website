Google AI Is Killing The Human Internet, According To A New Report
============================================================

A new Cloudflare report reveals that non-human traffic now exceeds 50% of internet activity, driven largely by Google’s Gemini AI scraping content and reducing human search referrals by 40%.

### Key Points
- Cloudflare data shows human traffic across major industries declined by at least 35% between June 2025 and April 2026.
- Google accounts for 88% of referral traffic, but its "AI Overview" feature often displays content without directing users to the original source websites.
- AI training requests now comprise 52% of all web crawler activity, a significant increase from 22% in early 2025.
- Google currently uses a single crawler for both search indexing and AI training, forcing publishers to choose between AI inclusion or total search deindexing.
- The UK’s Competition and Markets Authority is forcing Google to allow publishers to opt out of AI scraping without losing their search engine rankings.

### Why it Matters
This shift toward "Google Zero" threatens the financial viability of digital publishers by stripping away the traffic necessary to sustain content creation. If search engines continue to prioritize AI-generated summaries over direct referrals, the resulting loss of revenue could lead to a collapse of the independent web ecosystem.
