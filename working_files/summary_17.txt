Xiaomi 17 Max gets disassembled on video
============================================================

Xiaomi has officially launched the 17 Max smartphone, a new flagship device featuring a Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor and an expansive 8,000 mAh battery capacity.

### Key Points
- The Xiaomi 17 Max features a 6.9-inch 1200x2608 LTPO AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate and 3,500-nit peak brightness.
- Internal hardware includes the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset, up to 16GB of RAM, and 512GB of internal storage.
- The camera system consists of a 200MP main sensor, a 50MP periscope telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom, and a 50MP ultrawide lens.
- The device supports 100W wired and 50W wireless charging, alongside 22.5W reverse wired and wireless charging capabilities.
- A detailed teardown video by the YouTube channel WekiHome provides a comprehensive look at the internal components of the new handset.

### Why it Matters
The introduction of the 17 Max expands Xiaomi's flagship lineup with a high-capacity battery that significantly exceeds standard industry benchmarks for premium smartphones. This release signals a strategic push to capture power-user segments by combining top-tier processing performance with extended battery longevity.
