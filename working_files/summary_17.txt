Circle debuts cirBTC on Ethereum to challenge Coinbase in the wrapped bitcoin market
============================================================

Circle has launched cirBTC, a 1:1 bitcoin-backed token on the Ethereum network, aiming to capture institutional market share from competitors like Coinbase and BitGo in the synthetic bitcoin sector.

### Key Points
- Circle introduced cirBTC to enable bitcoin holders to utilize their assets within decentralized finance protocols like lending and decentralized exchanges.
- The new token competes directly with established products, including the market-leading wrapped bitcoin (wBTC) and Coinbase’s recently launched cbBTC.
- Circle is leveraging its reputation as the issuer of the $75 billion USDC stablecoin to attract institutional investors to its new synthetic bitcoin offering.
- The total market capitalization for all synthetic bitcoin tokens currently ranges between $12.5 billion and $13.5 billion.
- Synthetic bitcoin tokens allow users to bridge the gap between the Bitcoin network and the native programmability of the Ethereum blockchain.

### Why it Matters
This launch intensifies competition among major crypto infrastructure providers for control over the growing synthetic bitcoin market. By offering a trusted alternative for institutional capital, Circle aims to increase the utility of bitcoin within the broader decentralized finance ecosystem.
