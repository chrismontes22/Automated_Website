OLED iPad Mini: Release Date, Pricing, and What to Expect
============================================================

Apple is reportedly developing a next-generation iPad mini featuring an upgraded A19 Pro chip, a transition to OLED display technology, and a new water-resistant, hole-less chassis design.

### Key Points
- The upcoming iPad mini, codenamed J510/J511, is expected to utilize the A19 Pro chip, potentially manufactured on an advanced 3-nanometer N3P process.
- Apple is transitioning the tablet from LCD to an OLED display, which may increase the screen size from 8.3 inches to 8.7 inches.
- A new vibration-based speaker system is being developed to eliminate traditional speaker holes, significantly improving the device's water and dust resistance.
- Industry analysts and supply chain reports suggest a potential product launch occurring in the second half of 2026.
- The new model is expected to carry a price premium, with estimates suggesting a cost increase of up to $100 over the current $499 starting price.

### Why it Matters
The integration of OLED technology and enhanced water resistance signals Apple's intent to bring premium hardware features from its flagship iPhone and iPad Pro lines to the smaller tablet form factor. These upgrades could justify a higher price point while positioning the device as a more durable and high-performance option for users seeking a compact, professional-grade tablet.
