Amazon has discounted the Beats Solo 4 on-ear headphones to $99.95 for Prime Day, offering a significant price reduction for users seeking lightweight audio gear with long battery life.

- The Beats Solo 4 are currently priced at $99.95, representing a $100 discount from their standard retail price.

This deal provides a budget-friendly entry point for consumers looking for reliable, portable audio equipment from a major brand. By prioritizing essential features like long battery life and versatile wired connections, the Beats Solo 4 offer a practical alternative to more expensive, feature-heavy over-ear models.
