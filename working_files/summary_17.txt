Closing the AI literacy gap is the real gender equalizer in tech
============================================================

Artificial intelligence is lowering technical barriers to entrepreneurship, offering a critical opportunity to close the gender gap in technology by enabling women to become creators rather than just users.

### Key Points
- No-code AI platforms allow individuals to build digital products and automate workflows without requiring traditional software engineering expertise or large development teams.
- AI literacy is becoming a fundamental skill for career progression and economic mobility across sectors including healthcare, education, and retail.
- Major corporations like Meta, Microsoft, and Amazon are restructuring workforces to prioritize AI, increasing the necessity for workers to master AI-driven workflows.
- By automating repetitive tasks like scheduling and data handling, AI provides female founders with more capacity to focus on leadership and strategic business growth.
- Inclusive participation in AI development ensures that future technologies reflect diverse perspectives, preventing the reinforcement of existing societal and gender-based inequalities.

### Why it Matters
The shift toward AI-driven production represents a fundamental change in who can participate in the digital economy and influence future technological systems. If women are actively encouraged to build with these tools, they can overcome historical barriers to funding and technical access, ultimately fostering a more equitable and innovative business landscape.
