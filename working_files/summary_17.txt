Review: $750 easy-to-use robot mower handles lawns like its pricy peers
============================================================

The Anthbot M9 is a compact, wire-free robotic lawn mower that utilizes RTK GPS and dual cameras to maintain lawns up to 1,000 square meters at an affordable price.

### Key Points
- The M9 retails for $899, with current sale pricing available at $769, significantly undercutting premium competitors.
- It features a 20-cm five-blade cutting disc and can navigate slopes up to 24 degrees while operating at under 58 dB.
- The unit weighs 10.2 kg and uses full-band RTK GPS combined with dual HDR cameras for navigation instead of LiDAR.
- A 5-Ah battery provides approximately 90 minutes of runtime, covering up to 150 square meters per charge cycle.
- Setup is designed for beginners, with the manufacturer claiming a 10-minute process from unpacking to initial lawn mapping.

### Why it Matters
The Anthbot M9 democratizes smart lawn care by offering high-end navigation technology at a fraction of the cost of established premium brands. Its user-friendly interface and compact design make automated lawn maintenance accessible to consumers who may lack experience with complex smart home devices.
