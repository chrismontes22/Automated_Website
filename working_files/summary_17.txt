Era Ends For IMG Today As Premier League Takes Production In-House: “It Was Inevitable They Would Want It”
============================================================

IMG is concluding its 22-year partnership with the Premier League as the organization transitions to an in-house production model for its global broadcast coverage starting in 2026.

### Key Points
- IMG-owned Premier League Productions (PLP) has managed global broadcast content for the league since 2004.
- The Premier League will launch its own production arm, Premier League Studios, beginning with the 2026/27 season.
- IMG will maintain a working relationship with the league regarding distribution and archive management.
- PLP currently produces content for international licensees including Sky, Prime Video, TNT Sports, Canal+, DAZN, and JioHotstar.
- The transition follows a vote by the 20 Premier League clubs to gain greater control over content and future direct-to-consumer options.

### Why it Matters
This shift marks a significant change in sports media as the Premier League moves to internalize production to capture more value and operational control. While IMG loses a major contract, the company is pivoting its resources toward other global sports properties and new technical initiatives to maintain its market position.
