You, too, can build this open-source smart home keyboard that works with Home Assistant
============================================================

A new DIY Zigbee touch keypad offers Home Assistant users a slim, screwless, and cloud-free alternative to expensive, ecosystem-locked smart home hardware available on the current market.

### Key Points
- The device features a compact, Pez-sized design that mounts easily to walls without visible screws.
- It operates wirelessly for approximately six months on a single battery charge.
- The project is entirely cloud-free and integrates directly with Home Assistant via Zigbee signals.
- All necessary build instructions, bill of materials, and code are publicly available on GitHub.

### Why it Matters
This project provides smart home enthusiasts with a customizable, privacy-focused hardware solution that avoids the limitations of proprietary ecosystems and subscription fees. By offering an open-source alternative, it empowers users to maintain full control over their home automation infrastructure while reducing long-term hardware costs.
