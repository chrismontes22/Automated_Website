Hey Siri, give us weather reports that work outside California
============================================================

Apple users in regions like London are calling for Siri to include precipitation forecasts in default weather reports, as the current system often omits vital rain information.

### Key Points
- Siri currently provides temperature highs and lows but fails to mention rain probability in its initial weather summary.
- Users in rainy climates like London and Seattle must issue follow-up voice commands to determine if they need umbrellas or raincoats.
- Critics argue that Apple’s software design often prioritizes California-centric weather patterns over the needs of international users.
- Previous complaints regarding Apple’s Weather app, such as the inability to distinguish between London boroughs, were eventually addressed through user feedback.

### Why it Matters
The limitation highlights a persistent gap in localized user experience for Apple’s voice assistant in international markets. Improving Siri’s predictive capabilities could significantly enhance the utility of HomePod devices for daily planning in regions where weather conditions change rapidly.
