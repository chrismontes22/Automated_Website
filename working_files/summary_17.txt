Apple still has three unannounced iOS 27 features in the pipeline: report
============================================================

Apple is expected to release several unannounced features for iOS 27 and the Apple Watch by September, including expanded Siri AI integrations and a simplified Modular Ultra watch face.

### Key Points
- Apple plans to introduce a simplified version of the Modular Ultra watch face for Apple Watch models this September.
- The company is developing a new Siri Extensions API to allow direct integration with third-party AI models like Claude and Gemini.
- Discussions are ongoing with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google regarding the technical requirements for these new third-party Siri integrations.
- A fully customizable Camera app, allowing users to rearrange controls, is expected to launch alongside the iPhone 18 Pro.
- Delays in these features are reportedly linked to EU regulatory concerns, internal marketing strategies, and potential litigation risks.

### Why it Matters
These upcoming software updates represent a significant shift in Apple's strategy toward opening its ecosystem to broader third-party AI competition. By moving away from exclusive partnerships, Apple aims to provide users with more flexibility while navigating complex legal and privacy challenges.
