Mantle’s xStocks Rollout Pushes Tokenized Equities Closer to Real Onchain Liquidity
============================================================

Mantle has launched xStocks, a new platform offering tokenized equity exposure to major companies like Tesla and Nvidia, aiming to bridge traditional financial assets with decentralized on-chain markets.

### Key Points
- Mantle’s xStocks platform provides fully collateralized tracker certificates for global equities, including Tesla, Nvidia, Apple, and Meta.
- The system utilizes Fluxion’s hybrid automated market maker and request-for-quote infrastructure to address liquidity and execution challenges.
- Bybit serves as the primary gateway for direct deposits and withdrawals, facilitating user access to the tokenized assets.
- All xStocks offerings are compliant with the Swiss DLT Act to ensure regulatory alignment for institutional and retail participants.
- Future roadmap plans include the integration of xChange support and the launch of an xPoints loyalty program for platform users.

### Why it Matters
This launch signals a shift in the digital asset sector toward prioritizing the functional scalability and liquidity of real-world assets over mere existence on a blockchain. By integrating institutional-grade rails, Mantle aims to prove that tokenized equities can operate as native, programmable instruments within the broader decentralized finance ecosystem.
