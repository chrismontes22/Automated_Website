Billionaire Saylor’s Strategy Buys Bitcoin Worth $101 Million—After Earlier Sale Sparked Sell-Off
============================================================

Strategy disclosed a $101.3 million purchase of 1,550 bitcoin last week, signaling continued institutional commitment despite recent market volatility and the firm's rare sale of assets for dividends.

### Key Points
- Strategy acquired 1,550 bitcoin at an average price of $65,332 per coin using $181 million generated from recent stock sales.
- The firm currently holds 845,256 bitcoin, representing a total market value of approximately $63.9 billion.
- This purchase follows a rare sale of 32 bitcoins last week, which the company executed to fund dividend payments to its stockholders.
- Bitcoin prices recovered to $63,926 on Monday after dropping below $60,000 for the first time since October 2024.
- Cardano’s ADA token reached its lowest valuation since December 2020, falling below $0.16 following the cancellation of its flagship summit.

### Why it Matters
This move highlights the ongoing volatility in the cryptocurrency market as institutional investors balance aggressive accumulation strategies against the need for liquidity. The firm's actions serve as a bellwether for broader market sentiment, reflecting how macroeconomic factors like interest rate expectations continue to influence digital asset valuations.
