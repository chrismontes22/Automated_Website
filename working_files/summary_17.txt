The Shift to Answer Engine Optimisation: Why Melbourne Brands are Rethinking Traditional SEO
============================================================

The rise of generative AI and conversational search engines is shifting digital marketing from traditional SEO toward Answer Engine Optimisation to secure visibility in direct, AI-generated responses.

### Key Points
- Generative AI platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google’s AI Overviews are replacing traditional link-based search results with direct, conversational answers.
- Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) prioritizes structured data, entity relationships, and authoritative content over traditional keyword stuffing and backlink hoarding.
- Melbourne-based agency Cortexa Solutions is leading the transition by helping brands optimize for how AI models retrieve, synthesize, and cite information.
- Modern digital marketing is moving toward flexible, month-to-month contracts and transparent performance reporting rather than rigid, long-term agency agreements.
- Brands must establish themselves as niche authorities to ensure they are cited as primary sources within AI-generated search responses.

### Why it Matters
This transition fundamentally alters how businesses capture online traffic, as failing to be cited by AI models renders a brand invisible to modern search users. Companies that adopt AEO strategies can maintain visibility in an evolving digital landscape, while those relying on legacy SEO risk losing significant market share.
