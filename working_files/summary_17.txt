Xbox's big "reset" cuts: Compulsion, Double Fine, Undead Labs, Ninja Theory to leave Xbox — 3,200 roles to be removed
============================================================

Xbox CEO Asha Sharma has announced a major restructuring plan that will result in 3,200 job cuts across the company’s gaming division by the end of fiscal year 2027.

### Key Points
- Microsoft will eliminate 3,200 roles through FY27, with an initial wave of 1,500 layoffs impacting the Xbox division.
- Four studios—Compulsion, Double Fine, Undead Labs, and Ninja Theory—will transition to independent status or new management to avoid closure.
- Microsoft is currently negotiating with the French government to find a new owner for Arkane Studios to prevent its shutdown.
- The company plans to streamline middle management and shift focus toward major franchises like Halo and Fallout.
- Development of the next-generation Xbox Helix console will continue despite the broader corporate restructuring and hardware market challenges.

### Why it Matters
This significant downsizing reflects a strategic pivot toward higher profitability and operational efficiency as Microsoft attempts to address declining margins within its gaming division. By divesting smaller studios and consolidating resources, the company aims to stabilize its core console business and better compete against major industry incumbents.
