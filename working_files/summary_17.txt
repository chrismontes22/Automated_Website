We really need a way to hand over ownership of an Apple Home
============================================================

Apple Home users face significant challenges when transferring smart home systems to new property owners because the ecosystem lacks a formal mechanism to migrate administrative ownership between accounts.

### Key Points
- Apple Home settings are tied to the original owner's Apple account rather than the physical hardware, complicating property handovers.
- Current workarounds involve adding new owners as "Residents," which limits their ability to manage core devices like HomePods or Apple TVs.
- Retaining the original owner's account access creates potential security and privacy risks for the new occupants.
- Philips Hue bridges allow for easier scene and automation transfers, but this does not resolve the underlying Apple Home account ownership issue.
- A proposed solution is for Apple to introduce an "Owner" transfer feature that allows administrative rights to be passed to a new user.

### Why it Matters
As smart home technology becomes a standard feature in real estate, the lack of a seamless transfer process creates unnecessary friction for buyers and sellers. Implementing a formal ownership migration tool would improve security and ensure that integrated home systems remain functional during property transitions.
