Tested: Cricut's new Joy 2 machine is teeny tiny (but mighty powerful), and I love it
============================================================

The Cricut Joy 2 is a compact, portable vinyl cutting machine designed for beginners and crafters seeking an affordable, travel-friendly device for quick projects and creative customization.

### Key Points
- The machine weighs 2.7 lbs (1.2 kg) and features a maximum cut size of 4.5 inches by 48 inches when using smart materials.
- It is compatible with over 75 different materials and retails for $99, offering a budget-friendly alternative to larger flagship models.
- Setup is performed via Bluetooth through the Cricut Design Space software, which is available on mobile devices and laptops.
- The device is optimized for portability and small-scale crafting, though it lacks the engraving and debossing capabilities found in larger machines.

### Why it Matters
The Cricut Joy 2 lowers the barrier to entry for digital crafting by providing a space-saving, low-cost option that does not sacrifice precision. Its portability makes it a practical tool for hobbyists who need to work outside of a dedicated studio or workbench environment.
