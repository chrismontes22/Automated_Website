Bitcoin hovers below $60,000 as crypto braces for a pivotal week
============================================================

Bitcoin remains under pressure below $60,000 as traders reduce risk and derivatives data indicates that bearish sentiment continues to dominate the broader cryptocurrency market heading into month-end.

### Key Points
- Bitcoin is trading near $59,800, while ether remains below $1,600 amid a broader market structure that continues to skew bearish.
- Over $200 million in futures positions were liquidated in the last 24 hours, with long positions accounting for the majority of the losses.
- The BVIV volatility index dropped 5% to 47%, signaling a potential shift toward market consolidation and calmer trading conditions.
- Solana open interest remains elevated at 72.70 million tokens, while AVAX open interest has declined to its lowest level since April 1.
- The CoinMarketCap Altcoin Season index is stagnant at 49/100 as investors wait for a clear directional move from bitcoin.

### Why it Matters
The current market stagnation reflects widespread investor caution as traders avoid speculative bets while awaiting a definitive trend from bitcoin. This lack of conviction suggests that price volatility may persist until the market establishes a clear support level above $60,000.
