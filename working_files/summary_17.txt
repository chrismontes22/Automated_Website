Bernie Sanders’s New, Necessary, Bold Act: Taking on the AI Oligarchs
============================================================

Senator Bernie Sanders is leading a legislative and public campaign to regulate artificial intelligence, warning that the technology threatens to exacerbate wealth inequality and cause mass unemployment.

### Key Points
- Senator Sanders and Representative Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez introduced a bill proposing a moratorium on the construction of new AI data centers.
- The senator argues that AI development risks concentrating wealth among billionaires like Jeff Bezos, Elon Musk, and Mark Zuckerberg.
- Sanders is building a cross-partisan coalition, citing concerns from tech experts like Geoffrey Hinton and data from polls showing 55% of Americans fear AI harm.
- AI companies are spending millions on political lobbying, creating significant hurdles for lawmakers attempting to implement stricter industry regulations.
- Local opposition to data centers is growing, with Maine lawmakers currently considering the first statewide ban on such construction.

### Why it Matters
Sanders’s advocacy highlights a growing political tension between rapid technological innovation and the potential for widespread economic displacement. By framing AI as a central issue of corporate power, he is forcing a national conversation that could influence future labor policies and the regulatory landscape for Big Tech.
