G. Love Loses Reported $424,000 in Crypto Account Hack
============================================================

Singer-songwriter G. Love lost approximately $424,000 in Bitcoin after downloading a malicious, fake Ledger cryptocurrency application from the Apple App Store, compromising his long-term retirement savings.

### Key Points
- Musician Garrett Dutton, known as G. Love, reported the theft of 5.92 Bitcoin on X.
- The loss occurred after Dutton mistakenly downloaded a fraudulent app disguised as the official Ledger crypto management tool.
- Blockchain investigator ZachXBT confirmed the details of the incident, which wiped out a decade of the artist's savings.
- Dutton publicly accepted responsibility for the security oversight and issued a warning to other cryptocurrency users regarding sophisticated digital scams.

### Why it Matters
This incident highlights the persistent security risks associated with third-party app stores and the vulnerability of individual cryptocurrency holders to sophisticated phishing attacks. It serves as a stark reminder for users to verify the authenticity of financial software before managing high-value digital assets.
