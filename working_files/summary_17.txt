Apple could fix the iPhone Air’s biggest issues with the iPhone Air 2
============================================================

Apple is reportedly developing the second-generation iPhone Air, codenamed V62, which aims to address previous hardware limitations by adding an ultrawide camera and improving overall battery performance.

### Key Points
- The iPhone Air 2 is currently undergoing advanced testing and is expected to launch in spring 2027.
- New hardware upgrades include the addition of an ultrawide-angle lens to complement the primary rear camera.
- The device will be powered by the A20 Pro processor, matching the chip technology used in the iPhone 18 Pro series.
- Apple plans to release the iPhone Air 2 alongside the standard iPhone 18 model during a spring event.

### Why it Matters
This shift toward a staggered release schedule allows Apple to distribute revenue more evenly throughout the fiscal year rather than relying solely on fall launches. By refining the iPhone Air's hardware, the company also aims to better compete with rivals like Samsung that maintain a consistent cadence of smartphone releases year-round.
