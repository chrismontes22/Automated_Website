Controller for HomeKit app adds AI feature: ‘Just say it’
============================================================

The Controller for HomeKit app has introduced a new AI-powered feature that allows users to create complex smart home automations and scenes using simple, natural language voice commands.

### Key Points
- The new "just say it" feature automatically generates HomeKit scenes and workflows based on user-provided descriptions.
- Users can define specific triggers and actions, such as adjusting lighting levels or operating smart shades based on time or occupancy.
- Controller for HomeKit operates on a subscription model, with an Essentials tier at $40 per year and a Plus tier at $80 per year.
- A seven-day free trial is available for new users to test the AI automation capabilities before committing to a subscription.

### Why it Matters
This update simplifies the often complex process of configuring smart home automations by removing the need for manual programming. By lowering the technical barrier to entry, the app could increase user engagement with advanced HomeKit features and influence how developers integrate generative AI into home management software.
