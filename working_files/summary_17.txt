New infosec products of the week: April 17, 2026
============================================================

Axonius, Broadcom, Siemens, and Sitehop have released new cybersecurity and infrastructure products this week, focusing on AI integration, industrial automation, and quantum-resistant encryption for enterprise and government networks.

### Key Points
- Axonius updated its Asset Cloud with AI-powered remediation, IoT support, and a new data trust standard for verified assets.
- Siemens introduced an AI-ready Industrial Automation DataCenter featuring pre-configured virtualization and industrial demilitarized zones for secure OT environments.
- Broadcom launched VMware Tanzu Platform agent foundations to provide a secure, governed runtime for scaling autonomous AI applications.
- Sitehop released SAFEcore Edge, a hardware-based encryption device offering quantum-resistant security with significantly lower latency than software alternatives.

### Why it Matters
These product updates reflect a growing industry shift toward integrating AI governance and quantum-ready security directly into core enterprise and industrial infrastructure. By automating remediation and hardening network edges, these companies are helping organizations manage the increasing complexity of securing both IT and operational technology environments.
