Monai’s New Baby Crib Monitors Heart Rate With 4K Sensors
============================================================

The Monai Sleepod Baby Crib, unveiled at CES and Milan Design Week 2026, integrates advanced AI-driven health monitoring and air purification into a collapsible, minimalist furniture design for modern nurseries.

### Key Points
- Designed by Wen Rui, Shi Zongjing, and Shen Jiahao for the Shenzhen-based team Kaiwang.
- Features a 4K camera, millimeter-wave radar, and thermal infrared sensors to track breathing, heart rate, and sleep cycles.
- Includes a built-in negative-ion air purification system and automated audio features to soothe infants.
- Utilizes a one-button folding mechanism for portability and adjustable railings to accommodate a child's growth.
- Marketed as the first application of "embodied intelligence" in baby care to actively respond to an infant's needs.

### Why it Matters
The Sleepod represents a shift in the baby gear industry by merging high-end aesthetic design with sophisticated medical-grade monitoring technology. By automating infant surveillance, the product aims to reduce parental anxiety and cognitive load, though it also highlights growing consumer concerns regarding data privacy and AI-driven monitoring in the home.
