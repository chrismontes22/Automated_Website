9to5Mac Daily: May 8, 2026 – Apple EDU store changes, AirPods with cameras
============================================================

Apple faces increased scrutiny from lawmakers regarding its first unionized retail location while simultaneously updating its Education Store verification requirements and advancing development on camera-equipped AirPods models.

### Key Points
- U.S. lawmakers are questioning Apple over the recent closure of its first unionized retail store.
- Apple has implemented new verification requirements for customers accessing its online Education Store.
- The company is now offering discounted Apple Watch models through the updated education portal.
- Reports indicate Apple has reached a significant development milestone for future AirPods featuring integrated cameras.

### Why it Matters
These developments highlight Apple's ongoing challenges with labor relations and its efforts to tighten control over educational discount eligibility. The potential introduction of camera-equipped AirPods suggests a strategic shift toward integrating advanced sensor technology into wearable audio devices.
