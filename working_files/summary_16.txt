Apple Turns 50, and Its Most Ambitious Phone Hasn’t Even Launched Yet — 5 iPhone Fold Concepts
============================================================

Apple is preparing to launch its first foldable iPhone in late 2026, a highly anticipated device expected to feature a 7.8-inch OLED display and the powerful A20 Pro chip.

### Key Points
- The upcoming iPhone Fold is expected to retail for approximately $2,400, with a potential announcement in September 2026 and shipments beginning in December.
- Leaked specifications include a 2nm A20 Pro processor, a 5,500mAh battery, and a 7.8-inch creaseless inner OLED display.
- Concept designs from creators like 4RMD and Michal Dufka highlight various form factors, ranging from traditional book-style foldables to compact clamshell designs.
- Engineering challenges for the device include ensuring long-term hinge durability and minimizing the visibility of the display crease.
- A functional "iPhone V" mod, created by a YouTuber using an iPhone X and a Motorola Razr chassis, has already demonstrated the viability of running iOS on a folding frame.

### Why it Matters
The release of a foldable iPhone represents a significant shift for Apple as it marks the company's 50th anniversary and its entry into a competitive, high-end hardware segment. By entering the foldable market, Apple aims to redefine premium mobile productivity while setting a new benchmark for build quality and user experience in the foldable category.
