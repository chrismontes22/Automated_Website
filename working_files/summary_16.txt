Google’s May 2026 data reveals that AI-referred traffic now converts 42% higher than traditional search, as users arrive with high intent after completing their research within AI platforms.

### Key Points
- Google reports one billion monthly active users in AI Mode, with query lengths triple those of traditional search.
- Planning-related queries in AI Mode are growing 80% faster than overall search volume, indicating a shift toward task execution.
- Adobe’s Q2 2026 report confirms that AI-referred retail traffic converts 42% better than non-AI traffic due to pre-qualified buyer intent.
- Chrome’s upcoming "auto-browse" feature, launching in late June 2026, will enable autonomous agents to complete tasks directly on websites.
- Experts recommend prioritizing task-completion surfaces, such as booking forms or pricing, at the top of landing pages to reduce friction for high-intent visitors.

### Why it Matters
The shift toward AI-driven research means websites must move away from traditional persuasion funnels that assume visitor ignorance. Businesses that fail to streamline the path to action for these pre-qualified users risk losing conversions to competitors who prioritize immediate task completion.
