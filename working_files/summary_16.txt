Anthropic to restore access to Fable 5 after negotiations with White House
============================================================

Anthropic has restored public access to its most powerful AI model, Fable 5, following a successful resolution of export control disputes with the Trump administration.

### Key Points
- Anthropic reinstated Fable 5 and Mythos 5 after the Department of Commerce lifted federal export controls on Wednesday.
- Paid subscribers can access Fable 5 at no extra cost until July 7, subject to a 50% limit on weekly token usage.
- The models were previously disabled on June 12 due to government concerns regarding potential security vulnerabilities and foreign access.
- Anthropic is currently engaged in an ongoing lawsuit against the Trump administration regarding the company's designation as a supply chain risk.
- The company recently filed a confidential S-1 draft, signaling plans for an initial public offering as early as this year.

### Why it Matters
This resolution highlights the ongoing regulatory friction between leading AI developers and the federal government regarding national security and export oversight. The outcome is critical for Anthropic as it navigates these compliance challenges while preparing for a high-profile public market debut.
