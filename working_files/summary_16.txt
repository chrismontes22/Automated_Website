FTMining Launches Free Mining Service For BTC, ETH, XRP And DOGE Holders, With Daily Earnings Of Up To $9,900?
============================================================

UK-based cloud computing platform FTMining has launched a new mobile application and free mining service, allowing holders of BTC, ETH, XRP, and DOGE to earn passive cryptocurrency income.

### Key Points
- FTMining offers cloud mining contracts ranging from a $100 starter plan to a $50,000 advanced investment option.
- New users receive a sign-up bonus between $15 and $100, plus a daily login reward of $0.75.
- The platform utilizes security technologies from McAfee and Cloudflare to protect user assets and mining data.
- Founded in 2021, the company claims a global user base of over 6 million people across 180 countries.
- Earnings are credited within 24 hours, with a minimum withdrawal threshold of $100 required for payouts.

### Why it Matters
This service lowers the barrier to entry for cryptocurrency mining by removing the need for specialized hardware or technical expertise. It reflects a growing market trend toward mobile-first financial platforms that promise passive income through cloud-based infrastructure.
