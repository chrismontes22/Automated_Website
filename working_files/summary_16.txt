Samsara Q1 Earnings Call Highlights
============================================================

Samsara reported a strong start to fiscal 2027 with 31% revenue growth and increased annual recurring revenue, driven by significant expansion among its largest enterprise and industrial customers.

### Key Points
- First-quarter revenue reached $479 million, marking a 31% increase year over year.
- Annual recurring revenue (ARR) grew 30% to nearly $2 billion, with 169 new customers contributing over $100,000 annually.
- The company achieved its third consecutive quarter of GAAP profitability, supported by a $30 million legal arbitration award.
- Emerging products, including Connected Asset Maintenance, accounted for over 20% of net new annual contract value.
- Full-year fiscal 2027 revenue guidance was set between $2.005 billion and $2.013 billion.

### Why it Matters
Samsara’s consistent growth and profitability demonstrate the increasing demand for AI-driven operational intelligence in asset-heavy industries like construction and logistics. By successfully cross-selling emerging products to large-scale clients, the company is solidifying its position as a critical infrastructure provider for modernizing physical operations.
