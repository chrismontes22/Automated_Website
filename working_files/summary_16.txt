This RTX 5070 Alienware laptop is perfect for everything announced at Summer Game Fest — especially at this price

Dell is currently offering a 27% discount on the Alienware 16X Aurora gaming laptop, bringing the price down to $1,999.99 for a high-performance machine featuring an NVIDIA RTX 5070.

### Key Points
- The discounted Alienware 16X Aurora (AC16251) features an Intel Core Ultra 9 275HX processor, 16GB of RAM, and a 1TB SSD.
- Performance testing shows the laptop runs demanding titles like Cyberpunk 2077 at 60 FPS with Ray Tracing enabled at 1600p resolution.
- The device includes a 16-inch WQXGA display with a 240Hz refresh rate and a durable aluminum chassis.
- Users can upgrade the internal hardware, supporting up to 64GB of DDR5 RAM and 4TB of SSD storage.
- Connectivity options include multiple HDMI, USB-C, and Thunderbolt 4 ports for versatile peripheral support.

### Why it Matters
This deal provides a significant price reduction on a high-end gaming laptop capable of handling the latest graphically intensive titles announced at Summer Game Fest. It offers a viable hardware solution for gamers seeking to balance high-fidelity performance with a more accessible price point.
