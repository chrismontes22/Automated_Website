Autozi Internet Technology (Global) Ltd. Receives NASDAQ Notification Regarding Minimum Market Value of Listed Securities
============================================================

Autozi Internet Technology has received a formal notification from Nasdaq indicating that the company failed to meet the minimum $50 million market value requirement for continued listing.

**Key points**
- Nasdaq issued the notification on March 25, 2026, following a 30-day period where Autozi’s market value remained below the $50 million threshold.
- Autozi has until September 21, 2026, a 180-day window, to regain compliance with Nasdaq Listing Rule 5450(b)(2)(A).
- Compliance will be restored if the company’s market value closes at or above $50 million for at least ten consecutive business days.
- The company’s securities will remain listed and continue trading on the Nasdaq Global Market during this grace period.

**Why it matters: **
This notification highlights the financial challenges facing the automotive service platform as it navigates strict exchange listing requirements. Failure to regain the minimum market value by the September deadline could lead to the delisting of Autozi’s shares from the Nasdaq Global Market.
