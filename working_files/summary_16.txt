iPhone 18 Pro leaks, Redmi K90 Ultra arrives, Week 27 in review
============================================================

Apple is preparing for its September 8, 2026, launch event, featuring the new iPhone 18 Pro series, the foldable iPhone Ultra, and significant battery capacity upgrades for flagship models.

### Key Points
- The iPhone 18 Pro Max will feature a 5,235 mAh battery for physical SIM models and a 5,425 mAh capacity for eSIM versions.
- Xiaomi announced the Redmi K90 Ultra in China, featuring a Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset, 165Hz AMOLED display, and an 8,550mAh battery.
- Nothing is scheduled to launch its Phone (4b) on July 7, which will include a 6,000 mAh battery and a Snapdragon 6 Gen 4 processor.
- The OnePlus N6 launched in India for INR 22,999, boasting an 8,000mAh battery and a seven-year battery health guarantee.
- Leaked documents from an Apple supplier cyberattack suggest the iPhone 18 Pro series will utilize a mix of Qualcomm and proprietary Apple C2 modems.

### Why it Matters
These hardware developments highlight a growing industry trend toward significantly larger battery capacities and extended device longevity to support intensive mobile gaming and daily usage. As manufacturers like Apple, Xiaomi, and Nothing compete for market share, these technical specifications will likely influence consumer purchasing decisions throughout the second half of 2026.
