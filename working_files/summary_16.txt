Netflix is prepping a mobile revamp with a TikTok-style vertical video feed and all
============================================================

Netflix will launch a redesigned mobile application in late April 2026, featuring a new vertical video discovery feed designed to improve user engagement with its expanding entertainment library.

### Key Points
- The updated mobile interface will officially roll out to all subscribers by the end of April 2026.
- The new vertical feed allows users to swipe through clips from movies and shows to watch, save, or share content.
- Netflix announced the redesign in its Q1 2026 shareholder letter to better align with evolving mobile viewing habits.
- The company is prioritizing mobile engagement as its video podcast offerings continue to see high daytime viewership on smartphones.
- This update follows successful internal testing of vertical video discovery and generative AI search tools conducted throughout the previous year.

### Why it Matters
This redesign signals a strategic shift as Netflix adapts its platform to compete with the short-form, vertical video formats popularized by social media competitors. By integrating these discovery tools, the company aims to increase time spent on the app and better showcase its diverse content catalog to mobile users.
