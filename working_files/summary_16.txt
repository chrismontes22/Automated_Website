Can AI Orchestrate Omnichannel Advertising?
============================================================

Advertising agencies are shifting their focus from individual campaign optimization to AI-driven workflow orchestration to manage the increasing complexity of omnichannel digital advertising and improve overall business efficiency.

### Key Points
- Modern advertising campaigns span fragmented channels including search, social, programmatic, and retail media, creating significant coordination burdens for agencies.
- The next wave of AI technology aims to connect planning, activation, reporting, and financial workflows rather than just automating isolated tasks.
- Workflow orchestration allows agencies to scale operations and improve profitability by reducing manual administrative tasks and human error.
- Successful implementation requires high-quality data, interoperability between specialized best-of-breed systems, and the ability to move intelligence directly into campaign execution.
- Emerging connectivity frameworks, such as the Model Context Protocol (MCP), are facilitating the integration of disparate advertising tools.

### Why it Matters
Transitioning from task-specific automation to comprehensive workflow orchestration represents a fundamental shift in agency economics and competitive strategy. By synchronizing disconnected systems, agencies can achieve greater operational leverage, allowing them to support more clients and drive higher-margin growth without increasing headcount.
