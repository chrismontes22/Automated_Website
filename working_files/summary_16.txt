Available in beta: Convert your client-side encrypted Slides after a Vault or Takeout export
============================================================

Google Workspace has launched a beta program allowing administrators to convert client-side encrypted Google Slides files following a data export through Google Vault or Google Takeout services.

### Key Points
- The beta program enables interoperability for client-side encrypted (CSE) Slides after exporting data.
- Eligible editions include Enterprise Plus, Education Standard, Education Plus, Frontline Plus, and Assured Controls.
- Interested administrators must apply via a registration form to gain access to the CSE Office Interop beta.
- This update follows the recent general availability of similar conversion capabilities for Google Sheets files.
- The rollout is currently active for both Rapid Release and Scheduled Release domains upon successful beta acceptance.

### Why it Matters
This update simplifies data management for organizations that rely on client-side encryption for sensitive document security. By enabling file conversion after exports, Google provides administrators with greater flexibility when migrating or auditing encrypted content across enterprise environments.
