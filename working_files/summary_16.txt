Best External Hard Drives in 2026 – For Mac, Windows, and Backup
============================================================

The 2026 market for external storage offers diverse options, ranging from high-speed Samsung SSDs for professional workflows to cost-effective Seagate and Western Digital HDDs for bulk data backups.

### Key Points
- The Samsung T9 leads portable SSDs with 2,000 MB/s speeds, while the LaCie Rugged SSD Pro reaches 2,800 MB/s via Thunderbolt 3 for creative professionals.
- The Samsung T7 Shield provides IP65-rated water and dust resistance, making it the top choice for rugged, outdoor environments.
- WD My Passport remains the standard for portable HDDs, offering up to 5TB of storage at a budget-friendly price point.
- Seagate Backup Plus Desktop drives provide the most economical solution for large-scale home backups, with capacities reaching 16TB.
- SSDs cost $60–$100 per terabyte and offer superior speed, whereas HDDs cost $15–$25 per terabyte and are best for archival storage.

### Why it Matters
Choosing the correct drive depends on balancing the need for high-speed performance against the requirement for massive, low-cost storage capacity. Selecting the right technology ensures that users optimize their hardware investment while protecting critical data from potential drive failure.
