iOS 26.5 Beta 2: Maps Ads, RCS Encryption, and New Subscription Rules
============================================================

Apple has released the iOS 26.5 developer beta 2, introducing privacy-focused advertisements within the Maps app and streamlined Bluetooth connectivity for Magic accessories across its hardware ecosystem.

### Key Points
- Apple Maps now features contextually integrated, privacy-focused advertisements that are not linked to individual Apple IDs.
- Magic keyboards, trackpads, and mice now automatically pair via Bluetooth when disconnected from a wired connection.
- The update is approximately 1.61 GB for users upgrading from the previous beta version.
- The release includes general bug fixes and performance optimizations to improve overall system stability.
- Apple maintains a consistent two-week release cycle, with the next beta expected between April 20 and April 27, 2026.

### Why it Matters
This update signals a strategic expansion of Apple's advertising revenue while reinforcing the company's commitment to user privacy. By simplifying hardware connectivity and refining system performance, Apple continues to strengthen the integration of its ecosystem for both professional users and developers.
