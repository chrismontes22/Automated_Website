The RayNeo Air 4 Pro AR Glasses Drop to a New Low Price for Prime Day
============================================================

The RayNeo Air 4 Pro wearable glasses are currently discounted by $60 for Amazon Prime Day, offering users a portable 201-inch virtual screen experience for gaming and media consumption.

### Key Points
- The RayNeo Air 4 Pro features a 120Hz refresh rate, HDR10 support, and 360-degree spatial audio by Bang & Olufsen.
- The device weighs 76 grams and connects via USB-C to compatible hardware like the Steam Deck, laptops, and mobile phones.
- Users require a separate USB-C-to-HDMI adapter or the RayNeo JoyDock to connect the glasses to a Nintendo Switch.
- The glasses function as a plug-and-play wearable monitor and do not require an internal battery or independent charging.
- Competing Viture Beast XR glasses are also discounted by 20% during the Prime Day sale, including a bundled 8BitDo controller.

### Why it Matters
These wearable displays represent a growing market for portable, high-fidelity gaming solutions that eliminate the need for traditional bulky monitors. By leveraging existing hardware power, these devices offer a convenient way for mobile gamers to enhance their visual experience while traveling.
