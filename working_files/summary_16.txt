Eros Innovation Founder Kishore Lulla On Need For ‘Sovereign’ & Culturally Specific AI
============================================================

Eros Innovation has launched ErosADI, a sovereign AI model trained on Indian cultural data, designed to provide authentic content generation while reducing film production costs by 70 percent.

### Key Points
- Eros Innovation developed the ErosADI model in partnership with the Indian Institute of Technology Madras using a library of over 10,000 Indian films.
- The company secured approximately $150 million in funding to build its AI infrastructure and expand its intellectual property portfolios.
- ErosADI features five foundational layers, including Large Cultural Models, a rights registry, and AI-native agents for video generation, editing, and dubbing.
- The technology aims to address cultural inaccuracies in global AI models, such as incorrect regional signage or character depictions in Indian settings.
- Beyond entertainment, Eros is collaborating with the Indian government to apply its AI models to sectors including healthcare, education, and tourism.

### Why it Matters
This development highlights a growing global trend toward sovereign AI, where nations and companies prioritize indigenous data to ensure cultural and linguistic accuracy. By significantly lowering production costs, this technology could disrupt traditional filmmaking and empower a new generation of creators in emerging markets.
