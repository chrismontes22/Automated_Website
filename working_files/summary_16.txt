F&O Talk: Nifty slips below 20 & 50EMA. Sudeep Shah's tips to ride monthly expiry, trade 6 big weekly movers
============================================================

Nifty faces short-term technical weakness as it slips below key moving averages, prompting analysts to recommend cautious, stock-specific strategies amid ongoing market consolidation and sector-wide earnings volatility.

### Key Points
- Nifty has fallen below its 20-day and 50-day EMA levels, with crucial support identified in the 23,650–23,700 range.
- Analysts suggest a short iron condor strategy for the upcoming monthly expiry, targeting a Nifty range between 23,696 and 24,204.
- The Nifty IT index corrected by 10.31% last week due to disappointing quarterly earnings, while the banking sector shows relative resilience.
- Reliance Industries remains at a critical support zone of 1,330–1,340, with technical indicators currently signaling bearish undertones.
- Midcap and smallcap indices continue to show underlying strength, outperforming frontline indices despite recent consolidation.
- The Nifty Metal index remains near all-time highs, serving as a tactical hedge against geopolitical uncertainty despite its inherent cyclical nature.

### Why it Matters
The current market divergence between struggling IT stocks and resilient banking and metal sectors suggests a period of range-bound trading for the Nifty. Investors are advised to prioritize selective stock picking over broad market exposure to navigate the ongoing volatility and earnings-driven pressure.
