SUI Group Treasury Rises to 108.8 Million SUI Tokens
============================================================

SUI Group Holdings Limited reported its total SUI token holdings reached 108.79 million as of May 19, valued at approximately $115 million based on current market prices.

### Key Points
- The company’s SUI holdings increased through native staking rewards and lending activity within the Sui ecosystem.
- Nearly all held tokens are currently staked, generating an estimated yield of approximately 5,200 SUI per day.
- SUI Group’s stock is trading at 0.91 times its net asset value, reflecting a narrowing discount compared to previous months.
- The firm holds 1.34 SUI tokens per share, with a current dollar value per share of $1.43.
- New strategic investments include Nof1 and Recursive Superintelligence to support the company's expansion into agentic finance.

### Why it Matters
This update highlights the evolving strategy of public companies managing crypto treasuries by prioritizing yield-generating activities over simple token accumulation. The firm's ability to trade closer to its net asset value suggests growing investor interest in professionalized management of digital asset portfolios.
