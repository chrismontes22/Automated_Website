Senate Democrats call for hearings into Trump’s ties to crypto amid CLARITY Act discussions
============================================================

Five Democratic senators are demanding committee hearings to investigate potential national security risks and conflicts of interest regarding President Donald Trump’s $1.4 billion in cryptocurrency-related financial holdings.

### Key Points
- Five Democratic ranking committee members requested hearings to examine Trump’s ties to crypto ventures, including World Liberty Financial.
- Lawmakers expressed concerns that Trump’s personal financial interests may influence his support for the Digital Asset Market Clarity (CLARITY) Act.
- Trump reported earning approximately $1.4 billion from crypto-related activities in his 2025 financial disclosure.
- Democrats are specifically seeking to investigate potential influence from United Arab Emirates-linked entities on the President’s policy decisions.
- A bipartisan bill banning the Federal Reserve from issuing a central bank digital currency until 2030 is set to become law automatically.

### Why it Matters
These calls for oversight highlight growing partisan friction over the intersection of presidential ethics and digital asset regulation. The outcome of these investigations could significantly impact the legislative path of the CLARITY Act, which requires bipartisan cooperation to overcome Senate filibuster thresholds.
