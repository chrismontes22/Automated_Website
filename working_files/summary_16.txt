Huawei promises up to 90:1 compression technology by using a proprietary dynamic algorithmic technique and a lot of SSDs
============================================================

Huawei has launched new OceanProtect X8100 and X9100 all-flash backup systems featuring a proprietary compression card that achieves data reduction ratios of up to 90:1 for enterprise storage.

### Key Points
- The new hardware compression card utilizes patented HZU algorithms to outperform existing industry standards by approximately 20%.
- Data reduction occurs across four stages, including preprocessing, multi-layer deduplication, HZBC compression, and byte-level compaction.
- The system offloads 22% of processing demand from the main CPU, improving overall performance and efficiency.
- Huawei’s latest OceanProtect models utilize QLC flash media with adaptive SLC zones to accelerate data recovery times.
- The 90:1 compression ratio represents a significant improvement over the 72:1 ratio achieved by previous generation storage systems.

### Why it Matters
By maximizing storage density on expensive all-flash media, this technology significantly lowers the cost-per-terabyte for enterprise backup infrastructure. This advancement allows businesses to maintain high-performance recovery capabilities while reducing the physical hardware footprint required for long-term data retention.
