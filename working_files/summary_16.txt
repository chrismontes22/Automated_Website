Budget-Friendly $299 Meta Glasses vs Meta Ray-Ban: Which Frames Should You Buy?
============================================================

Meta has launched its new $299 smart glasses, offering a budget-friendly alternative to the Meta Ray-Ban line with improved comfort, customizable frame styles, and advanced Meta AI integration.

### Key Points
- The glasses feature a 12-megapixel 3K camera and open-ear speakers for hands-free media capture and communication.
- Battery life lasts up to 8 hours, with an included foldable charging case providing an additional 40 hours of power.
- Connectivity is upgraded to Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.4 for faster file transfers and more reliable device pairing.
- Users can choose from three frame styles—Fury, Adventure, and Starpire—with options for prescription or transition lenses.
- The hardware includes a customizable button and an integrated touchpad for gesture-based control of volume and media playback.

### Why it Matters
By pivoting to its own branded hardware, Meta is positioning smart eyewear as a mass-market consumer product rather than a luxury accessory. This strategy could accelerate the adoption of wearable technology by lowering the entry price while maintaining high-utility features for everyday users.
