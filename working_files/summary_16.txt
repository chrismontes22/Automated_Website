Hashdex’s diversified crypto ETF adds options for hedging, income generation
============================================================

**Hashdex has launched options trading for its Nasdaq CME Crypto Index ETF (NCIQ) on Nasdaq, providing institutional investors with new tools for hedging, income generation, and risk management.**

**Key points**
- The NCIQ ETF tracks a market-cap-weighted basket of digital assets, including bitcoin, ether, XRP, and solana.
- New options contracts allow investors to implement yield-generating strategies and downside protection without liquidating their underlying ETF positions.
- The launch addresses institutional requirements for defined-outcome structures and risk-management frameworks previously unavailable for diversified crypto holdings.
- This development enables the creation of sophisticated structured products, such as capital-protected notes and defined-outcome ETFs.

**Why it matters**
The availability of options for a diversified crypto ETF removes a significant barrier for institutional capital that requires hedging capabilities before entering the market. By aligning crypto investment products with standard TradFi risk-management tools, this move facilitates larger-scale allocations and more complex financial strategies.
