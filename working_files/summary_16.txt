'AirPods Ultra' Rumored to Feature a Major Upgrade Over AirPods Pro
============================================================

Apple is reportedly developing high-end "AirPods Ultra" featuring integrated infrared cameras designed to enhance Siri’s spatial awareness and support the Visual Intelligence features found on newer iPhone models.

### Key Points
- The new earbuds will utilize infrared cameras for computer vision rather than traditional photo or video capture.
- Apple is considering the "AirPods Ultra" branding to position the device above the current $249 AirPods Pro 3.
- The technology aims to feed environmental data to Siri, similar to the infrared sensors used in iPhone Face ID systems.
- Analyst Mark Gurman clarified that these specific AirPods are not expected to support in-air gesture controls.
- The product may launch alongside a rumored "iPhone Ultra" as part of a broader expansion of Apple's premium hardware lineup.

### Why it Matters
This development signals Apple's intent to integrate advanced spatial computing and AI-driven environmental awareness directly into wearable audio hardware. By expanding the "Ultra" brand, the company aims to create a premium ecosystem of devices that leverage sophisticated sensor technology to improve user interaction with digital assistants.
