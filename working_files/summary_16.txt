Three insights you might have missed from theCUBE’s coverage of Boomi World 2026
============================================================

At Boomi World 2026, Boomi LP shifted its focus from generalized AI models toward enterprise-grade data activation, governance, and agentic workflows to help businesses scale real-time operations.

### Key Points
- Boomi introduced Agentstudio and Boomi Connect to provide unified governance and cost management for enterprise AI agents.
- The company announced a collaboration with Red Hat Inc. to pair Agentstudio with Red Hat AI, enabling private, containerized agentic environments.
- Boomi Companion was launched as an open-source suite allowing coding agents to build production-ready solutions using natural language.
- Lexitas, a Chronicle Bidco Inc. company, has successfully automated nearly 50% of its regulated payment processing using Boomi’s agentic platform.
- Boomi is prioritizing "liquid data" and low-latency pipelines to support the transition toward a "headless enterprise" driven by agent-to-agent communication.

### Why it Matters
By shifting focus from model hype to infrastructure, Boomi is addressing the critical enterprise need for secure, domain-specific AI that delivers measurable return on investment. This approach allows organizations to integrate autonomous agents into business processes while maintaining strict control over data privacy and operational governance.
