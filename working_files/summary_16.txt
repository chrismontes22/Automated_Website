Dreame’s Pet-Friendly Air Purifier Collects Fur Before It Clogs Your Filters
============================================================

Dreame is expanding its appliance portfolio with the FP10 air purifier, a new device that utilizes robotic vacuum technology to autonomously remove pet hair and maintain consistent airflow.

### Key Points
- The FP10 features a 360-degree rotating roller brush that strips hair and debris from intake air before it reaches the primary filter.
- Collected debris is compressed into a 460ml sealed bin, eliminating the need for manual pre-filter washing and maintenance.
- The unit includes a HEPA H13 filter and a CataFresh system that uses activated carbon and a metal catalyst to decompose odor molecules.
- An optional integrated weighing tray tracks pet health data and automatically adjusts fan speeds to prevent startling animals.
- The device offers a 350 cubic meter per hour airflow rate and integrates with the Dreamehome app, Google Assistant, and Amazon Alexa.
- Dreame plans to ship the FP10 in early May, positioning the product as a premium solution for households with shedding pets.

### Why it Matters
By applying mechanical self-cleaning technology to air purification, Dreame addresses the common issue of filter degradation caused by pet hair accumulation. This shift from passive filtration to active debris management could set a new standard for appliance maintenance and performance consistency in the home air quality market.
