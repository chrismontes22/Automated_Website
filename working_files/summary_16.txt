iOS 27: Here’s all the new Apple Music features
============================================================

Apple announced several updates to Apple Music during its annual WWDC conference, including redesigned artist pages, AI-enhanced AutoMix transitions, and improved performance features for the upcoming iOS 27.

### Key Points
- Apple redesigned artist pages with a prominent shuffle button and updated layout elements.
- The AutoMix feature now uses upgraded AI algorithms to create seamless transitions based on song key and tempo.
- AutoMix support is expanding to Apple TV and HomePod, while Apple TV users gain access to hi-res lossless streaming.
- The Now Playing screen now supports both portrait and landscape orientations to accommodate various device form factors.
- Performance updates include faster loading times, improved streaming reliability, and deeper integration with the new Siri AI system.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal Apple's commitment to refining the user experience through smarter automation and better cross-device consistency. By integrating advanced AI and improving core performance, Apple aims to maintain its competitive edge in the crowded music streaming market.
