9to5Mac Daily: March 25, 2026 – iPhone Fold and Siri rumors
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
Apple is reportedly developing a standalone Siri application for future operating systems while expanding its OLED display technology and launching the updated AirPods Max 2 headphones for customers.

**Key points**
- Apple is working on new protective display technologies specifically designed for a future foldable iPhone model.
- A standalone Siri application is reportedly in development for upcoming iOS 27 and macOS 27 software releases.
- The upgraded AirPods Max 2 headphones are now available for purchase with initial deliveries scheduled for April 1.
- Reports indicate that two additional Apple hardware products are slated to receive OLED display upgrades in the near future.

**Why it matters**
These developments signal Apple's ongoing commitment to integrating advanced AI features and display hardware across its core product ecosystem. The shift toward a standalone Siri app suggests a strategic effort to improve voice assistant performance and user accessibility in future software updates.
