What is AI search optimization? (& why marketers should care)
============================================================

AI search optimization, or answer engine optimization, helps brands secure citations and visibility within generative AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews to drive high-intent traffic.

### Key Points
- AI search traffic converts at significantly higher rates than traditional search, despite representing a smaller overall share of total web visits.
- Optimization strategies include using semantic HTML, server-side rendering, and clear, direct answers to improve machine readability and citation potential.
- Off-page signals from authoritative sources like Reddit, YouTube, and industry news outlets are critical for building credibility with large language models.
- Brands should avoid "hacks" like llms.txt files or artificial content fragmentation, which can violate spam policies or hinder user experience.
- Effective AEO requires monitoring brand mentions, analyzing citation patterns, and maintaining consistent entity data across third-party platforms.

### Why it Matters
Optimizing for AI search allows brands to influence the narrative in conversational interfaces where users increasingly seek recommendations and product information. By securing these citations, companies can capture high-intent leads who are further along in their purchasing journey, even when a direct click-through to the website does not occur.
