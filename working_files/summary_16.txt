STATIK MagStack Pro
============================================================

The STATIK MagStack Pro is a versatile USB-C charging cable featuring magnetic self-organizing segments, 100W power delivery, and a durable braided nylon exterior for high-speed device connectivity.

### Key Points
- The cable utilizes built-in magnetic segments to fold and stack neatly without the need for clips or ties.
- It supports up to 100W power delivery and 480 Mbps data transfer speeds for phones, tablets, and laptops.
- The heavy-duty braided nylon construction is rated to withstand up to 66 lbs of pulling force.
- Compatibility extends to a wide range of USB-C devices, including iPhone 15 models, MacBooks, Samsung Galaxy, and Google Pixel.
- The self-coiling design is specifically engineered for compact storage in travel bags, car consoles, and desk drawers.

### Why it Matters
This product addresses common consumer frustrations regarding cable clutter and physical wear by combining high-wattage charging with a tangle-free, durable design. Its broad compatibility across major mobile and computing platforms makes it a practical universal solution for users managing multiple USB-C devices.
