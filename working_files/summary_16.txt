Apple has ‘six major new product categories’ coming, says Mark Gurman
============================================================

Bloomberg analyst Mark Gurman reports that Apple is currently developing six major new product categories, including smart glasses, a tabletop robot, and various home-focused artificial intelligence hardware devices.

### Key Points
- Apple is developing a smart display, a tabletop robot, and a security camera to expand its home product ecosystem.
- The company is working on smart glasses and an AI-powered wearable pendant, both expected to rely on iPhone connectivity.
- AI-enhanced AirPods are also in development, though they represent an iteration of an existing product line rather than a new category.
- The smart display, internally referred to as "HomePad," is currently targeted for a potential launch this fall.
- The tabletop robot project is reportedly facing development delays and may not reach the market until 2027 or 2028.

### Why it Matters
These developments signal a strategic shift for Apple as it seeks to diversify its revenue beyond the iPhone by integrating artificial intelligence into new hardware form factors. Successfully launching these products could solidify Apple's position in the competitive smart home and wearable technology markets.
