German TV Market Seriencamp Unveils Plot Next Tech Strand Featuring Showrunner Behind Netflix Portugal Series ‘The Marquise’
============================================================

The German television festival Seriencamp is launching a new program called Plot Next to examine the integration of generative artificial intelligence within modern storytelling and production workflows.

### Key Points
- The Plot Next strand debuts at the Seriencamp festival in Cologne, running from June 9 to June 11.
- Showrunner Pandora de Cunha Telles will demonstrate how generative AI was utilized during the production of the upcoming Netflix series The Marquise.
- Producer Jan Diepers will present practical examples of AI-supported writing processes used for the German telenovela Rote Rosen.
- The event features 30 international speakers, including representatives from the BBC, CoSTAR, and BetaSeries.
- Industry experts will discuss how emerging technologies are currently reshaping creative hierarchies, marketing strategies, and broadcaster planning.

### Why it Matters
This initiative highlights the shift from theoretical debates about artificial intelligence toward the practical integration of these tools into professional television production pipelines. By showcasing real-world use cases, the conference provides a critical look at how automation is fundamentally altering creative workflows and long-term business models in the media industry.
