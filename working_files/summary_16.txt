TORRAS x FPF Limited Edition Review: Portugal’s 2026 World Cup Case With a Stand
============================================================

The TORRAS Q3 Air Portugal National Football Team Limited Edition case for iPhone 17 Pro Max combines advanced protective features with a design inspired by Portuguese maritime heritage.

### Key Points
- The case features the award-winning Ostand system, which includes a 360-degree rotating magnetic ring stand.
- It is built with specialized airbag technology at the corners and edges to provide enhanced impact protection.
- The design incorporates Portugal’s national colors, gold championship accents, and a textured jersey-inspired back panel.
- The accessory is fully MagSafe compatible, ensuring seamless use with wireless chargers and magnetic peripherals.
- It is priced at $69.99 and is marketed as a limited-edition release tied to the 2026 World Cup cycle.

### Why it Matters
This product represents a shift in the premium mobile accessory market toward items that prioritize cultural storytelling alongside technical utility. By blending functional hardware with specific national branding, the case aims to increase user attachment and product longevity beyond standard, mass-produced alternatives.
