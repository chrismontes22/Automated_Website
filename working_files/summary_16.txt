Melania Trump Welcomes Humanoid Robot At White House Summit
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
First Lady Melania Trump hosted the Fostering the Future Together Coalition Summit at the White House, featuring a humanoid robot to promote the integration of AI into education.

**Key points**
- The event featured Figure 3, an AI-powered humanoid robot capable of multilingual communication and physical tasks.
- Attendees included international first spouses, such as Olena Zelenska of Ukraine, Sara Netanyahu of Israel, and Brigitte Macron of France.
- The summit focused on expanding access to educational tools while strengthening digital protections for children.
- Mrs. Trump advocated for a future where humanoid robots serve as educators and philosophers for children.
- This initiative marks the first time a humanoid robot has been formally incorporated into a modern first-lady policy platform.

**Why it matters**
The inclusion of humanoid robotics in a high-level policy summit signals a shift in how public figures are framing the role of artificial intelligence in social and educational development. This event highlights the growing intersection between emerging tech hardware and government-led initiatives regarding the future of child welfare and learning.
