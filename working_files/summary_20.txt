Viture's latest XR glasses are already seeing a steep $140 price cut
============================================================

Viture has launched its latest Beast XR glasses, featuring a 174-inch virtual display and 1,250 nits of brightness, currently available with a significant $140 discount for early buyers.

### Key Points
- The Viture Beast glasses offer a 1200p resolution display with a 120Hz refresh rate for high-fidelity visual performance.
- Integrated features include Harman-tuned audio, nine levels of dimming, and built-in 3DoF spatial tracking for an immersive experience.
- The device supports real-time 2D to 3D conversion and connects via USB-C to Android phones, laptops, tablets, and gaming consoles.
- Users requiring vision correction must purchase custom prescription lenses, as the device lacks the built-in diopter adjustment found in previous models.

### Why it Matters
These glasses represent a practical alternative to bulky AR headsets by focusing on portable, high-quality media consumption and productivity. The current price reduction makes this technology more accessible to consumers looking to enhance their mobile entertainment setups without committing to emerging, unproven XR platforms.
