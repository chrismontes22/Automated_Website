Cleaning up after AI rockstar developers
============================================================

Software development teams face growing technical debt and maintenance challenges as the rapid, uncoordinated use of generative AI tools creates complex, difficult-to-manage codebases that lack long-term architectural cohesion.

### Key Points
- Generative AI tools can rapidly produce thousands of lines of code without regard for system-wide maintainability or architectural standards.
- Over-reliance on AI-generated code often leads to exponential increases in system complexity and unmanageable technical debt.
- AI-generated codebases often lack a unified design, effectively functioning as if written by hundreds of disconnected developers.
- Developers are encouraged to maintain control by using AI for small snippets rather than allowing it to dictate entire system architectures.
- Prioritizing human craftsmanship and code readability remains essential for building sustainable, long-term software solutions.

### Why it Matters
Unchecked use of generative AI can lead to a state of dependency where teams lose the ability to understand or maintain their own software systems. By prioritizing human-led engineering over automated speed, companies can avoid the long-term costs of unmanageable technical debt.
