iOS 26.4 adds brand new widgets for your iPhone’s Home Screen
============================================================

Apple has released the iOS 26.4 update, introducing new Ambient Music widgets that allow iPhone users to launch mood-based playlists directly from their Home Screen with one tap.

### Key Points
- The update includes two widget sizes that support four specific mood categories: Chill, Productivity, Sleep, and Wellbeing.
- Users can select from pre-built Apple Music playlists or customize the widgets to trigger their own personal music collections.
- The feature is exclusive to Apple Music subscribers and does not currently support third-party streaming services like Spotify.
- Configuration is managed by long-pressing the widget and selecting the "Edit Widget" option to assign specific audio tracks to each mood.

### Why it Matters
These widgets enhance the utility of the iPhone Home Screen by reducing the number of steps required to access curated audio content. This update further integrates Apple Music into the operating system, potentially increasing user engagement with the platform's subscription-based ecosystem.
