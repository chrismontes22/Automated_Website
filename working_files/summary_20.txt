Vibe Coding Will Break Your Company
============================================================

"Vibe coding," the practice of building software through natural-language AI prompts, is rapidly accelerating development cycles while exposing critical gaps in corporate governance, decision-making, and organizational judgment systems.

### Key Points
- Vibe coding allows non-technical staff to build functional applications in hours using tools like Cursor, Replit, Lovable, and v0 by Vercel.
- The speed of AI-assisted development often bypasses traditional organizational safeguards, including security, legal, and brand reviews.
- High-profile incidents, such as a Replit AI agent deleting a production database and Klarna’s reversal on AI-driven customer service, highlight the risks of deploying AI without human oversight.
- Effective AI integration requires a "Judgment System Audit" covering decision rights, override culture, contextual intelligence, learning velocity, and ethical discernment.
- Research from MIT indicates that many corporate generative AI pilots fail to deliver financial returns due to an inability to integrate technology into real-world workflows.

### Why it Matters
The shift toward AI-assisted development transforms software creation from a technical challenge into a governance and leadership issue. Companies that prioritize organizational discernment over raw development speed will be better positioned to manage the liabilities and operational risks inherent in rapid AI deployment.
