Walmart’s Gemini speaker leak suggests Google is opening up its ecosystem again
============================================================

A new Walmart Onn smart speaker featuring Google’s Gemini AI has appeared on the Connectivity Standards Alliance website, signaling a potential shift toward third-party integration for the assistant.

### Key Points
- The unreleased Onn smart speaker includes Google Cast for Audio, smart home controls, and a hardware microphone kill switch.
- Technical specifications for the device list 10W power, a far-field microphone array, and integrated LED indicators.
- The device was discovered via a listing on the Connectivity Standards Alliance website, which certifies IoT devices for universal standards.
- This product marks the first potential third-party hardware launch to feature Gemini for Home instead of the legacy Google Assistant.

### Why it Matters
This development suggests Google is reopening its ecosystem to third-party manufacturers after a long period of prioritizing its own first-party hardware. By integrating Gemini into affordable devices like the Onn line, Google could significantly accelerate the adoption of its generative AI assistant across the smart home market.
