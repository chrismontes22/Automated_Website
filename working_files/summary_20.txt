Save up to $215 USD on the Lexar 1TB & 2TB SL500 USB-C 3.2 Gen 2×2 Portable SSDs
============================================================

Lexar is offering significant discounts on its SL500 portable SSD series, with the 1TB and 2TB models now available for $124.99 and $209.99 respectively to enhance mobile storage performance.

### Key Points
- The Lexar SL500 portable SSDs offer read speeds up to 2000 MB/s and write speeds up to 1800 MB/s.
- Drives feature MagSafe compatibility for direct attachment to iPhone 12 and newer models.
- The hardware includes IP54-rated dust and water resistance and survives drops from up to 6.6 feet.
- Security is managed via Lexar DataShield 256-bit AES encryption for sensitive file protection.
- The drives support high-resolution recording, including 4K120 video for iPhones and 8K30 video for Samsung devices.

### Why it Matters
These portable drives provide a high-performance storage solution for mobile content creators who require fast transfer speeds for professional video recording. By offering MagSafe integration and broad device compatibility, Lexar simplifies external storage workflows for smartphone and computer users alike.
