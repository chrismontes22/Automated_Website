Samsung launches 990 SSD with up to 2TB capacity in India
============================================================

Samsung has launched the new 990 SSD in India, offering high-speed PCIe 4.0 performance at a lower price point by utilizing 4-bit QLC NAND flash memory technology.

### Key Points
- The drive delivers sequential read speeds of 7,250MB/s and write speeds of 6,450MB/s.
- Available in 1TB and 2TB capacities, the SSD is priced at INR 25,649 and INR 50,349.
- It features an M.2 2280 form factor and includes AES 256-bit full-disk encryption.
- Endurance ratings are 400TBW for the 1TB model and 800TBW for the 2TB version.
- Sales are scheduled to begin in India during the final week of July 2026.
- The product includes a three-year limited warranty and support for Samsung Magician software.

### Why it Matters
This release provides a cost-effective storage alternative for budget-conscious users and secondary data setups amid ongoing global memory chip shortages. By prioritizing speed over long-term endurance, Samsung is targeting the entry-level market while maintaining its competitive presence in the high-performance hardware sector.
