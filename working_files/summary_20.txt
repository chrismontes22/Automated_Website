How to better protect your growing business in an AI-powered world
============================================================

Growing businesses face heightened security risks as cybercriminals leverage AI to increase the efficacy of phishing attacks by 4.5 times, necessitating integrated, automated defenses to ensure operational stability.

### Key Points
- Microsoft’s 2025 Digital Defense Report reveals the company processes over 100 trillion security signals and blocks 4.5 million new malware files daily.
- AI-driven cyberattacks are becoming more frequent and sophisticated, specifically targeting user identities and access points within cloud-based environments.
- Small and medium-sized businesses are disproportionately affected by cyber threats due to limited dedicated security staff and round-the-clock monitoring resources.
- Microsoft 365 Business Premium integrates productivity tools with automated security features to help smaller organizations manage risks without added complexity.
- Cybersecurity is now a core business risk that directly impacts long-term growth, customer trust, and the ability to maintain daily operations.

### Why it Matters
Cybersecurity has evolved from a technical IT concern into a fundamental business risk that can cause significant financial loss and reputational damage. By prioritizing integrated security solutions, growing companies can protect their operations and maintain customer trust while scaling in an increasingly hostile digital landscape.
