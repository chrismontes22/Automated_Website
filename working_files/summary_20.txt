"I'm not going to fight the Chinese Communist Party" – Why Eve Online firm CCP Games rebranded to Fenris Creations
============================================================

CCP Games has officially rebranded as Fenris Creations and regained independence from Pearl Abyss, while securing a new research partnership and investment from Google’s AI division, DeepMind.

### Key Points
- CCP Games completed a management buyout from Pearl Abyss for $120 million, ending a partnership that began in 2018.
- The company rebranded to Fenris Creations to avoid ongoing confusion with the acronym for the Chinese Communist Party.
- Google’s DeepMind has invested in the studio and will collaborate on researching artificial intelligence within the Eve Online universe.
- The studio is currently developing two major projects, Eve Frontier and the shooter title Vanguard, to expand the Eve franchise.
- Fenris Creations plans to focus on a three-year expansion arc for Eve Online, emphasizing modernized core systems and new player experiences.

### Why it Matters
This strategic pivot allows the studio to reclaim its autonomy and distance its brand from geopolitical associations while doubling down on its core sci-fi intellectual property. By integrating DeepMind’s AI expertise and pursuing ambitious cross-platform gameplay, the company aims to secure the long-term relevance of the 23-year-old Eve Online franchise.
