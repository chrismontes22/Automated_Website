Anemoi Leak Caused by VisualArts Server Breach
============================================================

VisualArts confirmed a significant cloud storage breach that exposed the unreleased Anemoi visual novel and compromised the personal data of over 8,000 customers, job applicants, and staff members.

### Key Points
- Unauthorized third parties accessed VisualArts cloud storage via an intracompany portal, leaking Anemoi master data on April 19, 2026.
- Compromised data includes 6,000 customer email addresses, 500 Japanese individual card numbers, and 1,000 job applicant records containing home addresses.
- The breach also exposed contact information for 1,500 company representatives and personal details of over 100 current and former employees.
- VisualArts took the VA Store offline for maintenance and reported the incident to the Japanese Personal Information Protection Commission on May 11, 2026.

### Why it Matters
This security failure highlights the critical risks companies face when centralizing sensitive personal data and intellectual property within cloud-based portal systems. The incident forces a temporary suspension of digital sales channels and necessitates a costly overhaul of the company's internal cybersecurity infrastructure.
