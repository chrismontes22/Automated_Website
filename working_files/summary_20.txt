The AI-Powered Status Quo: AI Transformation is a Test of Leadership
============================================================

ServiceNow leaders argue that businesses must move beyond simple automation to fundamentally transform their operations and business models by adopting an AI-first mindset for future competitiveness.

### Key Points
- Organizations often use AI to accelerate legacy processes rather than redesigning them for modern, intelligent operations.
- The "WWAID" (What Would AI Do) framework encourages leaders to question existing workflows before applying automation to them.
- Companies should pursue two growth curves simultaneously: iterative AI for efficiency and innovative AI for creating new business value.
- AI maturity evolves from basic task automation to human augmentation and, eventually, autonomous agentic workflows.
- Leaders must avoid the trap of using AI solely for cost reduction, which risks reinforcing outdated organizational structures.

### Why it Matters
Focusing exclusively on efficiency risks creating a faster version of an obsolete business model that fails to innovate. By shifting from automation to strategic reinvention, leaders can leverage AI to build more resilient, competitive organizations capable of long-term growth.
