South Korean startup FuriosaAI is expanding its global footprint by deploying its RNGD artificial intelligence accelerators within Equinix data centers located in Lisbon, Portugal.

### Key Points
- FuriosaAI is targeting the European market with its specialized RNGD hardware designed for high-performance AI workloads.
- The deployment utilizes Equinix’s infrastructure in Lisbon to provide localized access to advanced machine learning acceleration.
- RNGD accelerators are engineered to optimize efficiency and performance for enterprise-grade AI applications.
- This move marks a significant international expansion for the Seoul-based chip manufacturer as it competes with established silicon providers.

### Why it Matters
This expansion highlights the growing demand for specialized AI hardware as companies seek alternatives to traditional GPU-heavy infrastructure to manage rising computational costs. By establishing a presence in European data centers, FuriosaAI aims to capture market share from incumbents while providing enterprises with more flexible options for scaling their artificial intelligence operations.
