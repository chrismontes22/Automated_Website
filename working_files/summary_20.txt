Elon Musk Says SpaceX Will Be Worth More Than the Entire Earth Because Musk Will Harvest the Sun
============================================================

Elon Musk claims SpaceX could eventually exceed Earth’s total GDP by leveraging space-based solar energy and orbital compute infrastructure to overcome terrestrial power and labor constraints for future industries.

### Key Points
- SpaceX is generating over $26 billion annually from AI compute contracts with companies like Anthropic and Google.
- Anthropic is paying $1.25 billion monthly through 2029 for access to the Colossus 1 data center in Memphis.
- Musk proposes using orbital solar satellites to capture vast amounts of solar energy, potentially increasing available power by 100,000 times.
- The strategy relies on Starship launch costs continuing to decline to make space-based manufacturing and energy production economically viable.
- China recently achieved its first orbital-class reusable rocket landing, signaling increased competition for SpaceX’s core launch business.

### Why it Matters
This vision positions SpaceX as the foundational infrastructure provider for a future economy driven by AI and unlimited space-based energy. If successful, the company could fundamentally shift global productivity, though it currently faces significant financial hurdles and rising international competition in the aerospace sector.
