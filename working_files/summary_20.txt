Ross Gerber Says 'Pay Attention' To Nvidia And Micron As Jensen Huang Predicts AI Explosion
============================================================

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang and Dell Technologies CEO Michael Dell suggest the semiconductor industry is entering a decade-long AI expansion cycle that will fundamentally reshape global computing demand.

### Key Points
- Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang and Dell CEO Michael Dell stated the industry is in the early stages of a long-term AI infrastructure buildout.
- The executives anticipate the deployment of billions of AI agents, which will function as digital workers across various industries.
- Increased reliance on AI agents is expected to drive sustained demand for advanced computing resources, memory, and data center storage.
- Investor Ross Gerber highlighted the comments on social media, specifically pointing to Nvidia Corp. and Micron Technology Inc. as key companies to watch.

### Why it Matters
This shift suggests that semiconductor demand may no longer follow traditional boom-and-bust cycles, potentially signaling a new era of structural growth for hardware providers. Investors are closely monitoring these developments as the integration of AI agents into enterprise operations necessitates massive investments in global computing capacity.
