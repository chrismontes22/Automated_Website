Google Voice launches two new personal plans with Workspace perks
============================================================

Google has introduced two new paid subscription tiers for Google Voice, named Starter and Standard, offering enhanced business features like 24/7 support, call routing, and Gemini-powered voice notes.

### Key Points
- The Google Voice Starter plan costs $10 per month, while the Standard plan is priced at $20 per month.
- Google is currently offering a 50% discount on the Standard plan for the first six months of service.
- Starter features include 24/7 support, three-way calling, and support for desk phones and analog telephone adapters.
- The Standard plan adds professional tools such as auto-attendants, call routing, and Gemini-integrated voice note capabilities.
- Existing free Google Voice accounts remain available for users who do not require these additional business-oriented features.

### Why it Matters
These new tiers provide small businesses with more affordable, scalable options for managing professional communications without committing to high-end enterprise plans. By integrating AI tools like Gemini, Google is positioning Voice as a more competitive solution for companies seeking to modernize their telephony infrastructure.
