Bitcoin advances, holds above key technical level that ether, solana can't break through
============================================================

Bitcoin’s market dominance has climbed to 59% as the cryptocurrency maintains key technical support levels while major altcoins like ether and solana struggle to sustain similar momentum.

### Key Points
- Bitcoin rose 2.4% to approximately $62,800, while the CoinDesk 20 Index increased by 2.3% to 1,690.
- Altcoins BEAT and VELVET recorded significant gains of over 500% and 800% respectively, despite concerns regarding concentrated ownership and market volatility.
- Derivatives data shows $378 million in liquidations over 24 hours, with traders favoring put options as a hedge against potential market instability.
- Synthetic pre-IPO futures for companies like SpaceX and OpenAI are driving speculative interest, though these contracts carry high risks and lack underlying equity ownership.
- Bitcoin’s 30-day implied volatility remains steady below 50%, suggesting investors do not anticipate major crypto market disruptions from the upcoming SpaceX IPO.

### Why it Matters
The shift in capital toward Bitcoin highlights a broader trend of investor risk aversion as major altcoins fail to hold critical technical support lines. This consolidation suggests that market participants are prioritizing the stability of the largest cryptocurrency over speculative assets while hedging against potential volatility in the wider financial sector.
