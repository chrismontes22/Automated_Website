Nomad has released a compact 65W slim charger featuring dual USB-C ports, offering a portable power solution for laptops, tablets, and mobile devices at a discounted price point.

### Key Points
- The Nomad charger utilizes Silicon Carbide (SiC) technology to maintain a credit-card-sized footprint with a 1.2-inch thickness.
- It provides a maximum output of 65W when using a single port or a split output of 45W and 20W when using both ports simultaneously.
- The device is designed for high portability, making it suitable for travel or as a gift for students.
- The product is currently available at one of its lowest recorded prices for a limited time.
- The package does not include charging cables, requiring users to provide their own compatible hardware.

### Why it Matters
This charger represents a growing market trend toward high-wattage, ultra-compact power adapters that can replace bulkier laptop bricks. Its ability to charge multiple devices simultaneously provides a versatile, space-saving solution for professionals and students who prioritize portability.
