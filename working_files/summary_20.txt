Samsung Galaxy Unpacked Summer 2026: How to watch and what to expect
============================================================

Samsung is expected to host a Galaxy Unpacked event on July 22, 2026, in London to unveil its latest foldable smartphone lineup, including a rumored new Wide Fold model.

### Key Points
- Samsung may introduce up to four foldable devices, including the Galaxy Z Fold 8, Z Flip 8, and a budget-friendly Z Flip 8 FE.
- The rumored "Wide Fold" device is expected to feature a 7.6-inch inner display and a 4:3 aspect ratio to compete with upcoming foldable iPhone models.
- Hardware upgrades for the Z Fold 8 likely include a 5,000mAh battery, 45W fast charging, and the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset.
- The event is expected to showcase improved hinge technology designed to minimize the visible display crease across the new foldable range.
- Samsung may also debut the Galaxy Watch 9 alongside its new mobile hardware during the summer presentation.

### Why it Matters
This event represents a strategic shift for Samsung as it expands its foldable portfolio to defend its market share against anticipated competition from Apple. By introducing a wider form factor and a budget-friendly model, the company aims to solidify its dominance in the premium foldable smartphone segment.
