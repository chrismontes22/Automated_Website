S&P launches blockchain fundamentals index for digital assets
============================================================

S&P Dow Jones Indices and Pantera Capital have launched a new blockchain fundamentals index that ranks digital assets based on protocol revenue rather than traditional market capitalization metrics.

### Key Points
- The index tracks 18 blockchain networks, with Ether, BNB, Solana, TRON, and Hyperliquid serving as the five largest holdings.
- Eligible assets must meet specific thresholds for liquidity, market capitalization, and protocol revenue over the previous two quarters.
- Constituents are rebalanced quarterly, with individual holdings capped at 35% for the largest asset and 20% for others.
- The benchmark excludes assets like Bitcoin and XRP that do not meet the specific protocol revenue criteria required for inclusion.
- This rules-based framework aims to provide institutional investors with a tool to distinguish established blockchain activity from speculative market exposure.

### Why it Matters
This index represents a shift toward fundamental analysis in the digital asset space, moving away from price-based benchmarks toward metrics that reflect actual network utility. By prioritizing protocol revenue, the product offers institutional investors a more structured approach to portfolio diversification as the crypto market matures.
