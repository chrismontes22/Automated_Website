Trump made $1 billion from crypto, financial disclosure shows
============================================================

President Donald Trump reported earning over $1.4 billion from cryptocurrency ventures in 2025, according to a new financial disclosure filed with the Office of Government Ethics.

### Key Points
- CIC Digital LLC generated $636 million, primarily from a $635 million licensing deal for the $TRUMP meme coin.
- World Liberty Financial contributed $526 million through token sales and $65 million from equity sales.
- Stablecoin Holdco, LLC, the parent company of World Liberty Financial, provided an additional $196.8 million in income.
- Trump’s global real estate portfolio, including Mar-a-Lago, earned at least $389 million throughout the year.
- Legal settlements against media and technology companies resulted in $86 million in revenue for the president.
- The disclosure lists over $370,000 in gifts, including a $250,000 statue from Sticker Mule CEO Anthony Constantino.

### Why it Matters
These figures highlight the significant financial intersection between the president's personal business interests and the rapidly evolving cryptocurrency market. The scale of these earnings raises ongoing questions regarding potential conflicts of interest and the influence of digital asset ventures on executive policy.
