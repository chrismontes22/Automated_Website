Semafor’s new AI tool helped boil down its entire flagship conference into nine takeaways
============================================================

Semafor has launched Semafor Intelligence, an AI-assisted editorial product that uses custom machine learning tools to synthesize transcripts and insights from its global economic events into thematic reports.

### Key Points
- Semafor Intelligence debuted following the April 13 Semafor World Economy event, which featured over 500 executives and nine U.S. cabinet officials.
- The tool utilizes embedding models from Voyage to vectorize and cluster themes from hundreds of hours of event transcripts.
- Journalists curate and edit all AI-generated outputs to ensure accuracy, with all quotes linked to timestamped YouTube source videos.
- Development of the custom tool took only days to complete, with the total operational cost for the first report totaling a few hundred dollars.
- Future reports are planned for upcoming events, including Silicon Valley & The World and The Next Three Billion.

### Why it Matters
This initiative demonstrates how newsrooms can leverage AI to rapidly process massive datasets that would be impossible for human teams to analyze manually. By integrating these tools into its events business, Semafor aims to increase editorial output and provide high-value insights while maintaining human oversight to prevent AI hallucinations.
