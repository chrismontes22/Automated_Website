Timekettle X1 Meeting Hub Translates 52 Languages for up to 50 People
============================================================

Timekettle has launched the X1 Meeting Interpreter Hub, an AI-powered device that facilitates real-time translation for up to 50 participants across 52 languages and 106 different accents.

### Key Points
- The X1 Meeting Interpreter Hub supports simultaneous interpretation for up to five languages in a single session.
- Up to 10 hubs can be synced together to accommodate larger groups, with remote participants joining via secure meeting links.
- The device weighs 199 grams and eliminates the need for traditional interpretation booths or complex hardware setups.
- Participants can access live translated audio and text on their own devices by scanning a QR code without downloading an app.
- The hub features 32GB of local storage to ensure meeting transcripts remain confidential by avoiding cloud-based data processing.
- The standard package is priced at $849 and is currently available through Timekettle’s website, Amazon, and global retail partners.

### Why it Matters
This device lowers the barrier to entry for international collaboration by replacing expensive, professional interpretation infrastructure with a portable, automated solution. By prioritizing local data storage, it addresses critical corporate security concerns regarding sensitive business communications and proprietary information.
