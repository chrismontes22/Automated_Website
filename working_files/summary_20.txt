Navigating AI Rules in Licensing: Don’t Let “Magic” Tools Hurt Your Earnings
============================================================

500px has implemented strict new guidelines for AI-assisted photography, mandating that contributors limit digital retouching to 10% of an image while banning generative AI for all commercial content submissions.

### Key Points
- Retouching is limited to 10% of total pixels, covering minor tasks like dust removal and color correction.
- AI tools cannot be used to alter human subjects, including facial expressions, body proportions, or physical features.
- Generative AI features, such as text-to-image prompts or canvas expansion, are strictly prohibited for all commercial licensing.
- All submissions must originate from a physical camera sensor and include valid model releases for any individuals depicted.
- Violating these policies can lead to immediate image rejection, account suspension, or a permanent ban from the platform.

### Why it Matters
These regulations protect the integrity of the 500px marketplace by ensuring that commercial assets remain authentic and legally compliant. Photographers must adapt their post-processing workflows to avoid account penalties and maintain their ability to generate licensing revenue.
