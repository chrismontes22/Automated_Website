nubia is launching an AI agent smartphone later this month
============================================================

Nubia will debut its new AI agent smartphone featuring deep system-level integration at the World Artificial Intelligence Conference in Shanghai, scheduled for July 17 through July 20.

### Key Points
- The upcoming device is expected to be the successor to the nubia M153, which launched earlier this year with ByteDance’s Doubao AI assistant.
- Nubia claims the new handset will be the world’s first smartphone to feature a system-level AI agent capable of autonomous cross-platform operations.
- The official unveiling is set to take place at the World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC) held in Shanghai, China.
- Previous hardware iterations, such as the M153, utilized the Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset alongside 16GB of RAM and 512GB of internal storage.

### Why it Matters
This launch signals a shift toward mobile devices that prioritize autonomous software agents over traditional app-based navigation. By integrating AI at the system level, manufacturers aim to streamline user workflows and increase the functional capabilities of modern smartphones.
