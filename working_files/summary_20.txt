Google Cloud fraud defense, the next evolution of reCAPTCHA
============================================================

Google Cloud has launched Fraud Defense, a new security platform designed to verify and manage autonomous AI agents and bots while protecting businesses from evolving digital fraud threats.

### Key Points
- Google Cloud Fraud Defense serves as the next evolution of reCAPTCHA, providing tools to identify, classify, and analyze both human and AI agent traffic.
- The platform includes an agentic policy engine that allows businesses to block or permit automated traffic based on risk scores and specific agent identities.
- A new AI-resistant, QR code-based challenge forces human verification to deter malicious automated requests and make large-scale fraud economically unviable.
- The service leverages Google’s global fraud intelligence graph, which currently protects over 14 million domains and 50% of Fortune 100 companies.
- Existing reCAPTCHA customers are automatically transitioned to the Fraud Defense platform with no required migration, pricing changes, or integration updates.

### Why it Matters
As autonomous AI agents increasingly perform complex online transactions, businesses face new risks from sophisticated synthetic identity fraud and automated abuse. This platform provides a unified security model that secures the entire customer journey, enabling companies to safely embrace AI-driven commerce while maintaining trust and conversion rates.
