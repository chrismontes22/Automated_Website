Media And Tech Veteran Dawn Ostroff Joins Board Of AI Advertising Firm Moloco
============================================================

Former Spotify executive Dawn Ostroff has joined the board of directors at Moloco, an AI-native advertising firm, to help guide the company's global expansion and digital strategy.

### Key Points
- Dawn Ostroff previously served as Chief Content & Advertising Business Officer at Spotify and president of Condé Nast Entertainment.
- Moloco operates an AI-driven advertising platform that supports mobile apps, connected-TV platforms, and retail media businesses.
- The company maintains a global presence, operating in more than 200 countries and territories with offices in the U.S. and UK.
- Ostroff currently holds board positions at Mattel and Sweetgreen and previously advised on the acquisition of Activision Blizzard by Microsoft.

### Why it Matters
Ostroff’s appointment signals Moloco’s intent to scale its influence within the competitive digital advertising sector as AI-driven data systems become increasingly central to the internet economy. Her extensive background in media transformation will likely assist the company in navigating complex shifts in how businesses monetize content and reach global audiences.
