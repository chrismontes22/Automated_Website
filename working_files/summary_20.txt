U.S. regulatory developments and earnings, ECB rate decision: Crypto Week Ahead
============================================================

Crypto markets face a busy week of regulatory deadlines in Washington D.C., major macroeconomic data releases, and significant token unlocks that could influence digital asset price volatility.

### Key Points
- The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency closes its comment period for proposed GENIUS Act stablecoin regulations on July 24.
- The Commodity Futures Trading Commission concludes its comment window regarding 24/7 trading and perpetual-style bitcoin futures on July 27.
- The European Central Bank is scheduled to announce its latest interest-rate decision on July 23, with markets widely anticipating a hold.
- Major token unlocks are scheduled throughout the week, including significant distributions for Worldcoin, Avalanche, and Toncoin.
- Corporate earnings reports from Tesla, Nasdaq, and CME Group provide additional data points for broader market sentiment.

### Why it Matters
These regulatory and macroeconomic events represent critical inflection points for institutional adoption and the legal framework governing stablecoins and derivatives. Investors should monitor these developments closely, as they may dictate market liquidity and the long-term compliance landscape for digital assets.
