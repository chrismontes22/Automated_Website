"The best gaming keyboard I've ever used": ASUS gave its ROG Azoth Extreme a golden glow-up to celebrate 20 years, and the results are spectacular
============================================================

ASUS has launched the limited-edition ROG Azoth Extreme Edition 20 mechanical keyboard, a premium 75% peripheral celebrating the brand's 20th anniversary with gold accents and high-end performance features.

### Key Points
- The keyboard retails for $599.99 and is scheduled for a full retail launch on July 19.
- It features a 1.47-inch OLED display, a customizable control knob, and an adjustable gasket for varying typing resistance.
- Connectivity options include USB-C, Bluetooth, and a 2.4GHz wireless mode utilizing SpeedNova technology for extended battery life.
- The device supports an 8,000Hz polling rate via an included booster dongle and uses hot-swappable ROG NX mechanical switches.
- Design elements include a black aluminum alloy chassis with 24K gold-plated accents and transparent keycaps.

### Why it Matters
This release highlights the shift of gaming peripherals into the ultra-premium luxury market, targeting dedicated enthusiasts willing to pay a significant price for aesthetic and build quality. While the high cost may limit its mass-market appeal, the keyboard serves as a technical showcase for ASUS's engineering capabilities in the competitive gaming hardware space.
