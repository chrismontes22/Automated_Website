Acer Swift Air 14 is the first Acer in years that I actually might want to use: Another $699 Windows laptop trades blows with MacBook Neo
============================================================

Acer unveiled the Swift Air 14 at Computex 2026, a $699 Windows laptop featuring an Intel Core Ultra 3 processor designed to compete directly with Apple’s MacBook Neo.

### Key Points
- The Acer Swift Air 14 features a 14-inch 120Hz display, 8GB of RAM, and a lightweight 1.19kg metal chassis.
- Internal hardware includes Intel’s latest "Wildcat Lake" Core Ultra 3 chipset, promising up to 16 hours of battery life.
- Connectivity options include two USB-C ports, one USB-A port, and a 3.5mm headphone jack.
- The device includes a 1080p webcam with Windows Hello facial recognition support for secure authentication.
- It is positioned as a direct rival to the MacBook Neo, offering a higher refresh rate display and more versatile port selection.

### Why it Matters
The emergence of the Swift Air 14 signals a shift in the midrange laptop market as Windows OEMs respond to increased competition from Apple. This trend benefits consumers by driving innovation and value in the $600 to $700 price segment.
