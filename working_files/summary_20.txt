Andrej Karpathy Joins Anthropic
============================================================

**Renowned AI researcher Andrej Karpathy has joined Anthropic to lead a team focused on using Claude to accelerate pre-training research, marking a significant shift in the competitive AI landscape.**

### Key Points
- Andrej Karpathy, a founding member of OpenAI and former Tesla AI director, joined Anthropic on May 19, 2026.
- Karpathy’s new team will focus on recursive self-improvement, using Claude to optimize the pre-training phase of future model development.
- Anthropic has recently attracted numerous high-level executives, including former CTOs from companies like Workday, Instagram, and You.com, into individual contributor research roles.
- Anthropic reached $30 billion in annual recurring revenue (ARR) in early 2026, surpassing OpenAI in growth velocity.
- The company is leveraging a partnership with SpaceX to utilize xAI’s Colossus 1 data center for increased compute capacity.

### Why it Matters
The migration of top-tier technical talent to Anthropic serves as a strong market signal that the industry's most influential researchers view the company's infrastructure and research trajectory as the current frontier. This trend suggests that the next phase of AI advancement will be driven by recursive research capabilities, potentially widening the performance gap between Anthropic and its competitors.
