AI is everyone's job now
============================================================

New data from Indeed reveals that AI-related job postings are rapidly expanding beyond the technology sector into diverse fields like healthcare, education, and logistics across major global markets.

### Key Points
- AI-labeled job titles in the United States have more than tripled since 2022.
- Most AI-focused roles are now located outside the tech industry in five of the six major markets studied.
- Hiring demand for AI-proficient workers has spread to roles including truck drivers, physical therapists, HR managers, and teachers.
- Germany, France, the UK, and the Netherlands are seeing significant AI integration across white-collar professions.
- Spain remains the only studied market where AI hiring is still primarily concentrated within traditional technology companies.

### Why it Matters
The shift indicates that AI is evolving from a specialized technical field into a fundamental tool for augmenting productivity across the broader global economy. This trend suggests that workforce demand is moving toward employees who can effectively utilize AI applications rather than those who exclusively build the underlying software.
