This Tech Billionaire's Net Worth Crossed $300 Billion In April On AI Rally As Elon Musk Stays Richest In The World
============================================================

Google co-founder Larry Page joined the $300 billion club in April as an artificial intelligence-driven stock market rally significantly boosted the net worth of the world's wealthiest individuals.

### Key Points
- Larry Page’s net worth reached $313 billion following a 33% surge in Alphabet Inc. shares during April.
- Elon Musk remains the world's richest person with a $782 billion fortune, despite a $35 billion decline due to revised SpaceX stake estimates.
- The S&P 500 and Nasdaq recorded their strongest monthly gains since 2020, lifting the combined wealth of the top ten billionaires to $2.7 trillion.
- Sergey Brin rose to third place with $289 billion, while Jeff Bezos moved to fourth despite a $49 billion increase in his Amazon holdings.
- For the first time in over three years, the top ten richest people globally are all American citizens, following Bernard Arnault’s exit from the list.

### Why it Matters
This shift highlights how investor optimism surrounding artificial intelligence and cloud computing is heavily concentrating wealth within the U.S. technology sector. The dominance of American billionaires underscores the significant influence that domestic market performance and AI-driven growth have on global economic rankings.
