New Company Hopes to Build Age-Verification Tech into Vape Cartridges
============================================================

**Ispire Technology and Chemular have launched Ike Tech, a new biometric and blockchain-based verification system designed to prevent underage access to disposable vape cartridges through smartphone-linked Bluetooth authentication.**

**Key points**
- Ike Tech uses ID scanning and facial recognition to verify user age before unlocking a vape device via Bluetooth.
- The system utilizes anonymized blockchain tokens to protect consumer privacy during the identity verification process.
- Once authorized, the device remains unlocked only while in proximity to the user's smartphone.
- Future planned features include geo-fencing to automatically disable devices near schools or on airplanes.
- Developers intend to license this biometric technology to other e-cigarette manufacturers and potentially expand into firearm safety.

**Why it matters**
This technology addresses significant regulatory pressure regarding youth vaping by providing a verifiable, automated method for age restriction. If successfully implemented, the system could set a new industry standard for hardware-based compliance and safety across various consumer product categories.
