Gemini for Home is finally expanding to more countries, and you can try it now
============================================================

Google is expanding its Gemini for Home voice assistant to 16 new countries and seven additional languages while introducing performance upgrades that reduce smart home command latency by 40%.

### Key Points
- Gemini for Home is rolling out to 16 new countries across Europe and the Asia-Pacific region, including France, Japan, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
- The assistant now supports seven additional languages, bringing the total to 10, including German, Italian, Japanese, and Dutch.
- Google claims a 40% improvement in smart home response times for common tasks like operating lights.
- New features include enhanced context awareness for device control, world-aware alarms, and improved live camera search for Google Home Premium subscribers.
- Users can opt into the early access program via the Google Home app, with the rollout expected to reach eligible regions within one week.

### Why it Matters
This expansion transforms Gemini for Home into a truly global product, significantly increasing its accessibility for international users within the Google ecosystem. By improving latency and device understanding, Google is positioning its AI to compete more effectively against established smart home assistants in the global market.
