Nuki’s one-touch retrofit smart lock got its first-ever discount
============================================================

The Nuki Smart Lock is currently available for $149 on Amazon during the Big Spring Sale, marking the lowest price ever for this feature-packed, retrofit home security device.

**Key points**
- The Nuki Smart Lock is currently discounted to $149, down from its previous retail price of $199.99.
- The device features Matter-over-Thread support, which improves battery life and connectivity compared to standard Wi-Fi.
- Nuki recently removed its $5.90 monthly subscription requirement, making remote unlocking and activity notifications free for all users.
- The retrofit design allows for installation over existing deadlocks in minutes without replacing the entire door hardware.
- The lock includes proximity sensing to automatically unlock doors as users approach.

**Why it matters: **
This discount offers consumers a more affordable entry point into smart home security while providing full access to premium features that previously required a monthly subscription. The integration of Matter-over-Thread ensures the device remains compatible with major smart home ecosystems, increasing its long-term utility for homeowners.
