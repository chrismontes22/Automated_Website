The Dan Ives era at Wedbush is over. These are some of the calls — and colorful fits — people will remember.
============================================================

Prominent tech analyst Dan Ives is departing Wedbush Securities after eight years to launch a new merchant bank focused on technology, energy, infrastructure, and financial sectors.

### Key Points
- Dan Ives spent eight years at Wedbush Securities, gaining recognition for his bullish tech market predictions and distinctive personal fashion style.
- The new merchant bank will provide specialized financial services to large corporations and high-net-worth individuals.
- Ives is noted for early, accurate calls on Apple’s services growth, Microsoft’s enterprise AI integration, and Nvidia’s dominance in the semiconductor market.
- Beyond finance, Ives previously chaired the board of Eightco Holdings and launched a collaborative streetwear clothing line with Brooklyn-based label Snow Milk.

### Why it Matters
The departure of a high-profile analyst like Ives signals a potential shift in the market research landscape as he transitions from traditional equity analysis to merchant banking. His move highlights the growing intersection between tech-focused financial advisory and the broader capital markets as AI infrastructure continues to drive significant investment.
