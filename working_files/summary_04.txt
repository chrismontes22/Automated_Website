Aqara Smart Lock U400 Review: Hands-free Unlocking at Its Best
============================================================

The $270 Aqara Smart Lock U400 offers advanced hands-free unlocking for Apple users by utilizing ultra-wideband technology, while providing universal smart home compatibility through the Matter connectivity standard.

**Key points**
- Features hands-free unlocking via Apple Home Key using ultra-wideband (UWB) signals from iPhones and Apple Watches.
- Supports the Matter smart home standard and Thread networking for broad ecosystem integration without requiring a proprietary hub.
- Includes multiple entry methods: a fingerprint scanner, backlit keypad, NFC-based unlocking, and a physical keyhole.
- Powered by a USB-C rechargeable battery pack with an estimated life of up to six months per charge.
- Compatible with Samsung Galaxy devices via Aliro, though hands-free functionality for Android remains limited or pending future updates.

**Why it matters**
The U400 represents a significant step forward in smart home convenience by leveraging UWB for precise, proximity-based access that outperforms traditional Bluetooth-based locks. Its reliance on the Matter standard ensures long-term interoperability, making it a versatile choice for users invested in diverse smart home ecosystems.
