The versatile Play speaker is a great way into the Sonos world
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
The new $299 Sonos Play is a versatile, portable Wi-Fi and Bluetooth speaker designed to bridge the gap between home audio systems and rugged, grab-and-go outdoor listening.

**Key points**
- The Sonos Play features an IP67 dust and water-resistant rating, a 24-hour battery life, and weighs 2.87 pounds.
- Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3, AirPlay 2, and a USB-C port that supports line-in audio via a separate adapter.
- The speaker utilizes Auto Trueplay for environmental tuning and supports voice control through Amazon Alexa or Sonos Voice Control.
- It sits between the Roam 2 and Move 2 in the product lineup, offering a balance of portability and sound output.
- The device does not include a wall adapter for its charging base, requiring an 18W charger for operation.

**Why it matters**
The Sonos Play serves as a flexible entry point for new users and a functional expansion tool for existing owners who want to move their audio system between rooms or outdoors. It represents a strategic middle ground in the company's portable lineup, prioritizing versatility over the specialized stationary performance of the Era series.
