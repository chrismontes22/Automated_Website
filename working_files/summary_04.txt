Volkswagen blocks Home Assistant by requiring client assertion
============================================================

Users of the Home Assistant Volkswagen Carnet integration are reporting authentication failures that prevent login, despite the official Volkswagen mobile app and web portal remaining fully functional for them.

### Key Points
- Users report an "authentication expired" error when attempting to log in through the Home Assistant integration.
- The official Volkswagen Connect Android app and the vwgroup.io web portal continue to operate without issues.
- Affected users have confirmed their credentials are valid and that no multi-factor authentication is enabled on their accounts.
- The bug prevents the integration from connecting to vehicle data, rendering the Home Assistant dashboard unable to interact with the car.

### Why it Matters
This issue disrupts the ability of Home Assistant users to monitor and control their Volkswagen vehicles through their smart home interface. It highlights a potential compatibility break between the third-party integration and Volkswagen's authentication servers, requiring a technical update to restore service.
