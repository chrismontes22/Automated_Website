Aura’s delightful Aspen photo frame is on sale for $30 off this weekend
============================================================

Aura has launched a pre-Mother’s Day sale on its connected digital photo frames, including the Aspen model, which is currently discounted to $199 at major retailers like Amazon.

### Key Points
- The Aura Aspen digital frame is currently $30 off, matching its all-time lowest price at Amazon, Best Buy, and the official Aura website.
- Users can upload unlimited photos and 30-second video clips via the Aura app without paying any recurring subscription fees.
- The 12-inch Aspen display features a 1600 x 1200 resolution and an antiglare finish designed to mimic the appearance of traditional printed photographs.
- Remote family members can instantly add new images or captions to the frame from anywhere in the world using the dedicated mobile application.
- Additional models, including the 10.1-inch Carver Mat and the 15-inch Walden, are also available for consumers seeking different sizes and mounting options.

### Why it Matters
These discounts provide a cost-effective entry point for consumers looking to purchase connected home technology for gift-giving occasions. By removing subscription barriers and simplifying remote photo sharing, Aura continues to capture market share in the digital home decor space.
