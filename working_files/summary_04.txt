Bitcoin rebounds to $64K after AI-led selloff triggers crypto rout. Here's what experts say
============================================================

Bitcoin and Ethereum experienced a modest recovery following a period of volatility as investors balanced concerns over U.S. macroeconomic signals against broader risk-off sentiment in global financial markets.

### Key Points
- Bitcoin recovered to approximately $64,000 after briefly dipping below the $62,500 threshold during a period of market instability.
- Ethereum reached a high of $1,946 before experiencing profit-taking in alignment with broader U.S. market trends.
- Major altcoins including BNB, XRP, Solana, and Dogecoin recorded gains of up to 4.81% over the past 24-hour period.
- Analysts noted that softer-than-expected U.S. inflation data helped mitigate investor fears regarding potential interest rate hikes.
- Market participants remain cautious while awaiting further clarity on U.S. regulatory developments and upcoming macroeconomic indicators.

### Why it Matters
The recent price fluctuations highlight the sensitivity of cryptocurrency markets to U.S. economic data and shifting investor risk appetite. Continued volatility suggests that digital assets remain closely tied to broader macroeconomic trends and regulatory uncertainty, impacting short-term trading strategies for institutional and retail investors.
