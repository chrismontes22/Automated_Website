Hue’s wired wall modules bring non-smart lights into its ecosystem
============================================================

Philips Hue has introduced its first wired wall switch modules in Europe, alongside new affordable Play lamps and upgraded E14 candle bulbs featuring Matter-over-Thread compatibility for global markets.

### Key Points
- The new in-wall relay modules allow users to integrate non-smart lighting fixtures into the Philips Hue app ecosystem.
- Wired wall switch modules are currently exclusive to the European market, with prices starting at €44.99.
- New Play table and floor lamps offer a lower-cost alternative to the Signe series, starting at $79.99.
- Upgraded E14 candle bulbs now support Matter-over-Thread, enabling direct connectivity with Apple Home and Google Home without a Hue Bridge.
- The updated candle bulbs feature an expanded white light spectrum of 1000-20,000K and improved dimming capabilities down to 0.2%.

### Why it Matters
These product additions expand the Hue ecosystem by bridging the gap between traditional wired lighting and smart home automation. By adopting the Matter-over-Thread standard, Philips Hue is positioning its hardware to be more interoperable with competing smart home platforms.
