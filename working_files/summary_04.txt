Aqara Smart Lock U400 Review: Hands-free Unlocking at Its Best
============================================================

The $270 Aqara Smart Lock U400 offers advanced hands-free entry for Apple users by utilizing ultra-wideband technology to unlock doors automatically as residents approach with an iPhone or watch.

**Key points**
- The U400 supports Matter and Thread, allowing for seamless integration across various smart home ecosystems without requiring a proprietary hub.
- Unlocking methods include a fingerprint sensor, backlit keypad, NFC-based digital keys, and physical keys for emergency access.
- Apple Home Key integration enables hands-free unlocking via ultra-wideband (UWB) signals, though performance can vary based on device placement.
- The device features a USB-C rechargeable battery pack, which Aqara estimates will last up to six months on a single charge.
- While currently optimized for Apple users, the lock supports Samsung Galaxy tap-to-unlock via the Aliro standard, with broader Android support expected later.

**Why it matters: **
The Aqara Smart Lock U400 represents a significant step forward in smart home convenience by leveraging UWB technology to eliminate the need for manual authentication. By adopting universal standards like Matter and Aliro, the device offers consumers greater flexibility and interoperability in an increasingly fragmented smart home market.
