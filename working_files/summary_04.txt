The latest Matter update improves camera streaming
============================================================

**The Connectivity Standards Alliance has released Matter 1.5.1, introducing performance enhancements for smart home cameras, including optimized multi-stream delivery and expanded support for modern media streaming formats.**

**Key points**
- Matter 1.5.1 enables cameras to simultaneously deliver multiple video streams tailored to specific device requirements, such as high-resolution archival and low-resolution mobile viewing.
- The update adds support for HEIC image files, which provide superior quality and smaller file sizes compared to traditional JPEG formats.
- New compatibility features include support for HLS and DASH streaming protocols to better integrate with modern cloud and media platforms.
- Pan-tilt-zoom camera controls have been improved, and smart doorbells now support custom chime sounds and automated triggers.

**Why it matters**
These technical refinements reduce bandwidth consumption and improve interoperability across diverse smart home ecosystems. By standardizing how cameras handle multi-stream data, the update helps manufacturers deliver more reliable and responsive security experiences for end users.
