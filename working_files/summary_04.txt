Fiona Li, a UC Davis graduate, successfully launched her career as an AI startup director by completing ten internships throughout college to build a competitive professional portfolio.

### Key Points
- Fiona Li completed eight paid and two unpaid internships while attending the University of California, Davis.
- Her early experience included roles at an e-commerce store, which helped her secure positions at DocuSign, Intel, and Nvidia.
- Li prioritized skill acquisition over academic performance, resulting in a 3.1 GPA during her undergraduate studies.
- She applied to Nvidia 13 times before successfully securing an internship through a referral from a hiring manager.
- Li currently serves as a director of marketing at an AI startup.

### Why it Matters
This career path highlights the growing trend of students prioritizing practical work experience over traditional academic metrics to gain a competitive edge in the tech industry. It underscores the potential long-term professional benefits of aggressive networking and early-career skill building for students at non-target universities.
