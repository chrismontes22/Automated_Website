Weekly Wrap: Bitcoin Treads Water As Stocks Rally
============================================================

U.S. equities reached near-record highs following Nvidia’s strong earnings, while the cryptocurrency sector saw increased regulatory scrutiny, new institutional investments, and expanded betting options on major private companies.

### Key Points
- The House Oversight and Government Reform Committee launched a probe into potential insider trading at prediction markets Kalshi and Polymarket.
- Ark Invest, led by Cathie Wood, purchased $12.5 million in Bullish stock as the crypto exchange's share price declined by 7%.
- Binance introduced a "Pre-IPO Perpetual Contract" allowing retail investors to speculate on the valuation of SpaceX ahead of its anticipated June public offering.
- Asset tokenization platform Securitize announced plans to go public through a merger with a special purpose acquisition company to trade on the Nasdaq.
- Bitcoin mining stocks, including Core Scientific and Hut 8, rose following Nvidia’s financial results due to their involvement in high-performance computing and AI data centers.

### Why it Matters
These developments highlight the growing intersection between traditional financial markets, artificial intelligence infrastructure, and the evolving regulatory landscape for digital assets. Increased oversight and institutional adoption suggest that crypto-related entities are becoming more deeply integrated into broader economic and political frameworks.
