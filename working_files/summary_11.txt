I replaced my Google TV remote with this app, and I'm never going back
============================================================

The ATVTools app offers advanced remote control and management features for Google TV users, providing a more functional alternative to the standard Google Home remote application.

### Key Points
- ATVTools provides advanced features including input switching, playback controls, and a dedicated swipe control panel.
- The app enables users to sideload applications, manage local storage, and transfer files directly from a smartphone to a TV.
- Users can perform system maintenance tasks like clearing app caches, force-stopping processes, and executing shell commands.
- The tool includes a screenshot and screen recording function for capturing and sharing media moments.
- The Pro version is available as a one-time purchase starting at $2, though initial setup may require troubleshooting developer options.

### Why it Matters
This application addresses common usability limitations in the official Google Home remote, offering power users greater control over their streaming hardware. By simplifying complex tasks like sideloading and file management, it enhances the overall functionality and customization potential of the Google TV platform.
