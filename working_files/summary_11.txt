'Suggested Genmoji' Are Coming to an iPhone Near You
============================================================

Apple is refining its Genmoji feature for the upcoming iOS 27 update to improve performance, reduce device overheating, and introduce automated suggestions based on user photos and text history.

### Key Points
- Apple is addressing performance issues in Genmoji that previously caused iPhone overheating and rapid battery drainage.
- The new "Suggested Genmoji" feature will automatically generate custom emoji ideas using a user's personal media and typing history.
- Users can manage the new functionality through an optional toggle located within the Keyboard settings menu.
- Apple plans to preview the iOS 27 software update at the Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) next month.
- A public release for the updated operating system is scheduled for this fall.

### Why it Matters
These updates reflect Apple's ongoing efforts to optimize on-device artificial intelligence while balancing complex processing demands with hardware limitations. By automating content creation, the company aims to increase user engagement with its generative tools while maintaining the performance standards expected of its mobile ecosystem.
