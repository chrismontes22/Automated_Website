What did Spotify say about its next 20 years? #tech
============================================================

Spotify is marking its 20th anniversary by outlining a long-term strategy to expand into diverse audio formats while implementing measures to protect its platform from AI-generated content saturation.

### Key Points
- Spotify plans to evolve beyond traditional music streaming by integrating new audio formats to maintain listener engagement.
- The company aims to defend its platform against the influx of AI-generated tracks that lack original licensing or human-curated quality.
- Management is prioritizing the preservation of user trust by developing systems to detect, label, and manage synthetic music releases.
- The strategy focuses on balancing platform expansion with the protection of its core catalog against potential dilution from automated content.

### Why it Matters
- This shift determines whether Spotify remains a reliable destination for human-made music or becomes cluttered with synthetic noise. For artists and rights holders, these policies will significantly influence the future economics of music distribution and content discovery.
