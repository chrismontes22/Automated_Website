'Why we must fight Palantir' — NymVPN CEO shares his anti-Palantir manifesto
============================================================

NymVPN CEO Harry Halpin has published an "Anti-Palantir Manifesto" challenging Palantir’s vision of nationalistic software development and advocating for global internet privacy through decentralized, anonymous technology.

### Key Points
- Harry Halpin released a 22-point manifesto on April 22, 2026, directly countering Palantir’s recent vision for a "Technological Republic."
- Palantir’s original manifesto, posted on April 18, argued that engineers have a moral obligation to support national defense and military power.
- Halpin characterizes Palantir’s approach as a form of "technofascism" that merges state violence with corporate efficiency.
- Nym clarified that the manifesto reflects Halpin’s personal philosophical views rather than official corporate policy for NymVPN.
- The response promotes the use of decentralized "mixnet" technology and post-quantum encryption to protect users from mass surveillance.

### Why it Matters
This clash highlights a growing ideological divide in the tech industry between companies prioritizing national security interests and those advocating for universal digital anonymity. The debate underscores the increasing importance of privacy tools as users navigate a landscape where data tracking and state-aligned surveillance continue to expand.
