Surface Laptop prices are skyrocketing — this deal might be your last chance to own one without taking out a mortgage
============================================================

Microsoft has announced a significant price increase for its Surface PC lineup, but the Surface Laptop 7th Edition is currently available at a 38% discount at Best Buy.

### Key Points
- Microsoft is raising the MSRP of its Surface PCs and laptops by $500 due to ongoing RAM supply constraints.
- The Surface Laptop 7th Edition is currently discounted to $1,049.99 at Best Buy, down from its original $1,699.99 price.
- The featured model includes a Snapdragon X Elite processor, 16GB of RAM, a 512GB SSD, and a 13.8-inch 120Hz touchscreen.
- As a Copilot+ PC, the device features a 45 TOPS Neural Processing Unit designed to accelerate local AI tasks and generative software.
- The laptop offers up to 20 hours of battery life and weighs 3.65 lbs, making it a portable option for professional and casual users.

### Why it Matters
This price hike reflects broader volatility in the hardware market as manufacturers struggle with rising component costs. Consumers can currently mitigate these increased costs by taking advantage of temporary retail discounts before the new, higher pricing becomes the standard across all sales channels.
