Transcript: Songyee Yoon, Principal Venture Partners
============================================================

Songyee Yoon, founder of Principal Venture Partners, discusses the evolution of artificial intelligence, the importance of AI-native business models, and the role of gaming in technological innovation.

### Key Points
- Songyee Yoon is a computational neuroscience expert and former executive at NCSoft and SK Telecom.
- Principal Venture Partners focuses on "AI-native" companies that build durable infrastructure and data-driven moats.
- Yoon emphasizes that gaming serves as a low-risk testing ground for emerging technologies like AI and cloud computing.
- The firm has invested in infrastructure-focused companies including Together AI, Cartia, and voice-application developer Sesame.
- Yoon argues that students should prioritize creativity and problem-solving skills over rote memorization to remain competitive in an AI-driven economy.

### Why it Matters
As AI shifts from a experimental tool to a foundational platform, businesses must move beyond simple automation to fundamentally redesign their workflows. Understanding this transition is critical for investors and leaders aiming to build sustainable, long-term value in an increasingly automated global economy.
