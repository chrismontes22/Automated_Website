I track Dell laptop prices, and this AU$1,139 Dell 14 Plus is the EOFY deal I’d recommend before time runs out

Dell is offering significant end-of-financial-year discounts on the 14-inch Dell 14 Plus laptop, which is now available for AU$1,139.05 when using a specific promotional coupon code.

### Key Points
- The Dell 14 Plus features a Ryzen AI 7 350 processor, 16GB of RAM, and a 1TB SSD.
- Customers can reduce the price to AU$1,139.05 by applying the coupon code FUTUREAU5% at checkout.
- A 2-in-1 version of the laptop is also available for AU$1,299.10 using the same discount code.
- The device includes a 14-inch 2K display and weighs 1.52kg, making it suitable for general productivity and school work.
- Reviewers awarded the laptop a 5 out of 5 for value, noting it is best suited for indoor environments due to its 300-nit screen brightness.

### Why it Matters
These discounts provide a cost-effective entry point for students and professionals seeking a reliable Windows machine for daily tasks. By offering high storage and memory capacities at a reduced price, Dell is positioning this model as a competitive option for budget-conscious buyers during the EOFY sales period.
