HP OmniBook 3 16 Review: Big-Screen Budget Laptop With Unbeatable Battery Life
============================================================

The HP OmniBook 3 16 sets a new industry record for battery longevity, lasting over 34 hours on a single charge while offering a durable, productivity-focused design for users.

### Key Points
- The HP OmniBook 3 16 achieved a record-breaking 34 hours and 5 minutes of battery life during video streaming tests.
- The laptop is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon X X1-26-100 processor and features a 68-watt-hour battery.
- Pricing starts at $1,000 for the base model, with configurations available up to 32GB of RAM and 1TB of storage.
- The device includes a 16-inch 2K IPS display, an IR webcam for facial recognition, and a mix of USB-C and USB-A ports.
- Build quality utilizes a combination of aluminum and plastic, weighing 3.7 pounds for a balance of portability and durability.

### Why it Matters
The OmniBook 3 16 demonstrates the extreme efficiency potential of the Qualcomm Snapdragon platform for users who prioritize unplugged productivity over cutting-edge processing power. While the hardware is a generation behind newer chips, its record-setting endurance makes it a compelling option for mobile professionals who need a reliable, long-lasting workstation.
