These New Govee Outdoor Smart String Lights Let You Customize Each Bulb
============================================================

**Govee launches new customizable, weather-proof smart string lights for outdoor spaces.**

**Key points**

*   **Advanced Customization:** Each bulb features "Uni-IC" technology, allowing users to create complex color gradients, animations, and patterns within a single bulb.
*   **Smart Integration:** The lights are controlled via the Govee app and are compatible with Amazon Alexa and Google Home.
*   **Music Sync:** The system includes 12 audio-reactive modes that allow the lights to change colors in sync with your music.
*   **Built for Durability:** Rated IP67 for water and UV resistance, these lights are designed to be left outdoors year-round with a lifespan of over 10 years.
*   **Pricing:** The lights are currently available in two lengths: 32 feet for $170 and 65 feet for $300.

**Why it matters**
These lights offer a high-end, permanent lighting solution for homeowners looking to upgrade their patio aesthetics with professional-grade color control. Their durability makes them a practical, long-term investment for year-round outdoor entertaining.
