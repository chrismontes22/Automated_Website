Stock market today: S&P 500, Nasdaq futures rise as focus turns to AI despitwar jitters
============================================================

US stocks climbed on Thursday as investors prioritized artificial intelligence prospects and the upcoming SK Hynix Nasdaq debut over escalating geopolitical tensions between the United States and Iran.

### Key Points
- The Nasdaq Composite rose 1.3%, while the S&P 500 gained 0.8% and the Dow Jones Industrial Average increased by 0.2%.
- South Korean memory chip manufacturer SK Hynix is set for its Nasdaq debut on Friday, with IPO demand currently seven times the available share supply.
- US military forces launched airstrikes on 90 Iranian targets, prompting retaliatory strikes from Iran against US-allied nations in the Middle East.
- Oil prices declined as markets adjusted to the ongoing instability in the Middle East and its potential impact on global energy supplies.
- Pepsi reported revenue that exceeded expectations, though the company noted that American consumers are increasingly tightening budgets due to broader economic concerns.

### Why it Matters
The market's resilience suggests that investor enthusiasm for artificial intelligence remains a primary driver of equity valuations despite significant geopolitical volatility. This trend highlights a shift in focus toward long-term tech growth as traders look past immediate macroeconomic and regional security risks.
