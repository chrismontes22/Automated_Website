Roku's home screen customization solves the one problem everyone complains about
============================================================

Roku users can significantly improve their viewing experience by customizing the home screen interface to remove unwanted promotional content, hide sidebar clutter, and reorganize frequently used streaming applications.

### Key Points
- Navigate to Settings and select Home screen to manually hide rows like Live TV, What to Watch, and other recommendation sections.
- Use the star button on the remote to access the Options menu and move preferred streaming apps to the top of the grid.
- Limit data collection by visiting the Privacy menu to disable advertisement tracking and the smart television experience feature.
- Removing sponsored content and promotional rows reduces visual clutter and prevents the interface from prioritizing paid suggestions over user preferences.
- Customizing the layout eliminates scrolling fatigue by ensuring essential streaming services remain easily accessible at the top of the home screen.

### Why it Matters
Customizing a streaming interface allows users to reclaim their television experience from aggressive advertising and algorithmic suggestions. By streamlining the home screen, viewers can reduce distractions and access their preferred content more efficiently.
