Samsung Wallet gets a Trips timeline, so all you've got to worry about is your tan
============================================================

Samsung is launching a new "Trips" feature for its Galaxy Wallet app in April, designed to organize travel itineraries, tickets, and reservations into a single, chronological timeline.

### Key Points
- The Trips feature automatically aggregates plane, train, hotel, and theme park bookings into one organized view.
- Users can add custom memos to their travel timelines to track specific activities or additional trip details.
- Samsung Wallet recently introduced a "Digital Home Key" feature, allowing users to unlock smart locks via UWB or NFC.
- The app now supports split in-store payments, offering users flexible installment plans for their purchases.
- The new travel organization tools are scheduled to begin rolling out to Galaxy smartphone users starting in April.

### Why it Matters
This update aims to reduce travel-related friction by centralizing scattered confirmation data into a single, accessible interface. By integrating these organizational tools with existing smart home and payment features, Samsung is positioning its Wallet app as a comprehensive utility for managing both daily life and travel logistics.
