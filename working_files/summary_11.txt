“This company reeks of desperation”: Microsoft is giving away $1 million and a Mercedes-Benz just to get you to use Edge
============================================================

Microsoft is incentivizing users to switch to the Edge browser by offering a $2 million sweepstakes featuring a $1 million grand prize and several Mercedes-Benz luxury vehicles.

### Key Points
- The sweepstakes includes a $1 million cash prize, three Mercedes-Benz cars, and thousands of instant-win rewards.
- Eligible participants reside in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and select European countries.
- The promotion runs through May 21, 2026, with a maximum limit of 465 entries per user.
- Users can earn entries by setting Edge as their default browser, enabling OneDrive backups, and using Microsoft services like Copilot and Bing.
- Participation is free, though users can leverage the Microsoft Rewards program to accumulate additional entries.

### Why it Matters
This promotion highlights Microsoft's aggressive strategy to capture market share from competitors like Google Chrome by gamifying the user experience. It reflects a broader industry trend where tech giants utilize high-value incentives and ecosystem integration to influence consumer browser preferences.
