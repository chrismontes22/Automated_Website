My tablet was gathering dust until a productivity app gave it another lease on life
============================================================

A tech writer repurposed a Xiaomi Pad tablet as a secondary monitor for a Windows laptop using the Spacedesk application to improve productivity while working from home.

### Key Points
- The Spacedesk app allows users to extend their Windows desktop to Android tablets over a local Wi-Fi network.
- The software is free for non-commercial, private use, while commercial applications require a paid license.
- Users can connect devices wirelessly, though a USB-C cable is often necessary to prevent rapid battery drain on the tablet.
- The setup is best suited for static feeds like email or Slack due to occasional wireless latency and minor cursor tracking issues.

### Why it Matters
This solution demonstrates how consumers can extend the utility of aging or underused hardware to create a more efficient mobile workstation. It highlights a cost-effective way to replicate a dual-monitor setup without purchasing additional dedicated computer displays.
