Weekly poll: Would you buy the Oppo Find X9 Ultra? What about the Oppo Find X9s?
============================================================

Oppo has introduced the Find X9 Ultra flagship smartphone at a €1,700 price point, positioning it to compete directly against high-end camera-focused devices from vivo and Xiaomi.

### Key Points
- The Oppo Find X9 Ultra features a professional-grade camera system and is compatible with a specialized Hasselblad Earth Explorer accessory kit.
- Oppo also announced the global launch of the Find X9s, a more affordable variant powered by the Dimensity 9500s chipset.
- The Find X9s includes a 7,025mAh battery with 80W wired charging but lacks the wireless charging capabilities found in the standard model.
- Both the Find X9 Ultra and the Find X9s are scheduled for a global release, including availability in the Indian market.

### Why it Matters
The release of the Find X9 Ultra intensifies the competition in the premium smartphone sector, where manufacturers are increasingly differentiating their devices through advanced camera hardware and specialized accessories. This strategy forces consumers to weigh the value of professional-grade photography features against the high price tags typical of current "Ultra" flagship models.
