Origin PC Millennium Desktop Review: A Smart Build but Not the Perfect Configuration
============================================================

The Origin PC Millennium is a highly customizable, large-format gaming desktop that delivers exceptional 1080p performance through its AMD Ryzen 7 9800X3D processor and 3D V-Cache technology.

### Key Points
- The system features an AMD Ryzen 7 9800X3D CPU, 32GB of DDR5-6000 memory, and an Nvidia GeForce RTX 5070 Ti graphics card.
- Base pricing starts at $3,139, though the tested configuration is listed at $4,196 with temporary discounts available until July 31.
- The 100-liter chassis offers excellent airflow, easy-to-clean dust filters, and significant room for future hardware upgrades.
- Internal components are heavily sourced from Corsair, including the iCUE Link Titan 360 liquid cooler and seven RGB case fans.
- Performance benchmarks show the machine excels at 1080p gaming but faces stiff competition from RTX 5080-equipped systems at 4K resolutions.

### Why it Matters
While the Millennium offers a premium, well-built platform with superior upgrade potential, its high price point makes it a difficult value proposition compared to more affordable, high-performance alternatives. Consumers must weigh the benefits of its modular design and aesthetic customization against the superior raw performance offered by similarly priced competitors.
