iPhone 18e Won't Get ProMotion Display, Says Leaker
============================================================

Apple’s upcoming iPhone 18e will reportedly retain a 60Hz LTPS display, continuing the company's strategy of excluding high-refresh-rate ProMotion technology from its entry-level smartphone lineup through 2027.

### Key Points
- The iPhone 18e will feature a 60Hz LTPS TFT panel, matching the display specifications of the current iPhone 17e.
- ProMotion 120Hz and Always-On display capabilities will remain exclusive to higher-end iPhone 18 models.
- Apple is not expected to adopt LTPO display technology for its budget-friendly iPhone series until at least 2028.
- Future display upgrades depend on the development of "LTPO+" technology, which aims to improve power efficiency for premium devices.
- The transition to advanced display panels for entry-level models remains subject to potential delays in manufacturing readiness.

### Why it Matters
This decision highlights Apple's ongoing strategy of segmenting hardware features to maintain a clear distinction between its budget-friendly and premium smartphone tiers. By withholding high-refresh-rate screens, the company faces increasing pressure from Android competitors that offer 120Hz displays at similar price points.
