I fixed Android Auto lag with 3 tiny changes
============================================================

Android Auto users experiencing lag, stuttering, or delayed touch responses can improve performance by adjusting battery optimization settings, reducing animation scales, and clearing accumulated app cache data.

### Key Points
- Set Android Auto battery usage to "Unrestricted" to prevent the operating system from killing background processes during use.
- Reduce window, transition, and animator duration scales to 0.5x within Developer Options to free up CPU resources.
- Clear the Android Auto app cache regularly to remove fragmented data that causes system bottlenecks and performance drops.
- Use high-quality, shielded USB 3.1 cables to ensure stable data transmission and reduce signal interference during wired connections.
- Disable background Wi-Fi scanning to minimize CPU interrupts and prevent thermal throttling during intensive projection tasks.

### Why it Matters
These adjustments help users resolve common connectivity issues without requiring new hardware or expensive vehicle upgrades. By optimizing how the smartphone manages resources, drivers can ensure a safer and more responsive navigation and entertainment experience on the road.
