HomeKit Weekly: The Onvis Smart Button is the perfect way to share Apple Home access with guests
============================================================

The Onvis Smart Button offers a reliable, Thread-enabled physical remote solution for controlling Apple Home accessories, providing a simple alternative to app-based or voice-activated smart home management.

### Key Points
- The Onvis Smart Button features five programmable buttons that support single, double, and long-press triggers for up to 15 distinct HomeKit functions.
- The device utilizes Thread networking technology to ensure instant, local communication with smart home hardware without requiring cloud connectivity.
- It is powered by a standard coin battery and includes a wall mount for convenient placement throughout the home.
- Users can configure button actions directly within the Apple Home app to control lights, blinds, plugs, and custom scenes.
- The remote is available for purchase through Amazon and the official Onvis website.

### Why it Matters
This device simplifies smart home accessibility for guests and residents by removing the need for complex app navigation or voice commands. By keeping all automation local, it enhances privacy and reliability for users invested in the Apple Home ecosystem.
