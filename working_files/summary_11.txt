Incogni Review: Comprehensive and Transparent Data Removals
============================================================

Incogni is a prominent data removal service that offers extensive coverage of over 3,000 data broker sites, providing users with high transparency and a detailed activity history for monitoring.

### Key Points
- The service offers four subscription tiers, with annual plans providing the best value compared to monthly billing.
- An independent audit by Deloitte confirmed the company’s data removal processes and verified that it does not sell user information.
- Users can track removal progress via a minimalist dashboard that assigns sensitivity scores to different data sources.
- The platform is limited by its inability to scan for multiple name variations or more than three addresses per account.
- Incogni maintains a data retention policy of up to 24 months, which is longer than many competing services.

### Why it Matters
Incogni stands out for its broad reach and commitment to transparency, making it a strong candidate for users prioritizing comprehensive data broker coverage. However, the service's limitations regarding personal information tracking and its lengthy data retention policy may require users to weigh convenience against specific privacy needs.
