Attention and effort
============================================================

AI agents are increasingly being deployed to automate personalized sales pitches at scale, threatening to erode consumer trust by mimicking the effort typically associated with human-led business interactions.

### Key Points
- AI agents now generate highly personalized sales pitches by leveraging individual data, mimicking the effort of human-led, high-value B2B sales processes.
- Unlike traditional door-to-door sales, AI-driven outreach operates at a massive scale, functioning similarly to bulk spamming techniques.
- The automation of personalized communication risks devaluing the trust and attention that buyers traditionally grant to high-effort sales presentations.
- The shift toward automated, high-speed outreach prioritizes volume over the quality of engagement, potentially damaging long-term marketplace relationships.

### Why it Matters
The rise of AI-driven sales outreach threatens to commoditize human attention by making mass-produced pitches appear deceptively personal. As these tools become more prevalent, businesses may find it increasingly difficult to build genuine trust with potential clients who are becoming desensitized to automated communication.
