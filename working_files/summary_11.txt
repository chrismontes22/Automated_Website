Virtru centers file collaboration around data-level protection
============================================================

Virtru has launched Virtru Collaborate, a FedRAMP-authorized platform that enables secure, data-centric file sharing by embedding encryption and access controls directly into files for organizations with strict compliance requirements.

### Key Points
- Virtru Collaborate uses the Trusted Data Format (TDF) to ensure encryption and access policies remain attached to files regardless of where they are stored or shared.
- The platform supports compliance with rigorous standards including CMMC Level 2, HIPAA, GDPR, ITAR, and FINRA for defense, healthcare, and financial sectors.
- Users can instantly revoke access to sensitive files at any time, even after documents have been shared across external environments like Microsoft Office 365 or Google Workspace.
- The architecture provides unified governance for both human users and non-human entities, such as AI agents and automated APIs, through attribute-based access controls.
- The service offers a cost-effective alternative to high-end cloud environments by consolidating secure storage and auditability into a single, managed workspace.

### Why it Matters
This platform shifts the security paradigm from perimeter-based protection to data-centric governance, allowing highly regulated industries to maintain productivity without sacrificing control. By embedding security directly into the file, organizations can securely collaborate across boundaries while meeting complex legal and compliance mandates.
