Say Goodbye to Spam Calls With This iPhone Trick
============================================================

Apple’s iOS 26 update introduces a new call screening feature that allows users to filter unknown callers, effectively reducing spam and unwanted interruptions on compatible iPhone models.

### Key Points
- The feature is available on all iOS 26-compatible devices, including the iPhone 14 Pro, and does not require Apple Intelligence.
- Users can access the settings by navigating to Settings, then Apps, and selecting Phone to find the Screen Unknown Callers menu.
- The "Ask Reason for Calling" option prompts unknown callers to state their purpose before the phone rings, providing a transcript for the user.
- The "Silence" option acts as a filter that automatically sends all calls from unsaved numbers directly to voicemail without notification.
- The default "Never" setting allows all calls to ring through, maintaining the traditional behavior of the iPhone Phone app.

### Why it Matters
This update provides users with granular control over their incoming communications, significantly reducing the frequency of disruptive spam calls. By offering automated screening, Apple helps users distinguish between legitimate contacts and unwanted solicitations without missing important information.
