SEC What Changed: 10-K Filing Snapshot for 16 June 2026
============================================================

Sonoma Pharmaceuticals and La-Z-Boy updated their annual 10-K filings on June 16, 2026, revealing significant shifts in consumer market strategy, equity dilution risks, and corporate cybersecurity governance structures.

### Key Points
- Sonoma Pharmaceuticals highlighted a strategic pivot toward consumer market expansion to drive revenue growth.
- Sonoma disclosed increased equity dilution risks, with outstanding options rising to 129,163 shares from 73,081.
- A new Nasdaq compliance warning in Sonoma’s filing notes potential delisting risks if minimum market value requirements are not met.
- La-Z-Boy formally appointed a Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) to enhance cybersecurity oversight and incident response coordination.
- La-Z-Boy’s updated governance structure mandates that the CISO and CIO report cybersecurity risks to the Audit Committee at least annually.

### Why it Matters
Analyzing changes in annual 10-K filings provides investors with early signals regarding a company's operational health and strategic direction. Academic research suggests that companies making significant modifications to their disclosures often underperform, making these updates essential for monitoring long-term financial and governance risks.
