Kraken Launches CFTC-Regulated Bitcoin Perpetual Futures for U.S. Traders
============================================================

Kraken has launched regulated perpetual futures for U.S. clients, marking the first time a major domestic exchange has offered these derivatives under direct oversight from the CFTC.

### Key Points
- Kraken now offers perpetual futures for bitcoin and eight other assets to eligible U.S. traders through its CFTC-registered subsidiary, Kraken Derivatives US.
- The platform utilizes an 8-hour funding rate mechanism to align contract prices with spot market values.
- Traders can manage these perpetuals alongside CME-listed products within a single collateral pool, reducing the need to move capital across multiple venues.
- The launch follows a May 2026 CFTC regulatory shift that created a pathway for domestic platforms to offer perpetual contracts.
- Kraken secured the necessary derivatives licenses through its 2026 acquisition of Bitnomial and its 2025 purchase of NinjaTrader.

### Why it Matters
This launch shifts a massive segment of crypto trading from unregulated offshore venues to a supervised domestic environment, potentially increasing institutional participation. By integrating these products into a regulated framework, Kraken is positioning itself to capture demand from U.S. investors seeking leveraged exposure within a compliant financial ecosystem.
