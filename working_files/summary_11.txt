ZTE and MediaTek unveil Tri-band Wi-Fi 7, targeting a relatively unexplored premium niche in Brazil
============================================================

Police departments are increasingly integrating real-time AI systems into emergency contact centers to monitor call handlers and perform automated database searches during active emergency response situations.

### Key Points
- Police agencies are deploying AI tools to listen to emergency calls in real time.
- The technology assists call handlers by conducting live database searches during active incidents.
- This integration aims to streamline emergency response workflows and improve information retrieval for dispatchers.
- The move reflects a broader trend of public sector organizations adopting generative AI to manage high-volume communication systems.

### Why it Matters
The adoption of AI in emergency services highlights the growing reliance on automated tools for critical public safety decision-making. While these systems offer potential efficiency gains, they also raise significant questions regarding data privacy, algorithmic accuracy, and the human oversight required in high-stakes law enforcement environments.
