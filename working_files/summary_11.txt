Amazon's Big Spring Sale has dropped this 65-inch Toshiba TV to only $299 — but the deals end tomorrow!
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
Amazon is currently offering a 44% discount on the 65-inch Toshiba C350 Series 4K Smart TV, reducing the price to $299 as part of its Big Spring Sale event.

**Key points**
- The 65-inch Toshiba C350 model is discounted by $230, bringing the final price down to $299.99.
- The television runs on the Amazon Fire TV platform, providing built-in access to streaming services and Alexa voice integration.
- Hardware features include 4K resolution, three HDMI inputs, two USB ports, and motion enhancement software that simulates a 120Hz refresh rate.
- Amazon is applying varying discount levels to other screen sizes within the C350 Series lineup during the sale.

**Why it matters**
This promotion highlights the increasing affordability of large-format 4K displays for budget-conscious consumers. By leveraging the Fire TV ecosystem, this deal offers a low-cost entry point for users seeking a centralized smart home and streaming experience.
