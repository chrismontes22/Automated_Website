Microsoft’s $1,950 Surface Pro 13 Gen 12 Got Smarter: Its Design Didn’t
============================================================

Microsoft has launched the 13-inch Surface Pro for Business, a Copilot+ PC featuring Intel Core Ultra processors designed to handle advanced on-device AI tasks for enterprise professionals.

### Key Points
- The device is powered by Intel Core Ultra 5 335 or Core Ultra 7 366H processors, delivering 50 TOPS for local AI processing.
- It features a 13-inch PixelSense Flow display with a 120Hz refresh rate and an optional OLED panel for improved contrast.
- Memory configurations reach up to 64GB of LPDDR5x RAM, and the unit includes a removable Gen 4 SSD for easier enterprise maintenance.
- The laptop offers up to 17 hours of battery life and supports the Surface Slim Pen for digital annotation and tablet-mode workflows.
- Pricing for the new model starts at $1,949.99, excluding the detachable keyboard and stylus accessories.

### Why it Matters
This release signals a shift toward integrating high-performance, on-device AI capabilities into standard enterprise hardware to improve productivity without cloud dependency. By maintaining a familiar form factor while upgrading internal components, Microsoft aims to provide IT departments with a reliable, long-term mobile workstation solution.
