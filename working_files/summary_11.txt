5 things you need to do with TCL NXTPAPER 70 Pro right now
============================================================

The TCL NXTPAPER 70 Pro, launched in April 2026, introduces advanced NXTPAPER 4.0 display technology to global markets, offering users a unique, eye-friendly alternative to traditional smartphone screens.

### Key Points
- The device features a specialized matte, anti-glare display designed to reduce eye strain and resist fingerprints without requiring a screen protector.
- Users can toggle between Color Paper, Ink Paper, and Max Ink modes using the physical NXTPAPER Key to optimize the screen for reading.
- The phone includes AI-driven tools like real-time subtitles, voice memo transcription, and text-based translation and rewriting features accessible via a sidebar.
- Owners can improve performance by disabling T-Mobile bloatware, turning off NXTVISION motion smoothing, and locking the refresh rate to 120Hz.
- The display supports ambient light alerts and "Rest your eyes" notifications to help users manage screen time and reduce potential visual fatigue.

### Why it Matters
Optimizing these settings allows users to fully leverage the unique hardware benefits of the NXTPAPER display while removing intrusive carrier software. By customizing the interface and AI tools, owners can transform the device into a more comfortable, distraction-free reading and productivity tool.
