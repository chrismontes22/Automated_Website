Security shops among the 'hundreds' of Klue hack victims
============================================================

Oracle has reduced its global workforce by 21,000 employees over the past year as the company shifts its financial focus toward massive investments in artificial intelligence and datacenter expansion.

### Key Points
- Oracle’s total headcount dropped from 162,000 to 141,000 employees during the most recent fiscal year.
- The company is aggressively reallocating capital to fund large-scale datacenter construction projects.
- Financial reports indicate a strategic pivot toward AI infrastructure to compete in the cloud services market.
- The workforce reduction represents a significant contraction of the company's total staff size.

### Why it Matters
This workforce reduction highlights the aggressive restructuring traditional tech giants are undertaking to prioritize AI-driven growth. Investors are closely watching whether these massive capital expenditures in datacenters will yield the long-term profitability required to offset the loss of human capital.
