Rare discount knocks the Bose portable speaker down to an all-time low price
============================================================

The Bose Portable Smart Speaker is currently available for a discounted price of $249, offering a versatile, weather-resistant audio solution for both home use and travel.

### Key Points
- The speaker features an IPX4 rating, providing protection against splashes and light weather conditions.
- Users can expect up to 12 hours of battery life on a single charge.
- Connectivity options include both Wi-Fi and Bluetooth streaming capabilities.
- The device supports voice control through integration with Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant.
- A built-in handle and compact design allow for easy portability in backpacks or bags.
- Setup and firmware updates are managed through the required Bose Music mobile application.

### Why it Matters
The significant price reduction makes this premium audio device a more competitive option for consumers seeking high-quality sound in a portable form factor. Its dual-assistant support and multi-room connectivity offer flexibility for users looking to integrate the speaker into an existing smart home ecosystem.
