Samsung’s Freestyle+ Gets Easier to Set Up, but It Still Needs an Outlet
============================================================

Samsung has launched the Freestyle+ portable projector for $1,199.99, focusing on automated image alignment and surface calibration tools to simplify setup for users in varied indoor environments.

### Key Points
- The projector features 3D Auto Keystone, Wall Calibration, and obstacle avoidance to automatically adjust images on irregular surfaces.
- It offers a Full HD resolution with a brightness rating of 430 ISO lumens and HDR10+ support.
- Integrated software includes Samsung’s Smart Hub, Gaming Hub, and a Vision AI Companion powered by Bixby and Gemini.
- The device lacks a built-in battery, requiring an external USB-C power source or the optional Freestyle Battery Base for portability.
- Audio features include a 360-degree speaker system with dual passive woofers and Q-Symphony compatibility for external soundbars.

### Why it Matters
This device prioritizes user convenience and rapid setup over high-end home theater specifications like 4K resolution or extreme brightness. It serves as a flexible secondary display for casual nighttime viewing rather than a direct replacement for a primary television.
