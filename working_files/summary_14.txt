10 Thursday AM Reads
============================================================

Global financial markets and corporate landscapes are shifting as record-breaking merger activity, rising personal wealth, and evolving remote work trends reshape the modern economy for businesses and employees.

### Key Points
- Global M&A activity reached a record $2.8 trillion as companies leverage artificial intelligence to drive strategic corporate takeovers.
- The UBS Global Wealth Report 2026 indicates global personal wealth grew by 10.8% last year, creating nearly 1 million new millionaires.
- Research from the Wall Street Journal suggests that remote work policies are increasingly hindering job prospects and retention for recent college graduates.
- Financial disclosures reveal former President Donald Trump earned $1.4 billion in cryptocurrency, highlighting the growing intersection of politics and digital assets.
- Supermarket data suggests that actual grocery price inflation is significantly higher than current headline Consumer Price Index figures indicate.

### Why it Matters
These trends reflect a broader transformation in how capital is deployed and how the labor market functions in a post-pandemic, AI-driven environment. Understanding these shifts is essential for investors and professionals navigating the disconnect between macroeconomic data and individual financial experiences.
