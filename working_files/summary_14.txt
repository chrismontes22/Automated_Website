10 Thursday AM Reads
============================================================

JPMorgan Chase is nearing a historic $1 trillion market valuation following record-breaking profits, positioning the financial giant to become the first bank to reach this significant milestone.

### Key Points
- JPMorgan Chase’s market capitalization reached approximately $935 billion following a period of sustained financial growth.
- The bank’s record profits and dominant market position have driven its stock price to near-record highs.
- BlackRock also saw a significant rally, with shares rising 6.6% after total assets under management surpassed $15 trillion.
- These gains contributed to the S&P 500 Financials index reaching a new record high.

### Why it Matters
The potential milestone for JPMorgan reflects the broader strength and profitability of the American banking sector despite ongoing economic uncertainty. This valuation shift highlights the increasing market dominance of major financial institutions as they leverage scale and record earnings to attract investor confidence.
