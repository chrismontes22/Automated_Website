Think You Know Windows 11? Here’s Why A Copilot+ PC Can Level Up Your Productivity
============================================================

Microsoft’s new Copilot+ PCs integrate advanced neural processing units into Windows 11 laptops to deliver enhanced on-device artificial intelligence performance, improved battery efficiency, and streamlined user productivity tools.

### Key Points
- Copilot+ PCs require a neural processing unit (NPU) capable of performing 40 trillion operations per second.
- On-device AI processing keeps data local, enhancing security and allowing functionality without an active internet connection.
- The "Click to Do" feature scans screen content to suggest immediate actions like summarizing text or formatting files.
- Integrated biometric security supports Passkey authentication, replacing traditional passwords with face or fingerprint scans.
- Copilot Vision and Voice tools provide accessibility features by reading text and answering user questions about on-screen content.

### Why it Matters
These devices represent a shift toward local AI processing, which reduces reliance on cloud computing and improves overall hardware efficiency. By integrating these capabilities directly into the operating system, Microsoft aims to standardize high-performance AI workflows for both professional and personal computing tasks.
