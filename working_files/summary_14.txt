Anker’s $70 Power Strip Clamps to Your Desk, Keeps Cables Off Floors
============================================================

Anker has launched the Nano Power Strip, a 10-in-1 desk-mounted charging solution featuring 70W GaN technology designed to reduce cable clutter for office and gaming workstation setups.

### Key Points
- The device features six AC outlets, two USB-A ports, and two USB-C ports distributed across a dual-zone design.
- It utilizes GaN technology to deliver up to 70W of power through a single USB-C port, sufficient for charging most modern laptops.
- The adjustable clamp fits desk edges between 0.6 and 1.8 inches thick, providing a stable, one-handed plug-in experience.
- Integrated safety features include 1,500J of surge protection and a manual reset button for the smart overload mechanism.
- The unit measures 0.75 inches thick and is currently available in the United States for $69.99.

### Why it Matters
This product addresses the growing demand for organized workspace management by moving power access from the floor to the desk surface. By integrating high-wattage charging into a compact, mountable form factor, it offers a practical solution for users looking to streamline complex multi-device setups.
