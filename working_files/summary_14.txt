Salesforce has acquired customer support AI specialist Fin for $3.6 billion, aiming to integrate advanced AI agents capable of resolving the majority of customer queries without human intervention.

### Key Points
- Salesforce purchased the AI support startup Fin for a total valuation of $3.6 billion.
- Fin specializes in AI agents designed to automate customer service interactions.
- The company claims its technology can resolve approximately 75% of customer support queries autonomously.
- This acquisition strengthens Salesforce's portfolio of AI-driven enterprise tools and automated service solutions.

### Why it Matters
This acquisition highlights the aggressive industry shift toward automating customer service through autonomous AI agents rather than traditional human-led support models. By integrating Fin’s capabilities, Salesforce aims to significantly reduce operational costs and improve response efficiency for its enterprise clients.
