‘The Metaverse Fever Dream’
============================================================

Meta’s ambitious pivot to the metaverse has largely stalled as the company and other tech giants shift their focus toward artificial intelligence following a decline in public interest.

### Key Points
- Meta rebranded from Facebook in 2021 to prioritize a "metaverse-first" strategy, but has since shuttered projects like Horizon Workrooms and scaled back virtual reality efforts.
- Mentions of the "metaverse" in Meta’s quarterly earnings calls peaked in 2021 and have plummeted as references to artificial intelligence have surged.
- Industry competitors, including Epic Games and Roblox, have also significantly reduced their public emphasis on the metaverse after failing to achieve widespread adoption.
- Market data shows a sharp decline in the value of virtual real estate and digital collectibles, with platforms like The Sandbox experiencing major layoffs and token devaluation.
- The hype cycle was heavily influenced by the COVID-19 pandemic, which temporarily increased demand for virtual social spaces before consumer interest returned to in-person activities.

### Why it Matters
The metaverse was marketed as the next major computing paradigm, but it ultimately functioned more as a rhetorical proxy for future tech growth than a viable consumer product. By pivoting away from these immersive environments, major tech companies have signaled that their long-term financial interests now lie in artificial intelligence rather than virtual worlds.
