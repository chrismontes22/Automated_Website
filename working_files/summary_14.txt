Bitcoin Price Nears $67,000, Lifting Crypto Stocks Amid Geopolitical Tensions
============================================================

Strategy Inc. and other major cryptocurrency-related stocks rallied this week as Bitcoin showed unexpected resilience despite ongoing U.S. military strikes in Iran and persistent macroeconomic pressure from high interest rates.

### Key Points
- Strategy Inc. shares rose above $100, while Coinbase and Marathon Digital gained 11 percent and 6 percent respectively.
- Strategy currently holds 843,775 Bitcoin, valued at approximately $56.2 billion, though the firm recently sold $225 million in shares to bolster its dollar reserves.
- Bitcoin remains down nearly 24 percent year-to-date in 2026, trading significantly below its October 2025 all-time high of $126,080.
- Institutional interest persists despite market volatility, with firms like Franklin Templeton continuing to expand their dedicated cryptocurrency divisions.
- Geopolitical tensions, including U.S. military strikes on Iranian targets, have contributed to elevated inflation concerns and restricted market liquidity.

### Why it Matters
The recent decoupling of crypto assets from geopolitical shocks suggests a potential shift in investor sentiment regarding Bitcoin's role as a resilient market indicator. However, sustained growth remains uncertain as high interest rates and global instability continue to challenge the liquidity necessary for a broader digital asset recovery.
