Deals: Galaxy S26 and S26 Ultra prices drop further, Xiaomi 17T and 17T Pro get first price cuts
============================================================

Retailers are offering significant post-Prime Day discounts on various smartphones, including the Samsung Galaxy S26 series, Xiaomi 17T models, and Motorola Razr 70 devices, following recent market shifts.

### Key Points
- The Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra 12/512GB model is currently discounted by €60, while the S26 12/256GB variant is €50 cheaper than two weeks ago.
- Xiaomi 17T and 17T Pro models have transitioned from launch promotions involving free tablets to direct price reductions.
- The Nothing Phone (1) has reached its end-of-life status after receiving its final security update, prompting potential upgrades to the Android 15-based Nothing Phone (3).
- Motorola Razr 70 and 70+ models are on sale, featuring 6.9-inch LTPO displays and distinct hardware specifications like the Snapdragon 8s Gen 3 chipset in the plus model.

### Why it Matters
These price adjustments provide consumers with opportunities to purchase premium mobile hardware at lower costs following major retail events. Tracking these fluctuations helps buyers navigate the product lifecycles of major manufacturers like Samsung, Xiaomi, and Motorola.
