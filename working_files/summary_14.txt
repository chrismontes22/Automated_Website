I switched to a TV with Google TV and spent a week fixing what Google got wrong
============================================================

Google TV users can significantly improve their smart TV experience by replacing the default launcher, removing pre-installed bloatware, optimizing network DNS settings, and disabling system animations for better performance.

### Key Points
- Replace the ad-heavy default home screen with the highly customizable Projectivy Launcher to gain a cleaner interface and easier app management.
- Use ADB TV to remove unnecessary pre-installed bloatware, which helps free up limited storage space on smart TV hardware.
- Configure custom DNS providers like AdGuard or NextDNS to improve network speed, enhance privacy, and block system-wide advertisements.
- Access Developer Options to adjust animation scales to 0.5x or disable them entirely, making the interface feel more responsive on mid-range or older hardware.
- Utilize Smart DNS services to bypass regional content restrictions without the performance overhead typically associated with traditional VPN connections.

### Why it Matters
These manual optimizations allow users to reclaim control over their hardware by reducing intrusive advertisements and improving overall system responsiveness. By bypassing manufacturer-imposed limitations, consumers can extend the functional lifespan of their smart TVs and create a more personalized, efficient streaming environment.
