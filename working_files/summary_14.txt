iOS 26.5 will bring this new feature to the Messages app
============================================================

Apple has released the iOS 26.5 developer candidate, confirming that the upcoming software update will introduce long-awaited end-to-end encryption support for RCS messaging between iPhones and Android devices.

### Key Points
- Apple released the iOS 26.5 release candidate, signaling a public launch is expected within the coming days.
- The update introduces end-to-end encryption for RCS messaging, enabling secure communication between iOS and Android platforms.
- Encryption features will initially launch in beta and require support from specific mobile carriers.
- Apple notes that the rollout of encrypted RCS messaging will occur gradually rather than being available to all users immediately.

### Why it Matters
This update marks a significant step toward bridging the long-standing messaging divide between Apple and Android ecosystems. By adopting encrypted RCS, Apple is improving cross-platform security and interoperability for millions of mobile users.
