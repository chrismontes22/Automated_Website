Xiaomi’s $80 Air Fryer Can Steam, Sous Vide, and Air Fry, Giving You A Crisp Outside and Juicy Inside
============================================================

Xiaomi has launched the Mijia Smart Air Fryer Pro Steam and Bake Edition, a 6.5-liter appliance that integrates steam technology to improve cooking results for various food items.

### Key Points
- The device features a 1.5-liter water tank and a 900W steam generator capable of reaching 130 degrees Celsius.
- It utilizes an 1,850W heating element and 360-degree air circulation to switch between dry and humid heat cycles.
- The unit supports specialized cooking modes including steam-frying and sous vide, with a temperature range of 30 to 230 degrees Celsius.
- The 6.5-liter capacity accommodates a whole chicken or 24 wings, with a dual-layer rack for simultaneous cooking.
- Launched on the Youpin platform in March, the appliance is currently priced at approximately $80 for crowdfunding participants.

### Why it Matters
This product signals a shift in the kitchen appliance market toward multifunctional steam-air fryers that offer professional-grade cooking techniques at an accessible price point. By delivering advanced features like sous vide and steam-frying, Xiaomi is challenging premium competitors and setting a new standard for versatility in home cooking hardware.
