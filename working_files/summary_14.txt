One UI 8.5 is here: Galaxy S25 owners finally get to enjoy some of Galaxy S26’s best features
============================================================

Samsung has officially released the stable One UI 8.5 update for the Galaxy S25 series, bringing advanced Galaxy AI features and performance enhancements to users worldwide.

### Key Points
- The update introduces Galaxy S26-exclusive features, including Agentic AI, Creative Studio, and real-time Log video previews.
- New security tools include enhanced theft protection, privacy alerts, and Knox Matrix device status monitoring.
- Connectivity upgrades allow Quick Share compatibility with Apple devices and improved family device sharing options.
- The rollout began in South Korea with the April 2026 security patch, with a global release expected to follow shortly.
- Additional features include automated Lock screen layouts, improved Bixby device control, and advanced document scanning capabilities.

### Why it Matters
This update significantly extends the lifecycle of the Galaxy S25 by providing access to high-end AI tools previously reserved for the newer Galaxy S26 series. By standardizing these features across its flagship lineup, Samsung strengthens its ecosystem and ensures that existing users benefit from the latest software innovations.
