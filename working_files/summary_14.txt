This AI Bathtub Concept Figures Out Your Tension Points, Then Runs Itself
============================================================

The AquaIntelli smart bathtub concept uses artificial intelligence to learn user bathing preferences, automatically adjusting water temperature, depth, and massage intensity to provide a personalized relaxation experience.

**Key points**
- Designers Yeongkyu Yoo, Soomin Oh, and Sebin Oh developed the AquaIntelli to automate bathtub setup and maintenance.
- The system utilizes AI to track user habits, specifically targeting massage jets toward areas of physical tension.
- The tub features a minimalist, freestanding design with hidden air jets and no visible hardware.
- Users can control settings via a rim-mounted touch dial or a dedicated companion mobile application.
- The technology is currently a design concept and is not yet available for commercial purchase.

**Why it matters: **
This concept represents a shift toward integrating smart home automation into personal wellness spaces that have historically remained analog. By removing the manual labor of temperature and flow management, the technology highlights a growing market interest in personalized, habit-learning appliances for the modern home.
