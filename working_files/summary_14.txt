Shadow Of The Colossus Director Fumito Ueda Says His New Studio Doesn’t Use AI For Development As Other Games Are Engulfed In A Backlash
============================================================

Director Fumito Ueda debuted a new trailer for his upcoming sci-fi adventure game Gen Atlas at Summer Game Fest 2026, confirming the title is developed entirely by human creators.

### Key Points
- Fumito Ueda, creator of Shadow of the Colossus, is developing Gen Atlas through his studio, genDESIGN.
- The project is being produced under a publishing agreement with Epic Games.
- Ueda stated that generative AI is restricted to administrative tasks like scheduling and meeting summaries.
- Unlike some recent industry titles, Gen Atlas will not use generative AI for game assets, code, or creative ideation.
- No official release date has been announced for the upcoming blockbuster title.

### Why it Matters
This commitment to human-only development distinguishes Gen Atlas from other modern reboots that have faced backlash for incorporating AI-generated assets. By maintaining a strict boundary, the studio aims to preserve artistic integrity while navigating growing player concerns regarding the role of automation in game design.
