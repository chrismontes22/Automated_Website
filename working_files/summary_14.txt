SpaceX Perps Become Hyperliquid’s Biggest Market
============================================================

Hyperliquid recorded $1.4 billion in trading volume for SpaceX perpetual futures on June 12, marking the asset as the exchange's most active market following the company's recent public offering.

### Key Points
- SpaceX perpetual futures accounted for 30% of total trading volume on the Hyperliquid exchange on June 12.
- Stock-linked perpetual futures generated $18.8 billion in total trading volume during the first half of June.
- SpaceX shares have increased by more than 20% since the IPO, reaching a current price of $192.50.
- Perpetual futures allow investors to speculate on asset prices indefinitely without expiration dates or direct ownership.
- Hyperliquid provides a 24-hour venue for traders to hedge or speculate on U.S. stocks outside of traditional market hours.

### Why it Matters
The rapid growth of stock-linked perpetual futures signals a shift in investor behavior toward continuous, round-the-clock speculation on high-profile equities. This trend highlights the increasing demand for derivative products that bypass traditional market hours and ownership requirements.
