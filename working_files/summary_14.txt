Marketing jobs are among the most exposed to AI. Adobe and LinkedIn are teaming up to ensure the industry is upskilled—not replaced
============================================================

Adobe and LinkedIn have launched a free "AI Essentials for Marketers" training program to address the growing skills gap as artificial intelligence rapidly transforms the global marketing industry.

### Key Points
- LinkedIn reports a 113% year-over-year increase in job postings requiring AI literacy, yet only 4% of marketing professionals have added these skills to their profiles.
- The new "AI Essentials for Marketers" program offers four specialized learning paths on LinkedIn Learning covering digital marketing, creative content, communications, and data analytics.
- Each course is designed to be completed in two to three hours, focusing on practical applications like audience segmentation, campaign building, and ROI analytics.
- Research from Anthropic indicates that marketing specialists and research analysts face a 65% exposure rate to AI-driven automation in their daily tasks.

### Why it Matters
This initiative addresses a critical labor market imbalance where the demand for AI-proficient talent is significantly outpacing the current workforce's technical capabilities. By providing accessible training, Adobe and LinkedIn aim to help professionals adapt to a landscape where AI is rapidly automating core marketing functions and reshaping industry hiring standards.
