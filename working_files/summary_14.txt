Weekly Wrap: Crypto Recovers To Start Third Quarter
============================================================

Cryptocurrency markets are showing signs of recovery as Bitcoin and Ethereum prices rise following a significant influx of capital into spot Bitcoin exchange-traded funds in early July.

### Key Points
- Bitcoin ETFs recorded $221.7 million in inflows on July 2, ending a 10-day streak of $2.73 billion in total outflows.
- Ethereum prices increased by 3% to $1,740 as investors rotated capital away from semiconductor stocks like Micron Technology.
- Cathie Wood’s Ark Invest purchased $75 million in crypto-related stocks during June, including $44 million in Coinbase Global shares.
- Cryptocurrency firms have spent $189 million on U.S. midterm election influence, accounting for over one-third of total corporate political spending.
- Analysts at Cantor Fitzgerald project the current "crypto winter" will likely reach a market bottom by late October.

### Why it Matters
The recent rebound suggests a shift in investor sentiment as capital flows back into digital assets following a period of significant market volatility. This trend, combined with increased political lobbying and institutional adoption, indicates that the cryptocurrency sector is positioning itself for a potential recovery in the coming months.
