Searching for Cancer Cures Is Part of Google's AI Story. It Deserves to Be More Than a Footnote
============================================================

Google DeepMind and Isomorphic Labs are leveraging the Gemini for Science initiative to address global challenges, including climate change, food insecurity, and the development of new medical treatments.

### Key Points
- Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis is leading efforts to use AI for hurricane prediction and environmental monitoring.
- The initiative includes creating digital twins of the planet to combat deforestation and address global food insecurity.
- Isomorphic Labs is currently in the preclinical stage for developing new treatments for cancer and various immune disorders.
- These scientific projects were presented as a concluding segment during the recent Google I/O keynote event.

### Why it Matters
- Prioritizing scientific breakthroughs over consumer-facing AI tools could help improve public perception and trust in artificial intelligence technology. By focusing on humanitarian applications, Google has the potential to demonstrate the long-term societal value of its research investments.
