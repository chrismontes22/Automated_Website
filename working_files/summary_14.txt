tvOS 27 and HomePod 27 public betas now available, here’s what’s new
============================================================

Apple has released the public beta versions of tvOS 27 and HomePod 27, allowing Apple TV 4K and HomePod users to test new software features ahead of the fall launch.

### Key Points
- Users can enroll in the Apple Beta Software Program for free at beta.apple.com to access the new updates.
- The tvOS 27 update introduces a redesigned Podcasts app, Hi-Res Lossless Audio for Apple Music, and faster AirPlay connectivity.
- HomePod 27 improves system responsiveness and AirPlay speeds while maintaining compatibility with all existing HomePod models.
- Apple has dropped support for two older Apple TV models in the tvOS 27 release.
- The current beta versions do not include the rumored Siri AI features, which are expected to require newer hardware later this year.

### Why it Matters
These updates provide early access to performance improvements and interface refinements for Apple’s home ecosystem. By testing these features now, users can prepare their hardware for the upcoming fall release while navigating potential compatibility changes.
