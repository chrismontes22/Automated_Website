Apple Has Released These 12 New Products This Year
============================================================

Apple has significantly expanded its 2026 hardware lineup by launching over 12 new products, including the MacBook Neo, M5-powered laptops, the iPhone 17e, and updated iPad Air models.

### Key Points
- Apple introduced the $599 MacBook Neo featuring the A18 Pro chip and a 13-inch display.
- The MacBook Air and MacBook Pro lines received M5, M5 Pro, and M5 Max chip upgrades alongside Wi-Fi 7 support.
- The iPhone 17e launched with the A19 chip, MagSafe, and a 256GB base storage capacity.
- New iPad Air models now feature the M4 chip, 12GB of RAM, and Wi-Fi 7 connectivity.
- The Studio Display XDR was unveiled with a 120Hz mini-LED display and Thunderbolt 5 support.
- AirPods Max 2 received the H2 chip, enabling features like Adaptive Audio and Live Translation.

### Why it Matters
These product releases represent a major refresh of Apple's core computing and mobile hardware, signaling a strategic push toward higher base storage and advanced connectivity standards like Wi-Fi 7. By introducing the budget-friendly MacBook Neo, the company is also aggressively targeting the education market while maintaining its premium positioning across the professional laptop and display segments.
