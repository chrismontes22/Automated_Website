Google Maps for Android Auto gets a subtle update that drivers will love
============================================================

Google is rolling out a navigation update for Google Maps on Android Auto that features more prominent road labels and clearer route highlighting for drivers during active navigation.

### Key Points
- The update is available in Google Maps version v26.17.03 and Android Auto version 16.7.661604.
- Active navigation now displays a prominent blue marker to identify the current street, road, or highway.
- The interface now shows more adjacent roads along the route rather than just the immediate next turn.
- These visual enhancements only appear when a destination is locked into the navigation system.

### Why it Matters
These interface refinements improve driver safety by allowing users to identify their route details with a quick glance at the dashboard. By optimizing visual clarity during active navigation, Google continues to iterate on the Android Auto experience to reduce driver distraction.
