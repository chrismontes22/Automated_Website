Top Stories: WWDC 2026 Recap With Siri AI, iOS 27, macOS Golden Gate, and More
============================================================

Apple concluded its WWDC 2026 keynote by unveiling a new Siri AI integrated across its operating systems, alongside performance-focused updates for iOS 27 and macOS 27 Golden Gate.

### Key Points
- Apple introduced a new Siri AI available as a standalone app and integrated throughout its next-generation operating systems.
- macOS 27, titled Golden Gate, prioritizes system performance, faster AirDrop transfers, and improved network file browsing.
- iOS 27 includes over 250 feature changes, with specific enhancements for Apple Maps, Find My, Apple Wallet, and Apple Music.
- iPadOS 27 and watchOS 27 significantly reduced device support, requiring at least an A14 or M1 chip for iPads.
- Advanced Siri AI features, including expressive voices and improved dictation, require 12GB of unified memory, limiting them to high-end iPhone 17 and M-series devices.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal a major shift toward hardware-dependent AI features that require significant memory upgrades for full functionality. This transition forces a clear performance divide between base-model devices and premium hardware, influencing future consumer purchasing decisions.
