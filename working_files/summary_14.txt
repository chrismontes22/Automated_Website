Final Fantasy Creator Calls Viral AI-Generated Final Fantasy 6 Remake Video ‘Amazing’
============================================================

A viral AI-generated video reimagining the classic SNES RPG Final Fantasy VI in a modern 3D style has garnered praise from franchise creator Hironobu Sakaguchi.

### Key Points
- X user desusanJP released a two-minute AI-generated sizzle reel on May 16 depicting a modern, third-person remake of Final Fantasy VI.
- The footage features iconic scenes, including the character Terra and Sabin suplexing a haunted train, set to the original game's musical score.
- Franchise creator Hironobu Sakaguchi publicly praised the video on social media, expressing surprise and admiration for the fan-made concept.
- Square Enix producer Yoshinori Kitase has previously noted that a full-scale remake of the title would be a massive undertaking, potentially requiring 20 years to complete.
- The video sparked debate among fans regarding the use of generative AI and whether a potential remake should prioritize hyper-realistic graphics or stylized art.

### Why it Matters
This viral project highlights the growing capability of generative AI to visualize long-requested fan projects and influence public discourse around classic game remakes. It also underscores the significant logistical challenges Square Enix faces in modernizing beloved legacy titles that fans continue to demand.
