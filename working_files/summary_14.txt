Datadog CEO Touts AI, Cloud Migration as Revenue Growth Accelerates
============================================================

Datadog CEO Olivier Pomel reports that the company is experiencing sustained growth driven by cloud migration and the increasing complexity of artificial intelligence workloads across its customer base.

### Key Points
- Datadog reported 32% revenue growth, marking the fourth consecutive quarter of accelerated performance for the observability and security provider.
- The company currently holds a 13.6% share of the observability market, which leadership views as evidence of significant long-term growth potential.
- AI-native customer adoption is scaling rapidly, with 22 clients spending over $1 million and five spending more than $10 million annually.
- Datadog is expanding into AI training workloads, noting a shift from specialized, homegrown systems to broader infrastructure requirements among 50 to 100 companies.
- The Bits AI SRE tool has seen strong adoption, with 2,000 customers conducting over 100,000 investigations since the product's launch.

### Why it Matters
Datadog’s ability to capture value from both AI-native startups and traditional enterprises modernizing their infrastructure positions it as a critical utility in the evolving digital economy. By expanding its observability platform to include AI training and automated resolution, the company is securing a central role in managing the increasing complexity of modern software development.
