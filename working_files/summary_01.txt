Dossier : Antivirus PC : lequel choisir pour protéger son ordinateur en 2026 ?
============================================================

In 2026, specialized antivirus software remains essential for comprehensive digital security, as premium suites from providers like Bitdefender and Norton outperform the native protections offered by Windows Defender.

### Key Points
- Bitdefender Total Security leads in detection performance and system efficiency for Windows, Mac, Android, and iOS devices.
- Norton 360 focuses on family safety, offering dark web monitoring, cloud backups, and protection for up to ten devices.
- Avast One provides a robust free version, while its Ultimate suite includes AI-driven anti-scam tools and performance optimization.
- Surfshark One prioritizes privacy and network security, allowing users to protect an unlimited number of devices under one subscription.
- Malwarebytes serves as a specialized tool for identifying and removing persistent malware that other security suites may overlook.

### Why it Matters
Modern cyber threats, including sophisticated ransomware and zero-day attacks, have evolved beyond the capabilities of basic built-in security tools. Investing in a comprehensive antivirus suite provides critical layers of protection, such as identity monitoring and encrypted VPNs, which are necessary to secure personal data across multiple platforms.
