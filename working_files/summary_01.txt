Apple's rumored iPhone 18 anti-theft feature is already on Android, and you should enable it ASAP
============================================================

Google has introduced the Android Advanced Protection suite and Theft Protection features for Android 16 devices to enhance physical security, data privacy, and protection against unauthorized device access.

### Key Points
- The Advanced Protection suite includes automated theft detection, remote locking, and offline device tracking capabilities.
- Security measures include blocking insecure 2G networks, preventing unauthorized USB connections, and forcing Google Play Protect app scanning.
- The tools are available on most Android 16 devices from major manufacturers like Samsung, OnePlus, Motorola, and Google.
- Devices lacking the full Google Services Suite, such as the Fairphone 6, are ineligible for these specific security features.
- Users can enable these protections through the Security & privacy settings menu under the Device unlock section.

### Why it Matters
These features provide a critical layer of defense for users by automating data protection the moment a device is compromised. By integrating these tools directly into the operating system, Google reduces the window of opportunity for thieves to access sensitive personal information or bypass security locks.
