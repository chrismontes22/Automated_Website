SpaceX Will Be Worth More Than Earth. NOT CRAZY. Here’s the Actual Math
============================================================

Futurist Brian Wang explores the potential for massive corporations to exceed Earth’s total economic output by leveraging rapid advancements in artificial intelligence and large-scale orbital infrastructure projects.

### Key Points
- Projections suggest AI and space-based energy could enable sustained 20-30% GDP growth, potentially allowing companies to surpass the economic scale of entire nations.
- SpaceX plans to deploy Star Mind satellites, featuring 150 kW compute payloads, with initial launches potentially beginning as early as the first half of next year.
- The goal of reaching 5 terawatts of orbital AI capacity by 2040 would exceed Earth’s current 3.5 terawatt installed base.
- SpaceX aims to become a nationwide mobile carrier by 2027, with projections estimating a global revenue potential of $500 billion annually by 2035.
- Achieving these milestones requires significant logistical scaling, including approximately 100 to 130 Starship launches to support a single gigawatt of orbital AI compute.

### Why it Matters
This shift toward mobilization-style economics suggests that private entities could soon command resources and influence previously reserved for sovereign states. If realized, these technological advancements would fundamentally redefine global market structures and the relationship between corporate growth and planetary economic limits.
