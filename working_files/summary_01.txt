Quote of the day by Apple CEO Tim Cook: 'Our own information is being weaponized against us with military efficiency' — a scathing critique of the modern advertising data pipeline
============================================================

Apple CEO Tim Cook has long advocated for stricter data privacy protections, warning that the weaponization of behavioral profiling poses significant risks to individual autonomy and global political stability.

### Key Points
- Tim Cook has consistently criticized the monetization of user data and the erosion of digital privacy throughout his tenure as Apple CEO.
- The European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) established a landmark framework for data privacy nearly a decade ago.
- Cook warned that behavioral profiling allows platforms to influence public opinion, citing political movements like Brexit as examples of data-driven manipulation.
- Emerging artificial intelligence technologies are intensifying concerns that existing privacy regulations may no longer be sufficient to protect users.

### Why it Matters
The rapid advancement of AI is complicating the enforcement of existing data protection laws, creating an urgent need for updated regulatory frameworks. As digital profiling becomes more sophisticated, the potential for platforms to influence political outcomes and consumer behavior continues to grow.
