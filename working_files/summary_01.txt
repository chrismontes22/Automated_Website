Your PC can be saved—Windows 11 Pro is just $9.97 through May 18
============================================================

Microsoft is offering a limited-time discount on Windows 11 Pro, allowing users to upgrade their operating system for $9.97 through May 18 to improve performance and security.

### Key Points
- The promotional price of $9.97 represents a significant discount from the standard $199 MSRP.
- Windows 11 Pro features integrated AI tools through Microsoft Copilot to assist with productivity and task management.
- The operating system includes organizational features like Snap Layouts and virtual desktops to streamline multitasking.
- Enhanced security protocols in the Pro version provide stronger protection against phishing, malware, and unauthorized system access.
- The offer is available through the retailer StackSocial and expires on May 18.

### Why it Matters
Upgrading to Windows 11 Pro can extend the functional lifespan of older hardware by optimizing system performance and interface efficiency. This deal provides a cost-effective way for individual users and small businesses to access professional-grade security and AI-driven productivity tools.
