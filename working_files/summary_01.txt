HP stuffed a PC into a keyboard. We took it for a spin
============================================================

The HP EliteBoard G1a is a unique, business-focused computer integrated into a full-size keyboard, designed to save desk space for employees who frequently transition between home and office workstations.

### Key Points
- The device features AMD Ryzen AI Pro processors, up to 64GB of RAM, and 2TB of SSD storage.
- It functions as a Copilot+ PC, including an NPU for local AI tasks and preinstalled HP Wolf Security.
- Pricing ranges from $1,499 to over $3,400, depending on the configuration and regional market.
- The unit weighs 1.49 pounds and connects to monitors via a USB4 port for power, video, and data.
- Internal components, including RAM and SSD, are user-accessible and replaceable for easier IT maintenance.

### Why it Matters
The EliteBoard G1a attempts to create a new hardware category for "dual-deskers" who want a portable, powerful system without the bulk of a traditional laptop. However, its high price point and the potential need for additional monitor investments make it a niche alternative to standard enterprise laptops and thin clients.
