This new app may free Galaxy Watch owners from Samsung’s phone-locked features
============================================================

The new GeminiMan Wellness Companion app allows Galaxy Watch users to record ECG data and manage health metrics independently of Samsung’s restrictive regional and smartphone-locked software ecosystem.

### Key Points
- Developer Dante63 released the GeminiMan Wellness Companion to bypass Samsung Health Monitor limitations.
- The app currently supports ECG recordings, AI-assisted analysis, and custom symptom note-taking.
- Users can create multiple profiles and export health data as encrypted CSV or PDF files.
- Future development phases aim to add blood pressure monitoring and comprehensive wellness tracking for metrics like sleep and skin temperature.
- Data is stored locally on the device to prioritize user privacy and security.

### Why it Matters
This third-party tool challenges Samsung's hardware-software integration strategy by offering users greater control over their own health data. It provides a viable alternative for those restricted by regional feature locks, potentially forcing manufacturers to reconsider how they gatekeep health-tracking capabilities.
