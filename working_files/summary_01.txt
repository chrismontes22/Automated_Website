Comcast Business SecurityEdge Preferred strengthens security for small businesses
============================================================

Comcast Business has launched SecurityEdge Preferred, a network-native cybersecurity solution designed to protect small businesses from digital threats without requiring additional hardware or dedicated IT staff for management.

### Key Points
- SecurityEdge Preferred integrates directly into the Comcast Business network to intercept malware, ransomware, and phishing attempts at the network edge.
- The service utilizes AI-powered threat intelligence to monitor traffic in real time and block malicious IP addresses or unauthorized software.
- Comcast reported that the system blocked threats at an average rate of over 230 per second during May 2026.
- The solution is available for $40 to $60 per month depending on internet speed, with no per-seat licensing or annual subscription fees.
- Setup requires no additional hardware beyond an existing Comcast Business router and can be activated in minutes.

### Why it Matters
Small businesses are increasingly targeted by cyberattacks, yet many lack the resources or technical expertise to implement enterprise-grade security measures. By embedding protection directly into the network infrastructure, this service lowers the barrier to entry for essential digital defense in an era of rising financial losses from data breaches.
