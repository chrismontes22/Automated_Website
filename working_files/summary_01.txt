Eliminate pop-ups, autoplay ads, and tracking on the web for just $30
============================================================

AdGuard DNS Personal is offering a five-year subscription for $29.99, providing users with advanced ad-blocking, parental controls, and enhanced security features across up to 20 different personal devices.

### Key Points
- The five-year subscription is available for a one-time payment of $29.99, a significant discount from the $719.64 MSRP.
- Users can protect up to 20 mobile or desktop devices under a single personal plan.
- Integrated features include ad-blocking, malware protection, phishing prevention, and real-time traffic statistics.
- Parental control tools allow users to filter adult content and schedule site-blocking to manage screen time.
- The service provides customizable filters that enable users to block, unblock, or redirect specific domains on individual devices.

### Why it Matters
This offer provides a cost-effective solution for users looking to improve browsing speeds and privacy by filtering intrusive ads and malicious web traffic. By centralizing DNS management, individuals can secure multiple devices against online threats while gaining granular control over their internet experience.
