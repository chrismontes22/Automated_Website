Moto G Stylus 2026 vs. Google Pixel 10a: Which is the best phone under $500?
============================================================

The Moto G Stylus 2026 and Google Pixel 10a offer premium features under $500, forcing buyers to choose between specialized stylus productivity or long-term software support and AI integration.

### Key Points
- The Moto G Stylus 2026 features an integrated pressure-sensitive stylus, 6.7-inch 1.5K AMOLED display, and expandable storage up to 1TB.
- The Google Pixel 10a provides seven years of OS and security updates, deep Gemini AI integration, and emergency satellite connectivity.
- Motorola’s device boasts superior durability with IP68/IP69 ratings and military-grade certification, plus faster 68W wired charging.
- The Pixel 10a utilizes the Google Tensor G4 processor and includes advanced photo editing tools like Magic Eraser and Best Take.
- Both phones are priced under $500 and include 8GB of RAM, though the Pixel 10a lacks a microSD card slot for storage expansion.

### Why it Matters
Choosing between these devices depends on whether a user prioritizes hardware-specific tools like the stylus or long-term software longevity. While the Moto G Stylus offers better physical durability and creative utility, the Pixel 10a provides significantly higher value through its extended update cycle and robust AI ecosystem.
