This buried Samsung feature helps keep my Galaxy S26 running like new
============================================================

Samsung Galaxy users can improve device performance and longevity by utilizing the Auto Restart feature found within the Device Care settings menu to schedule regular, automated system reboots.

### Key Points
- Samsung’s Device Care suite allows users to optimize memory, manage battery settings, and run diagnostics to maintain peak smartphone performance.
- The Auto Restart feature enables users to schedule specific days and times for their device to reboot automatically.
- Experts and the NSA suggest that regular reboots help clear background app data, resolve minor glitches, and reduce potential security risks.
- Enabling the "Restart when needed" toggle allows the phone to perform an automated reboot if it detects performance issues.
- Consistent maintenance through these built-in tools helps prolong battery health and supports the seven-year software lifecycle of flagship devices like the Galaxy S26.

### Why it Matters
Regularly restarting a smartphone is a simple maintenance practice that prevents performance degradation and clears out misbehaving background processes. By automating this task, users can ensure their devices remain responsive and secure throughout their extended multi-year support lifecycles.
