My Samsung phone stopped slowing down after I started using this widget
============================================================

Samsung Galaxy users can maintain optimal device performance and resolve common software slowdowns by utilizing the built-in Device Care tool, which offers manual optimization and automated restart scheduling features.

### Key Points
- The Device Care feature identifies and resolves issues like excessive background app activity, high memory usage, storage clutter, and potential malware.
- Users can access optimization tools via the Settings menu or by adding a dedicated Device Care widget to the home screen for one-tap maintenance.
- The Auto Optimization setting allows the phone to perform necessary reboots automatically when the device is idle and the battery is above 30%.
- A "Restart on schedule" option enables users to set specific times for the phone to reboot, ensuring a fresh system state without manual intervention.
- Device Care provides a 30-day report to help users identify recurring issues, such as specific apps that frequently crash or drain battery life.

### Why it Matters
Regular maintenance helps prevent the performance degradation that often occurs as smartphones accumulate data and background processes over time. By using integrated system tools rather than third-party cleaners, users can safely extend the longevity and responsiveness of their devices without risking system instability.
