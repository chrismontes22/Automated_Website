Deal Days has a 15-device VPN for less than $30
============================================================

FastestVPN Pro is currently available for $29.97 during the Deal Days event, offering a lifetime subscription for up to 15 devices along with a one-year password manager subscription.

### Key Points
- The promotional price of $29.97 represents a significant discount from the standard $600 retail value.
- Users gain access to over 900 servers across 100 countries with unlimited bandwidth and server switching.
- Security features include 256-bit AES encryption, a kill switch, IPv6 leak protection, and an integrated ad blocker.

- The package includes a one-year subscription to Passhulk Password Manager for secure credential storage.
- The Deal Days promotion is scheduled to run through June 28.

### Why it Matters
This offer provides a cost-effective solution for users looking to secure multiple devices across various platforms with a single subscription. By bundling a VPN with a password manager, the deal addresses two fundamental aspects of personal cybersecurity for a significantly reduced price.
