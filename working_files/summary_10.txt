Bluesky's Newest Product: an AI Tool That Gives You Custom Feeds
============================================================

Bluesky has introduced Attie, an AI-powered assistant that allows users to generate custom social media feeds using natural language prompts instead of traditional coding on the AT Protocol.

**Key points**
- Attie is a new experimental tool that translates user requests into functional social feeds without requiring programming knowledge.
- The assistant is built on the open-source AT Protocol, the same decentralized framework that powers the Bluesky social network.
- Users can sign up for the Attie beta at Attie.AI to begin testing the agentic coding interface.
- The project aims to democratize software development within the decentralized "Atmosphere" ecosystem, allowing non-coders to build custom social experiences.

**Why it matters: **
This initiative represents a shift toward user-controlled social media by allowing individuals to curate their own content streams through AI rather than relying on opaque platform algorithms. By lowering the barrier to entry for building on decentralized protocols, Bluesky is attempting to make open-source social infrastructure accessible to a broader, non-technical audience.
