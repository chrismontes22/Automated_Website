Here’s one of my feature requests for Visual Intelligence in iOS 27
============================================================

Apple is expected to expand its Visual Intelligence features in the upcoming iOS 27 update, potentially integrating Reminders functionality into screenshot analysis and Camera Control tools at WWDC26.

### Key Points
- iOS 26 introduced Visual Intelligence for screenshots, enabling users to create Calendar events, search for similar shopping images, and query ChatGPT.
- Analysts suggest iOS 27 could allow users to convert screenshots of text or images directly into Reminders for improved task management.
- Apple will officially unveil iOS 27 at WWDC26, which begins in just over one week.
- The upcoming software update is expected to feature a redesigned Siri app and an upgraded Camera app with a dedicated Siri mode.
- Apple is reportedly collaborating with Google to enhance the intelligence and capabilities of the Siri virtual assistant.

### Why it Matters
Expanding Visual Intelligence to include Reminders would transform the iPhone into a more effective productivity tool by streamlining how users capture and track information. This evolution reflects Apple's broader strategy to integrate advanced AI directly into daily workflows to maintain a competitive edge in the smartphone market.
