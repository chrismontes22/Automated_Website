Anthony Scaramucci Says He's 'Huge Fan' Of Michael Saylor, Breaks Down MSTR's 11% Yield Play
============================================================

SkyBridge Capital founder Anthony Scaramucci and Galaxy Digital CEO Mike Novogratz are analyzing the risks and rewards of MicroStrategy’s perpetual securities, which currently offer investors an 11.5% dividend yield.

### Key Points
- MicroStrategy offers perpetual securities that provide investors with four quarterly dividend payments totaling an 11.5% annual yield.
- Anthony Scaramucci highlighted the potential appeal of the strategy for Bitcoin supporters, though he confirmed SkyBridge Capital does not currently hold these specific securities.
- Mike Novogratz cautioned that the high yield relies on corporate leverage and a significant Bitcoin reserve acting as a safety cushion.
- The perpetual bonds have no maturity date, meaning investors receive continuous interest payments but cannot demand the return of their initial principal.
- Analysts warn that a severe decline in Bitcoin’s market price could threaten the sustainability of the dividend payouts and the company's underlying financial structure.

### Why it Matters
This strategy represents a complex intersection of corporate debt and cryptocurrency volatility that could redefine how firms leverage digital assets for shareholder returns. Investors must weigh the benefit of high-yield income against the structural risks inherent in perpetual debt instruments tied to volatile market prices.
