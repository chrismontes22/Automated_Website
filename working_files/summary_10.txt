Investment firm EQT has acquired a majority stake in Swiss cybersecurity company Acronis, valuing the firm at more than $3.5 billion to bolster its position in the global market.

### Key Points
- EQT purchased a majority share in the Swiss-based cybersecurity and data protection provider Acronis.
- The transaction values the entire company at approximately $3.5 billion.
- Acronis specializes in integrated cyber protection, combining data backup with advanced security features.
- The deal reflects ongoing consolidation within the cybersecurity sector as private equity firms seek stable, high-growth technology assets.

### Why it Matters
This acquisition highlights the significant market value placed on integrated security and backup solutions as organizations face rising ransomware threats. By securing a majority stake, EQT gains a major foothold in the enterprise data protection space, signaling continued investor confidence in the long-term stability of the cybersecurity industry.
