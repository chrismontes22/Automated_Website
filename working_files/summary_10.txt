Apple Releasing Two New iPhone Apps This Year
============================================================

Apple plans to launch a new Apple Business platform on April 14 alongside a standalone Siri application featuring advanced chatbot capabilities for iPhone, iPad, and Mac users.

**Key points**
- The Apple Business app launches April 14, replacing Apple Business Essentials, Business Manager, and Business Connect.
- Apple Business features include employee app installation, internal contact directories, and integrated support request tools.
- The upcoming Siri app will support both text and voice interactions, mirroring functionality found in ChatGPT and Gemini.
- The Siri app will allow users to access and review their historical conversation logs with the virtual assistant.
- Apple Business requires iOS 26, iPadOS 26, and macOS 26, while the Siri app is slated for release with version 27 updates.

**Why it matters: **
These releases signal Apple's strategic push to consolidate its enterprise services while modernizing its virtual assistant to compete with generative AI rivals. By integrating chatbot features directly into the Siri ecosystem, Apple aims to improve user engagement and maintain its competitive edge in the mobile software market.
