Surfshark is currently offering a promotional deal on its VPN services, providing comprehensive cybersecurity tools and identity protection features for as little as $1.78 per month.

### Key Points
- Surfshark's current promotion includes three months of free service, extending the total subscription period to 27 months.
- The "One" plan features unlimited device connections, antivirus software, data breach alerts, and an "Alternative ID" tool for identity masking.
- Subscription prices start at $1.78 per month, with the comprehensive One plan available for approximately $56.16.
- All plans are backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee for new users.
- While Surfshark offers robust mobile antivirus and identity tools, it lacks a built-in password manager found in competitors like NordVPN and ExpressVPN.

### Why it Matters
This deal provides a cost-effective entry point for users seeking to improve their online privacy and data security through a bundled suite of tools. By offering identity aliasing and mobile-ready antivirus, Surfshark positions itself as a practical, budget-friendly alternative to more expensive premium VPN providers.
