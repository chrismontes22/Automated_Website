iPhone 18 Pro Max Said to Be Thicker and Heavier Than Predecessor
============================================================

Apple’s upcoming iPhone 18 Pro Max is expected to be thicker and heavier than its predecessor due to the integration of a significantly larger battery and new cooling components.

### Key Points
- The iPhone 18 Pro Max will reportedly measure 9mm in thickness and weigh approximately 240 grams.
- Regulatory filings indicate the device will feature a battery capacity of up to 5,567mAh, an increase of nearly 500mAh.
- A new stainless steel vapor chamber is expected to contribute to the increased physical dimensions and weight.
- The device is projected to launch in September alongside Apple’s first foldable smartphone model.

### Why it Matters
This design shift highlights Apple's ongoing trade-off between maintaining a slim form factor and meeting consumer demand for extended battery life. The move suggests that future flagship performance and thermal management requirements may prioritize internal capacity over the ultra-lightweight designs seen in recent years.
