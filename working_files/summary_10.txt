Kling AI Partners With UK’s Evolutionary Films On Feature Animation ‘Minibots’
============================================================

Evolutionary Films has partnered with Kling AI for the upcoming animated feature Minibots, aiming to integrate generative technology into mainstream filmmaking while maintaining a strict ethical, artist-led production framework.

### Key Points
- Evolutionary Films and Kling AI have formed an exclusive global partnership to provide technological support and platform access for the animated feature Minibots.
- The production team includes writers Michael Ferris, Alistair Audsley, and Scott Christian Sava, alongside a specialized team of seven international AI artists.
- The film follows three teenage geniuses who create sentient robots, exploring themes of identity and technology within a robotics summer camp setting.
- The project operates under a "performance-first" charter, ensuring all character performances remain human-created and actor-owned to address industry concerns regarding generative AI.
- Kling AI, a division of the Chinese tech company Kuaishou, will serve as the official technological brand partner for the film's development.

### Why it Matters
This collaboration represents a significant attempt to establish an ethical standard for using generative AI in high-budget commercial cinema. By prioritizing human-led performances, the production seeks to demonstrate that advanced technology can augment traditional storytelling without displacing creative talent.
