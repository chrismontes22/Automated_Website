Take a breath: purported Oura Ring 5 design details appear, but nothing is set in stone
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
Leaked reports suggest the upcoming Oura Ring 5 will feature a curvier, jewelry-inspired design and new color options, with a potential market release scheduled for late 2027.

**Key points**
- The Oura Ring 5 is expected to adopt a more traditional, curvier aesthetic compared to previous models.
- New color options may include "Deep Rose," a bronze-toned shade replacing the current Rose Gold, alongside Silver, Gold, and Matte Black.
- Battery life is projected to remain consistent with the performance levels seen in the current Oura Ring 4.
- The device may incorporate new gesture-based controls, potentially utilizing pinching movements, based on recent company patents.
- Oura reported significant growth in 2024, reaching $500 million in revenue and surpassing 5.5 million total units sold.

**Why it matters**
As a leader in the wearable health market, Oura’s design evolution and potential feature upgrades signal the company's strategy to maintain its competitive edge against emerging smart ring rivals. These early details highlight the brand's focus on blending high-end jewelry aesthetics with advanced biometric tracking technology.
