Anthropic scales up with enterprise features for Claude Cowork and Managed Agents
============================================================

Anthropic has officially launched Claude Cowork for macOS and Windows while introducing Claude Managed Agents to help businesses build and deploy scalable AI workflows more efficiently.

### Key Points
- Claude Cowork is now generally available for all paid subscribers, moving out of its initial research preview phase.
- New enterprise features include role-based access controls, group spend limits, usage analytics, and a Zoom MCP connector.
- Claude Managed Agents, now in public beta, provides a suite of APIs for deploying cloud-hosted agents at scale.
- Early adopters of the Managed Agents platform include companies such as Notion, Asana, and Sentry.
- Anthropic is collaborating with Apple to utilize its new Claude Mythos model for identifying and resolving software vulnerabilities.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal Anthropic’s aggressive push to capture the enterprise market by providing the infrastructure necessary for companies to move AI projects from prototypes to production. By simplifying agent deployment and adding administrative controls, the company is positioning its tools as reliable, scalable solutions for complex corporate workflows.
