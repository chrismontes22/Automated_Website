Meta's Muse Image can use public Instagram profiles for AI creations
============================================================

Meta has unveiled Muse Image, a new generative AI model for Meta AI that creates personalized images by incorporating public Instagram profiles and integrating across WhatsApp and Instagram.

### Key Points
- Muse Image allows users to @-mention Instagram contacts to include their public photos in AI-generated content.
- The tool supports complex prompts, web-based context searching, and the creation of legible infographics or how-to guides.
- Meta is rolling out 30 new AI-powered effects for Instagram Stories alongside image generation capabilities within WhatsApp chats.
- The model includes suggested presets for photo editing and room redesigns using products from Facebook Marketplace.
- Meta plans to expand Muse Image availability to more countries and integrate the technology into Facebook and Messenger.

### Why it Matters
This release signals Meta's strategy to differentiate its AI tools by anchoring them in existing social networks and personal user data. By integrating generative capabilities directly into its messaging and social platforms, Meta aims to increase user engagement and streamline content creation for its massive global audience.
