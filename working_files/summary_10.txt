Peak Design’s small Travel Backpack is the exact tech bag I’ve been looking for
============================================================

The Peak Design 20L Travel Backpack offers a minimalist, weather-resistant solution for everyday carry, balancing a compact 20-liter capacity with expandable storage for laptops and professional camera equipment.

### Key Points
- The 20L Travel Backpack features a 400D recycled nylon Versa Shell exterior with a 900D weatherproof base.
- It accommodates laptops up to 16 inches and includes C-clip loops for integrating Peak Design camera cubes.
- The bag measures 6 inches deep but expands to 8.4 inches, providing an additional 3 liters of internal volume.
- Peak Design also introduced a 3L Travel Crossbody bag featuring origami-style organization and a magnetic phone pocket for $99.95.
- The 20L backpack retails for $199, though early-bird pricing is often available through company crowdfunding campaigns.

### Why it Matters
This backpack represents a shift toward streamlined, versatile gear for professionals who prioritize mobility over carrying excess equipment. By offering a high-quality, durable option for daily use, Peak Design addresses the market demand for premium, compact bags that transition seamlessly from office environments to travel.
