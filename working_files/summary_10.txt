9to5Mac Daily: May 5, 2026 – iOS 26.5 RC, Apple chip partners

Apple is reportedly exploring chip manufacturing partnerships with Intel and Samsung while preparing new iOS features, including Wallet updates and enhancements for upcoming iPhone software releases.

- Apple is evaluating Intel and Samsung as potential chip manufacturing partners to reduce its current reliance on TSMC.
- The upcoming iOS 16.5 update will introduce three specific enhancements for iPhone users.
- Reports indicate that iOS 17 will feature a new "Create a Pass" tool within the Apple Wallet application.
- 9to5Mac Daily provides daily news recaps covering Apple product developments and software updates.

### Why it Matters
Diversifying chip production could help Apple mitigate supply chain risks and reduce its heavy dependence on a single manufacturer. These software updates reflect Apple's ongoing efforts to expand the functionality of its core ecosystem and digital wallet services.
