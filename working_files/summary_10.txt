Prime Day is done, but this best-selling 4K projector is still at its lowest price
============================================================

The XGIMI Horizon 20 Max is currently the top-rated 4K long-throw projector, offering significant improvements in brightness, ease of setup, and image quality for a discounted price of $2,199.

### Key Points
- The Horizon 20 Max features a built-in gimbal and optical zoom, allowing for flexible placement and easy screen alignment.
- It delivers up to 5,700 lumens of brightness, ensuring vibrant image quality even in rooms with significant ambient light.
- The device is powered by the MediaTek MT9679 platform with 4GB of RAM and 128GB of storage for a smooth Google TV experience.
- Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 6, dual HDMI ports with eARC, and support for Dolby Vision and IMAX Enhanced formats.
- XGIMI has discounted the projector to $2,199 for the Fourth of July, marking its lowest price point to date.

### Why it Matters
This projector simplifies the home theater experience by removing the technical barriers typically associated with installing long-throw hardware. Its high brightness and automated setup features make it a versatile option for users who want a premium, large-screen viewing experience without needing a dedicated dark room.
