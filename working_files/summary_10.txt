Save on your favorite tech over the 2026 Memorial Day weekend — grab deals on GPUs, gaming laptops, OLED monitors, and more
============================================================

The 2026 Memorial Day sales event is currently underway, offering significant discounts on gaming laptops, high-end PC components, monitors, and peripherals through Monday, May 25.

### Key Points
- Major retailers including Newegg, Amazon, Best Buy, Dell, Lenovo, HP, and Walmart are participating with discounts reaching up to 59%.
- Gaming hardware deals feature high-performance components like Nvidia RTX 50-series GPUs, Intel Core Ultra processors, and AMD Ryzen 7 7800X3D CPUs.
- Monitor sales include QHD and OLED panels with refresh rates up to 240Hz from brands like Predator.
- Peripheral discounts cover popular gaming headsets and mechanical keyboards, such as the Arctis Nova Pro and Logitech G413 SE.
- Many deals require specific promotional codes, such as "PCGLOWUP25" for HP Omen configurations or "absgamer5" for ABS gaming PCs.

### Why it Matters
Memorial Day has evolved from a traditional holiday for home goods into a significant mid-year retail event for the technology and gaming sectors. These sales provide a strategic opportunity for consumers to upgrade hardware ahead of the larger Prime Day event later in the summer.
