Apple's AI Era: Every AI Drop Since Last Year's WWDC
============================================================

Apple prepares for its upcoming WWDC 2026 conference as the company integrates advanced AI features across its ecosystem while transitioning to new leadership under incoming CEO John Ternus.

### Key Points
- WWDC 2026 begins on June 8, marking a pivotal moment for Apple's software strategy and the official transition to John Ternus as CEO.
- Tim Cook will conclude his 15-year tenure as Apple CEO on August 31, shortly before the company's annual September hardware event.
- Apple Intelligence has been integrated as a system-wide layer across iOS, iPadOS, and macOS to provide generative writing, smart summaries, and contextual tools.
- Siri has received significant upgrades, including the ability to perform multistep tasks across apps and integrate with third-party AI models.
- New generative editing tools have been added to Photos, while creative software like Final Cut Pro and iMovie now feature AI-assisted production capabilities.

### Why it Matters
This leadership transition and continued AI integration represent a critical shift in Apple's long-term strategy for its hardware and software ecosystem. Investors and users are closely watching how the new executive team will balance Apple's privacy-focused approach with the competitive demands of the rapidly evolving artificial intelligence market.
