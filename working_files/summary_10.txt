Boomer Shooter Dev Calls For Nvidia Boycott Over DLSS 5 Generative Upscaling ‘Trained On Instagram Models And Epstein Memes’
============================================================

New Blood Interactive CEO Dave Oshry and developer David Szymanski are calling for a widespread boycott of Nvidia to protest the implementation of generative AI in DLSS 5.

**Key points**
- New Blood Interactive CEO Dave Oshry urged developers and players to boycott Nvidia to pressure the company against using generative AI in gaming.
- Critics argue that DLSS 5 functions as a "slop filter" that degrades artistic intent by applying AI-generated textures over original game assets.
- Developer David Szymanski criticized the technology for replacing human-made art with AI-generated imagery, citing its recent appearance in Resident Evil Requiem.
- Nvidia has faced backlash for failing to disclose the specific training data used for the DLSS 5 generative upscaling technology.
- The movement draws parallels to previous industry pushback against NFTs, crypto games, and predatory microtransactions.

**Why it matters: **
This controversy highlights a growing tension between hardware manufacturers pushing AI-driven rendering and developers concerned about the preservation of artistic integrity in video games. The outcome could influence how major tech companies integrate generative tools into future gaming software and hardware ecosystems.
