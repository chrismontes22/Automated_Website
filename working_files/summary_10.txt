iOS 27 Adds Four New Features to Notes App, Including Markdown Copy-and-Paste
============================================================

Apple has unveiled new features for the Notes app in the upcoming iOS 27 update, including AI-powered content generation, Markdown formatting support, and improved organizational tools for users.

### Key Points
- Apple’s iOS 27 update is scheduled for a full public release in September, following a developer beta and a July public beta launch.
- The new Siri AI, available on iPhone 15 Pro and newer models, can generate note content from internet searches and suggest shortcuts from Messages.
- Users can now copy and paste text using Markdown formatting or convert existing rich text into Markdown directly within the Notes app.
- New organizational features include the ability to insert horizontal divider lines and create internal links that jump to specific sections within notes.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal Apple's ongoing effort to integrate generative AI and professional-grade formatting tools into its native productivity ecosystem. By streamlining note organization and content creation, Apple aims to maintain the competitiveness of its default applications against dedicated third-party note-taking platforms.
