The first Pixel 11 leak looks great, but it doesn’t tell us the most important thing
============================================================

**New CAD renders of the Google Pixel 11 reveal a thinner design and slimmer bezels, but industry analysts warn that rising component costs could lead to significant price hikes.**

**Key points**
- Leaked CAD renders indicate the baseline Pixel 11 will feature a refined camera bar, thinner chassis, and reduced display bezels.
- The device is expected to utilize the new Tensor G6 chipset, which is reportedly manufactured using an expensive 2nm process from TSMC.
- Rising global costs for RAM and hardware components have recently forced competitors like Samsung and Sony to increase prices on flagship devices.
- Analysts suggest the Pixel 11 could see a price increase above the $799 starting point established by the previous Pixel 10 model.

**Why it matters**
The potential price increase for the Pixel 11 could significantly alter its market positioning and consumer appeal compared to the more affordable Pixel 10a. If the base model becomes too expensive, it risks losing its competitive edge against higher-tier Pro models or alternative flagship smartphones.
