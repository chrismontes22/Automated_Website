iPhone Air 2 Could Get an 11% Battery Capacity Boost
============================================================

Apple is reportedly developing a second-generation iPhone Air for a spring 2027 release, featuring a larger 3,500mAh battery and a potential dual-camera system to improve device performance and versatility.

### Key Points
- The iPhone Air 2 is expected to feature a 3,500mAh battery, representing an 11% capacity increase over the current model.
- Apple plans to integrate an Ultra Wide lens to address user complaints regarding the current single-camera setup.
- The device will be powered by an A20 chip manufactured using a 2nm process to enhance power efficiency.
- New Samsung-made OLED technology, known as Color Filter on Encapsulation, may allow for a thinner display and more internal space.
- The smartphone is projected to launch in the first half of 2027 alongside the iPhone 18 and iPhone 18e.

### Why it Matters
These hardware upgrades suggest Apple is prioritizing battery longevity and camera versatility to better position the iPhone Air within its competitive smartphone lineup. By utilizing advanced manufacturing processes and display technologies, the company aims to balance high-end performance with the slim form factor characteristic of the Air series.
