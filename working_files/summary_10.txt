Claude Fable 5 cleared to return as US lifts Anthropic’s export control restriction
============================================================

Anthropic has restored global access to its Claude Fable 5 AI model following the lifting of US export controls that had suspended the service for nearly three weeks.

### Key Points
- Anthropic resumed Claude Fable 5 availability on July 1 across the Claude platform, Claude.ai, Claude Code, and Claude Cowork.
- The US Department of Commerce lifted export restrictions after Anthropic agreed to implement new security protocols and report malicious activity.
- Pro, Max, Team, and select Enterprise users receive Fable 5 access for up to 50% of weekly usage limits through July 7.
- Access to the model is being restored on AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Foundry as quickly as possible.
- The suspension originated from a June 12 directive following reports of a potential jailbreak vulnerability identified by Amazon researchers.
- Claude Mythos 5 remains restricted to a specific set of US organizations under the ongoing Project Glasswing program.

### Why it Matters
This resolution highlights the increasing intersection between national security policy and the rapid deployment of advanced artificial intelligence models. It establishes a new regulatory precedent where AI developers must maintain proactive security reporting and government coordination to ensure the continued global availability of their products.
