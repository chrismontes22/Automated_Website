7 photo gallery apps you should use instead of Samsung Gallery
============================================================

Samsung is ending OneDrive syncing support for its native Gallery app by September 30, prompting users to explore alternative photo management and cloud backup solutions for their Android devices.

### Key Points
- Samsung Gallery will officially discontinue OneDrive integration on September 30, requiring users to find alternative methods for cloud-based photo backups.
- Google Photos and Amazon Photos serve as primary cloud-focused alternatives, with Amazon offering unlimited full-resolution photo storage for Prime members.
- Piktures provides a versatile third-party option that supports multiple cloud platforms, including OneDrive, Google Photos, and Nextcloud, alongside encrypted storage features.
- Lightweight and privacy-focused alternatives like Focus Go, Aves Gallery, and Fossify Gallery offer specialized organizational tools without mandatory cloud-first architectures.
- Users can continue using Samsung Gallery for local viewing while utilizing the standalone Microsoft OneDrive app to maintain automated cloud backup functionality.

### Why it Matters
This transition forces many Samsung users to re-evaluate their mobile photography workflows and data backup strategies. Choosing a new platform now ensures a seamless migration of media before the official support cutoff date.
