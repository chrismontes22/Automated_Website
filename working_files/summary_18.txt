Anthropic’s @Claude Enters Workplace As A Slack Channel Coworker via @sejournal, @martinibuster
============================================================

Anthropic has launched Claude Tag, a new beta feature for Slack that allows enterprise and team users to assign persistent, multi-step tasks to the AI assistant within channels.

### Key Points
- Claude Tag enables the AI to utilize memory, scheduled tasks, and integrated tools to complete complex assignments over time.
- The feature is currently available in beta for Claude Enterprise and Team customers.
- Anthropic reports that its internal product team uses the tool to generate 65% of its codebase.
- Administrators maintain control over the AI's access to data sources, code repositories, and specific spending limits.
- The system is designed to build context within Slack channels, reducing the need for users to repeatedly explain task requirements.

### Why it Matters
This integration signals a shift toward AI agents acting as persistent workplace collaborators rather than simple, single-prompt chatbots. By embedding AI directly into communication platforms, businesses may see significant improvements in operational efficiency and automated project management.
