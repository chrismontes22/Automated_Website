Trump Mobile Just Acknowledged The T1 Phone Database Leak Issue In The Worst Way Possible: Customers Still Not Notified
============================================================

Trump Mobile is currently evaluating whether to notify customers after a data leak exposed personal information belonging to individuals who pre-ordered the company's new T1 Phone.

### Key Points
- The data leak exposed customer names, email addresses, mailing addresses, cell phone numbers, and T1 Phone order identifiers.
- Trump Mobile attributed the security incident to an unnamed third-party platform provider rather than its own internal network or infrastructure.
- The T1 Phone, which retails for $499, is reportedly a rebranded HTC U24 Pro featuring a 6.78-inch screen and 512GB of storage.
- Despite the exposure of sensitive user data, the company has not yet issued a formal notification to the affected customers.

### Why it Matters
This incident highlights the significant security risks associated with third-party dependencies in the hardware supply chain and the importance of transparent crisis communication. Failure to promptly disclose data breaches can damage consumer trust and potentially lead to regulatory scrutiny regarding data protection standards.
