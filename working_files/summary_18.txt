Hollyland Pyro Ultra Wireless Video System Review
============================================================

Hollyland has launched the Pyro Ultra, a high-end wireless video transmission system supporting 4K 60p resolution with both SDI and HDMI connectivity for professional production environments.

### Key Points
- The system supports UHD 4K60 video transmission over HDMI and Full HD 1080p over SDI.
- It features a claimed line-of-sight operating range of up to 4,900 feet (1.5 km).
- The unit utilizes "TWiFi" technology to combine 2.4GHz and 5GHz bands for more resilient connections.
- A dedicated "Focus Mode" reduces latency to as low as 22ms when using SDI connections.
- The hardware includes a built-in Sony NP-F battery plate and supports USB-C UVC plug-and-play for streaming.
- It is compatible with existing Hollyland Pyro series products, including the Pyro S, H, 5, and 7.

### Why it Matters
The Pyro Ultra provides a versatile, mid-range solution for filmmakers who require reliable 4K monitoring without the high cost of uncompressed, zero-latency professional systems. By offering both SDI and HDMI inputs, it bridges the gap between consumer-grade mirrorless setups and professional cinema camera workflows.
