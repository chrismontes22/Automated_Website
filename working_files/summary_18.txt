Motorola Just Built the Android Phone Samsung’s Galaxy S26 Almost Was
============================================================

The Motorola Edge 70 Max launches as the first mainstream Android flagship to feature integrated magnets and full 25W Qi2 wireless charging speeds without requiring additional accessories.

### Key Points
- The device supports the full 25W MPP25 wireless charging standard using built-in magnets for precise coil alignment.
- It is powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 processor, featuring a 5,500 mm² vapor chamber cooling system.
- The phone includes a 7,100 mAh silicon-carbon battery that supports 90W TurboPower wired charging.
- Photography is handled by a 50MP Sony LYTIA 710 main camera sensor with integrated AI-assisted processing.
- The handset debuts Motorola Qira, an AI system designed to synchronize context across Lenovo PCs, tablets, and mobile devices.
- The hardware is encased in an aircraft-grade aluminum frame with MIL-STD-810H durability certification.

### Why it Matters
This release marks a significant shift for the Android ecosystem by standardizing a seamless, magnetic wireless charging experience that previously required third-party workarounds. By integrating high-end hardware with a reliable charging standard, Motorola is setting a new benchmark for flagship convenience and cross-device functionality.
