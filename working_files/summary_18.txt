It's STILL a terrible time to buy a Surface, but it's not just Microsoft's fault
============================================================

Microsoft has unveiled the Surface Pro 12 and Surface Laptop 8 featuring Snapdragon X2 chips, though both devices launch with significantly higher price tags than their previous iterations.

### Key Points
- The Surface Pro 12 starts at $1,499, while the Surface Laptop 8 begins at $1,599, representing $500 and $600 increases over predecessor launch prices.
- Ongoing global component shortages, particularly regarding RAM, have forced major manufacturers including Dell, HP, Lenovo, and Apple to raise hardware prices.
- Budget-friendly laptops like the Dell XPS 13 and the $599 MacBook Neo are gaining market share by offering high-quality designs at lower price points.
- Current discounts on older models like the Surface Pro 11 and Surface Laptop 7 often only bring prices down to levels seen one year ago.

### Why it Matters
Rising component costs and premium pricing strategies are making high-end laptops increasingly difficult to justify for the average consumer. As a result, budget-tier devices are becoming the more practical choice for most users, shifting the competitive landscape for major PC manufacturers.
