What’s keeping IT leaders up at night in the AI era?
============================================================

IT leaders are prioritizing data security and regulatory compliance as they struggle to modernize legacy infrastructure while facing a critical shortage of skilled personnel in an AI-driven landscape.

### Key Points
- Data security is the primary concern for 69% of IT leaders, surpassing the need for general performance optimization.
- Regulatory frameworks like DORA and the EU AI Act now require granular, continuous oversight rather than static, one-off audit compliance.
- A 2024 Financial Conduct Authority study revealed that 92% of UK financial services firms still rely on legacy technology.
- The retirement of experienced professionals is widening the talent gap, forcing organizations to prioritize reskilling and internal knowledge retention.
- Successful AI integration depends on high-quality, traceable data, making robust data governance a prerequisite for modern digital initiatives.

### Why it Matters
Organizations must shift from reactive compliance to a strategy of continuous cyber resilience to protect expanding digital footprints. Failing to bridge the gap between legacy systems and modern AI requirements risks both regulatory penalties and an inability to compete in an increasingly automated market.
