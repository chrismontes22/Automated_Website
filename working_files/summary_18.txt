Eufy Brings Local AI Agents to Home Security, the First I've Seen Without a Subscription
============================================================

Eufy announced the upcoming Smart Security Shield, a new outdoor camera system featuring a local AI agent called EdgeAgent that provides advanced facial recognition without requiring monthly subscription fees.

### Key Points
- The Smart Security Shield features a 180-degree field of view and identifies individuals from up to 100 feet away.
- EdgeAgent processes data locally on the device, resulting in AI performance that is 63% faster than cloud-based competitors.
- The system supports automated security responses, including spotlight activation, voice warnings, and descriptive mobile alerts.
- Eufy utilizes digital security key technology and local storage to enhance user privacy and prevent unauthorized data access.
- The product is scheduled for a commercial launch in the second half of 2026.

### Why it Matters
This release challenges the industry standard of charging monthly subscription fees for advanced AI features like facial recognition and descriptive motion alerts. By prioritizing local processing, Eufy offers a privacy-focused alternative that reduces reliance on cloud servers and minimizes the risk of data breaches.
