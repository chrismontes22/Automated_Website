AI XRP Price Prediction: Claude Fable 5 Predicts the XRP Price for End of 2026
============================================================

### Key Points
- XRP peaked at $2.40 in January before declining over 50% to a June low of $1.02.
- Claude Fable 5 assigns a 50% probability to a base-case year-end price between $1.50 and $1.90.

- Price performance remains heavily dependent on the CLARITY Act, Federal Reserve interest rate decisions, and Bitcoin market trends.

### Why it Matters
The price of XRP remains highly sensitive to macroeconomic shifts and legislative developments regarding its legal status. Investors are closely monitoring these variables to determine if the asset can break its year-long downtrend or if it will face further downward pressure.
