Michael Saylor’s Latest Post Has the Market Looking for Strategy’s Next Bitcoin Buy
============================================================

MicroStrategy Executive Chairman Michael Saylor has sparked market speculation regarding another major bitcoin acquisition after posting a cryptic message alongside the company’s current treasury dashboard on social media.

### Key Points
- MicroStrategy currently holds 780,897 BTC, acquired at an average price of $75,577 per coin for a total cost of $59.02 billion.
- The company recently disclosed an April 13 SEC filing confirming the purchase of 13,927 BTC for approximately $1.0 billion.
- Funding for the latest acquisition was generated through the sale of STRC preferred stock.
- Current company data shows $2.25 billion in USD reserves and 10% net leverage, indicating potential liquidity for future purchases.

### Why it Matters
MicroStrategy’s consistent accumulation strategy serves as a significant bellwether for institutional bitcoin adoption and market sentiment. Investors closely monitor these signals as indicators of the company's ongoing commitment to scaling its digital asset treasury through aggressive capital market activity.
