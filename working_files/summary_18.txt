PUBG has genAI team mates now capable of "intelligent decision-making", and I must remind you that Krafton are working on bots for the military
============================================================

Krafton has launched a beta for PUBG Ally Duo, a new feature in PUBG: Battlegrounds that introduces generative AI teammates powered by Nvidia technology to simulate human-like combat behavior.

### Key Points
- The PUBG Ally Duo beta is available in the game's Arcade mode until June 30.
- AI teammates utilize Nvidia ACE technology to handle tactical decision-making and real-time voice communication.
- The system runs locally on RTX GPUs with at least 8GB of VRAM using Parakeet speech-to-text and Mistral-Nemo-Minitron language models.
- Krafton is using the beta period to collect player feedback to refine the performance of autonomous game agents.
- This initiative aligns with Krafton’s broader corporate strategy of investing in generative AI and large-scale virtual world simulation.

### Why it Matters
This integration signals a shift toward more sophisticated, conversational NPCs that could fundamentally change social dynamics and content creation within multiplayer gaming. By leveraging local hardware for real-time AI interaction, developers are setting a new standard for how players engage with virtual teammates in competitive environments.
