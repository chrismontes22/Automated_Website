The 90-Day GEO Playbook for Local Search: How To Show Up When AI Does The Searching
============================================================

Generative Engine Optimization is emerging as a critical strategy for brands to secure visibility as consumer behavior shifts toward AI-powered search and away from traditional website clicks.

### Key Points
- Research indicates $750 billion in consumer spending is shifting toward AI search, with 60% of queries now ending without a website click.
- Approximately 68% of brands are currently missing from AI-generated recommendations, creating a significant revenue and visibility risk.
- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) focuses on being cited and trusted by AI models rather than simply ranking on search engine results pages.
- A 90-day implementation plan includes auditing NAP data, engineering content to answer specific conversational questions, and building off-page authority.
- Early adopters of GEO strategies have reported up to double the citation rates and 3 to 9 times higher conversion rates than traditional SEO-focused competitors.

### Why it Matters
As zero-click search behavior becomes the standard, the AI-generated citation is replacing the traditional website link as the primary conversion surface. Businesses that fail to adapt their digital presence for AI models risk losing their status as recommended providers to competitors who have optimized for conversational search.
