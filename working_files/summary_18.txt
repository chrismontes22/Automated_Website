PlayStation Boss Embraces AI At Every Level: ‘AI Brings More Choices To Players Than Ever’
============================================================

PlayStation CEO Hideaki Nishino announced plans to integrate generative AI across Sony’s gaming ecosystem to streamline development, enhance visual fidelity, and improve personalized content discovery for global players.

### Key Points
- Sony is deploying internal AI tools like "Mockingbird" to automate 3D facial animation, reducing processing time from hours to fractions of a second.
- First-party studios including Naughty Dog and Guerrilla Games are using AI to accelerate labor-intensive tasks like rendering complex 3D hair models.
- The company is experimenting with AI-driven NPCs capable of unscripted dialogue and dynamic personalities to create more immersive, living game worlds.
- Sony plans to use AI algorithms to personalize the PlayStation Store, recommending games, accessories, and merchandise based on individual user behavior.
- Executives view AI as a critical solution to rising AAA development costs and the need to manage an increasingly crowded digital content landscape.

### Why it Matters
Integrating AI into game development could significantly reduce production costs and timelines for high-budget titles, potentially addressing the industry's current financial strain. However, the strategy also risks flooding the platform with automated content, placing pressure on Sony to effectively curate experiences for its massive user base.
