This is the fastest-growing job for young workers, LinkedIn says
============================================================

LinkedIn reports that AI engineer is the fastest-growing job title for young workers in the United States, as companies aggressively recruit talent to integrate artificial intelligence into workflows.

### Key Points
- LinkedIn data shows 75,000 AI engineer job postings were added in the U.S. between 2023 and 2025.
- AI engineers are primarily responsible for building, running, and evaluating Large Language Models and AI agents.
- The technology and financial services sectors are currently the leading employers for entry-level AI engineering roles.
- Despite high demand for AI skills, overall hiring for entry-level positions in the U.S. declined by 6% between late 2025 and early 2026.
- Young workers face a 6.4% unemployment rate, significantly higher than the national average of 4.3%.

### Why it Matters
Developing AI literacy is becoming a critical requirement for young professionals attempting to break into a competitive and tightening labor market. As businesses prioritize automation to improve efficiency, workers who can build and scale AI models are gaining a distinct advantage over those in roles involving repetitive tasks.
