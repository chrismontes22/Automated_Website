This $179 Speaker Has No Battery And Makes Your iPhone Louder Anyway
============================================================

Yanko Design has released the Battery-free Amplifying iSpeakers, a precision-machined metal acoustic amplifier that boosts iPhone audio volume using physical geometry instead of electricity, batteries, or Bluetooth connectivity.

### Key Points
- The iSpeakers utilize a horn-shaped resonance chamber to amplify sound waves, operating on the same acoustic principles as 19th-century phonographs.
- The device requires no power, pairing, or software, functioning immediately when an iPhone is placed into the precision-engineered metal slot.
- Construction features machined metal, which provides a crisper, more distinct acoustic profile compared to traditional wooden passive amplifiers.
- Pricing is set at $179 for the standard speaker and $299 for a version with extended iPhone compatibility.
- The design is exclusively optimized for iPhone speaker placement, limiting its utility for users with Android devices.

### Why it Matters
This product challenges the modern reliance on electronic peripherals by offering a zero-maintenance alternative for minimalist desk setups. By removing the need for charging cables and wireless connectivity, it appeals to consumers prioritizing aesthetic simplicity and long-term hardware reliability over digital features.
