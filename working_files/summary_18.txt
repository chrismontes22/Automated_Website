Samsung to unveil new ‘Wide’ foldable alongside Galaxy Z Fold 8, S Pen: Report
============================================================

Samsung is reportedly planning to unveil a new "Wide" foldable smartphone alongside the Galaxy Z Fold 8 during its upcoming Galaxy Unpacked event in London on July 22.

### Key Points
- The upcoming Galaxy Unpacked event is scheduled for July 22 in London, United Kingdom.
- Samsung may reintroduce S Pen stylus support for its new foldable device lineup.
- The event is expected to feature new Galaxy Watch models alongside the latest foldable smartphones.
- Samsung is expanding its foldable portfolio to maintain market competitiveness against rivals in China and future Apple products.

### Why it Matters
This expansion signals Samsung's strategy to diversify its foldable offerings to defend its market share against increasing global competition. By integrating new form factors and stylus support, the company aims to maintain its leadership position before Apple enters the foldable smartphone segment.
