The AI Perception Reality Gap
============================================================

HubSpot is challenging industry norms by prioritizing outcome-based AI strategies over mere activity, arguing that businesses should focus on measurable results rather than simply increasing token usage and automation.

### Key Points
- HubSpot transitioned to outcome-based pricing in April, citing a 25% faster ticket response rate and 76% more leads generated for its AI agent users.
- The company argues that AI should function as a layer on top of existing business foundations rather than a replacement for human roles or core infrastructure.
- Current AI development disproportionately favors large enterprises with dedicated engineering teams, leaving small and midsize businesses without accessible, integrated solutions.
- HubSpot emphasizes that vendors are often incentivized to prioritize high token consumption, which conflicts with the customer's need for cost-efficient, high-impact business outcomes.
- The firm advocates for a "human-in-the-loop" approach, asserting that trust, judgment, and relationship-building remain essential competitive advantages that AI cannot replicate.

### Why it Matters
This shift in perspective highlights a growing tension between AI vendors focused on volume-based revenue and businesses seeking tangible return on investment. By aligning pricing models with specific outcomes, companies can better integrate AI into their workflows without sacrificing human expertise or operational stability.
