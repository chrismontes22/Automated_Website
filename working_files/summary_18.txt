"MAGA Inc.": CorpWatch on Trump's World of Tech Titans, Crypto Czars & Prison Profiteers
============================================================

A new CorpWatch report titled "MAGA Inc." alleges that President Trump’s allies and family are profiting significantly from administration policies involving cryptocurrency, mass deportation, and large-scale data surveillance.

### Key Points
- The report identifies three primary sectors profiting from the administration: cryptocurrency ventures, tech data centers, and private prison operators.
- Trump family members are reportedly involved in World Liberty Financial, a cryptocurrency project linked to anonymous money transfers and stablecoin operations.
- Tech companies including Palantir, Oracle, and OpenAI are allegedly leveraging government support to expand data centers and surveillance capabilities.
- Private prison firms like GEO Group and CoreCivic, alongside air broker CSI Aviation, are identified as major beneficiaries of federal deportation contracts.
- Cantor Fitzgerald, led by Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick, is cited as a key financial backer for both data center infrastructure and crypto-related ventures.

### Why it Matters
This report highlights potential conflicts of interest between federal policy decisions and the financial gains of the president's inner circle. It raises significant ethical and regulatory questions regarding the intersection of government surveillance, immigration enforcement, and private sector profit.
