Leaker: Apple to Downgrade iPhone 18 in Two Ways
============================================================

Reports indicate that Apple plans to implement manufacturing and component downgrades for the standard iPhone 18 model to maintain current price points without increasing costs for consumers.

### Key Points
- Leaker "Fixed Focus Digital" claims the iPhone 18 will feature reduced display specifications and lower-tier chip performance.
- Potential hardware changes include a reduction from five to four GPU cores in the A-series processor.
- Apple reportedly intends to align the standard iPhone 18 specifications more closely with the budget-friendly iPhone 18e model.
- Engineering Validation Testing for the new iPhone 18 lineup is scheduled to begin in June.
- The standard iPhone 18 is expected to launch in the spring of 2027, following a separate fall release for Pro models.

### Why it Matters
This strategy suggests Apple is prioritizing stable pricing over hardware innovation to navigate rising manufacturing costs. By narrowing the performance gap between standard and budget models, the company may shift its market positioning for future iPhone generations.
