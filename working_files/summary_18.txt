Equity MFs gain over 5% last week with international funds leading. Check top 7 with over 3% return
============================================================

Equity mutual funds saw strong weekly gains, with the Invesco India Infrastructure Fund leading the market by delivering a 5.70% return amid broad performance across several thematic sectors.

### Key Points
- Invesco India Infrastructure Fund achieved the highest weekly return at 5.70%.
- Nippon India Taiwan Equity Fund recorded a 4.36% gain during the same period.
- Samco Active Momentum Fund and Union Active Momentum Fund returned 3.66% and 3.08% respectively.
- Motilal Oswal Digital India Fund posted a 3.35% return, highlighting growth in the technology sector.
- Mirae Asset’s international funds focused on electric vehicles and artificial intelligence returned between 3.08% and 3.25%.

### Why it Matters
These returns highlight a period of significant growth for thematic and international equity funds, reflecting investor confidence in infrastructure, technology, and global innovation sectors. This performance underscores the potential for sector-specific strategies to outperform broader market benchmarks during periods of high volatility.
