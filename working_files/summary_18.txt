The Bitcoin Mining ETF That Returned 52% in One Week, And Most Income Investors Have Never Heard of It
============================================================

The CoinShares Valkyrie Bitcoin Miners ETF (WGMI) is outperforming Bitcoin prices by leveraging diversified digital infrastructure, allowing mining companies to pivot their hardware toward high-demand sectors like artificial intelligence.

### Key Points
- The WGMI ETF has recorded a 243.77% one-year return, significantly outpacing pure-play Bitcoin funds despite recent volatility in the underlying cryptocurrency's market price.
- Bitcoin mining firms are mitigating hardware depreciation by repurposing server power and equipment for artificial intelligence, research, and animation projects.
- WGMI is an actively managed fund launched in February 2022 that holds 26 stocks, with top positions including Cipher Digital, IREN Limited, and TeraWulf, Inc.
- Mining companies benefit from operational leverage where fixed infrastructure costs allow for exponential revenue growth when Bitcoin prices rise, while diversification protects against price drops.

### Why it Matters
This trend highlights a strategic shift where crypto-mining infrastructure is being treated as a versatile asset for the broader technology sector rather than a single-purpose tool. Investors are increasingly viewing these diversified mining companies as a way to gain exposure to the high-growth artificial intelligence market while hedging against the inherent volatility of Bitcoin.
