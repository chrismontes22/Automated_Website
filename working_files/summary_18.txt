This Windows laptop may have a moderate battery life, but it burns bright with an OLED display, 16GB of RAM, and a 512GB SSD
============================================================

**ASUS Zenbook 14 featuring an OLED touchscreen and Intel Core Ultra 7 processor is currently available at Best Buy for a discounted price of $699.99.**

**Key points**
- The laptop features a 14-inch FHD+ OLED touchscreen with 500 nits of brightness and 100% DCI-P3 color coverage.
- Internal hardware includes an Intel Core Ultra 7 255H processor, 16GB of RAM, and a 512GB SSD that is user-upgradeable up to 2TB.
- The device is housed in a durable all-metal chassis designed for portability and daily productivity tasks.
- Performance limitations include a low 14 TOPS NPU, which falls below the current 40 TOPS industry standard for AI-powered computing.
- Reported drawbacks include muffled built-in speakers and a moderate battery life lasting approximately 9 hours.

**Why it matters**
- This deal offers significant value for users seeking a premium OLED display and solid multitasking performance at a sub-$700 price point. While the hardware is not optimized for intensive AI tasks or modern gaming, it remains a highly capable machine for general office work and media consumption.
