Best Buy's 4th of July sale has discounted Windows laptops with fast performance and plenty of RAM — soothing the memory crisis
============================================================

Best Buy has launched a 4th of July sale featuring significant discounts on various high-performance laptops, providing consumers with an alternative opportunity to secure deals following Amazon's Prime Day.

### Key Points
- The sale runs through Sunday, July 5, 2026, and is available to all U.S. customers without requiring a membership.
- The Acer Swift Go 16-inch AI laptop is discounted by 42%, bringing the price down to $899.99.
- The Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 3i is available for $449.99, a reduction from its original $899.99 listing price.
- Featured models include the HP OmniBook X Flip 14, Dell 14 Plus, and the Asus Zenbook S 16.
- All deals are exclusive to the United States, with no international shipping options provided by the retailer.

### Why it Matters
These discounts offer a timely opportunity for students and remote workers to purchase reliable hardware at reduced price points. By highlighting well-reviewed models, the sale helps consumers navigate the market for productive, long-lasting laptops without the need for a premium subscription.
