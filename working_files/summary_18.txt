I Quit. The Clankers Won
============================================================

Despite the rise of generative AI and concerns over content automation, industry commentators are increasingly advocating for personal blogging as a vital tool for preserving authentic human expertise.

### Key Points
- Personal blogging serves as a critical countermeasure against the proliferation of AI-generated content and automated digital mediocrity.
- Writing publicly helps professionals refine their own thinking, improve memory retention, and establish individual authority in their respective fields.
- The author argues that Big Tech’s AI models rely on predatory business practices and the mass scraping of human-created intellectual property.
- Creators are encouraged to prioritize the "indie web" and open platforms over centralized, algorithm-driven services that prioritize corporate profit over human expression.
- The piece explicitly rejects the integration of generative tools like OpenAI's Sora, labeling AI-produced media as a vapid mockery of genuine human creativity.

### Why it Matters
This perspective highlights a growing cultural pushback against the automation of creative work and the dominance of large-scale AI models. By emphasizing the value of human-led content, it encourages professionals to maintain ownership of their intellectual output rather than relying on platforms that may devalue individual expertise.
