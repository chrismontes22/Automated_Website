Chess Peace
============================================================

The new iOS puzzle game Chess Peace offers over 26,500 logic challenges based on chess movement patterns, available for free download on the Apple App Store for iPhone users.

### Key Points
- Features 26,500+ unique puzzles across five distinct game modes, including daily challenges and varying difficulty levels.
- Designed for all skill levels, the app includes a built-in reference guide explaining chess piece movement patterns.
- Supports offline play, iCloud progress syncing, and extensive accessibility options like VoiceOver and high-contrast themes.
- Monetized through a one-time purchase model for full content access, with no subscriptions, ads, or data tracking.
- Compatible with iOS 17 or later and includes Apple Family Sharing for the full puzzle collection.

### Why it Matters
This app provides a privacy-focused, ad-free alternative to typical mobile games by prioritizing offline functionality and a one-time purchase model. Its accessibility-first design makes complex logic puzzles approachable for a wide audience, regardless of their prior chess knowledge or visual needs.
