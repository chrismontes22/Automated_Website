I spent weeks testing the TCL QM8L TV, and I'm a believer in its new SQD mini-LED technology — this is a stunning TV for a great price, with just a couple of notable quirks
============================================================

The TCL QM8L mini-LED TV delivers a high-end, flagship-style experience featuring impressive brightness and advanced gaming capabilities, though it faces minor challenges with out-of-the-box color accuracy.

### Key Points
- The TCL QM8L series launched in April 2026 with sizes ranging from 65 to 98 inches.
- It utilizes SQD-Mini LED panel technology with up to 4,000 local dimming zones and 6,000 nits of peak brightness.
- Gaming features include four HDMI 2.1 ports, 144Hz refresh rates, and a 9.5ms latency.
- The TV integrates Google Gemini for hands-free voice control and runs on the Google TV platform.
- Audio is powered by a Bang & Olufsen-branded system that supports spatial audio and Dolby Atmos.

### Why it Matters
The TCL QM8L offers a compelling alternative to premium OLED displays by providing high-performance mini-LED technology at a more accessible price point. Its robust feature set and gaming-focused specifications make it a strong contender for users seeking a versatile, high-brightness display for both entertainment and next-gen console gaming.
