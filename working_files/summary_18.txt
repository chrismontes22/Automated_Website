I know YouTube Music is flawed, yet I prefer it over Spotify
============================================================

Despite lacking the polished features and native app support of competitors like Spotify, YouTube Music remains a preferred choice for many due to its vast, unmatched content library.

### Key Points
- YouTube Music provides access to unofficial tracks, indie recordings, and rare jam sessions not available on platforms like Spotify or Apple Music.
- Spotify offers superior cross-platform integration through Spotify Connect and native desktop applications, which YouTube Music currently lacks.
- YouTube Music includes a cloud upload feature for personal audio files and is often bundled with a YouTube Premium subscription.
- Licensing issues frequently cause songs to be removed from Spotify, whereas YouTube’s integration with its video platform ensures greater catalog availability.

### Why it Matters
The choice between music streaming services highlights a trade-off between platform convenience and content depth. While competitors prioritize social features and hardware integration, YouTube Music’s massive, user-generated catalog serves as a critical differentiator for listeners who prioritize music discovery over app aesthetics.
