Melania Trump Escorted by Talking Humanoid Robot at White House Tech Summit
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
Melania Trump introduced the Figure 03 humanoid robot at the Fostering the Future Together Global Coalition Summit to promote the potential of AI-powered technology in childhood education.

**Key points**
- The Figure 03 robot, developed by Silicon Valley firm Figure AI, uses the proprietary Helix engine to perform autonomous tasks and respond to voice commands.
- Priced at approximately $25,000, the robot is designed for both domestic chores and corporate roles like reception and delivery.
- The summit hosted representatives from 45 nations and major tech companies, including Google, OpenAI, Microsoft, and Palantir.
- Education Secretary Linda McMahon confirmed that the Department of Education is prioritizing AI infrastructure and has already awarded millions in related grants.
- The demonstration faced public backlash from critics concerned that AI integration could lead to teacher layoffs and the loss of essential human connection in classrooms.

**Why it matters**
The integration of humanoid robots into education highlights a growing tension between the push for personalized, tech-driven learning and concerns regarding the displacement of human educators. This initiative reflects broader administration efforts to modernize schooling while navigating significant public skepticism and ongoing policy shifts within the Department of Education.
