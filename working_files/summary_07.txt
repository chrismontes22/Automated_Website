I've finally found a premium Meta Quest 3 headstrap that doesn't suck
============================================================

The BoboVR S3 Pro headstrap for Meta Quest 3 and 3S enhances virtual reality sessions by integrating a cooling fan, adjustable comfort features, and a 10,000mAh battery pack.

### Key Points
- The BoboVR S3 Pro retails for $89 and is designed specifically for the Meta Quest 3 and Quest 3S headsets.
- It features a magnetically detachable 10,000mAh battery pack that provides approximately five hours of additional playtime.
- A built-in, adjustable cooling fan helps regulate forehead temperature and prevents lens fogging during extended use.
- The design utilizes a unique hinge system that allows the headset to swivel independently, reducing pressure on the user's face.
- The included battery pack requires roughly two hours to fully charge using a 30W USB-C power source.

### Why it Matters
The BoboVR S3 Pro sets a high standard for premium VR accessories by addressing common user complaints regarding weight distribution and thermal comfort. By prioritizing ergonomic adjustments and extended battery life, this product significantly improves the long-term usability of Meta's popular standalone headsets.
