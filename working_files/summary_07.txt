Amazon’s Echo Hub gets a customizable new look and Ring’s AI features
============================================================

Amazon is rolling out a free software update for Echo Hub devices, introducing a fully customizable homescreen interface and new AI-powered video search capabilities for integrated Ring cameras.

### Key Points
- Users can now organize, resize, and rearrange dashboard tiles by room or function using a new drag-and-drop interface.
- The update adds Ring AI Video Search, allowing users to locate specific smart home camera footage using natural language queries.
- Alexa Plus integration now provides automated summaries of detected camera events directly on the smart display.
- New granular controls allow for precise adjustments, such as 0-100% dimming and color selection for compatible smart lighting.
- Frequently used smart home routines can now be pinned to the homescreen for single-tap activation.

### Why it Matters
This update enhances the utility of the Echo Hub by transforming it into a more personalized and efficient command center for smart home management. By integrating advanced AI search and flexible interface design, Amazon is positioning the device to better compete in the increasingly crowded smart home display market.
