This $54.99 MagSafe Power Bank Hits the Same Charging Speed as a Wall Adapter
============================================================

The new INIU SnapGo Air magnetic power bank combines a 10,000mAh capacity with 25W wireless charging and 45W wired output to provide high-performance portable power for modern mobile devices.

### Key Points
- The SnapGo Air features the Qi2.2 wireless standard and an integrated 45W USB-C GoCord for fast charging.
- It utilizes the Temp Guard 3.0 system to monitor internal temperatures 9,000 times per second, keeping surface heat below 104 degrees Fahrenheit.
- The device is constructed from anodized aluminum, measures 0.5 inches thick, and weighs 195 grams.
- It supports simultaneous charging for up to three devices and reaches a full power bank recharge in approximately 1.8 hours.
- The product is UL-certified and includes an 18-layer SmartProtect system for safety against overvoltage and foreign objects.
- Available in four colors, the power bank is priced at $54.99 in the United States.

### Why it Matters
This device signals a shift in the portable charger market by prioritizing high-speed performance and thermal management over simple convenience. By offering charging capabilities that rival traditional wall adapters, it provides a viable daily-use solution for power-hungry smartphones, tablets, and lightweight laptops.
