Adobe brings Acrobat integration directly to WhatsApp Web
============================================================

Adobe has integrated its Acrobat PDF tools directly into WhatsApp Web and the Windows desktop application, allowing users to view, review, and annotate documents without leaving their chat windows.

### Key Points
- Users can now open and annotate PDF files directly within WhatsApp Web and the Windows app without downloading them.
- The integration supports password-protected documents while maintaining WhatsApp’s end-to-end encryption for all shared files.
- Adobe aims to streamline workflows for tasks like reviewing contracts, invoices, and assignments on larger screens.
- This update aligns with Adobe’s strategy to embed Acrobat functionality into popular platforms like Microsoft 365, ChatGPT, and Workday.

### Why it Matters
This integration significantly improves productivity for professionals who use WhatsApp as a primary tool for document collaboration and business communication. By removing the need to switch between applications, Adobe and WhatsApp are reducing friction in digital workflows and increasing the utility of messaging platforms for enterprise tasks.
