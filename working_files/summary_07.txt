ASUS’ New $20 Speaker Undercuts the JBL Go 5 by Two-Thirds
============================================================

ASUS has launched the Adol PO102 portable Bluetooth speaker in China, offering a feature-rich, budget-friendly alternative to the JBL Go 5 for approximately $20 USD at current exchange rates.

### Key Points
- The speaker features Bluetooth 6.0, a 6W output, and an IP67 dust and water resistance rating.
- It includes a 2,600 mAh battery providing up to 10 hours of playback at 50% volume.
- Additional hardware includes a built-in carrying strap, a microSD card slot, and a microphone for hands-free calls.
- The device is currently available exclusively on JD.com in Meteorite Gray, Iris Purple, and Sage Green.
- It supports TWS pairing and Auracast technology for multi-speaker connectivity.

### Why it Matters
The Adol PO102 represents a significant price disruption in the portable audio market by offering high-end specifications at roughly one-third the cost of established competitors like the JBL Go 5. While currently limited to the Chinese market, its aggressive pricing and feature set could force global competitors to reevaluate their entry-level product strategies if ASUS pursues an international release.
