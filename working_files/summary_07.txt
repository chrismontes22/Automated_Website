Specialized Keeps Digitizing the Commuter e-Bicycle
============================================================

Specialized has launched the Vado 3 EVO, a high-tech commuter e-bike series featuring advanced Apple and Garmin integration, fast-charging capabilities, and a powerful 105 Nm torque motor system.

### Key Points
- The Vado 3 EVO series is priced between $4,500 and $7,000 depending on the specific model and features.
- Integrated technology includes Apple Find My tracking, Garmin radar for traffic detection, and wireless phone charging.
- The bike features a high-speed charger capable of reaching an 80% battery charge in one hour with a maximum range of 93 miles.
- Specialized incorporated a dropper seatpost and an upright cockpit design to improve rider comfort and stability during urban stops.
- The system syncs with the Specialized app, Apple Watch, and Apple Health to track pedaling output, heart rate, and ride metrics.

### Why it Matters
This release highlights a growing trend of premium bicycle manufacturers integrating sophisticated software and hardware ecosystems to compete with consumer electronics. By merging fitness tracking with high-performance commuting, Specialized is positioning its bikes as essential tools for tech-focused urban professionals.
