OPPO puts the Find X9 Ultra and 'global' in the same sentence, teases April debut
============================================================

**OPPO is bringing its high-end Find X9 Ultra smartphone to the global market for the first time in April 2026.**

**Key points**

*   **Global Expansion:** OPPO has officially confirmed that the "Ultra" version of its Find X9 series will be released worldwide, marking a first for the company’s top-tier model.
*   **Camera-Focused Design:** The phone is being marketed as a professional-grade camera replacement, featuring a dedicated physical camera button and advanced imaging capabilities.
*   **High-End Specs:** Leaks suggest the device will include a 6.8-inch 144Hz display, the powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor, and a massive 7,050mAh battery.
*   **Advanced Photography:** Building on the success of the Find X9 Pro, the Ultra model is expected to push mobile photography boundaries with high-resolution sensors and specialized zoom lenses.

**Why it matters**
This release signals OPPO’s intent to compete directly with premium global flagship phones like the Samsung Galaxy S Ultra series. By bringing its most advanced camera technology to international markets, OPPO is positioning itself as a top choice for photography enthusiasts who want professional-level features in a smartphone.
