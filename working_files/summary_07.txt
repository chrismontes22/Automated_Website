Everything New in iOS 27 Beta 4
============================================================

Apple has released the fourth developer beta of iOS 27, introducing new settings for the TV app, Photos, Siri, and camera features ahead of the official September launch.

### Key Points
- The Apple TV app now includes an automatic download feature for upcoming episodes in the "Continue Watching" queue.
- New camera settings allow users to select between ProRes Log and Log 2 formats for enhanced color grading flexibility.
- Siri updates include expanded voice options, adjustable preview lengths, and a new transcription display toggle in Accessibility.
- A "Zoom Photos to Fill" setting has been added to the Photos app to optimize image display for specific screen aspect ratios.
- System code references a potential dual-battery design, fueling speculation about the development of a foldable iPhone Ultra model.

### Why it Matters
These refinements indicate that Apple is finalizing core system features and hardware-specific optimizations as the software nears its public release. The inclusion of dual-battery references suggests the company is preparing its operating system for future hardware innovations like foldable devices.
