SwitchBot Debuts Advanced Camera With AI Event Alerts, Wildlife Recognition
============================================================

SwitchBot has launched a new 3K outdoor pan/tilt security camera featuring advanced AI-powered video descriptions and wildlife identification capabilities for a starting price of $80.

### Key Points
- The camera offers 3K resolution with both wired and wireless connectivity options and supports up to 512GB of local storage.
- AI technology provides descriptive event notifications and daily video summaries, with subscription plans starting at $5 per month.
- Specialized wildlife recognition software can identify specific animal species, such as coyotes, raccoons, and possums.
- The device integrates with smart home ecosystems, including Amazon Alexa and Google Home, for live feed viewing on compatible displays.

### Why it Matters
This release highlights the increasing industry shift toward integrating generative AI into affordable home security hardware to provide more detailed user alerts. While these features offer enhanced monitoring capabilities, they also raise ongoing questions regarding data privacy and the potential for future facial recognition implementation.
