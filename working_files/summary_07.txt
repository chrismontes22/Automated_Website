Here's What's New With Visual Intelligence in iOS 27
============================================================

Apple has upgraded Visual Intelligence in iOS 27, integrating the feature directly into the Camera app’s Siri Mode and expanding its availability to iPad, Mac, and Vision Pro devices.

### Key Points
- Visual Intelligence is now accessible via a dedicated Siri Mode in the Camera app or by holding the Camera Control button.
- New capabilities include nutritional analysis, U.S.-exclusive bill splitting via Apple Cash, and importing contacts or calendar events from photos.
- Siri now processes complex visual queries internally, removing the previous requirement to hand off tasks to ChatGPT.
- The feature is available on iPhone 15 Pro or later, Apple silicon Macs, and specific iPad models including the A17 Pro iPad mini.
- Users can save and revisit visual search history within the Siri app for up to one year or indefinitely.

### Why it Matters
This update significantly enhances the utility of Apple’s ecosystem by centralizing AI-driven visual analysis across mobile, desktop, and spatial computing platforms. By reducing reliance on third-party tools like ChatGPT, Apple is positioning its native Siri AI as a more capable and integrated assistant for daily productivity tasks.
