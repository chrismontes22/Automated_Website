iOS 27: What's New With the Mail App
============================================================

Apple is introducing significant updates to the Mail app in iOS 27, featuring an overhauled search system and new artificial intelligence capabilities powered by Apple Intelligence for compatible devices.

### Key Points
- The new search system prioritizes relevance and user intent over simple keyword matching to improve result accuracy.
- Siri integration allows users to summarize emails, track packages, and perform tasks like deleting messages from specific senders.
- The "Write with Siri" tool drafts and refines emails while mimicking the user's personal writing style and tone.
- Call Context displays relevant reservation or order numbers from emails directly within the Phone app during business calls.
- Contextual suggestions now offer one-tap integration for adding flight, restaurant, and map data to Calendar, Wallet, or Maps.
- Advanced AI features require an iPhone that supports Apple Intelligence and are currently restricted in the European Union and China.

### Why it Matters
These updates represent a major shift toward integrating generative AI directly into core communication tools to streamline daily productivity. By automating routine tasks and surfacing information contextually, Apple aims to reduce the time users spend managing their inboxes and navigating between different applications.
