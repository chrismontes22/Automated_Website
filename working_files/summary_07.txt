$25 for Audiophile Earbuds? Meet Panasonic's New ErgoFit USB-C Wired Buds
============================================================

Panasonic has released the ErgoFit RP-TCM325, a $25 pair of USB-C wired earbuds that offer high-quality audio and clear voice calling performance for modern mobile devices and computers.

### Key Points
- The RP-TCM325 features a tangle-resistant cord and an inline remote for volume, track control, and call management.
- These earbuds utilize a USB-C connection with a built-in Digital-to-Analog Converter (DAC) for digital audio playback.
- Panasonic includes three sizes of silicone eartips, though users may need third-party tips to achieve an optimal acoustic seal.
- The product earned a CNET Editors' Choice award for its balanced sound profile and superior voice clarity compared to competitors.
- Unlike many modern wireless alternatives, these earbuds do not feature active noise canceling, relying instead on passive isolation.

### Why it Matters
These earbuds provide a high-value, wired alternative for users who prefer reliable, battery-free audio over more expensive wireless options. By updating a classic design for the USB-C standard, Panasonic demonstrates that budget-friendly wired hardware can still outperform significantly pricier wireless devices in sound and call quality.
