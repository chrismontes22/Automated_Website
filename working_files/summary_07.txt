These Bang & Olufsen earbuds are pricey— but that's nothing a 30% Prime Day discount can't fix
============================================================

Amazon Prime Day features a record-low price for the premium Bang and Olufsen Beoplay Eleven earbuds, offering a 30% discount that brings the cost down to $419.30.

### Key Points
- The Bang and Olufsen Beoplay Eleven earbuds are currently discounted from $599.00 to $419.30.
- This 30% price reduction represents the lowest price ever offered for this specific model.
- The deal is primarily available on the Eucalyptus Green color variant, with smaller discounts on other colors.
- Key hardware features include 9.2mm drivers, a frequency range of 20 to 22,000Hz, and water and dust resistance.
- Battery life is rated at six hours with active noise cancellation enabled and up to 20 hours with it disabled.

### Why it Matters
This discount makes high-end audio equipment more accessible to consumers who are typically deterred by the standard $600 retail price. It highlights the aggressive pricing strategies used by retailers during Prime Day to move premium electronics that rarely see significant markdowns.
