Gmail's new AI Inbox is here, but it'll cost you $250 a month
============================================================

Google has officially launched its AI Inbox feature for Gmail, integrating Gemini technology to help U.S.-based AI Ultra subscribers prioritize essential emails, appointments, and messages from key contacts.

### Key Points
- The AI Inbox feature automatically identifies and highlights critical information like upcoming bills and scheduled appointments.
- Access is currently restricted to Google AI Ultra subscribers in the United States.
- The AI Ultra subscription costs $250 per month and includes 30TB of cloud storage, YouTube Premium, and Google Home Premium Advanced.
- Gemini integration provides a personalized briefing space to help users manage high-volume email traffic more efficiently.
- Google plans to potentially expand this feature to AI Pro users and free accounts in the future.

### Why it Matters
This rollout signals Google's strategy to monetize advanced generative AI features by bundling them into high-tier subscription plans for power users. While the current price point is significant, the integration of Gemini into core productivity tools could eventually reshape how general users manage digital communication and information overload.
