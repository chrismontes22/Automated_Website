12 New Things Your iPhone Can Do in iOS 27
============================================================

Apple is set to release iOS 27 this September, featuring a major integration of Apple Intelligence across system apps, enhanced Siri capabilities, and new generative AI-powered creative tools.

### Key Points
- Apple Intelligence enables natural language processing for Reminders, Calendar entries, and custom Shortcuts creation.
- Safari now automatically organizes open tabs into topic-based collections using AI-driven categorization.
- The Camera app introduces a "Siri" mode for real-time object identification, including food calorie tracking and text translation.
- Image Playground receives a generative model update capable of producing high-quality, photo-realistic images with SynthID watermarking.
- Messages now supports inline replies for Android users, while the Wallet app expands its redesigned pass layout to loyalty and rewards cards.
- Users can now adjust the translucency of the Liquid Glass UI via a new slider in the system settings.

### Why it Matters
This update signals Apple's aggressive shift toward embedding generative AI directly into the core user experience to improve productivity and device personalization. By expanding these features to third-party messaging and everyday apps, Apple aims to maintain its competitive edge in the smartphone market against increasingly AI-capable rivals.
