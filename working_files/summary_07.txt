Apple accessories like this Mophie travel charging kit make me jealous as a Google Pixel user
============================================================

The Mophie Roam 3-in-1 Travel Charger offers a premium, portable solution for Apple users, featuring dedicated wireless pads for iPhones, Apple Watches, and AirPods in a compact design.

### Key Points
- The charger includes a 25W pad for iPhones, a 7.5W pad for Apple Watches, and a 5W pad for AirPods.
- It utilizes proprietary StealthCharge Technology to dissipate heat quietly without the need for internal fans.
- The kit is MagSafe and Qi 2.2-certified and includes a 40W USB-C wall adapter with international plug prongs.
- It features a foldable, travel-friendly design that fits into a minimalist leather pouch for portability.
- The 2026 fourth-generation model is currently available in Black or Cream for $149.95.

### Why it Matters
This product highlights the disparity in high-quality, all-in-one charging accessories between the Apple and Google Pixel ecosystems. While Apple users benefit from comprehensive, officially certified travel kits, Pixel users currently lack similar integrated solutions due to hardware fragmentation and a lack of standardized watch charging support.
