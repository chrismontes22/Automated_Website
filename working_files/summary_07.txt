Sam Altman Says Elon Musk Is ‘Obsessed’ With Him In Boast About Latest GPT Model
============================================================

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman and SpaceXAI chief Elon Musk have traded public insults following the simultaneous release of their competing flagship artificial intelligence models, GPT-5.6 and Grok 4.5.

### Key Points
- OpenAI released GPT-5.6, an agentic model designed for complex business workflows, cybersecurity, and broad reasoning tasks.
- SpaceXAI launched Grok 4.5, which focuses on autonomous coding and developer efficiency at a lower cost than OpenAI’s latest offering.
- Sam Altman claimed GPT-5.6 is the world's best model, prompting Elon Musk to accuse the OpenAI executive of misleading customers.
- The public dispute follows a recent legal battle where Musk unsuccessfully sued Altman for $150 billion over OpenAI’s transition to a for-profit structure.
- Musk is currently pursuing orbital data centers to address global AI energy constraints, a strategy Altman publicly criticized during their exchange.

### Why it Matters
The escalating rivalry between these two industry leaders highlights the intense competition to dominate the rapidly evolving autonomous AI agent market. This conflict underscores the broader tension between established AI developers and challengers as they race to secure market share through superior performance and operational efficiency.
