There's no doubt — the OnePlus Nord 6 is the battery champion of 2026
============================================================

The new OnePlus Nord 6 launches in India on April 9, featuring a massive 9,000mAh battery and powerful hardware designed to dominate the mid-range smartphone market segment.

### Key Points
- The OnePlus Nord 6 features a 9,000mAh silicon-carbon battery capable of lasting two days under heavy usage.
- Pricing starts at ₹38,999 for the 8GB/256GB model and ₹41,999 for the 12GB/256GB variant.
- The device is powered by the Snapdragon 8s Gen 4 chipset and includes a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate.
- It offers IP68 and IP69 ratings for dust and water resistance, ensuring durability in harsh weather conditions.
- Software support includes four Android OS updates and six years of security patches for the OxygenOS 16 interface.

### Why it Matters
The Nord 6 sets a new industry benchmark for battery endurance in the mid-range category without sacrificing a slim 8.5mm form factor. This release reinforces OnePlus's commitment to the Indian market while providing a high-performance alternative to more expensive flagship devices.
