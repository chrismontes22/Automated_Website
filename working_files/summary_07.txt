Which Oura Ring 5 color should you buy?
============================================================

Oura has officially launched the Oura Ring 5, a titanium smart ring that is 40% smaller than its predecessor and available in six distinct color finishes starting at $399.

### Key Points
- The Oura Ring 5 features a titanium construction and is available in ring sizes ranging from six to 13.
- Pricing begins at $399, representing a $50 increase over the previous generation Oura Ring 4.
- Consumers can choose between two entry-level glossy finishes, Silver and Black, at the base price point.
- Premium colorways, including Gold and matte finish options, require an additional $100 investment.
- Users must maintain a monthly subscription service to access the full suite of health tracking features.

### Why it Matters
The Oura Ring 5 represents a significant design shift toward a more compact form factor, though the increased entry price and ongoing subscription costs may influence consumer purchasing decisions. This release highlights the company's strategy to position its wearable technology as a premium fashion accessory while maintaining its core health-monitoring ecosystem.
