Google just pulled the plug on Pixel's AI image generator
============================================================

Google has officially discontinued the Pixel Studio image generation app for Pixel devices, redirecting users to the Gemini app to utilize advanced Nano Banana models for future AI creations.

### Key Points
- Google released version 2.3 of Pixel Studio, which completely disables all on-device image generation capabilities.
- The app now features an "Open Gemini" button that prompts users to transition to the Gemini platform for image tasks.
- Users retain access to their previously generated images, which remain viewable and shareable within the Pixel Studio interface.
- Pixel Studio was originally launched in 2024 alongside the Pixel 9 series to provide local AI-powered image creation tools.

### Why it Matters
This move reflects Google's broader strategy to consolidate its AI features under the Gemini brand rather than maintaining fragmented, device-specific applications. By shifting users to Gemini, the company aims to provide a more powerful and unified experience powered by its latest Nano Banana models.
