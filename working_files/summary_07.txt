This durable touchscreen Chromebook scored a 40% discount at Amazon — is it worth it?
============================================================

Amazon has discounted the Lenovo IdeaPad Flex 3i 2-in-1 Chromebook to $350, offering a 40% price reduction on the versatile 12.2-inch touchscreen device for a limited time.

### Key Points
- The Lenovo IdeaPad Flex 3i is currently priced at $350, down from its standard retail price of $580.
- The device features a 360-degree rotating hinge, allowing it to function as a laptop, tablet, or tent-style display.
- Hardware specifications include 128GB of storage, a 12.2-inch IPS touchscreen, and a 720p front-facing camera.
- Connectivity options consist of two USB-C ports, one USB-A port, an HDMI port, and a microSD card reader.
- Lenovo rates the battery life for this Chrome OS device at up to 10 hours per charge.

### Why it Matters
This deal provides a budget-friendly entry point for students and casual users seeking a flexible, multi-mode computing device. The significant price cut makes the hardware a competitive option for those prioritizing utility and battery life over a lightweight, modern aesthetic.
