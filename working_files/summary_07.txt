Framework has good news and bad news
============================================================

Framework is upgrading storage capacities for existing Laptop 13 Pro preorders at a lower cost, though the company warns that rising CPU prices may soon increase overall system costs.

### Key Points
- Framework is upgrading 500GB SSD preorders to 1TB drives at a reduced price following a new supply agreement with Adata.
- Customers can now select 1TB or 2TB PCIe Gen 5 SSD configurations for new Framework Laptop 13 Pro orders.
- The company expects to raise overall system prices in the coming weeks due to anticipated increases in CPU component costs.
- Initial shipments of the Laptop 13 Pro have been delayed from June to July to address issues with the display and haptic touchpad.

### Why it Matters
These price adjustments highlight the ongoing volatility in the global hardware supply chain, which continues to impact consumer electronics pricing across the industry. While early adopters benefit from storage upgrades, the potential for broader system price hikes reflects the persistent pressure of rising component costs on hardware manufacturers.
