3 reasons why this 140W GaN charger took over my desk
============================================================

Satechi has launched the ChargeView 140W, a compact four-port GaN charger featuring real-time power monitoring and USB PD 3.2 AVS support for efficient multi-device charging at a $99 price point.

### Key Points
- The ChargeView 140W features four USB-C ports and supports a total power output of 140W.
- It utilizes the USB PD 3.2 AVS standard on the primary port for precise voltage adjustments.
- The device includes an integrated LCD panel that displays real-time charging data and supports orientation switching.
- Satechi includes a versatile stand allowing the charger to be positioned either horizontally or vertically.
- The charger is priced at $99 and is designed to manage power distribution intelligently across multiple connected devices.

### Why it Matters
This charger represents a growing trend of high-wattage, compact GaN accessories that prioritize desk space without sacrificing performance. By offering advanced power standards and real-time monitoring at a competitive price, it provides a practical solution for users managing multiple high-power devices simultaneously.
