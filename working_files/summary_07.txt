iOS 26 Feels Faster Than iOS 18
============================================================

Apple has expanded the availability of the iOS 18.7.7 security update to all compatible devices, providing critical protection for users who have not yet upgraded to iOS 26.

### Key Points
- Apple released iOS 18.7.7 on April 1 to address severe security vulnerabilities identified as DarkSword and Coruna.
- The update is now available for all iPhones capable of running iOS 18, including those that support the newer iOS 26.
- Users with auto-update enabled can now receive these security patches without being forced to perform a full operating system upgrade.
- The update addresses previous concerns regarding Apple withholding security patches from users who choose to remain on older software versions.

### Why it Matters
This change ensures that users who prefer to delay major OS upgrades can still maintain device security against critical exploits. It highlights a shift in Apple's update policy, aligning iPhone security support more closely with the long-term maintenance standards typically seen in the macOS ecosystem.
