Weibo leaker says iPhone 18 Pro thickness will be ‘surprising’
============================================================

Leaked internal documents from Apple supplier Tata suggest the upcoming iPhone 18 Pro will feature a thicker chassis and redesigned camera plateau compared to the current iPhone 17 Pro.

### Key Points
- Leaked files from a Tata data breach include internal documents and videos showing iPhone 18 Pro models undergoing drop tests.
- Social media leaker Fixed Focus Digital claims the device will feature an aluminum-alloy backplate approximately 2mm thicker than previous models.
- The increased thickness is reportedly linked to a redesigned camera system, potentially incorporating variable aperture technology.
- Reports confirm the iPhone 18 Pro will continue utilizing aluminum-alloy midframes in its structural design.

### Why it Matters
The potential increase in device thickness suggests a significant shift in Apple’s hardware engineering to accommodate advanced camera components. This design change could influence future smartphone ergonomics and set a new standard for mobile photography capabilities within the premium handset market.
