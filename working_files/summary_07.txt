18 ways AI is rewriting the future of financial services: Key insights from the AWS Financial Services Symposium
============================================================

Financial institutions are rapidly shifting from experimental AI pilots to production-ready agentic systems to automate complex workflows, enhance operational efficiency, and improve real-time decision-making across global markets.

### Key Points
- Financial firms are prioritizing generative AI in 2025 budgets, with 41% of IT leaders focusing specifically on optimizing AI workflows already in production.
- AWS introduced Amazon Bedrock AgentCore, enabling AI agents to perform autonomous commercial transactions and payments in partnership with Coinbase and Stripe.
- Vanguard utilized Amazon Bedrock to automate document processing, reducing account inheritance cycle times from two weeks to a single day.
- Datadog and Dynatrace are providing unified observability platforms to help banks detect and remediate operational incidents automatically before they impact customers.
- Major institutions like Unum Group and various mortgage lenders are using agentic workflows to modernize millions of lines of legacy mainframe code in months rather than years.
- FICO and Numerix are deploying AI-powered decisioning tools to provide real-time risk assessment and personalized financial quotes, significantly reducing turnaround times for capital markets.

### Why it Matters
The transition toward agentic AI represents a fundamental shift in how financial services firms manage risk, compliance, and customer interactions. By moving beyond simple automation to integrated, autonomous platforms, these institutions are gaining the ability to respond to market volatility and operational demands at speeds previously impossible with legacy infrastructure.
