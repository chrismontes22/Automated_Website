Google Adds Scenario Planner, Performance Max Updates, And Veo – PPC Pulse via @sejournal, @brookeosmundson
============================================================

Google has introduced significant updates to its advertising ecosystem, including enhanced reporting for Performance Max, new budget planning tools in Google Analytics, and generative video capabilities via Veo.

**Key points**
- Performance Max now allows advertisers to exclude first-party customer lists and provides improved reporting on budgets, audience demographics, and network-specific placements.
- Google Analytics launched beta tools, including Scenario Planner and Projections, to help marketers model budgets and track campaign pacing using historical data.
- The new Veo generative video model is now integrated into Google Ads Asset Studio, enabling users to create 10-second videos from three static images.
- These updates aim to provide advertisers with greater control over campaign steering, creative production speed, and cross-channel financial planning.

**Why it matters: **
These updates provide marketers with more granular control and creative efficiency, reducing the reliance on traditional production resources. By integrating planning and generation tools directly into the Google ecosystem, advertisers can better align their budgets and creative strategies with real-time performance data.
