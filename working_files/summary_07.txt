Halide Mark III
============================================================

Halide Mark III has launched as a major update to the popular iPhone camera app, introducing professional-grade film simulations, a streamlined photo editing lab, and expanded manual controls.

### Key Points
- The update features five distinct "Looks" developed with colorist Cullen Kelly, including Valencia, Rembrandt, Nova, Zephyr, and Chroma Noir.
- The new Photo Lab allows users to edit RAW files with tools for exposure, grain, halation, and resolution adjustments.
- Mark III introduces support for importing RAW files from standalone cameras as a beta feature.
- The interface has been redesigned to prioritize essential photography tools while offering a "classic" mode for users who prefer the previous version.
- Pricing is set at $19.99 per year or a $59.99 one-time purchase, with the update provided free to existing subscribers and Mark II owners.

### Why it Matters
This release signals a shift toward high-end, film-inspired computational photography that aims to replace the need for secondary editing software. By offering professional tools in a simplified interface, the developers are positioning Halide as a comprehensive, all-in-one solution for both casual mobile photographers and serious enthusiasts.
