E-paper Dashboard Reimagines Smart Home’s Connection with Technology
============================================================

Developer Joel has created Timeframe, a custom wall-mounted e-paper dashboard designed to provide essential home and calendar information while helping his household maintain a healthy relationship with smartphone technology.

### Key Points
- The project utilizes a 23.5-inch e-paper display from Boox to provide high-resolution visuals that mimic the organic appearance of traditional paper.
- Joel developed a custom Ruby on Rails backend to manage data transmission and refresh rates for the wall-mounted hardware.
- The system serves as a centralized hub for home information, replacing the need for constant smartphone use for basic tasks like checking calendars.
- Future development plans include deeper integration with Home Assistant and efforts to reduce the overall manufacturing costs of the platform.

### Why it Matters
This project highlights the growing consumer interest in "digital minimalism" and the use of specialized hardware to reduce screen-based distractions in the home. By leveraging e-paper technology, developers are creating functional, low-glare alternatives to traditional LCD dashboards that integrate seamlessly into domestic environments.
