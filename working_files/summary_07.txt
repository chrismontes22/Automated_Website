Here’s the Perfect Excuse to See ‘Project Hail Mary’ Again
============================================================

MGM has partnered with the TheaterEars app to offer an exclusive in-theater director commentary for the sci-fi film Project Hail Mary, beginning this Friday for iPhone users.

### Key Points
- Directors Phil Lord and Christopher Miller recorded exclusive commentary for the film.
- The TheaterEars app allows users to listen to audio tracks while watching movies in theaters.
- The feature is currently available exclusively for iPhone users, with no Android support announced.
- Users must bring their own headphones to listen to the commentary during the theatrical screening.
- This initiative follows similar past experiments by directors like Rian Johnson and Kevin Smith.

### Why it Matters
This collaboration highlights an evolving trend in theatrical engagement by offering supplemental content that encourages repeat viewings of major box office releases. By leveraging mobile technology, studios can provide unique, value-added experiences that differentiate the big-screen experience from home streaming.
