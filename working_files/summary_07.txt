The Pixel colors might rule this year
============================================================

Leaked Amazon listings suggest the upcoming Google Pixel 11 lineup will feature a diverse range of new color options alongside a potential base price increase to $899.

### Key Points
- Leaked listings reveal potential Pixel 11 colors including Fuchsia, Moss, and Midnight, with Pro models potentially arriving in Pine, Light Fog, Sterling, and Dune.
- The base Pixel 11 model may start at $899, featuring 256GB of storage and 12GB of RAM, effectively removing the 128GB entry-level configuration.
- Listings referenced a new "Google Pixel Tag" tracking device, though details remain unconfirmed.
- Google is scheduled to officially unveil its new hardware lineup during a dedicated event on August 12th.
- Analysts advise caution regarding the leaks due to inconsistencies, such as references to outdated software versions and unnecessary hardware accessories.

### Why it Matters
These leaks suggest a strategic shift in Google's hardware pricing and aesthetic branding as the company prepares to compete in the premium smartphone market. If confirmed, the removal of lower-storage tiers and the introduction of new accessories like the Pixel Tag could signal a broader effort to increase average revenue per user.
