ServiceNow just unveiled an AI workforce that can run your entire company: ‘Enterprises need AI that senses, decides, and securely acts’
============================================================

ServiceNow unveiled a suite of autonomous AI specialists at its Knowledge 2026 conference, signaling a strategic shift from AI-assisted tasks to fully automated, end-to-end enterprise business processes.

### Key Points
- ServiceNow introduced autonomous AI specialists capable of completing workflows in IT, HR, finance, and legal without human intervention.
- The company integrated its AI Control Tower across all platforms to discover, risk-score, and govern AI agents, including those within the Microsoft 365 ecosystem.
- A new Autonomous Security & Risk product, incorporating technology from acquisitions Armis and Veza, helps enterprises manage non-human identities and security threats.
- Early adopters reported significant efficiency gains, including a 99% faster resolution rate for IT service desk cases and a 97% reduction in threat containment time.
- Expanded partnerships with Nvidia and Lenovo aim to accelerate AI deployment and enable proactive IT issue resolution for enterprise customers.

### Why it Matters
This shift toward autonomous AI agents represents a fundamental change in how large organizations manage operations, moving from human-in-the-loop systems to machine-led execution. By embedding governance and security directly into its platform, ServiceNow is positioning itself as the essential infrastructure for companies struggling to manage the risks and complexities of scaling AI.
