Nvidia, Microsoft, and Arm are all teasing Nvidia’s new N1X laptop processors
============================================================

Nvidia, Microsoft, and Arm are teasing a major announcement regarding new Arm-powered laptop processors expected to be unveiled during Nvidia’s keynote at the Computex trade show in Taipei.

### Key Points
- Nvidia is expected to debut its N1 and N1x laptop processors during a keynote event on Sunday at 8 p.m. PT.
- Official social media accounts for Nvidia, Microsoft, and Arm shared coordinated teasers pointing to the Computex venue in Taiwan.
- Reports indicate that major manufacturers including Lenovo and Dell are preparing to integrate these new chips into upcoming laptop hardware.
- The entry of Nvidia into the Windows on Arm market will end Qualcomm’s current exclusivity for the Windows 11 Arm operating system.

### Why it Matters
This development signals a significant shift in the PC hardware market by introducing direct competition to Qualcomm’s dominance in the Arm-based Windows laptop sector. Increased competition among chipmakers could accelerate innovation and provide consumers with more diverse performance options for next-generation AI-capable PCs.
