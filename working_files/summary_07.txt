How to Track Your Luggage (2026): AirTag, Pebblebee, CaseSafe
============================================================

WIRED Reviews provides essential travel advice and product recommendations for tracking luggage, highlighting the best Bluetooth trackers and suitcases to ensure your belongings arrive safely at your destination.

### Key Points
- July’s carry-on suitcase features a built-in CaseSafe tracker compatible with Apple Find My and Google Find Hub, plus an ejectable power bank.
- Apple AirTags remain the top choice for iPhone users due to Precision Finding capabilities and a vast network of compatible devices.
- Pebblebee’s Clip 5 is recommended for Android users, offering USB-C rechargeability, an IP66 rating, and a unique SOS siren feature.
- Experts recommend zipping trackers into interior pockets or using bags with dedicated slots to prevent signal blockage or accidental loss.
- Bluetooth trackers are preferred over GPS alternatives for luggage because they avoid recurring subscription fees and leverage dense crowdsourced networks at airports.

### Why it Matters
Using reliable tracking technology significantly reduces travel stress by providing real-time location data for checked or gate-checked luggage. These tools offer travelers peace of mind and practical solutions for recovering misplaced items during transit.
