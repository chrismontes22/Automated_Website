iOS 27 Will Add These New Features to Your iPhone
============================================================

Apple is expected to unveil iOS 27 at the WWDC 2026 keynote on June 8, introducing a dedicated Siri app, advanced satellite connectivity, and new Apple Intelligence features.

### Key Points
- Apple will debut a dedicated Siri app featuring text and voice interaction, chat history, and a new Dynamic Island interface.
- New satellite capabilities include 5G connectivity for iPhone 18 Pro models and expanded support for Maps, Photos, and third-party app integration.
- The update prioritizes system stability and performance, drawing comparisons to the "Snow Leopard" approach for improved quality and bug fixes.
- New Apple Intelligence tools include Visual Intelligence for scanning labels and tickets, alongside enhanced photo editing and Safari tab organization.
- iOS 27 will be compatible with iPhone 12 series and newer, effectively ending support for the iPhone 11 lineup and second-generation iPhone SE.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal Apple's strategic shift toward integrating generative AI and satellite infrastructure directly into the core iPhone experience. By prioritizing system stability alongside these new features, Apple aims to maintain its competitive edge in the smartphone market while deepening user reliance on its ecosystem.
