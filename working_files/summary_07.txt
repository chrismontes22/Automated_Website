Google Wallet can now track your packages
============================================================

Google is integrating automated package tracking into the Google Wallet app by scanning Gmail receipts to display real-time delivery updates directly on the user's home screen.

### Key Points
- Google Wallet now displays parcel tracking information alongside payment methods by automatically identifying digital receipts and tracking numbers within a user's Gmail inbox.
- The feature allows users to view detailed delivery status updates and dismiss completed shipments directly from the app interface.
- Users must enable "Google Workspace smart features" within their Gmail settings to activate the tracking functionality.
- The service is currently limited to users in the United States and supports most major retailers, though it may not work with smaller, independent stores.
- The integration operates independently of Gmail, meaning package management in Google Wallet does not alter or delete original confirmation emails.

### Why it Matters
This update streamlines the online shopping experience by centralizing delivery logistics within a single financial management tool. By leveraging existing data from Gmail, Google aims to increase user engagement with the Wallet app while providing a more convenient alternative to checking individual shipping emails.
