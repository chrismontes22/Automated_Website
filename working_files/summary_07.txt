Design Inspiration Weekly: Free Type, Bold Brands, AI Speed
============================================================

Google released its brand typeface under an open license while major updates to Midjourney and Adobe Photoshop significantly advanced the capabilities of AI-driven design and production workflows.

### Key Points
- Google Sans Flex is now available for free under the Open Font License, featuring six variable axes for flexible design implementation.
- Midjourney V8 Alpha launched with five times faster processing speeds and native 2K resolution output for improved production readiness.
- Adobe integrated its Firefly AI Assistant and Boards directly into Photoshop 2026 to streamline generative mood boarding within creative workflows.
- HuskyFox developed a new brand identity for the K-pop group KATSEYE, balancing energetic aesthetics with global market appeal.
- CSS Studio emerged as a notable tool allowing designers to generate production-ready code through visual interfaces.

### Why it Matters
These developments signal a shift toward higher-resolution AI outputs and more integrated design infrastructure that bridges the gap between conceptualization and final production. As professional tools become more cohesive, designers are gaining the ability to execute complex, high-fidelity projects with greater speed and technical precision.
