I was laid off from eBay. Now I run a business with 27 AI agents — these are the human parts I still handle myself.
============================================================

After being laid off from eBay in 2024, Linara Bozieva founded a marketing agency that utilizes a sophisticated three-layer workflow powered by 27 custom AI agents to manage operations.

### Key Points
- Linara Bozieva, a former eBay employee, launched her San Jose-based agency, Ravenopous, following her 2024 layoff.
- The automated system features 27 custom AI agents organized into three layers: directives, orchestration, and execution.
- The workflow manages full-cycle marketing, including ad creation, performance analysis, and reporting, for a monthly subscription cost under $1,000.
- Bozieva utilizes various AI tools, including Claude Code, ChatGPT, HeyGen, and ElevenLabs, to power her agency's infrastructure.
- The system has been trained on 14 client profiles, allowing Bozieva to oversee multiple accounts with only two hours of work per client each week.

### Why it Matters
This model demonstrates how individual entrepreneurs can leverage autonomous AI agents to scale complex business operations without a large traditional staff. It highlights a shift toward "human-in-the-loop" management, where domain expertise and interpersonal skills remain essential for overseeing AI-driven workflows.
