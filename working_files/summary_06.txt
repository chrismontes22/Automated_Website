iOS 27 Rumored to Feature All-New Siri App With 'Extensions' Feature
============================================================

Apple is reportedly developing a dedicated Siri app for iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27 that integrates third-party AI chatbots through a new "Extensions" marketplace feature.

Key points
- The new Siri app will support both text and voice interactions while maintaining a history of past user conversations.
- An "Extensions" section in the App Store will allow users to download and integrate various third-party AI agents directly into the Siri ecosystem.
- iOS 27 will introduce a redesigned Siri interface, including a systemwide "Ask Siri" button and a "Write with Siri" tool located above the keyboard.
- Apple plans to release the first developer beta for iOS 27 in June, followed by a public launch scheduled for September.
- It remains uncertain whether the new app will be available on all devices or restricted to models supporting Apple Intelligence.

Why it matters: 
This update signals a major shift in Apple’s strategy to compete directly with standalone AI platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude. By creating an extensions marketplace, Apple is positioning its ecosystem as a central hub for third-party artificial intelligence tools rather than relying solely on its own proprietary models.
