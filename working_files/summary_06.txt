This $214 Modular Backpack System Zips Apart Into 3 Separate Bags You’ll Actually Want to Use
============================================================

Onli Travel has launched the Modevo Modular Travel Pack, a versatile three-bag system designed to function as high-quality individual components while offering up to 73 liters of combined capacity.

### Key Points
- The system includes the 28-liter Core Pack, the 18-liter Link briefcase, and the expandable 12-to-27-liter Go Daypack.
- Bags connect via perimeter YKK zippers, allowing for a full Trio configuration or a smaller Duo setup.
- The Core Pack features a professional suspension system with load lifters, a removable hip belt, and a 17-inch laptop sleeve.
- Materials include water-resistant, Bluesign and OEKO-TEX certified fabrics to meet environmental and safety standards.
- Pre-order pricing starts at $174 for the Duo and $224 for the Trio, with estimated delivery scheduled for June 2026.

### Why it Matters
This modular design addresses the common travel dilemma of choosing between a single oversized bag and multiple low-quality luggage pieces. By prioritizing the functionality of each individual component, the system offers travelers greater flexibility and utility throughout various stages of a trip.
