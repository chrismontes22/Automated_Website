Here are 4 things I do to make a cheap phone feel more premium
============================================================

Smartphone users can improve the performance and feel of budget Android devices by performing simple software optimizations and replacing low-quality accessories included in the original product packaging.

### Key Points
- Remove pre-installed bloatware apps and unused Google services to declutter the home screen and improve system responsiveness.
- Register the same fingerprint multiple times in the security settings to increase the speed and reliability of budget-tier biometric scanners.
- Disable "RAM Boost" or "Virtual RAM" features in settings, as these often slow down device performance rather than improving it.
- Discard low-quality, oily-feeling in-box cases in favor of third-party alternatives that offer better ergonomics, protection, and a more premium aesthetic.

### Why it Matters
These adjustments allow consumers to bridge the performance gap between affordable handsets and expensive flagship models without requiring technical expertise. By optimizing software and hardware choices, users can significantly enhance the daily usability and longevity of their budget-friendly devices.
