I tested the Oura Ring 5 for a month, and it's exactly what other smart rings should aspire to be
============================================================

The Oura Ring 5 introduces a significantly thinner, lighter design and improved battery life, solidifying its position as a leading wearable for passive health tracking and daily wellness monitoring.

### Key Points
- The Oura Ring 5 features a slimmed-down profile weighing approximately 2 grams, making it more discreet than the previous Oura Ring 4.
- Battery performance has increased to between six and nine days per charge, despite the device being 40% smaller than its predecessor.
- The ring includes advanced sensors for heart rate, blood oxygen, and temperature, though workout-specific heart rate tracking remains less accurate than dedicated fitness watches.
- New software features include an AI-powered Oura Advisor for health insights and a "Locate" tool to help users find their ring via Bluetooth proximity.
- The device is water-resistant up to 100 meters and supports integration with Apple Health and Android Health Connect.

### Why it Matters
The Oura Ring 5 represents a significant step forward in wearable miniaturization, proving that smart technology can be both unobtrusive and highly functional for long-term health monitoring. While the reliance on a subscription model and limitations in high-intensity fitness tracking persist, the device sets a new standard for comfort and design in the smart ring market.
