Siri AI Is Becoming Apple’s Everything Tool
============================================================

Apple has released the iOS 27 public beta, introducing a revamped Siri AI that offers deeper system integration, chatbot-style interactions, and improved context awareness for iPhone users.

### Key Points
- The new Siri AI is available via the iOS 27 public beta, though users must join a waitlist after installation.
- Features include a dedicated chatbot app, onscreen awareness, and the ability to pull personal context from texts, calendars, and photos.
- The assistant is integrated into the system-wide search function and includes a "Write with Siri" tool for drafting messages and notes.
- Hardware support is limited to the iPhone 15 Pro and newer models, as well as compatible iPads, Macs, and Vision Pro headsets.
- Users can manage privacy settings by toggling app access or choosing how long Siri retains conversation history.

### Why it Matters
This update represents a significant shift in how Apple users interact with their devices by transforming Siri from a basic voice command tool into a context-aware personal assistant. By indexing on-device data, Apple aims to provide a more personalized experience that could fundamentally change daily smartphone usage patterns and increase competition with existing AI assistants on Android.
