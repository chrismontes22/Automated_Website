The MacRumors Show: Goodbye MacBook Pro? MacBook Ultra Is Coming
============================================================

Apple is reportedly planning to launch a high-end "MacBook Ultra" featuring an OLED touchscreen in early 2027, while skipping M6 Pro and M6 Max chip generations entirely.

### Key Points
- The upcoming MacBook Ultra will debut with M5 Pro and M5 Max chips, with M7-series processors expected to follow in late 2027.
- New hardware features include a tandem OLED display, a thinner chassis, a Dynamic Island, and the first-ever cellular connectivity for a Mac.
- Apple is shifting its design philosophy to include "touch-friendly" input, supported by a reinforced hinge to stabilize the screen during interaction.
- Supply chain constraints and recent price hikes have delayed the initial launch window from late 2026 to early 2027.
- The product roadmap includes a crowded release schedule featuring base M6 models, redesigned M7 laptops, and multiple high-end Ultra configurations.

### Why it Matters
This shift represents a significant reversal of Apple's long-standing resistance to touchscreen Mac interfaces, potentially changing how users interact with macOS. The complex chip roadmap and premium pricing strategy suggest that Apple is segmenting its high-end laptop market to prioritize experimental hardware features over immediate processor upgrades.
