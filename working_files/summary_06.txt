Everything New in Calendar and Reminders in iOS 27
============================================================

Apple has introduced new Apple Intelligence features to the Calendar and Reminders apps in iOS 27, enabling natural language event creation, improved Siri integration, and enhanced document scanning capabilities.

### Key Points
- Apple Intelligence allows users to create Calendar events and Reminders by typing natural language descriptions for dates, times, and locations.
- Visual Intelligence in iOS 27 enables users to scan documents, such as sports schedules, to import multiple events into the Calendar app simultaneously.
- Siri now features deeper integration, allowing it to search across apps and create events directly from information found in Mail or Messages.
- The Reminders app includes improved grocery list sorting and new automation actions within the Shortcuts app for managing lists and sections.
- New extra-large widget sizes are available for both apps, and the Calendar app now offers holiday-aware alarms to adjust wake-up times.
- These features require an iPhone 15 Pro or later to function.

### Why it Matters
These updates streamline personal organization by reducing the manual data entry typically required to manage complex schedules and task lists. By leveraging on-device intelligence, Apple is making its native productivity tools more competitive with third-party alternatives while deepening the ecosystem's automation capabilities.
