Dreame’s L20 Ultra robovac is an unbeatable deal for $280
============================================================

The Dreame L20 Ultra robot vacuum and mop hybrid is currently available for a record-low price of $279 at Wellbots using the discount code L20VERGE.

### Key Points
- The Dreame L20 Ultra features 7,000Pa suction power and AI-powered obstacle avoidance for automated home mapping.
- Its base station automatically empties the dustbin, washes and dries mop pads, and refills the water tank.
- The device includes dual oscillating mop pads that extend to clean corners and lift automatically when detecting carpets.
- Users can operate the vacuum for up to 75 days without manual intervention due to the large capacity dustbin.
- The current promotional price represents a significant discount from the original $1,400 launch price set in 2023.

### Why it Matters
This deal offers premium, high-end automation features at a price point typically reserved for entry-level or mid-range robot vacuums. It provides significant value for consumers seeking advanced cleaning technology without the high cost of the latest flagship models.
