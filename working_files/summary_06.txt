============================================================

Cloudflare is laying off approximately 1,100 employees, representing 20 percent of its total workforce, as the company shifts its strategic focus toward artificial intelligence and automation initiatives.

### Key Points
- Cloudflare notified roughly 20 percent of its staff that their positions were being eliminated.
- Affected employees received termination notices via email with one hour of notice.
- The workforce reduction is part of a broader corporate pivot toward AI-driven operations.
- The company is restructuring its internal teams to prioritize AI development and integration.

### Why it Matters
This significant reduction in headcount highlights the aggressive shift toward AI-centric business models currently impacting the broader technology sector. It underscores the potential for automation to displace human roles as companies prioritize AI efficiency over traditional operational structures.
