Nanoleaf's New $80 Smart Ceiling Light Works With Matter and HomeKit
============================================================

Nanoleaf has launched the Smart Multicolor Ceiling Light, an affordable, Matter-compatible fixture featuring dual-sided illumination, 2600 lumens of brightness, and advanced color-changing capabilities for modern smart home setups.

### Key Points
- The light features 196 LEDs with 28 customizable color zones and a high Color Rendering Index of 95.
- It supports Matter over Wi-Fi, enabling integration with Apple HomeKit, Siri, and the Nanoleaf mobile application.
- The hardware includes RG0 Low Blue Light technology designed to minimize eye strain and support natural circadian rhythms.
- Users can control the main downlight and upward backlight independently to create layered ambient or task lighting.
- The 13.8-inch fixture is available now through Amazon and the official Nanoleaf website for a retail price of $80.

### Why it Matters
This product expands the accessibility of Matter-compatible smart lighting by offering a versatile, dual-sided ceiling solution at a competitive price point. Its integration capabilities allow users to incorporate sophisticated, multi-zone lighting scenes into broader smart home ecosystems without requiring proprietary hubs.
