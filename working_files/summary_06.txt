Walmart is updating its 4K streaming box with Gemini and Matter support
============================================================

Walmart has quietly listed an updated 2026 version of its Onn 4K Pro streaming device, featuring integrated Google Gemini support and Matter-over-Thread compatibility for enhanced smart home control.

### Key Points
- The new Onn 4K Pro is priced at $59.88, representing a $10 increase over the original May 2024 model.
- Key hardware specifications include 3GB of RAM, 32GB of storage, Wi-Fi 6, and an ethernet port.
- The device now supports Google Gemini for improved voice search and recommendations alongside Matter-over-Thread connectivity.
- The updated remote control includes a new voice-activated location feature to help users find misplaced units.
- Walmart recently joined the Connectivity Standards Alliance to further integrate the Matter protocol into its electronics ecosystem.

### Why it Matters
This update positions Walmart’s budget-friendly hardware as a more direct competitor to premium streaming devices by integrating advanced AI and smart home standards. By adopting Matter and Gemini, the retailer is lowering the barrier for consumers to access sophisticated home automation and voice-assisted media discovery.
