Workspace account? No problem; Google Home rolls out support for you, too
============================================================

Google is rolling out support for Workspace accounts in the Google Home app, allowing users to manage Nest devices and share home access without switching between personal accounts.

### Key Points
- Workspace users can now set up new hardware, including the 4th-gen Nest Learning Thermostat and 3rd-gen Nest Doorbell.
- The update enables Workspace accounts to invite others to their home or accept invitations to join existing smart home setups.
- Users can now migrate legacy Nest accounts created in 2019 or earlier to their preferred Workspace account.
- Workspace accounts remain excluded from the "Gemini for Home" early access program and certain personal Google Assistant features.
- Recent Google Home updates have improved Gemini’s response times by 30% to 40% and enhanced the AI's ability to understand natural language commands.

### Why it Matters
This update removes a significant friction point for professional users who previously had to toggle between accounts to manage their smart home devices. By integrating Workspace support, Google is streamlining its ecosystem for power users while simultaneously refining the performance of its Gemini AI integration.
