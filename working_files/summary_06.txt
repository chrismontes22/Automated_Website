LG will release the first 1000Hz, 1080p gaming monitor this year
============================================================

LG has announced the UltraGear 25G590B, the first gaming monitor to feature a native 1000Hz refresh rate combined with a 1,920 x 1,080 resolution for competitive esports performance.

### Key Points
- The 24.5-inch IPS display supports a 1000Hz refresh rate at full 1080p resolution.
- LG plans to release the monitor during the second half of 2026.
- Integrated AI features include Scene Optimization for visual adjustments and AI Sound for spatial audio.
- The hardware design features a minimalist stand, customizable lighting, and a built-in headset storage hook.

### Why it Matters
This monitor represents a significant technical milestone by overcoming previous resolution limitations associated with ultra-high refresh rate displays. The integration of on-device AI features suggests a shift toward hardware-level optimization to enhance competitive gaming experiences and user immersion.
