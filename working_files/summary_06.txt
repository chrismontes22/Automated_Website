StablecoinX Begins Nasdaq Trading With $275M ENA Treasury After TLGY Merger
============================================================

StablecoinX Inc. has officially debuted on the Nasdaq under the ticker USDE, providing public equity investors with direct exposure to the Ethena synthetic dollar ecosystem and its infrastructure.

### Key Points
- StablecoinX holds approximately 3.03 billion Ethena (ENA) governance tokens, valued at roughly $275 million.
- The company’s business model includes operating decentralized verifier nodes, developing middleware software, and providing institutional distribution services.
- Ethena currently maintains about $5.4 billion in digital dollar circulation across its USDe and USDtb product lines.
- The firm’s holdings represent approximately 20% of the total ENA token supply, equating to $11.42 in assets per fully diluted share.
- The global stablecoin market has recently surpassed $300 billion in total capitalization.

### Why it Matters
This listing creates a formal bridge between the rapidly growing decentralized stablecoin sector and traditional public equity markets. It offers investors a unique vehicle to gain exposure to digital asset infrastructure while testing market appetite for companies that blend treasury management with operational software services.
