I changed one Android 17 setting and made my Pixel home screen so much cleaner
============================================================

The latest Android 17 update introduces a new customization feature for Google Pixel devices, allowing users to hide app names on their home screens for a cleaner, minimalist aesthetic.

### Key Points
- Android 17 adds a dedicated "Names" tab within the "Icons" settings menu on Pixel devices.
- Users can toggle the "Show app names" setting to off to remove text labels from home screen icons.
- App names remain visible in the app drawer even when hidden from the home screen.
- The update includes additional icon customization options like "Disco," "Scribbles," and "Stardust" styles.
- These settings are accessible by long-pressing the home screen and selecting "Wallpaper & style."

### Why it Matters
This update addresses a long-standing request from Pixel users for greater control over their device's visual interface. By allowing a cleaner, text-free home screen, Google is providing more flexibility for users who prioritize a minimalist design language.
