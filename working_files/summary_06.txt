Google Photos wants to fix your face in one tap, but I’m not sure people want the help
============================================================

Google has introduced new AI-powered touch-up tools to the Google Photos app on Android, allowing users to make subtle, natural-looking refinements to facial features in their portrait photos.

### Key Points
- New editing tools include Heal, Smooth, Under eyes, Irises, Teeth, Eyebrows, and Lips for targeted facial adjustments.
- The features utilize machine learning to automatically analyze lighting, texture, and skin tone for realistic results.
- Users can adjust the intensity of edits using a simple slider interface integrated directly into the existing photo editor.
- The update requires an Android device running version 9.0 or higher with a minimum of 4GB of RAM.
- Google is rolling out these enhancements gradually to global users as part of its ongoing investment in AI-driven image processing.

### Why it Matters
These tools represent a shift toward automated, subtle image enhancement that prioritizes ease of use over complex manual editing. While this provides a convenient way for users to polish social media content, it also highlights the increasing role of AI in defining digital perceptions of natural appearance.
