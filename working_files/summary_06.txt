Prime Day 2026 early deals are already live — as someone who's covered every Amazon sale, these are my 60 picks to shop now
============================================================

Amazon Australia has launched early Prime Day 2026 deals ahead of the official July 7 start date, offering record-low prices on various tech, home appliances, and smart devices.

### Key Points
- Amazon Prime Day 2026 is scheduled to begin on July 7, but early discounts are currently live across the platform.
- Notable record-low prices include 30% off the Kindle Colorsoft, 40% off the Fire TV Stick Plus, and significant savings on the Roborock Qrevo Curve 2 Flow.
- Prime members receive exclusive access to the best pricing, with 30-day free trials available for new subscribers.
- Additional deals feature deep discounts on JBL speakers, Logitech gaming peripherals, and various kitchen appliances like the Nespresso Vertuo Pop.
- Extended free trials are currently available for Amazon services, including three months of Kindle Unlimited and Audible, plus four months of Music Unlimited.

### Why it Matters
Early access to these discounts allows consumers to secure popular items before potential stock shortages occur during the main event. While some products are already at all-time low prices, shoppers should remain strategic as additional, deeper discounts may emerge once the official sale period begins.
