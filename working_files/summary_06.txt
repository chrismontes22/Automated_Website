You can grab a four-pack of Govee’s color-changing smart bulbs for just $27
============================================================

Govee is offering a four-pack of its color-changing A19 smart LED bulbs for $26.99 through March 31 as part of Amazon’s Big Spring Sale event.

### Key Points
- The four-pack is discounted by $13, reaching a price point just $3 above its all-time low.
- Each 800-lumen bulb supports over 16 million colors and features 30 preset lighting scenes.
- The bulbs are Matter-compatible and integrate with Amazon Alexa and Google Home for voice control.
- Users can sync lighting to music via a smartphone microphone or customize settings using the Govee app.
- The devices connect via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth without requiring a separate smart home hub.

### Why it Matters
This promotion offers a significant discount on smart home hardware that supports the Matter standard, which improves interoperability across different ecosystems. For consumers, this deal provides an affordable entry point into automated lighting systems that can be easily integrated into existing smart home routines.
