Amazon is offering significant discounts on top-rated fitness technology, including smartwatches, smart rings, recovery gear, and walking pads, as part of its latest summer sale event.

- Oura Ring 4 is discounted by $134, while the Urevo CyberPad walking pad is marked down by $140.

- Recovery tools, including the Hyperice Hypervolt 2 massage gun and Therabody JetBoots Pro Plus, are currently on sale.

### Why it Matters
These discounts provide a timely opportunity for consumers to acquire premium health and fitness hardware at a lower cost. By tracking these price drops, users can upgrade their workout and recovery routines with high-end technology that is typically more expensive throughout the rest of the year.
