Apple Updates App Store Connect With More Than 100 New Metrics
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
Apple has launched a significant update to App Store Connect, introducing over 100 new metrics and advanced analytical tools to help developers better track app performance and monetization.

**Key points**
- Apple added more than 100 metrics, including detailed data on in-app purchases and subscription performance.
- New cohort analysis tools allow developers to track user behavior based on specific attributes like download source or region.
- Peer group benchmarks now provide anonymized data on download-to-paid conversion rates and proceeds per download.
- Developers can now export two new subscription reports via the Analytics Reports API for offline data integration.
- The interface update includes enhanced filtering capabilities, allowing users to apply up to seven simultaneous filters to their metrics.

**Why it matters**
These enhanced analytics provide developers with deeper insights into user behavior and revenue streams, enabling more data-driven strategies for app growth. The addition of peer benchmarks and cohort tracking helps businesses better compete and optimize their monetization models within the App Store ecosystem.
