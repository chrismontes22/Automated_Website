No way! The best 4K TV streaming stick is somehow just $17 for Prime Day
============================================================

Amazon has launched significant Prime Day discounts on its Fire TV Stick 4K lineup, with prices for the streaming devices dropping as low as $17 for a limited time.

### Key Points
- The entry-level Fire TV Stick 4K Select is discounted by 55% to a price of $17.
- The Fire TV Stick 4K Plus, which includes Dolby Vision and Dolby Atmos, is reduced by 50% to $24.
- The high-end Fire TV Stick 4K Max, featuring improved connectivity and storage, is now available for $34.
- The Fire TV Stick 4K Plus model includes GeForce Now integration for cloud gaming.
- These promotional prices are part of Amazon's broader Prime Day 2026 sales event.

### Why it Matters
These aggressive price cuts provide a cost-effective solution for users looking to upgrade laggy television interfaces with reliable, high-resolution streaming hardware. By discounting these devices, Amazon is positioning its ecosystem as a highly accessible entry point for home entertainment compared to premium alternatives like the Shield TV Pro.
