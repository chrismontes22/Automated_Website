Motorola's Souped Up Folding Phone Is Almost Half Off
============================================================

Motorola has discounted the 2025 Razr Ultra folding smartphone to $700 on Amazon, offering a significant $600 price reduction for the high-performance model with 512 GB of storage.

### Key Points
- The 2025 Motorola Razr Ultra features the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite processor and 16 GB of RAM.
- The device includes a 4,700-mAh battery supporting 68-watt wired and 30-watt wireless charging.
- Hardware upgrades include a titanium-reinforced hinge and ceramic glass coating for improved durability.
- Both the internal AMOLED and external pOLED displays support refresh rates up to 165 Hz.
- The $700 promotional price applies to the Pantone Scarab color, while other color options cost $800.

### Why it Matters
This aggressive discount positions the premium foldable device as a highly competitive option against other flagship Android smartphones currently on the market. By significantly lowering the entry price, Motorola is making advanced folding technology more accessible to mainstream consumers while clearing inventory for its latest hardware.
