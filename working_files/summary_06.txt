Michael Dell has nailed his relationship with Donald Trump, and it's paying off
============================================================

Dell Technologies CEO Michael Dell has seen his net worth surge by $83.5 billion this year amid a strengthening public rapport with President Donald Trump and record-breaking company growth.

### Key Points
- Michael Dell’s net worth reached $223 billion in 2026, making him the world's sixth-richest person as Dell Technologies shares rose 240%.
- The Michael and Susan Dell Foundation contributed $6.25 billion to the government-backed Trump Accounts program for children's savings.
- President Trump publicly endorsed Dell laptops twice, contributing to recent stock price increases for the company.
- Dell Technologies reported $43.8 billion in quarterly revenue in May, bolstered by a 181% year-over-year increase in AI infrastructure sales.
- The company secured a $10 billion contract renewal with the U.S. Department of Defense in February following a competitive evaluation.

### Why it Matters
The deepening relationship between Michael Dell and the White House highlights a shift in corporate strategy where executives increasingly align with the administration to secure influence and favor. This dynamic underscores the complex intersection of political engagement and market performance for major tech firms operating in the current regulatory and economic climate.
