iPhone 17 Pro Max buried in America’s 250th anniversary time capsule: to be opened in 2276
============================================================

A time capsule containing an iPhone 17 Pro Max in Cosmic Orange has been buried to commemorate America’s 250th anniversary, with plans to unseal the device in 2276.

### Key Points
- The time capsule includes representative items from every U.S. state to document the current era.
- The iPhone 17 Pro Max is stored with digital artifacts saved within the device's Notes application.
- Experts suggest the phone's battery will likely degrade long before the scheduled 250-year storage period concludes.
- The capsule is officially designated to remain sealed until America’s 500th anniversary in the year 2276.

### Why it Matters
This project highlights the challenges of digital preservation as physical hardware and battery technology struggle to survive over multi-century timelines. It serves as a cultural experiment regarding how future generations will access and interpret the personal technology defining our current digital age.
