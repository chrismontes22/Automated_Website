Gemini for Google Home can now use your cameras to trigger automations
============================================================

Google is introducing a Gemini-powered automation feature for Google Home that allows Nest and select third-party cameras to trigger smart home routines based on visual object recognition.

### Key Points
- Users can create custom automations by describing specific visual events, such as detecting mail deliveries or animals near trash bins.
- The feature requires a Google Home Premium Advanced subscription, costing $20 per month or $200 annually.
- It is currently available in English for US users enrolled in the Google Home Public Preview program.
- Gemini for Home now supports complex, multi-action voice commands and offers improved response times for natural language requests.
- Google advises against using these visual automations for time-sensitive security or safety purposes due to processing latency.

### Why it Matters
This update marks a significant shift toward context-aware smart homes that react to environmental changes rather than just manual inputs. By integrating generative AI with home security hardware, Google is positioning its ecosystem to offer more personalized and intuitive automation for premium subscribers.
