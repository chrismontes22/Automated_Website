Google Home's latest update brings AI 'Memory' for your fur babies at home
============================================================

Google is rolling out a new "Pet Memory" feature for its smart home cameras, allowing users to personalize AI alerts with specific pet names and animal types.

### Key Points
- The "Pet Memory" feature enables Google Home cameras to identify pets by name rather than generic descriptions.
- Users can configure pet details through the Ask Home interface to improve notification accuracy.
- Google is expanding the Home Brief feature, which provides voice-activated video summaries, to additional international markets.
- The Gemini for Home voice assistant is now officially launching for users located in Germany.

### Why it Matters
These updates reflect Google's ongoing strategy to integrate generative AI into smart home ecosystems to provide more intuitive and personalized user experiences. By refining how devices communicate, the company aims to increase the practical utility of home security systems for pet owners and international consumers.
