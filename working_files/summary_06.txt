Honor takes a stand: solidifies seven years of Android for Magic series in Malaysia launch
============================================================

Honor is launching its Magic V6 foldable smartphone in Malaysia while committing to seven years of Android OS and security updates for users across the U.K. and EU.

### Key Points
- The Honor Magic V6 is available for pre-order in Malaysia until June 11 at a price of RM 7,699.
- The device features a 2800MPa "Super Steel" hinge rated for 500,000 folds and measures 4mm thick when unfolded.
- Honor confirmed a seven-year software support policy for the entire Magic series in the U.K. and European markets.
- The smartphone includes a high-performance camera system and a long-lasting battery housed within a slim, durable frame.
- A special edition red colorway was developed in collaboration with artist Yoni Alter to accompany the global launch.

### Why it Matters
This extended software support policy aligns Honor with industry leaders like Samsung and Google, significantly increasing the long-term value of its premium foldable devices. By prioritizing durability and longevity, the company aims to strengthen consumer trust and compete more effectively in the global flagship smartphone market.
