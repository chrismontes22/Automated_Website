iOS 27 Rumored to Feature All-New Siri App With 'Extensions' Feature
============================================================

Apple is reportedly developing a standalone Siri app for iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27 that integrates third-party AI chatbots through a new "Extensions" marketplace feature.

**Key points**
- The upcoming Siri app will support both text and voice interactions, including access to historical conversation logs.
- An "Extensions" feature will allow third-party AI agents to integrate directly with Siri and other system-level device functions.
- Apple plans to launch a dedicated App Store section specifically for these third-party AI integrations.
- iOS 27 will introduce a redesigned Siri interface, featuring a systemwide "Ask Siri" button and a "Write with Siri" tool above the keyboard.
- Developer betas for the new operating systems are expected in June, followed by a public release in September.

**Why it matters: **
This update signals Apple's shift toward a more open AI ecosystem by allowing users to choose between various third-party chatbots directly within the Siri interface. By creating a dedicated marketplace for AI extensions, Apple is positioning its hardware as a central hub for diverse generative AI tools.
