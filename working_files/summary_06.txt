New Google Home speaker incoming? The Nest Mini and Nest Audio are suddenly hard to find
============================================================

Google is preparing to launch a new Gemini-powered smart speaker as inventory for older Nest Audio and Nest Mini devices depletes across major U.S. retailers ahead of June.

### Key Points
- The new Google Home speaker is expected to launch on June 25, 2026, with a retail price of approximately $100.
- Hardware features include 360-degree audio, stereo pairing capabilities, and full integration with Gemini and Gemini Live.
- Available color options for the upcoming device reportedly include Hazel, Porcelain, Jade, and Berry.
- Major retailers, including the Google Store and Best Buy, are currently showing limited stock for existing Nest smart speaker models.

### Why it Matters
This release marks Google's first major refresh of its smart speaker lineup since 2022, signaling a strategic shift toward integrating generative AI directly into home hardware. By replacing aging Nest products with Gemini-powered devices, Google aims to revitalize its smart home ecosystem and compete more effectively in the evolving AI-driven consumer electronics market.
