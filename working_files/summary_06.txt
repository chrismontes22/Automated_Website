Trump’s posting even more AI-generated Trump-Jesus fanart
============================================================

Donald Trump continues to share AI-generated images on Truth Social, including controversial depictions of himself alongside religious figures, despite ongoing scrutiny regarding the origin and content of these posts.

### Key Points
- Donald Trump recently posted an AI-generated image of himself as a religious figure, which social media users noted contained bizarre, demon-like alterations.
- The former president confirmed he personally manages his social media posts, often bypassing advisers who might otherwise discourage the sharing of controversial content.
- Trump followed the initial post with another AI-generated image depicting himself embracing Jesus, signaling a continued trend of using such imagery for political messaging.
- Investigations by social media users revealed that the images often originate from MAGA influencers and undergo subtle, sometimes unsettling, transformations before reaching Trump’s feed.

### Why it Matters
The use of AI-generated religious imagery by a political leader highlights the growing intersection of synthetic media and political discourse. This trend raises concerns about the potential for misinformation and the normalization of manipulated content in high-level political communication.
