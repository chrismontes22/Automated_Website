Enhance your password security with up to 50% off Keeper plans — secure credential generation, storage, and autofill to help protect all your online accounts
============================================================

Keeper Security is offering discounts of up to 50% on its password management plans, providing users with a secure way to store credentials and prevent unauthorized account access.

### Key Points
- Keeper is discounting Personal and Family plans by 50% for the first year of service.
- The Business Starter plan is currently available at a 30% discount for small teams.
- The platform utilizes zero-knowledge architecture and device-level encryption to protect stored user data.
- Features include a password generator, biometric authentication, and secure sharing vaults for household or team use.
- The Family plan provides five separate private vaults to manage credentials across multiple users.

### Why it Matters
Adopting a password manager is a critical step in mitigating the risks associated with credential stuffing and data breaches. These discounts lower the barrier to entry for individuals and small businesses looking to implement robust, centralized security practices.
