I held the Trump phone
============================================================

A tech journalist recently gained hands-on access to the elusive Trump phone, confirming the device is a rebranded midrange Android handset that remains largely unavailable to preordering customers.

### Key Points
- The Trump phone features a plastic back, curved edges, and prominent American flag branding with a gold-colored finish.
- iFixit analysis confirms the device is a modified midrange HTC phone preloaded with the Truth Social application.
- Despite being announced nearly a year ago, the device has not yet shipped to the majority of customers who placed preorders.
- Only a limited number of units have surfaced among select influencers and journalists, leaving most buyers waiting for their orders.

### Why it Matters
The continued delay in shipping raises significant questions regarding the operational status and fulfillment capabilities of the Trump Mobile brand. For consumers, the device serves as a case study in the risks associated with long-term preorders for niche hardware products.
