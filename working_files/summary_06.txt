I added 512GB to my Android phone with a USB-C gadget smaller than my thumbnail
============================================================

Compact USB-C flash drives, such as the SanDisk Extreme Fit, offer a practical and portable way to expand smartphone storage without relying on expensive cloud subscriptions or device upgrades.

### Key Points
- The SanDisk Extreme Fit USB-C drive provides storage capacities ranging from 64GB to 1TB in a miniature form factor.
- It utilizes USB 3.2 Gen 1 technology, delivering data transfer speeds of up to 400MB/s.
- The drive is compatible with most modern Android smartphones, tablets, and laptops featuring a USB-C port.
- Users can manage files directly through the phone's interface, making it suitable for storing large media libraries like photos, videos, and music.
- Despite recent price increases in flash storage components, these drives remain a cost-effective alternative to purchasing higher-capacity smartphone models.

### Why it Matters
Using external flash storage allows users to bypass the high premiums manufacturers charge for increased internal device capacity. This approach provides a flexible, physical solution for managing large media files while preserving the limited internal space required for optimal operating system performance.
