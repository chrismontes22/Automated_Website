iOS 27 Rumored to Feature All-New Siri App With 'Extensions' Feature
============================================================

**Apple is reportedly developing a standalone Siri app for iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27 that features advanced third-party chatbot integrations and a redesigned, text-and-voice interface.**

**Key points**
- The new Siri app will support "Extensions," allowing users to connect third-party AI agents similar to ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude.
- Apple plans to launch a dedicated App Store section specifically for these AI-powered extensions.
- New interface features include a systemwide "Ask Siri" button and a "Write with Siri" tool integrated directly above the keyboard.
- The first developer beta for iOS 27 is scheduled for release in June, followed by a public launch in September.
- It remains uncertain if the app will be limited to devices supporting Apple Intelligence or available across all compatible hardware.

**Why it matters**
This development signals Apple's intent to transform Siri from a basic voice assistant into a versatile, platform-wide AI hub that competes directly with standalone chatbot applications. By opening the ecosystem to third-party extensions, Apple is positioning its hardware as a central gateway for diverse generative AI services.
