iOS 27: All the New Safari Features
============================================================

Apple is introducing significant updates to the Safari browser in iOS 27, leveraging Apple Intelligence to provide automated tab organization, custom AI-generated extensions, and enhanced website monitoring tools.

### Key Points
- Safari now uses Apple Intelligence to automatically group open tabs into relevant topics for improved browsing organization.
- Users can generate custom browser extensions by describing desired functions, such as focus timers or webpage styling, through a new interface.
- A "Notify Me" feature allows Safari to monitor specific websites for updates, such as product restocks or ticket availability, on a daily or weekly basis.
- The Passwords app can now automatically navigate to websites to update weak or compromised credentials using Apple Intelligence.
- New parental controls include an "Ask to Browse" feature requiring approval for children to visit new websites.
- These advanced features require an iPhone 15 Pro or later and will launch with the full iOS 27 release in fall 2026.

### Why it Matters
These updates represent a shift toward integrating generative AI directly into the browsing experience to automate routine tasks and improve user productivity. By offloading complex management duties to Apple Intelligence, the browser aims to reduce manual effort while increasing the functional capabilities of mobile web navigation.
