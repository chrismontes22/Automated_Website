T-Mobile 5G Home Internet Delivers Fast Speeds, Low Monthly Prices and Cash Back
============================================================

T-Mobile is offering new customers a choice between 5G Home Internet and 2-Gig Fiber, featuring monthly discounts, a five-year price guarantee, and up to $300 in prepaid rewards.

**Key points**
- T-Mobile 5G Home Internet plans include Rely ($30/month), Amplified ($40/month), and All-In, all requiring a voice line and AutoPay.
- New subscribers can receive virtual prepaid Mastercards of $100, $200, or $300 depending on their chosen plan.
- The Amplified and All-In plans offer download speeds up to 498 Mbps and include advanced hardware like the Wi-Fi 7 gateway.
- The All-In plan bundles streaming services such as Hulu and Paramount+ Essential with an automatic gateway upgrade after three years.
- T-Mobile’s 5G service is currently ranked as the fastest 5G home internet provider according to Ookla Speedtest Intelligence data from 2H 2025.

**Why it matters: **
This strategy allows T-Mobile to compete directly with traditional cable providers by offering flexible, contract-free broadband options tailored to different household data needs. The long-term price guarantee and bundled streaming perks provide significant value for consumers looking to reduce monthly utility costs.
