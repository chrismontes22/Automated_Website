The RTX 3060 is becoming this decade's GTX 1060, but that's not a bad thing
============================================================

The Nvidia RTX 3060 has solidified its status as the most popular graphics card among gamers, mirroring the legendary longevity and market dominance of the iconic GTX 1060.

### Key Points
- The GTX 1060 held the top spot on the Steam Hardware Survey for five years, from late 2017 through 2022.
- The RTX 3060 surpassed the GTX 1060 in early 2023 and has remained the most used GPU for the majority of the last three years.
- Key factors for the RTX 3060's popularity include its 12GB of VRAM and consistent performance in 1080p gaming.
- Newer models like the RTX 4060 and RTX 5060 are often criticized for having only 8GB of VRAM, which limits their appeal compared to the 3060.
- Nvidia’s 60-class and 50-class cards currently account for eight of the top ten most popular desktop GPUs on Steam.

### Why it Matters
The enduring popularity of the RTX 3060 highlights a growing trend where gamers are holding onto hardware longer due to rising prices and diminishing value in newer GPU generations. This shift suggests that mid-range cards with higher VRAM capacities provide better long-term utility than newer, more expensive models with lower memory specifications.
