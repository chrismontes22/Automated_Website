NymVPN's new 'Pay as You Go' option ditches accounts and subscriptions for true anonymity
============================================================

NymVPN has launched a new Pay as You Go service that allows users to access its network anonymously using $NYM tokens without requiring accounts, subscriptions, or personal identity details.

### Key Points
- Users deposit $NYM tokens into a smart contract to receive anonymous cryptographic credentials known as zk-nyms.
- The service requires a minimum payment of 225 $NYM, which provides approximately 25GB of network data usage.
- Access is currently limited to the nym-vpnc command-line client, requiring users to manage their own cryptocurrency wallets.
- zk-nym credentials are unlinkable to original payment data, preventing the network from tracking user identity or transaction history.
- The system replaces traditional subscription models and account numbers with decentralized, re-randomizable authentication tokens.

### Why it Matters
This service eliminates the paper trail typically associated with VPN providers by removing the need for email addresses, billing profiles, or persistent account identifiers. By shifting from trust-based no-logs policies to cryptographic anonymity, NymVPN offers a decentralized alternative for users prioritizing extreme privacy over traditional user-friendly interfaces.
