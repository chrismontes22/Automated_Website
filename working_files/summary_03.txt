Bitcoin analysts flag triggers for a massive surge to $88,000 even as war risks linger
============================================================

Bitcoin analysts are projecting a potential rally toward $88,000, citing strong institutional inflows, favorable technical indicators, and limited supply as key drivers despite ongoing geopolitical tensions.

### Key Points
- MicroStrategy recently purchased $330 million in bitcoin, bringing its total holdings to 766,970 BTC.
- U.S. spot bitcoin ETFs recorded $787 million in net inflows this week, the highest level since early March.
- The Coinbase Premium Index reached 0.0586%, signaling robust buying pressure from U.S. investors compared to offshore markets.
- Analysts note that only 1% of circulating bitcoin supply exists between the $72,000 and $80,000 price levels, potentially easing the path for rapid gains.
- Market participants are pricing in a 65% probability that the Clarity Act will be signed into law this year, providing regulatory certainty.

### Why it Matters
The convergence of institutional demand and reduced supply suggests that bitcoin may decouple from short-term geopolitical volatility. If these market conditions persist, the asset could see significant price discovery as it moves toward the $88,000 target and beyond.
