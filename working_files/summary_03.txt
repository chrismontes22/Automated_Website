Patreon CEO Jack Conte on supporting artists in the AI slop era
============================================================

Patreon CEO Jack Conte is pivoting the company toward a creator-led discovery model to protect independent artists from the volatility and restrictive algorithms of major social media platforms.

### Key Points
- Patreon has shifted from a simple payment processor to a comprehensive media platform featuring native video, chat, and discovery tools.
- The company now supports 185 million free memberships, providing creators with a "deterministic" way to reach fans without relying on third-party algorithms.
- Conte is actively integrating AI for internal operations and business management while strictly avoiding AI-generated content in creative workflows.
- Patreon has developed a "hot-swappable" payments architecture to maintain leverage against payment processors and navigate complex content policy requirements.
- The platform is currently navigating a contentious relationship with Apple regarding App Store fees, forcing creators to choose between higher costs or web-based signups.

### Why it Matters
Patreon’s strategic evolution reflects a broader industry shift as creators move away from "interest-based" social media platforms that prioritize platform control over creator-fan relationships. By building its own discovery and communication infrastructure, Patreon aims to insulate small media businesses from the "enshittification" of major tech giants like Meta and Google.
