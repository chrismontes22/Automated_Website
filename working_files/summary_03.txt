Samsung just made the Galaxy Z Fold 7 and Flip 7 slightly easier on your wallet
============================================================

Samsung has added the Galaxy Z Fold 7 and Flip 7 to its Certified Re-Newed program, though current pricing makes new models cheaper than refurbished units for base configurations.

### Key Points
- Samsung’s Certified Re-Newed program now includes the Galaxy Z Fold 7 and Galaxy Z Flip 7 in the United States.
- Base model refurbished Z Fold 7 units are currently priced at $1,699, while new units are available for $1,599.
- The refurbished Z Flip 7 costs $939, which is $40 more than the current $899 retail price for a new device.
- Refurbished models only offer cost savings on high-capacity versions, such as the 1TB Galaxy Z Fold 7.
- Certified Re-Newed devices include new batteries, repairs, and the same warranty coverage as brand-new handsets.

### Why it Matters
This pricing discrepancy highlights the importance of comparing current retail promotions against manufacturer-refurbished programs before making a purchase. Consumers who fail to check active store discounts may inadvertently pay a premium for used hardware instead of opting for new devices.
