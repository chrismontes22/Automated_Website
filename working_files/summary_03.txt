AI together
============================================================

**Purple.space is testing a collaborative AI model that integrates multiple AI voices into group conversations to foster deeper peer interaction and move beyond isolated, one-on-one user experiences.**

### Key Points
- The platform incorporates AI voices into discussion categories to prevent stalled conversations and ensure all participants are heard.
- Collaborative AI environments aim to reduce user isolation and gullibility by maintaining context and encouraging patient, nuanced dialogue.
- Successful AI startups should prioritize value creation and network effects over simple cost-reduction models, which are prone to competitive price wars.
- Business-focused AI tools should target "forcing functions"—existing crises or urgent needs—to ensure adoption within organizations.
- Sustainable AI projects should build proprietary, trusted data stacks that provide specific utility rather than relying solely on third-party LLM gateways.

### Why it Matters
Moving away from solo, transactional AI interactions toward network-based models allows startups to build defensible, high-value platforms that solve complex organizational problems. By focusing on creating economic or social tension that necessitates adoption, developers can build sustainable businesses that prioritize long-term utility over fleeting cost-saving advantages.
