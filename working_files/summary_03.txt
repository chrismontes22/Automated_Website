Elon Musk poised to make history as world’s first trillionaire
============================================================

**Elon Musk is poised to become the world’s first trillionaire as SpaceX debuts on the Nasdaq stock exchange with a valuation of $1.77 trillion this Friday.**

### Key Points
- SpaceX will begin trading on the Nasdaq with an initial share price of $135, totaling 555.6 million shares.
- Musk’s 42 percent stake in SpaceX is estimated to be worth between $743 billion and $866.5 billion.
- The valuation positions Musk to surpass the wealth of historical figures like John D. Rockefeller and Andrew Carnegie relative to US GDP.
- Economic historians estimate Musk’s fortune could command the labor of approximately 557,800 people, a record for a private individual.
- Musk’s wealth remains tied to volatile market valuations, similar to the 60 percent decline Tesla experienced during the 2022 inflation surge.

### Why it Matters
This milestone highlights the unprecedented concentration of wealth in the modern era and raises questions about the societal role of ultra-high-net-worth individuals. Unlike the "Robber Barons" of the Gilded Age who engaged in large-scale public philanthropy, Musk’s influence is increasingly defined by his direct involvement in federal politics and government policy.
