Why pay for the Galaxy S26 Ultra? This phone makes way more sense — and it just received a historic discount for Amazon's Big Spring Sale
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
Amazon’s Big Spring Sale features a $100 discount on the OnePlus 15, allowing consumers to purchase the flagship smartphone for $800, significantly undercutting competitors like the Galaxy S26 Ultra.

**Key points**
- The OnePlus 15 is currently priced at $800, representing a $100 discount during Amazon's Big Spring Sale.
- The device features the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset, a 7,300mAh battery, and an 80W fast charger included in the box.
- It holds IP68, IP69, and IP69K durability ratings, exceeding the protection standards of many competing flagship smartphones.
- The phone offers a 165Hz OLED display but lacks built-in Qi2 magnetic support and advanced AI features found in Samsung or Google devices.
- OnePlus guarantees at least four years of Android software updates for the device.

**Why it matters**
This deal positions the OnePlus 15 as a high-performance, budget-friendly alternative to premium flagships, offering significant savings for users who prioritize hardware specifications over ecosystem-specific AI features. It highlights the ongoing market competition between "flagship killer" devices and top-tier handsets from major manufacturers.
