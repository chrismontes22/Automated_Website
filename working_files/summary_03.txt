'Tokenomics is beginning': The firm behind the viral AI doomsday post says rising costs will reshape the market's hottest trade
============================================================

Citrini Research reports that the era of free AI is ending as rising token costs force companies to shift from centralized cloud computing toward more efficient edge AI solutions.

### Key Points
- Citrini Research identifies "tokenomics" as a critical new economic factor as businesses struggle with the high costs of scaling AI usage.
- The trend of "tokenmaxxing" has increased productivity but created unsustainable financial burdens currently subsidized by venture capital and hyperscalers.
- Companies are expected to prioritize cost-efficiency by adopting local inference, which runs AI models directly on hardware like PCs and smartphones.
- Nvidia is actively supporting this transition by developing specialized chips designed to facilitate local AI processing.
- The market is shifting toward "edge AI," creating new investment opportunities in distributed inference, specialized hardware, and orchestration software.

### Why it Matters
This transition signals a move away from the initial AI boom phase, which relied heavily on expensive, centralized cloud computing infrastructure. Businesses must now balance productivity gains with the economic reality of token costs, likely driving a surge in demand for localized, hardware-based AI solutions.
