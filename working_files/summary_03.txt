4 New Must-Use Google Shopping Feed Attributes for Maximum Visibility
============================================================

Google announced major AI-driven updates on May 20, introducing conversational search features and shopping tools that require optimized product feeds to maintain visibility and performance in search results.

### Key Points
- Google introduced an intelligent search box, a universal shopping cart, and AI-Max features to facilitate conversational, multi-media, and end-to-end shopping experiences.
- Paid visibility in AI Overviews and AI Mode now requires the use of Broad Match, PMax, or AI-Max campaign structures to avoid significant impression drops.
- Shopping feeds now function as essential data warehouses, requiring new attributes like Question and Answer, Document Links, Related Products, and Popularity Rank.
- Brands can integrate up to 30 Q&A pairs and five PDF documents per product to provide AI surfaces with detailed, accessible information for potential buyers.
- Advertisers can implement guardrails, such as brand exclusions and text guidelines, to maintain control while adopting AI-driven campaign automation.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal a fundamental shift where Google’s AI prioritizes brands that provide structured, high-quality data directly within their shopping feeds. Businesses that fail to optimize their product information risk losing visibility as traditional search results are increasingly supplemented or replaced by AI-generated shopping experiences.
