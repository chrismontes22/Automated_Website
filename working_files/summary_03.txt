Why is Meta raising Quest headset prices? #tech
============================================================

Meta is increasing the retail prices of its Quest 3 and Quest 3S virtual reality headsets starting this Sunday due to ongoing global supply constraints for essential RAM components.

### Key Points
- Meta cited a persistent shortage of DRAM and related memory components as the primary driver for the price adjustments.
- The price hikes apply to both the Quest 3 and the more affordable Quest 3S hardware models.
- Increased demand for memory in AI development and data centers has contributed to rising costs across the electronics industry.
- The company confirmed that the price changes are based on supply-chain economics rather than updates to the headset hardware.

### Why it Matters
These price increases could slow the adoption rate of Meta’s VR hardware by creating economic friction for mainstream consumers and developers. The situation underscores how global memory shortages directly impact the affordability and scalability of consumer electronics that rely on high-performance computing components.
