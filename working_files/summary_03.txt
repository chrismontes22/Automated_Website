Hisense’s AI Agent Suite Brings Connection and Emotional Value to Smart Home Appliances
============================================================

**One-sentence headline summary**
Hisense has launched its AI Agent Suite, a connected ecosystem of kitchen and laundry appliances designed to simplify household management through the centralized ConnectLife platform and intelligent automation.

**Key points**
* The suite includes an intelligent refrigerator, smart oven, dishwasher, washer, and dryer, all integrated via the ConnectLife platform.
* The refrigerator features a TFT ConnectLife Hub display for centralized monitoring, AI food management, and ingredient tracking.
* The smart oven utilizes an AI Cooking Agent to suggest recipes and automatically sync program parameters for multi-dish cooking.
* The dishwasher incorporates AI Super Clean technology to adjust washing cycles and detergent usage based on soil levels.
* Laundry appliances offer AI voice control and energy-efficient cycles designed to optimize fabric care and reduce utility costs.

**Why it matters**
This ecosystem addresses the fragmentation of smart home technology by unifying disparate appliances into a single, coordinated interface. By automating routine chores, the suite aims to reduce household stress and improve energy efficiency for consumers.
