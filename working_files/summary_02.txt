Who is OpenAI’s global head of ads, David Dugan?
============================================================

OpenAI has hired former Meta executive David Dugan to lead its global advertising business, tasking him with building a commercial strategy to diversify the company's revenue streams.

### Key Points
- David Dugan spent over a decade at Meta, where he led global agency partnerships and managed relationships with major advertisers.
- OpenAI is aggressively expanding its advertising capabilities to offset high operational costs and prepare for a potential public listing.
- Dugan previously held leadership roles at Digitas, BzzAgent, and Arnold Worldwide, gaining experience in both strategic consulting and commercial sales.
- The appointment follows a trend of tech companies hiring veteran ad-industry leaders to build trust with agencies and brands for new platforms.
- Dugan will work closely with OpenAI’s CEO of applications, Fidji Simo, to bridge the gap between advertiser needs and AI product development.

### Why it Matters
OpenAI’s move into advertising signals a critical shift in its business model as the company seeks to reduce its reliance on subscription revenue and capital-intensive funding. By hiring a seasoned veteran like Dugan, OpenAI aims to secure the advertiser trust necessary to compete for major marketing budgets against established tech giants.
