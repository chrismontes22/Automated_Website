iOS 26.5: New features, release date, more
============================================================

Apple has released the first beta of iOS 26.5, introducing new Apple Maps advertising features, end-to-end encryption for RCS messaging, and expanded subscription billing options for developers.

### Key Points
- Apple Maps is adding local advertising support and a new "Suggested Places" feature based on trending locations and recent user search history.
- The Messages app now includes an end-to-end encryption toggle for RCS messaging, which is enabled by default for users.
- App Store developers gain new subscription billing options, specifically allowing for monthly payments tied to a 12-month commitment.
- iPhone users in the EU receive expanded support for third-party accessories, including Live Activities, proximity pairing, and audio switching.
- Magic Keyboard, Trackpad, and Mouse accessories now pair automatically to iPhones via Bluetooth after an initial USB-C wired connection.
- Additional updates include a new Inuktitut keyboard, "Year In Review" features for Apple Books, and refined data transfer settings for switching to Android.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal Apple's ongoing efforts to monetize its platform through integrated advertising and flexible subscription models while addressing regulatory requirements in the European Union. By streamlining accessory connectivity and messaging security, the company continues to refine the ecosystem experience ahead of the upcoming iOS 27 announcement.
