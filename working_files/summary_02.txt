SwitchBot Lock Vision Series Review: Face ID for Your Front Door
============================================================

The SwitchBot Lock Vision Series introduces advanced 3D structured-light facial recognition and palm vein biometrics to the smart deadbolt market, offering a secure, hands-free entry experience for homeowners.

### Key Points
- The Lock Vision ($169.99) and Lock Vision Pro ($229.99) feature 3D facial mapping technology similar to iPhone Face ID.
- The Pro model adds contactless palm vein and semiconductor fingerprint scanning for enhanced security and hygiene.
- A three-tier power system includes a 10,000mAh rechargeable battery, a CR123A emergency backup cell, and a USB-C port.
- Integrated mmWave radar manages power consumption by waking sensors only when a person approaches the door.
- The device supports Matter-over-WiFi, eliminating the need for a dedicated smart home hub for connectivity.
- The exterior is rated IP65 for weather resistance, operating in temperatures ranging from -20 to 55 degrees Celsius.

### Why it Matters
This product represents a significant shift in smart home security by prioritizing passive, biometric-based entry over traditional manual methods. By integrating high-end authentication technology into a consumer-grade deadbolt, SwitchBot addresses common user pain points like battery anxiety and slow motor response times.
