The 4 Security Companies That Earn Highest Marks for Data Protection
============================================================

Ecobee, Lockly, Blink, and Reolink are identified as top-tier smart home security brands due to their strong track records in data privacy, encryption standards, and minimal security breach histories.

### Key Points
- Ecobee maintains a clean security record by refusing to sell user data and warning customers against using reused, insecure passwords.
- Lockly utilizes AES 128-bit encryption and local data storage, with security protocols independently verified by the assurance firm LRQA.
- Blink, an Amazon-owned brand, offers local storage options via USB drives and has remained largely free of major data breaches since 2017.
- Reolink prioritizes user-controlled local storage through microSD cards and NVRs, while utilizing standard TLS and RSA encryption for cloud services.
- Google Nest remains a mixed option, as recent research highlighted potential vulnerabilities where AI prompt injections could compromise connected smart home devices.

### Why it Matters
Choosing a security provider with a proven history of data protection is essential for preventing unauthorized access to private video feeds and personal information. Prioritizing companies that offer local storage and opt-in data sharing helps users maintain control over their digital footprint in an increasingly connected home environment.
