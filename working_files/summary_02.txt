**Anthropic’s Claude AI experiences a surge in user retention and app store rankings following public disputes with the Pentagon and successful marketing campaigns targeting competitor OpenAI.**

**Key points**
- Anthropic’s Claude app reached the number one spot on the Apple App Store in early 2024 after previously ranking outside the top 40.
- TechCrunch data indicates a record-high return rate for previous users and increased subscriber gains for the platform.
- The company’s growth follows a high-profile refusal to meet Pentagon demands regarding autonomous weapons and surveillance technology.
- Claude currently maintains the second-place position in the App Store, trailing only OpenAI’s ChatGPT.
- New developer and productivity tools have contributed to the platform's sustained engagement levels.

**Why it matters**
Anthropic’s ability to maintain a top-tier market position despite regulatory friction suggests that consumer trust and product utility are becoming critical differentiators in the AI sector. This trend highlights a shifting competitive landscape where startups can successfully challenge established incumbents by emphasizing ethical stances and independent tool functionality.
