The best tools for sending an email if you go silent
============================================================

Google’s Inactive Account Manager and specialized services like Alcazar offer reliable ways to automate email delivery or account access if you become unresponsive for an extended period.

### Key Points
- Google Inactive Account Manager allows users to nominate up to 10 contacts to receive account data after a set period of inactivity.
- Alcazar provides a dedicated check-in system supporting encrypted file attachments and custom messages sent via email, Signal, or Telegram.
- The long-running Dead Man’s Switch service offers a simple, classic interface for scheduled message delivery, though it is not intended for high-stakes security.
- LastSignal is an open-source, self-hosted option that allows users to audit code and maintain full control over their own encrypted data.
- Other tools like Apple Legacy Contact and Bitwarden Emergency Access are designed for account recovery rather than automated silence-triggered messaging.

### Why it Matters
These tools provide a practical way to manage digital handoffs and ensure important information reaches designated contacts during unexpected absences. While they offer significant convenience for personal organization, they do not replace formal legal estate planning or provide legal authority over assets.
