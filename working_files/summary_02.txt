Take Secure Wi-Fi Anywhere With a Pocket-Sized ASUS Mini Router
============================================================

ASUS has introduced a new line of compact, portable mini routers designed to provide secure, private Wi-Fi connectivity for travelers, remote workers, and those living in small-footprint spaces.

### Key Points
- The ASUS RT-BE58 Go features Wi-Fi 7 technology, speeds up to 3,600 Mbps, and tri-mode connectivity for home or travel use.
- The RT-AX57 Go offers Wi-Fi 6, 3,000 Mbps speeds, and subscription-free network security in a lightweight, portable design.
- The ultra-compact RT-AX50 Go weighs only 4 ounces and is designed for maximum portability for digital nomads and frequent travelers.
- All models support USB-C charging and provide VPN features to protect sensitive data when connecting to public Wi-Fi networks.
- These devices can function as standalone routers, mesh nodes, or media bridges to eliminate dead zones and reduce streaming lag.

### Why it Matters
These portable routers address the growing security risks associated with public Wi-Fi by allowing users to maintain a private, encrypted network anywhere they travel. By offering high-performance hardware in a pocket-sized form factor, ASUS provides a practical solution for professionals and travelers who require reliable, secure internet access without sacrificing portability.
