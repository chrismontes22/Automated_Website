A looming crisis could limit some of your favorite AI tools
============================================================

Leading AI companies including Anthropic and Microsoft’s GitHub are imposing stricter usage limits and service restrictions as surging demand for agentic AI tools strains finite global compute resources.

### Key Points
- GitHub Copilot paused new signups for Pro and Student plans while implementing tighter usage limits to manage high resource consumption.
- Anthropic is testing restrictions on Claude Code access for lower-tier subscribers due to unexpected demand and infrastructure pressure.
- Agentic AI tools that run for hours are consuming significantly more compute power than the chat-based models originally envisioned by developers.
- Regional data center constraints prevent companies from pooling global compute capacity, leading to localized service outages and performance bottlenecks.
- Industry analysts suggest companies will prioritize users, increase pricing, or sunset older, less efficient models to stabilize unsustainable unit economics.

### Why it Matters
The current infrastructure crunch signals a shift from the era of unlimited AI access to a more restrictive, cost-conscious model for consumers. As companies struggle to balance profitability with the high compute demands of autonomous agents, users should expect higher prices and reduced availability for popular AI tools.
