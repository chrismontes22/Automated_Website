Need storage? This early Prime Day deal slashes 31% OFF the Samsung T7 1TB SSD, knocking the price down to $0.19 per GB
============================================================

Amazon has launched an early Prime Day promotion offering a 31% discount on the 1TB Samsung T7 portable SSD, reducing the price to $189.99 for a limited time.

### Key Points
- The 1TB Samsung T7 portable SSD is currently discounted to $189.99, down from its original $274.99 price point.
- Additional storage capacities, including 2TB and 4TB models, are also on sale with discounts of 35% and 27% respectively.
- The device features read speeds up to 1,050MB/s and write speeds up to 1,000MB/s with integrated hardware encryption.
- The T7 is built with a shock-resistant aluminum chassis capable of surviving drops from up to six feet.
- Each purchase includes a three-year limited warranty from Samsung.

### Why it Matters
This sale provides a rare opportunity to secure high-speed external storage at a lower cost during a period of global memory shortages and rising hardware prices. For content creators and mobile professionals, these discounts offer a cost-effective way to upgrade portable storage capacity before the broader Prime Day event begins.
