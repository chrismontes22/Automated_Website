Samsung's 1TB portable T9 SSD just scored a rare discount at Amazon — score 1TB of storage for $0.25 per gig
============================================================

Amazon is currently offering a 13% discount on the 1TB Samsung T9 portable SSD, reducing the price to $249.99 amid ongoing market volatility in storage and RAM pricing.

### Key Points
- The 1TB Samsung T9 portable SSD is discounted to $249.99, with larger 2TB and 4TB models seeing deeper price cuts of 17% and 30% respectively.
- The device features transfer speeds reaching 2,000MB/s and includes AES 256-bit hardware encryption for enhanced data security.
- Designed for durability, the T9 is built to withstand drops from heights of up to 9.8 feet.
- The drive incorporates an advanced thermal cooling system to prevent overheating during intensive data transfer tasks.

### Why it Matters
This discount provides a rare opportunity for consumers to acquire reliable external storage during a period of rising hardware costs. It may also signal broader price adjustments for portable drives ahead of the upcoming Amazon Prime Day 2026 sales event.
