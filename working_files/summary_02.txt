ExpressVPN is the cheapest way to ensure you're secure no matter where you celebrate Memorial Day 2026 — here's the best way to pick up a plan for yourself
============================================================

Travelers heading out for Memorial Day weekend can protect their personal data on public Wi-Fi networks by using a virtual private network like ExpressVPN, NordVPN, or Proton VPN.

### Key Points
- ExpressVPN currently offers subscription plans starting at $2.79 per month, which includes four additional months of service.
- ExpressVPN maintains servers in all 50 U.S. states and features the Aircove router for simplified plug-and-play security.
- NordVPN is recommended for users prioritizing streaming performance on platforms like Netflix, Disney+, and Prime Video.
- Proton VPN, based in Switzerland, provides robust privacy protections and supports port forwarding for specialized users.
- Public Wi-Fi networks at cafes and gas stations often lack encryption, making them vulnerable to data interception by third parties.

### Why it Matters
Using a VPN is a critical security measure for travelers to prevent unauthorized monitoring of sensitive online activity while connected to unsecured public networks. Selecting the right service ensures a balance between data encryption, streaming accessibility, and ease of use for mobile users.
