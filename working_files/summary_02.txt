iOS 26.5 will add new features to three popular iPhone apps
============================================================

Apple is set to release iOS 26.5, introducing end-to-end encrypted RCS messaging, personalized location suggestions in Apple Maps, and a new flexible annual subscription billing option for App Store developers.

### Key Points
- The Messages app introduces beta end-to-end encryption for RCS messaging to improve communication security between iPhone and Android devices.
- Apple Maps now features a "Suggested Places" tool that recommends nearby locations based on user search history and trending data.
- The App Store is adding a "monthly with 12-month commitment" subscription model, allowing users to pay for annual plans in 12 smaller installments.
- Apple Maps may eventually incorporate promoted locations or advertisements within the new suggested search slots.

### Why it Matters
These updates represent a significant shift in how Apple manages cross-platform messaging standards and app monetization strategies. By offering flexible payment plans and enhanced search discovery, Apple aims to increase user engagement and lower financial barriers for long-term service subscriptions.
