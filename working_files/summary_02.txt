Apple Seeds iOS 26.5 and iPadOS 26.5 Release Candidates to Developers
============================================================

Apple has released the release candidate versions of iOS 26.5 and iPadOS 26.5 to developers, signaling that the final public software update is expected to launch next week.

### Key Points
- Registered developers can access the release candidate updates through the Software Update section in the Settings app.
- The update introduces a Suggested Places feature in Apple Maps and prepares the application for future advertising integration.
- Apple is testing enhanced interoperability for third-party wearables in the European Union, including notification forwarding and Live Activities.
- The software includes a new Pride-themed wallpaper to accompany the latest seasonal Apple Watch band release.
- Siri updates are notably absent from this release, with major improvements reportedly reserved for the upcoming iOS 27.

### Why it Matters
These updates represent a significant shift in Apple's regulatory compliance and advertising strategy, particularly regarding third-party device integration in the European Union. By refining Maps functionality and wearable support, Apple is balancing ecosystem control with the evolving requirements of global digital markets.
