9to5Mac Daily: April 27, 2026 – iOS 26.4.2, more on Ternus and Cook
============================================================

The latest 9to5Mac Daily podcast episode covers the base model Mac mini stock shortages, a critical iOS security patch, and internal leadership discussions regarding the future of Apple TV.

### Key Points
- The $599 base model Mac mini is currently listed as completely out of stock.
- Apple released iOS 16.4.2 to resolve a bug that allowed users to retrieve deleted notifications.
- Apple executive John Ternus expressed interest in making the Apple TV hardware more competitive in the streaming market.
- A recent company town hall featured CEO Tim Cook discussing significant career milestones and past strategic missteps.

### Why it Matters
These updates highlight ongoing supply chain fluctuations for entry-level hardware alongside Apple's commitment to addressing software vulnerabilities. Understanding these shifts provides insight into both the company's immediate product availability and its long-term strategic focus on the home entertainment sector.
