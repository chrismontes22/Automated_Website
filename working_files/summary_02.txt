iPhone 18 Rumor Check: 20 Claims Ranked by Likelihood
============================================================

Apple is preparing to launch its iPhone 18 Pro models and a new foldable device within two months, signaling a strategic shift toward a split-release schedule for 2027.

### Key Points
- The iPhone 18 Pro models will feature a 48-megapixel variable aperture camera and are expected to start at $1,299 and $1,399.
- Apple’s first foldable device, potentially named the iPhone Ultra, will feature a book-style design with a 7.8-inch inner display and a price exceeding $2,000.
- The iPhone 18 lineup will include increased RAM to support advanced Apple Intelligence features and utilize the efficient 2nm A20 Pro chip.
- The iPhone Air 2 is in advanced testing with a new dual-camera system, while the standard iPhone 18 and 18e are slated for a spring 2027 release.
- Apple is transitioning to its own C2 modems for international models, though U.S. versions may continue to rely on Qualcomm hardware for mmWave support.

### Why it Matters
These upcoming releases represent a significant evolution in Apple's hardware strategy, balancing premium foldable innovation with a diversified, multi-stage product launch. The shift toward higher price points and specialized features reflects the company's attempt to sustain growth in a maturing smartphone market through advanced AI integration and hardware differentiation.
