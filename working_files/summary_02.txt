5 essential features for small business printers — Check your chosen printer has these if you're printing from the office or home office
============================================================

Selecting the right printer for a small business requires balancing essential features like all-in-one functionality, wireless connectivity, and robust security to ensure long-term operational efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

### Key Points
- Prioritize all-in-one devices that combine printing, scanning, and copying to save office space, energy, and administrative time.
- Ensure wireless and mobile support, such as Wi-Fi Direct or Apple AirPrint, to facilitate flexible printing for hybrid and remote teams.
- Utilize Automatic Document Feeding (ADF) and automatic duplexing to streamline high-volume tasks and reduce paper consumption.
- Calculate the total Cost Per Page (CPP) by factoring in ink, paper, and maintenance to avoid the false economy of low-cost hardware.
- Implement security protocols like SSL/TLS encryption, secure boot, and PIN-based authentication to protect sensitive data from cyberattacks.

### Why it Matters
Investing in the correct printing technology helps small businesses streamline workflows and maintain data security without the need for a dedicated IT department. By focusing on total cost of ownership and future-proof features, organizations can achieve better long-term value and operational agility.
