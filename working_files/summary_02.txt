"What an insane screw up": Xbox itself leaks 'Forza Horizon 6' PC files in full a week before launch — and pirates already cracked it
============================================================

Microsoft has confirmed that an unencrypted build of the upcoming racing game Forza Horizon 6 leaked online, prompting the company to issue franchise-wide bans for unauthorized users.

### Key Points
- Forza Horizon 6 files were discovered circulating on piracy sites and streaming platforms days before the official May 19, 2026, release date.
- Microsoft officially denied that the leak originated from a pre-load error on their part, though the exact source of the breach remains under investigation.
- The company is actively enforcing strict penalties, including long-term franchise-wide bans, for any individuals caught accessing or distributing the leaked game files.
- Legal teams are currently working to remove unauthorized download links from platforms like Reddit to mitigate the spread of the unencrypted build.

### Why it Matters
This security breach poses a significant financial risk to Xbox during a period of declining hardware and software sales. The incident highlights ongoing challenges for major publishers in protecting high-profile titles from piracy, which can negatively impact revenue for one of the platform's most successful franchises.
