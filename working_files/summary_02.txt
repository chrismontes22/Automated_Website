Encrypted Emails Are Now Available for Some Gmail Phone App Enterprise Customers
============================================================

Google has expanded end-to-end encryption for Gmail to iOS and Android mobile apps, allowing Enterprise Plus subscribers to send and receive secure messages without requiring third-party software.

### Key Points
- The feature is exclusively available to Google Workspace Enterprise Plus customers with Assured Controls or Assured Controls Plus add-ons.
- Users can enable encryption by selecting the lock icon within the Gmail mobile app interface.
- Encrypted messages remain secure even when sent to recipients using non-Gmail email clients or web browsers.
- Administrators must manually enable the client-side encryption (CSE) settings within the Google Admin Console to grant user access.
- This update aims to improve digital sovereignty and data compliance for large organizations handling sensitive information on mobile devices.

### Why it Matters
This update enhances mobile security for large organizations by integrating high-level encryption directly into the native Gmail experience. It allows businesses to maintain strict data compliance and security standards while providing employees with the flexibility to manage sensitive communications from anywhere.
