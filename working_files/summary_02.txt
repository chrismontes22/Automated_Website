WinX Mediatrans 8.4
============================================================

WinXDVD is offering a free, limited-time license for WinX MediaTrans version 8.4, a software tool designed to help users manage and transfer media files on iOS devices without iTunes.

### Key Points
- The giveaway celebrates the 20th anniversary of WinXDVD and is available through the Giveaway of the Day platform.
- WinX MediaTrans 8.4 supports iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch devices, including compatibility with the latest iOS 26 and iPhone 17 series.
- Key features include hardware-accelerated file transfers, automatic HEIC to JPG conversion, and local data encryption for privacy.
- The software allows users to manage music, photos, and videos while bypassing traditional iTunes syncing restrictions and iCloud storage limitations.
- System requirements include Windows Vista through Windows 10, a 1 GHz processor, and at least 512 MB of RAM.

### Why it Matters
This promotion provides a cost-effective alternative for users seeking to bypass the limitations of Apple’s native ecosystem and subscription-based cloud storage. By offering a lifetime license for a tool typically valued at $39.95, the company aims to increase its user base while providing a practical solution for local media management.
