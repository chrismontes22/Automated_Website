iOS 26.5 release date: Here’s when to expect new iPhone features
============================================================

Apple is expected to release the iOS 26.5 software update for iPhone users in mid-May, following the company's consistent historical release patterns for mid-cycle operating system versions.

### Key Points
- The public launch for iOS 26.5 is projected to occur between the weeks of May 11 and May 18.
- Apple Maps is introducing a new Suggested Places feature and may begin displaying advertisements in the United States and Canada.
- The Messages app is testing end-to-end encryption for RCS messaging, which is expected to be included in the final release.
- New App Store functionality will include a monthly subscription option that requires a 12-month commitment.
- European Union users will gain support for third-party accessories to utilize the Live Activities feature.

### Why it Matters
This update represents a significant mid-cycle refresh that introduces both new monetization strategies and expanded messaging security for the global iPhone user base. By aligning these features with a predictable May release schedule, Apple maintains its software ecosystem stability ahead of the major iOS 27 announcement at WWDC in June.
