Surfshark VPN review: Affordable protection with solid performance
============================================================

Surfshark is a budget-friendly VPN provider that offers unlimited simultaneous device connections, robust privacy features, and high-speed performance, making it a top choice for households and multi-device users.

### Key Points
- Surfshark operates a network of servers across 100 countries using 100% RAM-only, diskless hardware for enhanced data security.
- The service supports industry-standard encryption protocols including WireGuard, OpenVPN, and IKEv2 to ensure secure and fast connections.
- Independent audits by Cure53 and Deloitte have verified the company’s strict no-logs policy regarding user activity and connection data.
- Key features include a kill switch, split tunneling, MultiHop, and the CleanWeb ad and malware blocker.
- The provider offers three subscription tiers with a 30-day money-back guarantee, though introductory pricing increases significantly upon renewal.

### Why it Matters
Surfshark’s unlimited connection policy provides significant value for families and power users who need to secure multiple devices under a single subscription. While the company's merger with Nord Security and its Netherlands-based jurisdiction may concern some privacy purists, its audited no-logs policy and consistent performance make it a highly competitive option in the crowded VPN market.
