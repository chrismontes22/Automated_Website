We have more numbers on Tesla's Robotaxi progress. Here are the 7 that matter most.
============================================================

Tesla reported steady growth for its Robotaxi platform during its second-quarter earnings call, highlighting expanded service in seven U.S. regions and significant progress in autonomous manufacturing capacity.

### Key Points
- Tesla reached nearly 2.5 million cumulative paid miles and over 380,000 unsupervised miles across six cities.
- The Robotaxi service is currently active in seven U.S. metropolitan regions, including recent expansions into Orlando and Tampa.
- Weekly mileage for the autonomous fleet is growing at a rate exceeding 10% as the company scales operations.
- Gigafactory Texas has established an annual manufacturing capacity of more than 125,000 purpose-built Cybercabs.
- Tesla reported 1.48 million paying Full Self-Driving customers, marking a 56% increase compared to the previous year.

### Why it Matters
The expansion of Tesla’s autonomous ride-hailing service signals a critical shift toward commercializing its AI technology despite ongoing regulatory and safety scrutiny. Successfully scaling this platform could significantly impact the competitive landscape of the autonomous vehicle market, currently led by established players like Waymo.
