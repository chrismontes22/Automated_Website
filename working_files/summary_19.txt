iPhone 18 Pro series, foldable iPhone likely to debut on September 8
============================================================

Apple is reportedly planning to unveil its new iPhone 18 Pro lineup and the company's first foldable smartphone, the iPhone Ultra, during a launch event in early September.

### Key Points
- Bloomberg analyst Mark Gurman predicts the Apple event will occur on September 8 or September 9, following the Labor Day holiday.
- The upcoming product lineup is expected to feature the iPhone 18 Pro, the iPhone 18 Pro Max, and the debut of the foldable iPhone Ultra.
- Pre-orders for the new devices are projected to begin on September 10 if the company adheres to its traditional post-Labor Day release schedule.
- Rumors suggest that the iPhone 18 Pro series will carry higher retail price tags compared to previous generations of Apple smartphones.

### Why it Matters
The introduction of a foldable device marks a significant shift in Apple's hardware strategy as it attempts to compete in the growing foldable smartphone market. Higher price points for the new lineup could impact consumer demand and influence the company's overall revenue growth for the upcoming fiscal quarter.
