The new Google Home Speaker might finally arrive this month after a long wait
============================================================

Best Buy Canada has listed June 25, 2026, as the potential release date for Google’s upcoming Gemini-powered Home Speaker, though the company has not yet confirmed an official launch.

### Key Points
- Best Buy Canada lists the Google Home Speaker for release on June 25, 2026, while the US site remains marked as "Coming Soon."
- Google originally announced the $99.99 device in October 2025 with a general "Spring 2026" release window.
- The new hardware is designed specifically for Gemini integration and will be available in Porcelain, Hazel, Berry, and Jade colors.
- Key features include 360-degree audio, stereo pairing, and compatibility with the Google TV Streamer for home theater configurations.
- Google has not officially confirmed the June date, stating only that more details regarding availability will be shared soon.

### Why it Matters
This device represents a strategic shift for Google as it moves away from the Nest brand to focus on hardware built natively for its Gemini AI. The launch is a critical test for the company's smart home ecosystem as it attempts to integrate advanced generative AI features into consumer audio products.
