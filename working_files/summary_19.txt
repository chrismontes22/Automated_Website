Anker Just Built a Coin-Sized AI Voice Recorder That Transcribes 150 Languages
============================================================

Anker has launched the SoundCore Work, an ultra-compact AI-powered voice recorder featuring multi-language transcription, 32-hour battery life, and advanced security measures for professional and personal audio documentation.

### Key Points
- The device measures 23.2 mm in diameter, weighs 10 grams, and includes 64 GB of internal storage.
- It supports AI-driven transcription in over 150 languages with up to 97% accuracy and speaker differentiation.
- Battery life reaches 8 hours per charge, extending to 32 hours with the included portable charging case.
- Security features include AES-256 encryption and SOC2 Type I certification to protect sensitive audio data.
- The recorder is MFi-certified for seamless integration with Apple devices and features one-touch recording controls.

### Why it Matters
This device represents a growing trend of integrating sophisticated AI processing into ultra-portable hardware to streamline professional workflows. By automating transcription and summarization, it offers a practical solution for users who need to manage large volumes of audio data securely and efficiently.
