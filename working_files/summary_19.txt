Galaxy Digital Wins Major Money Transmitter License in New York
============================================================

Galaxy Digital has secured a BitLicense and Money Transmitter License from the New York State Department of Financial Services, enabling direct service to institutional clients throughout the state.

### Key Points
- The New York State Department of Financial Services granted the licenses to GalaxyOne Prime NY on May 18.
- Galaxy Digital can now provide regulated trading and custody services to hedge funds, family offices, and registered investment advisors.
- The firm manages approximately $9 billion in client assets and holds over 50 global licenses.
- Fewer than 40 companies currently hold an active BitLicense, which requires rigorous cybersecurity, capital reserve, and anti-money laundering standards.
- The licensing process typically takes 18 to 24 months and can cost applicants seven figures in legal and compliance fees.

### Why it Matters
This approval allows Galaxy Digital to capture the largest concentration of institutional capital in the United States by providing a compliant, direct gateway for professional investors. While the high barrier to entry favors established firms, it also signals a shift toward increased institutional adoption of digital assets within a strictly regulated framework.
