Targus Thunderbolt 5 Triple Video dock review: Beware the catch
============================================================

The Targus Thunderbolt 5 Triple Video Docking Station offers advanced connectivity features, but its full triple-display functionality is restricted exclusively to laptops equipped with Thunderbolt 5 ports.

**Key points**
- The dock retails for $399.99 and includes two 2-meter Thunderbolt-to-HDMI adapter cables.
- Full support for three simultaneous displays requires a host laptop with a Thunderbolt 5 interface.
- The device features 140W power delivery, 10Gbps USB-A ports, and a 2.5Gbps Ethernet connection.
- It supports Thunderbolt Share technology, allowing data transfer and control between two connected laptops.
- Performance testing showed stable data throughput, though the dock lacks active cooling fans.

**Why it matters: **
While the dock provides future-proof technology for high-end workstations, its high price and limited compatibility with current Windows laptops make it a questionable investment for most users. It is primarily recommended for those who already own Thunderbolt 5-enabled hardware or require specific niche features like Thunderbolt Share.
