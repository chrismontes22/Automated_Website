Strategy Leaves Bitcoin Holdings Unchanged
============================================================

MicroStrategy maintains its Bitcoin holdings at 843,775 tokens while increasing its cash reserves by $466.7 million through recent at-the-market stock sales to support preferred dividend payments.

### Key Points
- MicroStrategy holds 843,775 Bitcoin acquired at an average price of $75,476 per coin.
- The company raised $466.7 million in cash, bringing total U.S. dollar reserves to $3 billion.
- Funds are designated to support dividend payments for the company's preferred stock, NASDAQ: STRC.
- MicroStrategy shares have declined 79% over the past 12 months, currently trading at $94.64.
- Bitcoin is facing renewed market pressure as global geopolitical tensions and rising oil prices impact investor sentiment.

### Why it Matters
This strategic shift toward liquidity highlights the company's need to balance its aggressive cryptocurrency investment strategy with the financial obligations of its preferred stock dividends. By bolstering cash reserves, MicroStrategy aims to maintain operational stability despite significant unrealized losses on its Bitcoin portfolio and ongoing volatility in the broader digital asset market.
