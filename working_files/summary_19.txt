The Engineered Collapse of Work and Freedom: My Case Against the Coming AI Dystopia and UBI Financial Enslavement
============================================================

NaturalNews editor Mike Adams argues that a technocratic elite is orchestrating a global transition toward AI-driven labor displacement, digital financial control, and the erosion of individual human autonomy.

### Key Points
- Mike Adams claims that automation and AI will render approximately 4 billion people economically obsolete within the next decade.
- The article characterizes Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs) and Universal Basic Income (UBI) as tools for state-enforced social and financial surveillance.
- Adams asserts that public health and financial institutions are being consolidated to prioritize elite control over individual freedom and prosperity.
- The author advocates for building decentralized, self-reliant communities to resist what he describes as a high-tech return to feudalism.
- Recommended survival strategies include local food production, the use of physical assets like gold and silver, and the adoption of private communication platforms.

### Why it Matters
This perspective highlights growing concerns regarding the societal implications of rapid AI integration and the centralization of digital financial systems. It reflects a broader movement that prioritizes local self-reliance and decentralized infrastructure as a defense against perceived systemic overreach by global institutions.
