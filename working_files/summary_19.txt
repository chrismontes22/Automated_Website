Samsung Wallet adds a travel feature you can’t ignore on your next trip
============================================================

Samsung has introduced a new Trips feature to its Wallet app, allowing Galaxy users in select regions to consolidate flight, hotel, and event itineraries into a single timeline.

### Key Points
- The Trips feature organizes travel bookings, including hotel reservations, flight itineraries, car rentals, and tickets for events or public transit, into one view.
- Users can manually add itinerary items and personal memos to their trip timeline to supplement information that does not automatically sync.
- The feature is currently available only to Samsung Galaxy users in the United States, the United Kingdom, and South Korea.
- Access requires Samsung Wallet version 5.9.32 or higher in Korea, 6.4.97 or higher in the U.S., or 6.4.98 or higher in the U.K.

### Why it Matters
This update provides Samsung users with a centralized travel management tool that offers more organizational clarity than the standard Google Wallet experience. By streamlining fragmented travel data, Samsung aims to increase the utility of its ecosystem for frequent travelers and improve user retention on Galaxy devices.
