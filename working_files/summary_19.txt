Soundcore has a 3D scanner for Nebula X1 projectors that transforms your home into art
============================================================

Soundcore has introduced the Nebula SpaceFlow, a 3D scanner accessory for its X1 and X1 Pro projectors that uses generative AI to map rooms and project custom animated scenes.

### Key Points
- The SpaceFlow features a dual-camera system with a time-of-flight sensor and structured light emitter to create 3D environment models.
- Users can input text prompts to generate custom, perspective-corrected graphics that adapt to room geometry and avoid obstructions like windows.
- The system supports animated visuals, allowing for complex, theatrical-style projections without manual design or physical decoration.
- The accessory is compatible with the triple-laser 4K Nebula X1 and the Nebula X1 Pro projectors.
- The device carries an MSRP of $799, with an introductory promotional price of $399 currently available.

### Why it Matters
- This technology lowers the barrier to entry for high-end spatial projection mapping by automating complex environmental scanning and content generation. It signals a shift toward integrating generative AI into home entertainment hardware to create immersive, interactive living spaces.
