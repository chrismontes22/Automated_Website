Qualcomm's New Snapdragon C Processors Herald an Old Spin on Cheap Laptops
============================================================

Qualcomm is introducing the new Snapdragon C processor to power budget-friendly laptops, with Acer’s upcoming Aspire Go 15 serving as one of the first devices to feature the chip.

### Key Points
- The Snapdragon C processor is designed for entry-level laptops targeting a price point of approximately $300.
- Acer is the first partner to announce a device using the chip, the 15-inch Aspire Go 15, with HP and Lenovo expected to follow.
- The Aspire Go 15 features a 512GB SSD, a 1080p display, and a 1080p webcam housed in a plastic chassis.
- The device is limited to a maximum of 8GB of RAM, raising concerns about long-term performance for Windows-based productivity tasks.

### Why it Matters
This processor launch represents Qualcomm's attempt to capture the low-cost laptop market by offering a more affordable alternative to its high-performance Snapdragon X series. However, the reliance on minimal memory configurations may limit the practical utility of these devices for modern computing needs.
