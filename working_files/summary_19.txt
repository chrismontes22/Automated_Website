A 115-Inch Mini LED TV Exists Now, and It Has 330Hz Gaming
============================================================

LG has launched its 2026 QNED evo Mini LED TV series, headlined by a massive 115-inch flagship model featuring advanced AI processing and high-performance gaming capabilities for global markets.

### Key Points
- The flagship 115-inch QNED90 features Precision Dimming Ultra and a 165Hz variable refresh rate with 330Hz Motion Booster technology.
- The lineup is powered by the Alpha 8 AI Processor Gen 3, which utilizes deep learning for image upscaling and sound optimization.
- Dynamic QNED Color Pro technology provides 100 percent Color Volume, ensuring accurate color reproduction in bright HDR environments.
- New software features include the Sports Portal hub for live updates and AI-driven tools like Google Gemini and Microsoft Copilot integration.
- The series spans six tiers, ranging from the premium QNED90 down to the entry-level QNED70, with immediate availability in Korea, the U.S., and Europe.

### Why it Matters
This release signals a strategic shift for LG, which is positioning its Mini LED technology as a high-end competitor to its own long-standing OLED dominance. By integrating advanced AI processing and ultra-large screen sizes, LG aims to capture the premium home theater and gaming markets that prioritize brightness and scale.
