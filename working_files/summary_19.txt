Weekend Recommender: Small Tech That Solves Real-Life Annoyances
============================================================

The latest product roundup highlights five practical gadgets designed to reduce daily friction, including tracking devices, ergonomic office tools, portable power solutions, and specialized sleep accessories for travelers.

### Key Points
- The eufy SmartTrack Card E40 ($35) offers cross-platform compatibility with Apple Find My and Android Find Hub.
- ProtoArc’s EM25 ergonomic mouse features a fast-scroll flywheel, 8000 DPI, and multi-device connectivity for improved productivity.
- The UGREEN MagFlow Air ($80) provides 10,000mAh capacity and Qi2-certified 15W magnetic charging with an integrated USB-C cable.
- iLive’s $50 sleep mask includes built-in Bluetooth headphones and 10 pre-loaded nature sounds for travel or relaxation.
- The Anker Nano Power Strip features a desk-clamp design, 10-in-1 outlet layout, and 70W USB-C charging capabilities.

### Why it Matters
These products focus on incremental improvements to daily workflows and travel convenience rather than major technological overhauls. By addressing common pain points like cable management and device tracking, these tools offer practical value for professionals and commuters seeking to optimize their personal setups.
