Spotify building AI-powered tool that will let subscribers make music covers and remixes
============================================================

Spotify has announced new generative AI tools for fan-made remixes and a concert ticket reservation feature for Premium subscribers as part of its latest Investor Day 2026 updates.

### Key Points
- Spotify reached a landmark licensing agreement with Universal Music Group to allow fan-made covers and remixes on the platform.
- A new generative AI tool will enable Premium subscribers to create and share remixes while providing revenue to original artists.
- The new Reserved feature allows U.S. Premium subscribers to hold up to two concert tickets based on their personal artist listening history.
- These updates follow the company's previous AI-driven initiatives, including the AI DJ feature and integration with Claude.
- Additional upcoming changes will focus on enhancing the user experience for podcasts and audiobooks.

### Why it Matters
These features represent Spotify's most significant effort to integrate generative AI into user-generated content while creating new revenue streams for artists and labels. By prioritizing listening history for ticket reservations, the company aims to deepen fan engagement and provide a competitive alternative to traditional ticket-buying platforms.
