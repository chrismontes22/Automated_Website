iOS 26 has new Home Screen setting for app icons, here’s how to use it
============================================================

Apple has introduced new customization features in iOS 26 that allow users to automatically tint their Home Screen app icons to match the color of their iPhone or case.

### Key Points
- iOS 26 adds two specific tinting options that sync icon aesthetics with hardware colors.
- Users can access these settings by selecting the Customize menu after long-pressing the Home Screen.
- The feature is compatible with official Apple MagSafe cases and select third-party accessories.
- This update builds upon the custom icon design capabilities first introduced in iOS 18.

### Why it Matters
This update reflects Apple's ongoing strategy to provide deeper personalization options that bridge the gap between hardware aesthetics and software interfaces. By simplifying the process of color-matching, the company enhances user engagement and encourages the continued use of its proprietary MagSafe accessory ecosystem.
