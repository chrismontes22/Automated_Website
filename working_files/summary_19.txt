Samsung is bringing One UI to laptops
============================================================

Samsung is reportedly developing a new range of Galaxy Book laptops powered by Android 17 and One UI 9, potentially integrating advanced Galaxy AI features across multiple hardware tiers.

### Key Points
- Samsung is planning a full lineup of Android-based Galaxy Book laptops ranging from budget to flagship models.
- The devices will feature One UI 9, which is expected to function as an enhanced version of the existing Samsung DeX interface.
- The laptops are rumored to include integrated Galaxy AI capabilities to compete with current mobile and desktop productivity tools.
- These products may launch before the end of the year, aligning with broader industry efforts to merge Android and ChromeOS architectures.

### Why it Matters
This shift signals a major strategic move by Samsung to unify its mobile and computing ecosystems under a single software platform. If successful, this transition could challenge the dominance of traditional Windows laptops by offering a more cohesive, AI-driven experience for mobile-first users.
