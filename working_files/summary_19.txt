Gemini for Home update brings better weather forecasts, news briefs, and more
============================================================

Google is rolling out a significant update to Gemini for Home, enhancing conversational capabilities, media discovery, and news interactions through the latest version of the Google Home app.

### Key Points
- The update introduces precise, hour-by-hour weather forecasting and improved temperature unit accuracy.
- Users can now engage in natural, multi-turn conversations to refine media searches and control device volume.
- News briefings are now interactive, allowing users to request additional context on specific headlines during playback.
- Core performance improvements provide faster response times for reminders, alarms, and smart home device management.
- The update is currently deploying to users via Google Home app version 4.18 in a gradual rollout.

### Why it Matters
These enhancements signal Google's shift toward making AI assistants more intuitive and context-aware for daily household tasks. By prioritizing conversational flow over rigid commands, the update aims to increase user engagement with smart home ecosystems and media services.
