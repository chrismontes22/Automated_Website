How Long Do Owners Say Smart Bulbs Actually Last?
============================================================

While manufacturers claim smart bulbs last up to 25,000 hours, real-world user reports suggest most units function for a decade, though individual experiences vary based on usage and hardware.

### Key Points
- Industry standards estimate smart bulbs last approximately 13 years based on five hours of daily use.
- User reports on platforms like Reddit indicate many smart bulbs remain operational for seven to 10 years.
- Premature bulb failure is often linked to external factors like faulty home wiring or switch incompatibility.
- Smart features, including automated scheduling and motion sensors, help extend bulb lifespan by reducing unnecessary operation.
- Smart switches offer a cost-effective alternative by enabling automation for traditional bulbs, which typically have shorter lifespans.

### Why it Matters
Understanding the actual longevity of smart lighting helps consumers weigh the higher upfront costs of these devices against traditional alternatives. This data allows homeowners to make more informed decisions regarding energy efficiency, long-term maintenance, and the potential benefits of upgrading to smart home infrastructure.
