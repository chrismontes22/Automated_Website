Asus offers up to 36% off its ZenWiFi mesh routers — tri-band Wi-Fi 7 mesh routers starting from $297
============================================================

Asus is offering significant discounts on its ZenWiFi BT and BQ series Wi-Fi 7 mesh router systems during the Amazon Prime Day 2026 sales event this week.

### Key Points
- The entry-level ZenWiFi BT6 2-pack is discounted by 20% to $297, providing 5,800 square feet of coverage and 9.4 Gbps speeds.
- The ZenWiFi BT8 2-pack is currently 36% off at $374, featuring upgraded 14 Gbps speeds and additional 2.5 GbE port connectivity.
- The high-performance ZenWiFi BT10 2-pack is priced at $579.99, offering 18 Gbps speeds and dual 10 GbE ports for advanced networking.
- The flagship ZenWiFi BQ16 Pro quad-band system is discounted to $928.99, supporting 30 Gbps speeds and up to 8,000 square feet of coverage.
- All ZenWiFi models include AsusWRT software, which provides free malware protection and device management features without recurring subscription fees.

### Why it Matters
These discounts make high-end Wi-Fi 7 networking hardware more accessible to consumers looking to upgrade their home infrastructure for faster speeds and better coverage. By including free management software, Asus offers a competitive alternative to rivals that typically charge subscription fees for similar security and administrative features.
