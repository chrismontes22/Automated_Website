Prime Day Brings Major Deals on Headphones and Speakers From Popular Brands
============================================================

Amazon Prime Day 2026 enters its second day with significant discounts on premium audio equipment from brands including Apple, Beats, Sony, Sonos, and Soundcore for Prime members.

### Key Points
- The AirPods Max 2 are available at an all-time low price of $399.00 during the final hours of the event.
- Beats Powerbeats Pro 2 are discounted to $179.95, while Beats Studio Buds+ are marked down to $89.95.
- Sony WH-1000XM5 noise-canceling headphones are priced at $198.00, representing a substantial reduction from the original $399.99 retail price.
- Sonos Ace Headphones have reached a new record low of $279.00, a $120 discount from their standard $399.00 cost.
- Amazon is offering a 50% discount on Prime memberships for young adults aged 18-24, reducing the annual fee to $69.00.

### Why it Matters
These deep discounts on high-end audio hardware provide a significant opportunity for consumers to upgrade their personal technology at record-low price points. The event also highlights Amazon's strategy to drive long-term platform loyalty by offering aggressive membership incentives for younger demographics.
