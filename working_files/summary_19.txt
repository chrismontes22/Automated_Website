Top Stories: iPhone Rumors, Apple's MacBook Neo Dilemma, and More
============================================================

Apple is preparing for the upcoming iPhone 18 Pro launch while navigating supply chain challenges for the popular MacBook Neo and releasing new software updates for its devices.

### Key Points
- The iPhone 18 Pro is expected to feature a smaller Dynamic Island rather than a fully under-screen Face ID system.
- Apple faces a supply shortage of binned A18 Pro chips used in the popular MacBook Neo, potentially forcing the company to disable cores on functional chips.
- Apple released iOS and iPadOS 26.4.1 to address iCloud syncing bugs and macOS 26.4.1 to resolve Wi-Fi issues on M5-powered MacBook models.
- CarPlay added support for ChatGPT, Google Meet, and Audiomack, alongside a revamped WhatsApp interface.
- Rumors suggest a foldable iPhone, potentially branded as the "iPhone Ultra," may launch alongside the iPhone 18 Pro with a price point near $2,000.

### Why it Matters
Apple's current supply chain constraints highlight the delicate balance between maintaining high-performance hardware standards and meeting consumer demand for popular products. These developments also underscore the company's ongoing efforts to refine its software ecosystem through frequent updates and expanded third-party app integration for CarPlay.
