XREAL Launches New X By XREAL Brand and Debuts a01 AR Glasses Starting at $299
============================================================

XREAL has launched a new sub-brand called X By XREAL and introduced the a01, a lightweight pair of augmented reality glasses designed for accessible, everyday media and gaming consumption.

### Key Points
- The a01 glasses weigh 62 grams and feature an interchangeable front-frame system that supports 3D-printed customization.
- The device utilizes dual-layer MicroOLED displays with 1600-nit brightness and HDR10 support to simulate a 147-inch screen.
- Integrated spatial anti-shake technology is designed to stabilize images for users during travel on trains, subways, or flights.
- The product includes AI-powered SDR-to-HDR conversion to enhance contrast and color presentation for streaming and gaming.
- The a01 is currently available in China and is scheduled for a United States release in July with a $299 starting price.

### Why it Matters
This launch represents a strategic shift for XREAL to capture the mass-market consumer segment by prioritizing affordability and personal style over high-end professional specifications. By lowering the barrier to entry, the company aims to normalize augmented reality hardware as a standard accessory for mobile entertainment and daily commuting.
