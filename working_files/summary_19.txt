Amazon Cognito unlocks advanced capabilities with next-generation infrastructure
============================================================

Amazon Cognito has upgraded its storage infrastructure to support high-throughput workloads, customer-managed encryption keys, and multi-Region replication, all while maintaining zero-downtime for existing user authentication applications.

### Key Points
- The new architecture supports tens of millions of users per pool and thousands of transactions per second.
- Customers can now utilize AWS Key Management Service (KMS) to manage their own encryption keys for data at rest.
- Multi-Region replication allows for synchronized user data and configurations to improve business continuity and regional failover.
- The migration utilized shadow mode validation, dual-write operations, and anti-entropy scans to ensure data integrity without service interruptions.
- These infrastructure improvements are being rolled out automatically to all existing Amazon Cognito customers without requiring manual action.

### Why it Matters
These updates provide enterprise-grade security and reliability features that allow developers to meet strict compliance and availability requirements. By modernizing the underlying storage layer, Amazon ensures the service can scale to meet the demands of modern, high-traffic applications while maintaining consistent performance.
