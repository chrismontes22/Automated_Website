Doug Liman’s AI-Enabled Crypto Movie ‘Bitcoin’ To Feature Bezos, Zuckerberg, Putin, Eric Trump & More As Characters
============================================================

Director Doug Liman’s upcoming satirical film Bitcoin, starring Casey Affleck, will feature AI-enhanced portrayals of prominent figures including Jeff Bezos, Mark Zuckerberg, and Eric Trump to explore cryptocurrency origins.

### Key Points
- The film stars Casey Affleck as Craig Wright, the computer scientist who unsuccessfully claimed to be Bitcoin creator Satoshi Nakamoto.
- Supporting cast members include Pete Davidson, Gal Gadot, and Isla Fisher, with AI-enhanced cameos from figures like Vladimir Putin and Kim Jong Un.
- Produced by Ryan Kavanaugh and Lawrence Grey, the project utilizes a $70 million budget and proprietary AI technology to replace traditional location shooting.
- The script depicts Eric Trump promoting a "Trump coin" and includes a brief reference to Elon Musk within its satirical narrative.
- Production was completed in 20 days using a markerless performative capture stage, adhering to current Hollywood guild AI guidelines.

### Why it Matters
The film represents a significant test case for the integration of AI-enhanced performances in high-budget Hollywood productions. By blending satire with controversial real-world figures, the project highlights the evolving legal and creative boundaries surrounding the use of digital likenesses in cinema.
