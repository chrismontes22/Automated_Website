Growth Analytics Is What Comes After Growth Hacking
============================================================

Databricks Genie enables growth teams to unify acquisition, behavioral, and revenue data, allowing leaders to perform complex cohort analysis and optimize budget allocation using natural language queries.

### Key Points
- Databricks Genie integrates disparate data sources to eliminate the need for manual stitching between acquisition, product, and billing systems.
- The platform allows users to query complex metrics, such as 90-day lifetime value (LTV) by channel, using natural language instead of technical code.
- Early adopters report a 50% relative lift in acquisition rates by identifying high-quality cohort signals previously hidden in disconnected tools.
- The tool reduces insight cycles from months to weeks, enabling faster experimentation and more agile responses to changing market conditions.
- It is designed to support high-velocity decision-making for growth leaders who require weekly budget adjustments based on real-time performance data.

### Why it Matters
Modern growth organizations face a competitive landscape where tactical "growth hacking" is no longer sufficient to maintain market share. By removing the technical bottleneck of fragmented data architectures, companies can achieve a structural competitive advantage through faster, more precise economic modeling and budget reallocation.
