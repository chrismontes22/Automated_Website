Hit List: Iran Threatens US and Israeli Corporations
============================================================

The Iranian Revolutionary Guard Corps has issued a formal warning to 18 international corporations, threatening attacks against facilities in Israel and the Gulf Cooperation Council nations.

### Key Points
- The IRGC identified 18 companies, including ExxonMobil, Chevron, BlackRock, and Lockheed Martin, as espionage entities supporting U.S. and Israeli military operations.
- The military statement specifically cited the use of artificial intelligence and internet communication technology services in designing operations and tracking assassination targets within Iran.
- Employees at the targeted institutions were advised to evacuate their workplaces immediately to ensure their personal safety.
- Residents living within a one-kilometer radius of the identified corporate offices and facilities across the region were also urged to relocate to safer areas.
- The list of targeted firms includes major global players in energy, finance, defense, and technology, such as JPMorgan, General Electric, Palantir, Oracle, Meta, and Nvidia.

### Why it Matters
This threat significantly escalates regional tensions by directly targeting the commercial infrastructure of multinational corporations operating in the Middle East. The warning creates substantial operational risks for global businesses and may force a reassessment of security protocols and regional presence for major firms in the energy and defense sectors.
