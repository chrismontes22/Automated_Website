Sunseeker Elite X7 Gen 2 Review: 24/7 Cutting with iToF Night Vision
============================================================

The Sunseeker Elite X7 Gen 2 is a wire-free robotic mower featuring advanced VSLAM 2.0 navigation and dual-disc cutting technology designed for residential lawns up to 0.75 acres.

### Key Points
- Uses AONavi technology to fuse RTK satellite positioning with VSLAM 2.0 vision for navigation under dense tree canopies.
- Features a 24/7 perception system utilizing binocular daylight cameras and iToF night-vision sensors to detect over 200 obstacle categories.
- Equipped with a floating dual-disc deck and independent suspension, allowing it to handle 70 percent slopes and uneven terrain.
- Operates at a quiet 60 dB(A) and includes an IPX5 rating for easy hose-down maintenance.
- Offers electronic height adjustment from 0.8 to 4.0 inches and app-based virtual boundary mapping, eliminating the need for perimeter wire installation.
- Priced at $2,499, with optional 4G anti-theft modules available for users requiring enhanced security features.

### Why it Matters
The Elite X7 Gen 2 represents a shift toward making high-end robotic mowing features accessible for standard-sized residential properties without requiring complex wire installations. By prioritizing vision-based navigation and terrain-adaptive hardware, it offers a practical solution for homeowners dealing with challenging landscapes like tree-heavy yards and steep slopes.
