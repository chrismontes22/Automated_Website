Why did Snap lay off 16% staff? #tech
============================================================

Snap Inc. is laying off approximately 1,000 employees, representing 16% of its global workforce, as the company pivots toward AI-driven operational efficiency to improve its long-term financial profitability.

### Key Points
- CEO Evan Spiegel announced the workforce reduction in an internal memo citing AI-assisted operations as a primary driver for the cuts.
- The layoffs affect roughly 1,000 staff members across the company's global offices.
- Snap aims to reduce operational costs and offset stagnant user growth by automating tasks previously handled by human employees.
- The decision follows significant pressure from activist investors and a declining stock price, prompting a shift toward leaner business operations.

### Why it Matters
This move signals a shift in the tech industry where AI is being used to justify large-scale corporate restructuring rather than just experimental pilot programs. It highlights how major social media platforms are increasingly prioritizing profitability and automation to navigate challenging economic conditions and investor demands.
