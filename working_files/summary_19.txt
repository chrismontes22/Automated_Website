NEAR Protocol surges 50% in 7 days, leads AI token rally
============================================================

NEAR Protocol’s native token surged 50% to a six-month high of $2.34, leading a broader rally in AI-themed cryptocurrencies amid increased trading volumes and positive market sentiment.

### Key Points
- NEAR Protocol reached a $2.34 valuation, marking a 115% gain over the last 90 days and daily trading volumes exceeding $1 billion.
- BitMEX co-founder Arthur Hayes publicly endorsed the token, categorizing it as part of his "holy trinity" of altcoins alongside HYPE and ZEC.
- The upcoming v2.13 network upgrade, scheduled for June 2026, will introduce dynamic resharding and post-quantum cryptographic security features.
- The protocol is expanding its AI-focused infrastructure by developing confidential treasuries and privacy tools specifically for autonomous AI agents.
- Increased investor interest has driven higher inflows into related financial products, such as the Bitwise Near Staking ETP.

### Why it Matters
This rally highlights the growing market correlation between AI-sector developments and blockchain infrastructure projects as investors seek exposure to decentralized artificial intelligence. The protocol's focus on scaling and security upgrades positions it as a significant player for institutional capital looking to integrate AI agents with blockchain-based financial systems.
