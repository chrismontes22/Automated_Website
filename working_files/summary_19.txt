A Security Camera Designed to Indicate When It's Not Watching (You)
============================================================

Xiaomi has introduced the Smart Camera 4 Zoom, a dual-lens home security device featuring a physical privacy mode that rotates the camera head downward to ensure recording has stopped.

### Key Points
- The device features an 8 MP ultra-wide lens and a 5 MP telephoto lens for comprehensive room coverage and detailed zooming.
- Users can activate privacy mode via a mobile app or manual adjustment, causing the camera to physically hide its lenses.
- All captured security footage is stored locally on the device rather than on cloud servers to enhance user data protection.
- The camera includes motion-tracking capabilities with full pan and tilt functionality for monitoring indoor spaces.
- The product is currently available exclusively for the Chinese market.

### Why it Matters
This design addresses growing consumer concerns regarding digital privacy and the potential for unauthorized remote access to home security feeds. By providing physical confirmation that recording has ceased, Xiaomi offers a tangible solution that may influence future industry standards for smart home hardware.
