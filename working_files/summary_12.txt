You can buy an Xbox Series X, PS5, and Switch 2 for less than the MSI Claw 8 EX AI+ — It feels to me like the handheld PC gaming era is over
============================================================

MSI has officially launched its Claw 8 EX AI+ gaming handheld, sparking significant debate due to its premium $1,799 price tag and high-performance Intel Arc G-series hardware.

### Key Points
- The MSI Claw 8 EX AI+ is scheduled for official release on June 23.
- Retail pricing is currently listed at $1,799 at Best Buy and MSI’s website, with Newegg transitioning from $1,699.
- The device features an 8-inch, 120Hz display and utilizes Intel’s new Arc G-series chips for improved gaming performance.
- MSI attributes the high cost to increased RAM and storage prices driven by global demand for AI data center components.
- Performance benchmarks show up to 42% higher 1080p performance compared to the ROG Ally X with AMD Z2 Extreme.

### Why it Matters
The high price point positions this handheld against full-featured gaming laptops and a combination of multiple major consoles, challenging the value proposition of portable PC gaming. This pricing strategy highlights how supply chain constraints for memory and storage are increasingly impacting consumer electronics costs across the industry.
