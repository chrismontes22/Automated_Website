ONEXStation is a Ryzen AI Max+ 395 mini PC with 128GB RAM and RBG lighting
============================================================

Chinese manufacturer One Netbook has launched the ONEXStation, a compact desktop workstation featuring an AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 processor and 128GB of high-bandwidth memory for $2,999.

### Key Points
- The ONEXStation features 128GB of LPDDR5x-8000 memory, allowing up to 96GB to be allocated as dedicated VRAM.
- The system is powered by an AMD Ryzen AI Max+ 395 processor with an adjustable thermal design power of up to 120 watts.
- Physical dimensions of the aluminum chassis are 186 x 193 x 62mm, housing two M.2 2280 PCIe 4.0 storage slots.
- Connectivity options include two USB4 ports, three USB 3.2 Type-A ports, HDMI, DisplayPort, and 2.5 GbE LAN.
- The cooling system utilizes three copper heat pipes, two turbo fans, and one downward-blowing fan to manage thermal output.

### Why it Matters
This device represents a shift for One Netbook from handheld gaming hardware into the high-performance mini workstation market. By offering massive memory capacity in a small form factor, the product targets users requiring significant local AI processing power despite its premium price point.
