UiPath (PATH), Deloitte Expand Collaboration to Integrate Test Cloud Into Ascend Platform
============================================================

UiPath and Deloitte have expanded their strategic partnership to integrate advanced agentic AI tools into the Ascend platform, aiming to automate software testing and accelerate global enterprise deployment cycles.

### Key Points
- UiPath is integrating its Test Cloud, Autopilot for Testers, and Agent Builder into Deloitte’s Ascend engineering platform.
- The collaboration utilizes agentic AI to autonomously generate, execute, and maintain software tests alongside human testers.
- The solution is designed to identify root causes of system failures and analyze large volumes of test data automatically.
- This integration aims to reduce manual testing tasks, improve overall test coverage, and increase the return on investment for automation.

### Why it Matters
This partnership highlights the growing industry shift toward autonomous software quality assurance driven by agentic AI. By streamlining development workflows, the integration helps global organizations reduce release times and operational costs while scaling their automation infrastructure.
