12 new security features coming to Android phones in 2026
============================================================

Google has announced 12 major security and privacy upgrades for Android 17, including automated banking scam protection, enhanced theft deterrents, and stricter app permission controls for user data.

### Key Points
- Android 17 will automatically block spoofed banking calls by verifying caller identity against supported financial institutions.
- New theft protection features, including biometric requirements for lost devices and default-enabled remote locking, will roll out globally.
- Chrome on Android will now scan APK files for malware before download to prevent the installation of malicious software.
- Users gain granular control over privacy with temporary precise location sharing and restricted access to specific contact list entries.
- Live Threat Detection will use AI to monitor for suspicious app behaviors, such as unauthorized SMS forwarding or hidden icon activity.
- The OS will introduce verification tools to confirm legitimate Android builds and protect one-time passwords from unauthorized app access.

### Why it Matters
These updates represent a significant shift toward proactive, AI-driven security that protects users from increasingly sophisticated financial scams and physical device theft. By integrating these safeguards directly into the operating system, Google aims to reduce the reliance on third-party security apps and improve overall platform integrity.
