Mony Group H1 Earnings Call Highlights
============================================================

Mony Group reported record first-half 2026 financial results, achieving a 6% revenue increase to GBP 227 million while expanding its AI-driven financial services and member-focused product ecosystem.

### Key Points
- Adjusted EBITDA rose 3% to GBP 75 million, marking the company's fifth consecutive period of interim growth.
- SuperSaveClub membership reached 2.5 million, an increase of 1 million users over the past year.
- The company launched new investment and digital insurance brokerage services to diversify its financial product offerings.
- Paid search cost inflation from Google PPC moderated to 8%, down significantly from the 20% levels seen previously.
- Management plans to return over GBP 90 million to shareholders through dividends and a GBP 25 million share buyback program.

### Why it Matters
Mony Group is successfully transitioning from a traditional price comparison site into a comprehensive financial companion by leveraging AI and internal product development. This strategic shift aims to increase customer lifetime value and deepen market penetration despite ongoing volatility in online advertising costs.
