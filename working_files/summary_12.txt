Zuvi ColorBox Review: A Hair Dye Printer That Struggles
============================================================

The Zuvi ColorBox, an at-home hair dyeing device designed to dispense custom color shades, fails to deliver a reliable user experience despite its innovative and compact design concept.

### Key Points
- The Zuvi ColorBox system includes a main dispensing machine, two base containers, and three primary color cartridges.
- The product packaging lacks essential components, including a wall adapter for the required USB-C charging cable.
- Included accessories, such as the mixing bowl, whisk, and tint brush, are described as flimsy or poorly sized for practical use.
- The kit provides only a single pair of gloves, which the reviewer notes is insufficient for a full hair dyeing process.

### Why it Matters
This device highlights the growing trend of bringing professional-grade beauty technology into the home environment. However, the poor quality of the included accessories and missing hardware suggest that the product currently lacks the refinement necessary for widespread consumer adoption.
