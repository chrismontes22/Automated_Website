Mission Produce (AVO) Insiders Just Bought Millions in Stock. Do They Know Something Wall Street Doesn’t?
============================================================

Mission Produce insiders, including Globalharvest Holdings Venture, have purchased millions of dollars in company stock following a recent decline in share prices and a challenging second quarter.

### Key Points
- Globalharvest Holdings Venture acquired over 1.9 million shares of Mission Produce between July 8 and July 9, 2026.
- Director Jay Pack purchased 40,000 shares for approximately $484,000 in late June.
- Mission Produce reported a 24% year-over-year revenue decline in Q2 due to a 36% drop in avocado prices.
- The company recently completed a $430 million acquisition of Calavo Growers, aiming for $25 million in annualized cost synergies.
- Management expects industry avocado volumes to increase by 5% to 10% in fiscal Q3 despite ongoing pricing pressures.

### Why it Matters
The significant insider buying suggests management and major stakeholders remain confident in a financial rebound despite recent margin compression and debt concerns from the Calavo acquisition. Investors are closely watching whether these cost synergies and volume projections can offset the risks posed by volatile avocado pricing and supply gluts.
