Adobe and Disney are teaming up to design the next generation of theme park rides with Foundry AI
============================================================

Walt Disney Imagineering is partnering with Adobe to integrate custom Firefly AI models into its design workflows for future theme park attractions, hotels, and cruise ship experiences.

### Key Points
- Disney will use Adobe’s Firefly Foundry to transform hand-drawn sketches into rendered 2D concept art and detailed 3D prototypes.
- The AI models are trained exclusively on Disney’s proprietary intellectual property, including franchises like Frozen, Moana, and Cars.
- The collaboration aims to accelerate development timelines and compress workloads while maintaining consistency with Disney’s established visual storytelling standards.
- Disney emphasizes that the technology is intended to support human creators rather than replace them in the design process.
- This partnership serves as a significant validation for Adobe’s enterprise AI strategy amid ongoing investor scrutiny regarding the company's generative AI business model.

### Why it Matters
This partnership provides Adobe with a high-profile endorsement for its Firefly Foundry service, helping to solidify its position in the competitive generative AI market. For Disney, the move represents a strategic effort to leverage AI for operational efficiency while maintaining strict control over its intellectual property rights.
