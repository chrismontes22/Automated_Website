This flagship Snapdragon X2 laptop from HP only just came out, and it's already $650 off in this anti-Prime Day sale
============================================================

HP is offering a $650 discount on the OmniBook Ultra 14, bringing the price of the premium Snapdragon-powered Windows laptop down to $1,249 during current retail sales events.

### Key Points
- The HP OmniBook Ultra 14 is currently discounted by $650, reducing its retail price to $1,249.
- The device features a Qualcomm Snapdragon X2 Plus SoC, 16GB of RAM, and a 512GB SSD.
- Key hardware includes a 1080p OLED touchscreen, a haptic trackpad, and a 1080p Windows Hello webcam.
- The laptop weighs 2.83 lbs and is housed in a premium brushed metal chassis.
- Connectivity options include three USB-C ports and a 3.5mm audio jack.

### Why it Matters
This promotion highlights the growing competitiveness of the Windows laptop market as retailers leverage major sales events to move high-end hardware. For consumers, the deal offers significant savings on a top-rated device that combines efficient ARM-based performance with premium design elements.
