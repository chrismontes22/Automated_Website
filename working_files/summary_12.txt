Ben McKenzie On Exposing “Scam” Of Cryptocurrency In His New Documentary And Trump Family Cashing In On Coins – Miami Film Festival
============================================================

Actor and economics graduate Ben McKenzie explores the risks of the cryptocurrency industry in his new documentary, arguing that the market functions primarily as a tool for manipulation.

### Key Points
- Ben McKenzie’s documentary, *Everyone Is Lying to You for Money*, premieres in New York and Los Angeles this Friday.
- The film characterizes the cryptocurrency market as a vehicle for gambling and crime, citing practices like wash trading and insider manipulation.
- McKenzie previously co-authored the 2023 book *Easy Money* with Jacob Silverman to detail his investigation into crypto fraud.
- The documentary critiques celebrity endorsements from figures like Matt Damon and Tom Brady, while highlighting the collapse of Sam Bankman-Fried’s FTX exchange.
- The film addresses the political shift of President Trump, who has embraced crypto ventures despite previously labeling the industry a scam.

### Why it Matters
This documentary highlights the growing tension between the burgeoning cryptocurrency industry and consumer protection advocates who warn of systemic financial risks. By framing the debate through a mix of humor and investigative journalism, McKenzie challenges the legitimacy of digital assets and the influence of high-profile political and celebrity endorsements.
