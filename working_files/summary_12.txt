Before iOS 27, Here's Everything You Should Know About iOS 26
============================================================

Apple announced the upcoming launch of iOS 27 with new AI features at its 2026 Worldwide Developers Conference, while highlighting the extensive capabilities introduced throughout the iOS 26 cycle.

### Key Points
- Apple unveiled iOS 27 at the June 2026 Worldwide Developers Conference, featuring new Siri AI capabilities scheduled for release this fall.
- The iOS 26 update, launched in September 2025, introduced the Liquid Glass design, marking the most significant visual shift since 2013.
- Key iOS 26 features include call and text screening, end-to-end encrypted RCS messaging, and enhanced privacy controls for Safari.
- Subsequent updates like iOS 26.4 added new emojis and video podcasts, while iOS 26.3.1 introduced support for Studio Display.
- Users can manage the Liquid Glass interface, utilize Adaptive Power for battery efficiency, and access new Apple Music tools like AutoMix.

### Why it Matters
The transition from iOS 26 to the AI-focused iOS 27 represents a major shift in Apple's mobile strategy toward integrated artificial intelligence. Understanding these updates helps users optimize their current device performance and prepare for the next generation of software features.
