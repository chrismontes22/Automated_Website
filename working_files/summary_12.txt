This Pocket Printer Turns Out Temporary Tattoos, Stickers, and Photos
============================================================

The INKWON Tag 4-in-1 pocket printer offers a portable, palm-sized solution for printing custom stickers, photos, temporary tattoos, and fabric heat transfers using a single compact, Bluetooth-enabled device.

### Key Points
- The device weighs 0.52 lbs and utilizes a magnetic ink cartridge system with self-suction paper feeding.
- It supports four distinct printing modes, including 600 dpi sticker production and skin-safe temporary tattoo printing.
- Users can perform precision fabric heat transfers on small items like collar tips, pockets, and socks.
- Connectivity is managed via Bluetooth 5.4 through a companion app compatible with both iOS and Android platforms.
- A single full charge provides enough power for 60 prints, matching the capacity of one ink cartridge.
- The product has raised over $138,000 in crowdfunding and is currently available for $169.

### Why it Matters
This device represents a shift toward miniaturized, multi-functional hardware that replaces bulky, single-purpose creative tools. By consolidating diverse printing capabilities into a pocket-sized form factor, it lowers the barrier for on-the-go personalization and physical media creation.
