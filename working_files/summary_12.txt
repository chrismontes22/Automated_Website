Apple to Upgrade These Two Devices With OLED Displays Later This Year
============================================================

Apple plans to transition its iPad mini and MacBook Pro lineups to OLED display technology, aiming to enhance visual performance across its portable device ecosystem in coming years.

### Key Points
- Apple will source new OLED panels from Samsung to replace existing LCD and mini-LED display technologies in future iPad mini and MacBook Pro models.
- The upcoming iPad mini is expected to feature an A19 or A20 Pro chip, a water-resistant design, and a vibration-based speaker system by late 2024.
- Future 14-inch and 16-inch MacBook Pro laptops are rumored to include M6 series chips, a thinner chassis, a touch screen, and a Dynamic Island.
- The transition to OLED technology is scheduled to expand to the iPad Air product line starting in 2025.

### Why it Matters
Upgrading to OLED displays provides Apple with a competitive advantage by offering superior color accuracy, higher contrast ratios, and deeper blacks compared to traditional LCD screens. This hardware shift aligns the company's entire mobile and laptop portfolio with premium display standards, potentially driving consumer upgrades and maintaining market dominance in the high-end electronics sector.
