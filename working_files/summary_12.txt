Mike Tyson, Tether CEO, Cathie Wood among speakers at Trump's 'most exclusive' crypto conference
============================================================

President Donald Trump is hosting an exclusive cryptocurrency conference at his Mar-a-Lago club featuring industry leaders like Tether CEO Paolo Ardoino and Ark Invest founder Cathie Wood.

### Key Points
- The private event is limited to the top 297 holders of the $TRUMP meme coin.
- Featured speakers include Mike Tyson, Tim Draper, Tony Robbins, and Alchemy CEO Nikil Viswanathan.
- Discussions will cover financial inclusion, the U.S. dollar, and the intersection of artificial intelligence with digital assets.
- The conference aligns with President Trump’s ongoing policy push to support the cryptocurrency industry since his 2025 return to the White House.
- The $TRUMP and $MELANIA tokens have experienced significant volatility, with both assets declining over 97% from their respective peak valuations.

### Why it Matters
This event highlights the growing intersection between presidential policy and speculative digital assets, raising ongoing questions regarding potential conflicts of interest. It underscores the administration's active efforts to court the crypto sector while drawing scrutiny over the financial ties between government officials and volatile meme coin markets.
