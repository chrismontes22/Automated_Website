One company just teased the E Ink phone of my dreams, and it's fully powered by Android
============================================================

Bigme has announced the upcoming HiBreak Dual, a unique smartphone featuring both a color E Ink display and an LCD panel, aiming to combine eye-friendly reading with modern performance.

### Key Points
- The Bigme HiBreak Dual is marketed as the world's first smartphone to integrate both color E Ink and LCD screen technologies.
- Bigme devices typically run on the Android operating system and provide full access to the Google Play Store for standard app usage.
- The company previously released the HiBreak Pro Color, which features a 42Hz refresh rate, currently the fastest E Ink display available on the market.
- While specific hardware specifications remain unconfirmed, Bigme is expected to provide full details regarding the device's capabilities in the coming days.

### Why it Matters
This dual-screen approach could bridge the gap between the superior battery life and readability of E Ink and the high-speed performance of traditional LCD or OLED displays. If successful, the device may offer a viable alternative for users seeking a more eye-friendly smartphone experience without sacrificing the functionality of modern Android applications.
