iOS 26.5 added new features to three popular iPhone apps
============================================================

Apple has released iOS 26.5, introducing end-to-end encrypted RCS messaging, personalized location suggestions in Apple Maps, and a new flexible annual subscription payment model for App Store developers.

### Key Points
- Apple introduced beta end-to-end encryption for RCS messaging within the Messages app to improve cross-platform communication security.
- The Apple Maps search interface now features "Suggested Places" based on trending nearby locations and individual user search history.
- Developers can now offer a "monthly with 12-month commitment" subscription plan, allowing users to pay for annual services in 12 installments.
- The new subscription payment option is currently available globally, excluding the United States and Singapore.

### Why it Matters
These updates reflect Apple's ongoing efforts to enhance user experience through improved messaging standards and more flexible digital payment options. By integrating these features, Apple is balancing platform utility with new monetization strategies that could influence how developers structure their subscription services.
