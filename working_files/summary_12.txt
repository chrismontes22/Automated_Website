iPhone 17 Pro Will Make Sports History This Weekend
============================================================

Apple will broadcast this Saturday’s Major League Soccer match between the LA Galaxy and Houston Dynamo FC using only the iPhone 17 Pro for all live camera footage.

### Key Points
- The match takes place on Saturday, May 23, at 7:30 p.m. Pacific Time and will be available to stream on Apple TV.
- This event marks the first time a major professional sporting event is broadcast entirely using iPhone hardware instead of traditional studio cameras.
- Multiple iPhone 17 Pro devices will be positioned throughout the stadium to capture unique angles, player warmups, and in-net perspectives.
- Apple previously utilized the iPhone 17 Pro for select footage during a Boston Red Sox versus Detroit Tigers baseball game last year.
- Starting with the 2026 season, Apple TV subscribers will gain access to MLS matches without requiring an additional MLS Season Pass subscription.

### Why it Matters
This broadcast represents a significant technological milestone for mobile cinematography in professional sports production. By replacing traditional broadcast equipment with consumer hardware, Apple is demonstrating the professional-grade capabilities of its latest devices to a global audience.
