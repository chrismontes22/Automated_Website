This Surface is the only laptop that supports Windows 11's best new feature
============================================================

Microsoft has launched the Surface Laptop 8, featuring the new Snapdragon X2 Elite chip and exclusive support for Windows 11’s advanced haptic feedback technology for improved user interaction.

### Key Points
- The Surface Laptop 8 is powered by the Snapdragon X2 Elite processor and Qualcomm Adreno GPU.
- It is the first and currently only laptop to support Windows 11’s advanced haptics, providing tactile feedback through the touchpad.
- The device includes 16GB of RAM and a 512GB SSD as standard hardware specifications.
- Third-party applications, including Filmora and Affinity, are beginning to integrate support for the new haptic API.
- The consumer model is recommended over the Intel-based business version for general users seeking the latest hardware features.

### Why it Matters
The introduction of advanced haptics represents a shift toward more tactile, intuitive user interfaces in the Windows ecosystem. By setting a new hardware standard, Microsoft is encouraging developers to integrate physical feedback into software, potentially changing how users interact with their PCs.
