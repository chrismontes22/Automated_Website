President's Family Crypto Firm Expected to Get Federal Banking Privileges...
============================================================

World Liberty Financial, a cryptocurrency platform co-founded by President Donald Trump and his family, is expected to receive a national trust bank charter from the federal government soon.

### Key Points
- World Liberty Financial applied for a national trust bank charter with the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) on January 5.
- The charter would allow the firm to issue its "USD1" stablecoin directly to the public and settle financial transactions without using intermediary institutions.
- OCC head Jonathan Gould has granted approximately a dozen similar crypto charters and is widely expected to approve the Trump family's application.
- President Trump holds a 70% stake in an LLC that owns a significant portion of the company, which has already generated millions in earnings.
- Critics and Democratic lawmakers argue the approval creates an unprecedented conflict of interest by providing federal backing to a private presidential business.

### Why it Matters
Granting a federal bank charter to a company owned by the president’s family could fundamentally alter the intersection of private business and government oversight. This decision may establish a new precedent for how presidential assets interact with federal regulatory bodies and influence domestic financial markets.
