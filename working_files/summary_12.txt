Will Solana (SOL) Make You a Millionaire?
============================================================

Solana is experiencing a resurgence in institutional adoption and developer activity, driven by record-breaking economic throughput and the upcoming Alpenglow network upgrade scheduled for the third quarter of 2026.

### Key Points
- Solana processed $1.1 trillion in economic activity during Q1 2026, with stablecoin volume reaching $832.7 billion.
- Major financial institutions including Visa, PayPal, Stripe, and Western Union are currently utilizing the network for production payment workflows.
- Active developer count on the network grew 83% year-over-year, surpassing Ethereum for the first time as of March 2026.
- The Alpenglow upgrade aims to reduce transaction finality from 12.8 seconds to 150 milliseconds, significantly increasing network speed.
- U.S. regulators classified Solana as a digital commodity in March 2026, providing legal certainty that helped drive $113 million in spot ETF inflows during May.

### Why it Matters
The transition of Solana from a speculative asset to essential financial infrastructure for global payment giants signals a shift toward long-term institutional viability. By securing its status as a regulated commodity and scaling its technical capacity, the network is positioning itself to capture a larger share of the global digital asset market.
