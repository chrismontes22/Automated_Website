If You're Looking to Buy a Smart Bird Feeder, Don't Make My Mistake
============================================================

Smart bird feeder owners can effectively deter squirrels from raiding their seed supplies by strategically relocating equipment, installing physical barriers, and incorporating spicy additives into their birdseed mixes.

### Key Points
- Relocate feeders away from trees and wooden fences to prevent squirrels from jumping or tight-rope walking onto the device.
- Install a dedicated bird feeder pole equipped with a squirrel shield to physically block climbing attempts.
- Use a slinky on the feeder pole as a low-cost, alternative deterrent method.
- Mix cayenne pepper into birdseed to discourage squirrels, as birds are generally unaffected by the spice.
- The Kiwibit Bird Feeder 2 is a top-rated smart model featuring solar panels and high-quality camera capabilities.

### Why it Matters
Preventing squirrel infestations protects expensive smart bird feeder hardware and prevents pests from potentially entering residential attics. Implementing these deterrents ensures that bird-watching technology remains focused on avian activity rather than managing unwanted wildlife.
