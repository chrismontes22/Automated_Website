I use these 5 simple ‘ChatGPT codes’ every day — and they instantly improve my results
============================================================

ChatGPT users can improve their interaction efficiency and output quality by utilizing specific prompt codes designed to refine the chatbot's tone, complexity, and summarization capabilities for various tasks.

### Key Points
- ELI5 (Explain Like I’m 5) simplifies complex topics into easy-to-understand concepts for beginners.
- TLDR (Too Long; Didn’t Read) generates concise summaries from long, dense blocks of text.
- Jargonize transforms simple language into complex, corporate-style phrasing for specific stylistic needs.
- Humanize adjusts the tone of written messages to sound more natural, empathetic, and less passive-aggressive.
- The Feynman Technique uses a four-step learning loop to help users master difficult subjects through simplification and self-testing.

### Why it Matters
These prompt shortcuts allow users to bypass generic AI responses and tailor output to their specific needs, saving significant time on daily tasks. Mastering these commands transforms ChatGPT from a basic search tool into a more precise and effective personal assistant.
