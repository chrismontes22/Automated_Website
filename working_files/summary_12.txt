Google's Nest Audio and Mini are officially discontinued, but here's what that actually means
============================================================

Google has officially launched the new Google Home Speaker, a $100 smart device featuring Gemini integration, while simultaneously discontinuing production of the older Nest Mini and Nest Audio models.

### Key Points
- The Google Home Speaker is priced at $100 and is available in four different color options.
- Hardware specifications include a 2.0GHz quad-core processor with an NPU, 1GB of RAM, and 4GB of storage.
- The device features a 58 mm full-range driver, three far-field microphones, Wi-Fi 6, and Bluetooth 5.4 connectivity.
- Google has officially ceased production of the Nest Mini and Nest Audio speakers.
- Existing Nest Mini and Nest Audio devices will remain functional, though future software support will be limited.

### Why it Matters
The release marks Google's first major update to its smart speaker lineup in years, signaling a shift toward AI-integrated hardware powered by Gemini. This transition forces a consolidation of the company's smart home ecosystem as older legacy products are phased out in favor of more capable, modern hardware.
