Our favorite Android gaming controller just crashed to $68 during Amazon's Big Spring Sale - but you're running out of time
============================================================

Amazon is offering a 15% discount on the GameSir G8 Plus Bluetooth controller during its Big Spring Sale, reducing the price of the popular mobile gaming accessory to $67.99.

**Key points**
- The GameSir G8 Plus is currently available for $67.99, down from its standard retail price of $79.99.
- Key features include Bluetooth connectivity, hall effect thumb-sticks, customizable back paddles, and full-size triggers.
- The controller is compatible with a wide range of devices, including Android phones, iOS devices, and the Nintendo Switch.
- Notable omissions include a dedicated headphone jack, and users with large camera islands on their phones may experience physical clearance issues.

**Why it matters**
This discount provides a more accessible entry point for mobile gamers looking to upgrade their hardware with a highly-rated, ergonomic controller. As mobile gaming continues to grow, high-quality peripherals like the G8 Plus are becoming essential for users seeking console-like precision on portable devices.
