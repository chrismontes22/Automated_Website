Hyperliquid Crypto: Why HYPE Became One of the Fastest-Growing DeFi Tokens in 2026
============================================================

Hyperliquid has emerged as a leading decentralized exchange in 2026, utilizing its own Layer 1 blockchain to provide high-speed perpetual futures trading and an integrated on-chain order book.

### Key Points
- The platform operates on a custom Layer 1 blockchain, enabling native execution of trades, liquidations, and funding without external layers.
- HYPE token utility includes network staking, governance voting rights, and reduced transaction fees within the HyperEVM ecosystem.
- A deflationary "buyback-and-burn" mechanism uses platform trading fees to reduce the total HYPE supply over time.
- Founded by Jeff Yan and a core team known as "Iliensinc," the project launched without venture capital funding to prioritize community-driven growth.
- The exchange supports diverse financial products, including perpetual futures, spot trading, and emerging markets for real-world assets.

### Why it Matters
Hyperliquid represents a significant shift in decentralized finance by offering the performance and liquidity of centralized exchanges while maintaining user self-custody. Its success highlights a growing market demand for transparent, high-speed trading infrastructure that rewards active participants through direct token utility.
