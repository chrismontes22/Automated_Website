There’s never been a better time to grab a new Google TV launcher
============================================================

Google TV users are increasingly turning to third-party launchers to bypass intrusive advertisements, sponsored content, and forced UI updates that prioritize promotional material over user-selected streaming applications.

### Key Points
- Google TV’s latest software update has drawn criticism for integrating YouTube Shorts and prominent sponsored advertisements directly onto the home screen.
- Third-party launchers like Projectivy, Dispatch, AT4K, Arc, and FLauncher allow users to remove ads and customize their interface layout.
- Projectivy offers extensive customization, including adjustable text sizes, thumbnail shortcuts, and granular control over content recommendations.
- Dispatch integrates Plex library content, while AT4K and FLauncher provide lightweight, minimalist alternatives for faster navigation.
- Replacing the default launcher typically involves installing an app from the Play Store or sideloading an APK and granting accessibility permissions.

### Why it Matters
The shift toward third-party launchers highlights a growing consumer demand for control over smart TV interfaces that are increasingly cluttered with unwanted advertisements. By reclaiming the home screen, users can improve system performance and prioritize their own content libraries over Google's promotional algorithms.
