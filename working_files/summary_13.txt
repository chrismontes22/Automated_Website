This external SSD is my must-have iPhone 17 Pro accessory — here's why
============================================================

The PD20M external SSD offers a portable, magnetic storage solution for smartphones, featuring a slim 8.2mm design and high-speed data transfer capabilities suitable for recording 4K video content.

### Key Points
- The PD20M SSD utilizes a USB 3.2 Gen2x2 interface, delivering tested read speeds of 1,750MB/s and write speeds of 1,590MB/s.
- Weighing 40g and measuring 8.2mm thick, the drive features a magnetic attachment system compatible with iPhones and Android devices using magnetic cases.
- The 2TB model is currently priced at $233 on Amazon, reflecting a $60 price increase since its launch two years ago.
- The device includes a rubber base to prevent phone surface damage and a flat cable for direct connectivity during mobile media capture.
- The drive lacks ingress protection against dust or water, making it less suitable for rugged outdoor environments compared to specialized alternatives.

### Why it Matters
This drive provides a practical solution for mobile users who frequently run out of internal storage while recording high-resolution video. Its magnetic integration simplifies the workflow for content creators by eliminating the need for bulky external mounting hardware.
