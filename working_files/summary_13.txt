I switched away from Cloudflare and Quad9 after finding this alternative
============================================================

NextDNS provides users with advanced network transparency and granular filtering controls, offering a customizable alternative to the simplified, one-size-fits-all approach used by popular services like Cloudflare and Quad9.

### Key Points
- NextDNS features an analytics dashboard that tracks domain requests, blocked traffic, and specific device activity in real time.
- Users can implement custom blocklists to filter ads, malware, and telemetry data from specific platforms like Microsoft or Samsung.
- The service supports multiple configuration profiles, allowing for different security and filtering levels across various devices.
- A free tier is available, while a Pro subscription offering unlimited queries costs $2 per month or $20 per year.
- Configuration management is supported through the NextDNS Manager app, which is available for Android and other operating systems.

### Why it Matters
The shift toward NextDNS highlights a growing demand for network visibility and personalized privacy controls among power users who find standard DNS providers too restrictive. By offering detailed logs and custom filtering, the service empowers individuals to manage their digital footprint and troubleshoot connectivity issues more effectively than "black box" alternatives.
