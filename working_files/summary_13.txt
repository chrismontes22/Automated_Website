Nintendo’s Leak Problem Is Bigger Than Ever: ‘I Can Promise You That Nintendo Is Absolutely Furious’
============================================================

**Nintendo faces significant internal security challenges as prominent leaker NateTheHate reveals detailed release plans for upcoming Switch 2 software, including a new Star Fox title and Zelda remake.**

**Key points**
- Podcaster NateTheHate leaked a roadmap for Nintendo’s upcoming software, including a Star Fox game and an Ocarina of Time remake.
- The leaks suggest a new 3D Mario title is not expected to launch until 2027.
- Reports indicate a potential Zelda-themed special edition Switch 2 console may arrive later this year.
- Former Nintendo of America marketer Kit Ellis stated that the company is frustrated by the inability to contain these high-level leaks.
- Nintendo is reportedly investigating its internal chain of custody and third-party partners to identify the source of the unauthorized information.

**Why it matters**
Nintendo relies heavily on the element of surprise to drive marketing and consumer excitement for its hardware and software releases. These persistent leaks undermine the company's strategic control and could potentially impact market expectations for the upcoming Switch 2 console.
