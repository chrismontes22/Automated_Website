Samsung Galaxy Z Fold8 goes wide, Fold8 Ultra aims for the foldable crown
============================================================

Samsung has officially expanded its foldable smartphone lineup with the introduction of the Galaxy Z Fold8 and the high-end Galaxy Z Fold8 Ultra, both featuring the Snapdragon 8 Elite.

### Key Points
- The Galaxy Z Fold8 features a 5.5-inch cover display and a 7.6-inch inner screen, while the Ultra model includes a 200MP main camera and a 5,000mAh Silicon-Carbon battery.
- Both devices are powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy chipset and run on Android 17 with One UI 9.
- New Flex Titanium technology has been integrated into the hinge and display structure to improve durability and reduce the visibility of the center crease.
- The Ultra model supports 45W fast charging and includes advanced camera features like 8K video recording and Cine LUTs.
- Pre-orders for both models are open now, with pricing starting at $1,899.99 for the standard Z Fold8.

### Why it Matters
These new models represent a strategic diversification of Samsung’s foldable portfolio by offering distinct form factors and hardware capabilities to target different user needs. The introduction of advanced battery technology and refined hinge materials signals a push toward greater hardware reliability and performance in the competitive premium smartphone market.
