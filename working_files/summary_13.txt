The Sculptural Speaker Concept That Sounds Good From Every Spot in the Room
============================================================

Canadian audio brand Mirage has partnered with designer Andrea Ponti to launch the Onda, a sculptural wireless speaker featuring omnidirectional sound technology for uniform room-filling audio performance.

### Key Points
- The Mirage Onda utilizes omnipolar technology to radiate sound in all directions, eliminating the need for a fixed listening sweet spot.
- The acoustic architecture includes four base-mounted woofers, an upward-facing midrange driver, and a dedicated tweeter with diffusers.
- Designed by Andrea Ponti, the speaker features a monolithic silhouette with polished aluminum pillars and a removable magnetic grille.
- The device includes a backlit touch interface and is available in three colorways: sand, charcoal, and all-black.
- Mirage, a brand known for its 1987 bipolar speaker innovation, aims to blend high-fidelity heritage with modern interior design aesthetics.

### Why it Matters
This collaboration highlights a growing market trend where high-end audio manufacturers prioritize sculptural industrial design to compete with lifestyle-focused home decor. By integrating advanced omnidirectional acoustics into a minimalist form, the Onda challenges the industry standard of directional speakers that often require specific room placement to achieve optimal sound quality.
