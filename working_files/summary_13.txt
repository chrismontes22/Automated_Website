The MacRumors Show: Apple Announces WWDC 2026
============================================================

Apple has announced its 37th annual Worldwide Developers Conference, where the company plans to unveil a major Siri overhaul alongside iOS 27 and other next-generation operating system updates.

**Key points**
- WWDC 2026 will be held as a primarily online event, with a limited in-person component at Apple Park in Cupertino.
- The revamped Siri will function as an AI chatbot, featuring a standalone app, Dynamic Island integration, and a new "Extensions" system for third-party AI support.
- Apple’s new AI features are built on Foundation Models developed through a partnership with Google using Gemini technology.
- iOS 27 and macOS 27 will focus on performance and stability, with macOS 27 officially ending support for all Intel-based Mac models.
- The conference will include over 100 video sessions and interactive labs for developers to engage with Apple engineers and designers.

**Why it matters: **
This event represents a critical turning point for Apple as it attempts to modernize its long-struggling Siri assistant to compete with advanced generative AI platforms. The shift toward a "Snow Leopard" style stability update suggests the company is prioritizing system performance and infrastructure to support these complex new AI capabilities.
