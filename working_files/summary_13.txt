The big AI companies are going to see their margins disappear
============================================================

The UK Government Commercial Agency has launched the £2.9 billion DIPS 2 defense technology framework, specifically inviting smaller suppliers to compete alongside traditional large-scale defense contractors for government contracts.

### Key Points
- The DIPS 2 framework is valued at £2.9 billion for defense technology procurement.
- The initiative aims to diversify the supply chain by moving beyond established, large-name defense suppliers.
- The Government Commercial Agency is actively encouraging participation from smaller, specialized technology firms.
- The framework is designed to support broader access to Ministry of Defence procurement opportunities.

### Why it Matters
This initiative signals a strategic shift in public sector procurement, aiming to foster innovation by lowering barriers for smaller technology companies. By diversifying the supplier base, the government hopes to increase competition and access more agile solutions for complex defense requirements.
