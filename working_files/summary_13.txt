‘More bang for your buck’: 7 easy ways to boost your MacBook Neo’s performance for free
============================================================

Maximize the performance of your $599 MacBook Neo by utilizing these practical software adjustments and hardware tips to ensure your budget-friendly laptop runs as efficiently as possible.

### Key Points
- Manage storage by offloading files to external SSDs or using macOS optimization tools to prevent system sluggishness.
- Regularly restart your Mac to clear temporary memory, cache files, and background processes that consume system resources.
- Use the USB-C port closest to the hinge for data transfers, as it supports 5Gbps speeds compared to the 480 Mbps limit of the front port.
- Disable unnecessary login items and background app activity in System Settings to reduce the strain on your processor.
- Reduce visual effects like transparency and motion in the Accessibility menu to lower the load on the graphics processor.
- Consider using a cooling pad to prevent thermal throttling, as the fanless MacBook Neo can slow down during demanding tasks.

### Why it Matters
Optimizing these settings allows users to extract maximum value from the entry-level MacBook Neo, bridging the gap between budget hardware and high-end performance. These maintenance habits help extend the longevity of the device while ensuring a smoother experience for everyday computing tasks.
