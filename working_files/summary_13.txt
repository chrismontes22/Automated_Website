Breaking Content & SEO Silos To Build Entity Authority in AI Search
============================================================

To improve search visibility in the era of AI-driven search, content and SEO teams must collaborate by aligning their strategies around shared entity authority and structured content frameworks.

### Key Points
- Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) requires brands to improve content quality, semantic associations, and technical foundations to secure AI citations.
- Entities are distinct, interconnected concepts that search systems use to evaluate a brand’s credibility, relationships, and external corroboration.
- A four-phase workflow involves conducting entity research, identifying content gaps, executing coordinated technical and creative plans, and refining strategies based on performance.
- SEO teams contribute by implementing schema markup, internal linking, and backlink strategies, while content teams provide comprehensive, full-funnel assets.
- Success is measured by tracking AI Overview citations, brand mentions in AI responses, and organic ranking improvements for specific entity clusters.

### Why it Matters
Aligning content and SEO teams around entity authority is essential for maintaining visibility as AI search surfaces increasingly prioritize concentrated, well-corroborated expertise. Brands that fail to integrate these disciplines risk losing critical retrieval opportunities and AI-generated citations to more cohesive competitors.
