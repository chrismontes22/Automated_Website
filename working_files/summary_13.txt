11 Smart Devices I’m Still Using at Home Even After My Testing Is Done
============================================================

These selected smart home devices, ranging from security sensors to kitchen appliances, represent the most reliable and functional technology currently integrated into a modern, automated living space.

### Key Points
- The $8 Ikea smart plug offers Matter support and a physical remote, making it an affordable choice for managing router reboots.
- SwitchBot’s air quality monitor tracks temperature, humidity, and CO2 levels, while the company’s leak detector provides essential flood alerts.
- The Schlage Sense Pro smart lock features UWB technology for hands-free entry and upcoming Aliro certification for broader mobile wallet compatibility.
- Google’s Nest Cam floodlight provides high-end outdoor security with advanced AI object detection and a flexible magnetic mounting base.
- The Kelvin smart heater functions as a space-saving, wall-mounted radiant unit that allows for remote scheduling and energy consumption tracking.
- Aqara’s P100 security sensor detects vibration and tilting without requiring a traditional two-part contact setup.

### Why it Matters
Integrating these specific devices demonstrates how modern smart home technology can improve daily convenience, security, and energy efficiency through cross-platform compatibility. By selecting products that support standards like Matter, users can build a cohesive ecosystem that functions reliably across different mobile operating systems and hardware brands.
