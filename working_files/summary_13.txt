Korean Brand BAENUE Finally Built a Lamp That Knows What Time It Is
============================================================

Lighting brand BAENUE, a subsidiary of Baelux, has launched a new line of premium lamps featuring patented Dim2Amber technology designed to regulate circadian rhythms by shifting light spectrums.

### Key Points
- BAENUE utilizes patented Dim2Amber technology to automatically shift light from crisp, cool tones to warm, amber hues as brightness is reduced.
- The flagship product, The New Lamp, features a golden ratio design, wireless charging, and high color rendering index (CRI) lighting.
- Founder Dr. Jinwoo Bae, an MIT-trained engineer, designed the lamps to eliminate micro-flicker, a common cause of eye strain in standard LED lighting.
- The product lineup includes the stationary flagship desk lamp and the portable MINI, with prices ranging from approximately $575 to $635.
- The brand officially debuted its consumer-facing technology at the 3 Days of Design 2026 event held in Copenhagen.

### Why it Matters
BAENUE represents a shift in the lighting industry by prioritizing photobiology and human wellness over purely aesthetic design. By bringing industrial-grade LED engineering into the home, the company challenges consumers to consider how artificial light quality directly impacts sleep cycles and long-term eye health.
