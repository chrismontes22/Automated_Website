Is the Motorola Razr 2026 waterproof?
============================================================

The new Motorola Razr 2026 series, including the Razr, Plus, and Ultra models, features an IP48 certification providing water resistance for up to 30 minutes in fresh water.

### Key Points
- The Razr 2026, Razr Plus 2026, and Razr Ultra 2026 all carry an IP48 ingress protection rating.
- Devices can withstand submersion in up to 1.5 meters of fresh water for a maximum of 30 minutes.
- The IP48 rating does not protect against salt water, chlorinated pool water, or solid particles smaller than 1mm.
- The new flip-style lineup is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7450X processor and runs Android 16.
- Motorola also officially released its first book-style foldable, the Razr Fold, following its debut at CES 2026.

### Why it Matters
- This IP48 rating offers essential protection against accidental spills and rain, though it remains less durable than the dust-tight ratings found on many traditional slab-style smartphones. Consumers should note that water resistance naturally degrades over time, requiring careful handling of these premium foldable devices.
