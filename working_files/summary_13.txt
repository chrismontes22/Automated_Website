This ASUS Vivobook 16 OLED deal packs 32GB of RAM, a 3K display, and Intel’s latest AI chip for less than you’d expect
============================================================

The ASUS Vivobook 16 OLED is currently discounted to $1,159, offering high-end features like an Intel Core Ultra 7 processor and a 3K display in a portable chassis.

### Key Points
- The laptop features an Intel Core Ultra 7 255H processor, 32GB of LPDDR5X RAM, and a 1TB PCIe 4.0 SSD.
- The 16-inch Lumina OLED display offers a 2880×1800 resolution with HDR contrast and deep blacks.
- The device weighs 3.31 pounds, measures 0.55 inches thick, and includes a 75Wh battery rated for 16 hours of runtime.
- Integrated hardware includes an AI Boost NPU, a dedicated Copilot key, Wi-Fi 7 support, and Harman Kardon-tuned Dolby Atmos audio.
- The current sale price of $1,159 represents a $440 reduction from the original $1,599 retail price.

### Why it Matters
This deal provides flagship-level performance at a time when rising component costs and AI-centric hardware requirements are driving up prices across the broader PC market. Securing this hardware now offers consumers a future-proofed machine that remains competitive against anticipated industry-wide price hikes through 2027.
