I found 5 Google TV features hiding in plain sight that completely changed how I use the platform
============================================================

Google TV users can optimize their smart TV experience by utilizing hidden settings, such as Apps-Only mode, developer animation controls, and remote installation features to improve overall performance.

### Key Points
- Enable Apps-Only mode in settings to remove personalized recommendations and sponsored content from the home screen.
- Access the hidden Developer Options menu by clicking the Android TV OS Build version seven times to adjust animation scales for a snappier interface.
- Install apps remotely by selecting your Google TV device from the Google Play Store on a smartphone or web browser.
- Use the wireless casting feature to mirror content from Android, iOS, Windows, or macOS devices directly to the television screen.
- Perform a full system restart by holding the remote's power button to resolve performance slowdowns without unplugging the hardware.

### Why it Matters
These hidden features allow users to reclaim storage space and significantly improve the responsiveness of their Google TV interface. Mastering these settings transforms the platform from a cluttered advertising hub into a streamlined, personalized entertainment system.
