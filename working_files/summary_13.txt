Apple's MacBook Neo breaks the rules
============================================================

The new Apple MacBook Neo offers a budget-friendly entry point into the Mac ecosystem, delivering capable performance for daily productivity tasks at a starting price of $599.

**Key points**
- The MacBook Neo is powered by the Apple A18 Pro processor, the same chip found in the iPhone 16 Pro.
- It features a 13-inch LED-backlit IPS display, 8GB of RAM, and a choice of 256GB or 512GB SSD storage.
- The device includes two USB-C ports, Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 6, and a battery life averaging over 12 hours.
- Performance is sufficient for web browsing, word processing, and light image editing, though it struggles with intensive video rendering.
- The laptop is available in four colors, including Citrus, Blush, Indigo, and silver, but lacks keyboard backlighting.

**Why it matters: **
The MacBook Neo provides a high-quality, affordable alternative for students and office workers who need a reliable secondary machine without the premium cost of the Pro lineup. It signals Apple's renewed focus on the entry-level market by leveraging mobile-derived silicon to maintain a competitive price point.
