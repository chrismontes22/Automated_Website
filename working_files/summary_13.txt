TCL Unveils Cheap 27-inch Gaming Monitor With Mini LED Panel And 320 Hz
============================================================

TCL has launched the Thunderbird 27Q6A Pro, a 27-inch gaming monitor featuring a 320 Hz refresh rate, Mini LED backlighting, and professional-grade color accuracy for competitive eSports players.

**Key points**
- The 27-inch Fast IPS display offers 2K QHD resolution with 180 local dimming zones and 800 nits peak brightness.
- Performance specifications include a 320 Hz refresh rate, 1ms GtG response time, and support for NVIDIA G-Sync and AMD FreeSync Premium.
- Color-critical work is supported by 95% DCI-P3 coverage and factory-calibrated Delta E<2 accuracy.
- A specialized software mode allows users to shrink the active display area to 24.5 inches for tournament-standard competitive play.
- The monitor includes HDMI 2.1 and DisplayPort 1.4 connectivity with a fully adjustable ergonomic stand.
- Currently available in China for 1,665 yuan, the monitor is expected to retail between $249 and $299 if it reaches the U.S. market.

**Why it matters: **
The Thunderbird 27Q6A Pro challenges the premium monitor market by offering high-end Mini LED technology and extreme refresh rates at a mid-range price point. If released internationally, this aggressive pricing could force established competitors to lower costs or improve specifications for their own high-performance gaming displays.
