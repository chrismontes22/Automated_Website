Health Apps Know You’re Stressed, HealingFit TWS Helps You Act on It
============================================================

MobiFren’s HealingFit TWS earbuds integrate Transcranial Electrical Stimulation technology to provide users with actionable wellness interventions for sleep, focus, and stress relief through an open-access Bluetooth interface.

### Key Points
- The HealingFit TWS earbuds feature a Magnetic TES Link cable that enables non-invasive microcurrent stimulation across 15 adjustable intensity levels.
- Three dedicated wellness modes—Sleep, Healing, and Study—combine electrical stimulation with binaural audio to support nervous system regulation.
- The device utilizes an open Bluetooth LE interface (UUID 0xBE00), allowing third-party health platforms to trigger wellness sessions without proprietary licensing fees.
- Hardware specifications include Bluetooth 6.0, Hybrid ANC, and 6.5 hours of playback, with a design that received a 2026 CES Innovation Award.
- MobiFren has secured patent allowances in the U.S., Korea, and Japan for the core architecture, which uses 18 standard components for the TES circuit.

### Why it Matters
This technology bridges the gap between health-tracking platforms that identify stress or sleep issues and the lack of hardware tools available to address those signals in real time. By offering an open API, MobiFren aims to turn standard earbuds into a universal intervention layer for major health ecosystems like Apple, Google, and Samsung.
