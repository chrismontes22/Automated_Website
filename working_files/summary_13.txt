OpenAI upgrading ChatGPT and Codex with new GPT-5.6 models in limited release
============================================================

OpenAI has unveiled its new GPT-5.6 model series, featuring three distinct capability tiers named Sol, Terra, and Luna, currently available through a limited preview for select trusted partners.

### Key Points
- The GPT-5.6 series includes Sol (flagship), Terra (balanced), and Luna (fast and affordable) models.
- New reasoning features include "max" effort for deep thinking and "ultra" mode utilizing subagents for complex tasks.
- OpenAI is coordinating with the U.S. government to conduct a limited preview before a broader public release.
- Pricing is set per million tokens, ranging from $1 to $5 for input and $6 to $30 for output.
- The update introduces improved prompt caching with explicit breakpoints and a 30-minute minimum cache life.
- The models demonstrate performance advancements in coding, biology, and cybersecurity applications.

### Why it Matters
This release marks a shift in OpenAI’s deployment strategy by prioritizing government coordination and a phased rollout to ensure safety and security. The new tiered naming system and specialized reasoning modes provide developers with more granular control over balancing computational costs and model performance.
