This $349 cordless car-wash machine was designed for drivers without a driveway
============================================================

The Linyo H1 is a portable, closed-loop car cleaning system designed for urban residents, allowing users to wash and vacuum vehicles in shared parking spaces without external water access.

### Key Points
- The H1 uses "Lift & Extract" technology to remove grime with a rotating roller, preventing paint scratches caused by traditional wash mitts.
- Its closed-loop system consumes only 0.5 gallons of water per wash, which is 80 times less than a standard garden hose.
- The device functions as both a car washer and a 16,000 Pa handheld vacuum, weighing 21 pounds for easy transport in a vehicle trunk.
- A 5,000mAh battery supports two full exterior washes per charge, with a hybrid power option available for continuous use.
- The system applies a protective nano-ceramic coating during the 20-minute wash cycle, making it suitable for matte wraps and sensitive EV paint.
- Pricing starts at $349 during the pre-order period, with global shipping scheduled to begin in August 2026.

### Why it Matters
This product addresses the growing disconnect between modern urban housing and traditional vehicle maintenance requirements. By enabling water-efficient, mess-free cleaning in restricted parking environments, it provides a practical solution for car owners who lack private driveways or access to standard wash infrastructure.
