Leica’s 220-Inch Mini Projector Wants to Replace Your TV
============================================================

Leica has launched the Cine Compact 1, a premium 4K mini projector featuring a Summicron zoom lens and Triple RGB laser technology designed for flexible home cinema setups.

### Key Points
- The projector delivers 4K resolution at 1,700 ANSI lumens with a maximum screen size of 220 inches.
- It utilizes a 360-degree rotation system, allowing users to project images onto walls or ceilings.
- Hardware includes a Leica Summicron zoom lens, proprietary Leica Image Optimization (LIO) software, and Dolby Vision support.
- The device runs on the VIDAA smart streaming platform and features a durable aluminum housing.
- The light source is rated for a 25,000-hour lifespan, supporting years of daily use.

### Why it Matters
- This device offers a high-end alternative to traditional televisions by allowing users to reclaim living space from large, permanent screens. It signals a shift toward flexible, design-conscious home entertainment that prioritizes both aesthetic integration and professional-grade image quality.
