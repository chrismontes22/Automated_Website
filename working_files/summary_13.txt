Soaring revenue at Mar-a-Lago shows how Trump’s business interests and politics intersect
============================================================

President Donald Trump’s latest financial disclosure reveals record-breaking revenue at his Florida resorts, Mar-a-Lago and Trump National Doral, alongside significant income gains from new cryptocurrency business ventures.

### Key Points
- Mar-a-Lago generated $77.5 million in income for Trump in 2025, a triple increase from his 2020 earnings.
- Trump National Doral reported $122 million in income for 2025, marking a significant recovery from pandemic-era lows.
- Membership initiation fees at Mar-a-Lago were raised to $1 million in 2024, contributing to the property's revenue surge.
- Cryptocurrency ventures, including the $TRUMP memecoin and World Liberty Financial, generated over $1.4 billion, tripling the income from his traditional golf and hotel empire.
- Federal Election Commission data shows political committees and companies with federal business interests frequently host events at Trump properties to gain proximity to the president.

### Why it Matters
The overlap between Trump’s presidential activities and his private business interests has raised concerns among ethics experts regarding potential conflicts of interest and special access. This financial growth highlights the evolving intersection of political influence and personal wealth accumulation within the current administration.
