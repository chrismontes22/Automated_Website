How to refresh your phone without upgrading it
============================================================

Smartphone users can extend the lifespan of their aging devices and improve overall performance by managing background applications, clearing storage space, and performing a complete factory reset.

### Key Points
- Uninstall unused applications to reduce system clutter and improve device responsiveness on both Android and iOS platforms.
- Disable background app refresh settings to prevent inactive software from consuming system resources and battery life.
- Use built-in tools like the Android Files app or iPhone Storage settings to identify and delete large, unnecessary files.
- Samsung Galaxy users can reclaim storage space by disabling the RAM Plus feature within the Device care settings menu.
- Perform a factory reset to restore the device to its original state, ensuring all personal data is backed up to cloud services first.

### Why it Matters
Extending the life of a smartphone helps consumers avoid the significant financial cost of purchasing a new device prematurely. These maintenance steps also ensure that older hardware remains functional and secure for a longer period, reducing electronic waste.
