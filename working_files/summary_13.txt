WWDC 2026: June 8–12
============================================================

**Apple will host its annual Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) from June 8–12, 2026.**

**Key points**
* The event will be held primarily online, featuring over 100 video sessions and interactive labs for developers.
* A limited number of developers and students will be invited to Apple Park in Cupertino for an in-person kickoff event on June 8.
* The conference will highlight major software updates, new developer tools, and advancements in artificial intelligence.
* Attendees will have direct access to Apple engineers and designers to learn about new frameworks and features.
* The event is free to access via the Apple Developer app, website, and YouTube.

**Why it matters**
WWDC is Apple’s most important event for software, providing the first look at the new features and AI capabilities that will define the user experience for iPhones, Macs, and other devices in the coming year.
