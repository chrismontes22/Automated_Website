Bermuda to transition ‘key’ financial services to Stellar blockchain
============================================================

Bermuda Premier David Burt announced a strategic partnership with Circle and Coinbase to advance the island nation’s digital asset ecosystem and facilitate broader cryptocurrency adoption for financial services.

### Key Points
- Premier David Burt confirmed the government collaboration during the World Economic Forum in Davos.
- The initiative builds on Bermuda’s 2018 Digital Asset Business Act to attract global fintech companies.
- Bermuda aims to leverage its regulatory framework to support stablecoin issuance and cross-border payment infrastructure.
- The partnership involves major industry players Circle and Coinbase to integrate digital assets into the local economy.

### Why it Matters
This partnership highlights Bermuda's ongoing strategy to position itself as a global hub for digital finance despite its relatively small $9 billion economy. By fostering cooperation between the government and private sector, the nation provides a regulated environment that could serve as a blueprint for other jurisdictions integrating blockchain technology.
