Bluetti’s FridgePower provides key protection against power outages [Sponsored]
============================================================

Bluetti has launched the FridgePower, an ultra-slim, plug-and-play battery backup system specifically designed to keep refrigerators running during power outages while maintaining a quiet, space-saving kitchen aesthetic.

### Key Points
- The FridgePower unit features a 2,016Wh capacity and 1,800W output, with surge support up to 3,600W.
- Users can expand the system capacity to 8,064Wh by connecting up to three BlueCell 200 expansion batteries.
- The device utilizes LiFePO₄ battery cells, which are engineered for a 10-year lifespan and operate at a quiet 30dB.
- It includes a built-in smart UPS with a 10-millisecond response time for seamless power transitions during outages.
- The product is currently available via a Kickstarter campaign with early supporter pricing running through May 31.

### Why it Matters
This product addresses the specific need for food and medication preservation in smaller living spaces where traditional, bulky home backup systems are impractical. By offering a specialized, easy-to-install solution, Bluetti is targeting a niche market segment that prioritizes convenience and reliability over whole-home power coverage.
