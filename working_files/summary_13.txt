Michael Saylor teases more bitcoin buying even as Strategy stock continues to fall
============================================================

Michael Saylor has signaled continued bitcoin accumulation for Strategy as the company’s holdings reach 847,363 BTC, despite recent declines in the firm's stock price and mounting market skepticism.

### Key Points
- Strategy currently holds 847,363 bitcoin valued at approximately $50.9 billion as of June 28, 2026.
- The company’s average purchase price across 113 separate acquisition events stands at $75,653 per coin.
- Strategy stock (MSTR) recently fell to $82.31, reflecting investor concerns regarding the firm's funding model and dividend obligations.
- Ripple CEO Brad Garlinghouse publicly criticized Strategy’s aggressive acquisition strategy, suggesting it negatively impacts the broader cryptocurrency market.
- Despite stock volatility, Strategy maintains sufficient dollar reserves to cover its dividend obligations for approximately 10 months.

### Why it Matters
The ongoing tension between Strategy’s aggressive accumulation strategy and its declining stock performance highlights the risks associated with debt-funded corporate bitcoin holdings. This situation serves as a critical test for institutional crypto-investment models as market participants weigh the sustainability of dividend obligations against long-term digital asset appreciation.
