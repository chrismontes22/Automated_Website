Robert Wright’s *The God Test*
============================================================

Robert Wright’s upcoming book, Artificial Intelligence and Our Coming Cosmic Reckoning, explores the necessity of a fundamental human reorientation to navigate the potential risks and benefits of artificial intelligence.

### Key Points
- The book, scheduled for release on June 23, argues that current AI debates between accelerationists and doomers lack a necessary cosmic perspective.
- Wright contends that humanity must shift its understanding of AI toward a more constructive "awe spectrum" to ensure positive long-term outcomes.
- The author suggests that a major transformation in human thought is required, though he notes that few people are currently prepared for such a shift.
- Critics suggest the coming transformation may be driven by status-seeking behavior rather than the philosophical or cosmic goals publicly claimed by industry leaders.

### Why it Matters
This work challenges the current discourse surrounding AI by questioning whether modern society possesses the capacity to manage grand technological visions as effectively as historical movements like the Enlightenment. Understanding these shifts is critical for stakeholders as they navigate the potential societal restructuring and power dynamics inherent in the rapid development of artificial intelligence.
