Aqara releases new Thermostat Hub W200 with Apple Adaptive Temperature support
============================================================

Aqara has launched the W200 Thermostat Hub, a Matter 1.4-certified device featuring native Apple Adaptive Temperature integration and advanced occupancy sensing for automated, energy-efficient home climate control.

### Key Points
- The W200 supports Apple’s Adaptive Temperature and Clean Energy Guidance for automated, grid-conscious climate adjustments.
- Integrated mmWave radar technology enables the device to wake upon approach and detect room occupancy for precise temperature management.
- The hub functions as a Matter 1.4 controller with Thread and Zigbee support, connecting over 50 device types.
- Users can view live security snapshots from Aqara doorbells and manage smart locks directly through the thermostat’s display.
- The device is compatible with most North American HVAC systems, including heat pumps, boilers, and furnaces.
- Available now for $159.99 at major retailers, with an exclusive Matte Kit version arriving at the Apple Store in September for $169.99.

### Why it Matters
This release provides a significant alternative to established competitors like Nest by offering deep, native integration within the Apple Home ecosystem. By combining Matter 1.4 support with advanced automation features, the W200 simplifies energy management for homeowners while expanding the interoperability of the broader Aqara product line.
