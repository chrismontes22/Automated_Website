The best Snapdragon X2 Elite laptop yet: HP OmniBook Ultra 14 is the prettiest Windows laptop we've tested this year
============================================================

The HP OmniBook Ultra, powered by the new Snapdragon X2 Elite processor, stands out as a top-tier Windows laptop featuring a premium design, exceptional battery life, and a vibrant display.

### Key Points
- The laptop features an 18-core Snapdragon X2 Elite processor with an integrated 85 TOPS NPU for high-performance computing.
- It includes a 14-inch 3K OLED touchscreen with a 120Hz refresh rate and 100% DCI-P3 color gamut coverage.
- Base models start at $1,899, with configurations offering up to 64GB of RAM and 2TB of PCIe Gen5 storage.
- The device is equipped with a 70Wh battery that provides over 20 hours of real-world usage and supports fast charging.
- Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, and three USB Type-C 40Gbps ports, though it lacks USB-A and 5G support.

### Why it Matters
The OmniBook Ultra highlights the growing competitiveness of Windows on Arm devices, offering a viable alternative to high-end hardware from Apple and Intel. Its combination of power efficiency and performance signals a significant shift in the premium laptop market toward ARM-based architecture.
