A Founding Father of Blockchain Is Out to Reinvent the Prediction Industry
============================================================

Blockchain pioneer W. Scott Stornetta has joined the zero-wager prediction platform SafeBets.world as advisory chair to help integrate cryptographic verification into the forecasting industry to ensure user credibility.

### Key Points
- W. Scott Stornetta, a foundational figure in blockchain technology, is now leading the advisory council for the prediction platform SafeBets.world.
- SafeBets differentiates itself from traditional betting sites by operating a no-wager model that focuses on aggregating "Collective Intelligence" from accurate predictors.
- The platform plans to use cryptographic proof to verify the identity of predictors and ensure that forecasts cannot be altered after the fact.
- Stornetta previously co-authored the 1991 research on digital time-stamping that served as a primary reference for the 2008 Bitcoin whitepaper.
- The initiative aims to solve the prediction industry's credibility gap by providing verifiable, immutable track records for all user forecasts.

### Why it Matters
As artificial intelligence makes digital forgery increasingly common, the ability to prove the authenticity of information is becoming a critical competitive advantage for businesses. By treating trust as a verifiable product, companies like SafeBets are attempting to build durable moats in sectors where traditional credibility has been eroded.
