Meet the lawyer who beat Elon Musk — twice
============================================================

Attorney Bill Savitt of Wachtell, Lipton, Rosen & Katz successfully defended against Elon Musk in the recent Musk v. Altman trial, marking his second major legal victory over the billionaire.

### Key Points
- Bill Savitt previously represented Twitter during Elon Musk’s attempt to withdraw from his acquisition agreement.
- Savitt’s legal career includes high-profile work for Coinbase against the SEC, KKR, and Sotheby’s.
- During the Musk v. Altman trial, Savitt utilized a methodical, chronological presentation of facts to counter Musk’s testimony.
- Savitt emphasizes "massive preparation" and the ability to adapt to unexpected developments during cross-examination.
- The attorney maintains a practice of using music and cycling to manage the mental demands of complex litigation.

### Why it Matters
Savitt’s repeated success against high-profile figures like Musk highlights the critical role of strategic, disciplined legal counsel in navigating complex corporate disputes. His approach underscores how effective litigation often requires balancing courtroom advocacy with a broader understanding of a client's commercial and reputational objectives.
