Google I/O gets into a Flow: preps Flow Music app and generative editing for on-the-go
============================================================

Google has expanded its Flow and Flow Music platforms with the Gemini Omni Flash model, introducing conversational AI tools for video and music creation across mobile and web.

### Key Points
- Google integrated the Gemini Omni Flash model into Flow and Flow Music to enable conversational editing for video and audio tracks.
- New features include granular music editing, lyric rewriting, style transformations, and improved character consistency for AI-generated video content.
- The Flow Agent assistant is now available globally to help users automate complex editing tasks like scene lighting adjustments.
- Google launched a Flow app for Android and a Flow Music app for iOS, with cross-platform availability expanding soon.
- Users can now create custom tools without coding, while Google is implementing C2PA Content Credentials to improve AI transparency and verification.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal Google's push to make professional-grade AI creative tools accessible to casual users through conversational interfaces. By prioritizing content verification standards, the company aims to address industry concerns regarding AI-generated media authenticity and digital provenance.
