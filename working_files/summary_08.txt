Apple Just Dropped iOS 26.5 RC2: Here’s Everything New
============================================================

Apple has released the iOS 26.5 RC2 update, providing critical bug fixes, performance enhancements, and new features ahead of the official public rollout expected early next week.

### Key Points
- The update resolves cellular connectivity issues on iPads and fixes synchronization errors with the Pride Luminance wallpaper.
- Performance improvements include smoother app transitions, reduced lag in multitasking, and enhanced responsiveness for Apple CarPlay.
- New features include a redesigned Apple Games splash screen, improved RCS end-to-end encryption, and compliance with EU notification forwarding regulations.
- Known issues persist regarding specific iPhone charging bugs and inconsistent RCS encryption in regions with limited carrier support.
- Apple plans to launch the public version of iOS 26.5 next week, followed by the iOS 26.6 beta and a future iOS 27 preview.

### Why it Matters
This release serves as the final refinement phase before a broad public rollout, ensuring a more stable and secure experience for millions of iPhone users. By addressing long-standing connectivity and performance bugs, Apple aims to improve device reliability while preparing the ecosystem for upcoming major software iterations.
