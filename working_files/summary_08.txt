5 Of The Best Free Cloud Storage Options
============================================================

Several cloud storage providers offer viable free alternatives to major platforms like Google Drive, providing varying levels of security, storage capacity, and file-sharing capabilities for personal data backups.

### Key Points
- MEGA provides 20GB of free storage but includes dynamic bandwidth limits that can restrict file transfers and sharing.
- pCloud offers 10GB of storage after completing tasks and features a built-in media player for streaming files directly from the cloud.
- Filen is an open-source, privacy-focused service that provides 10GB of storage with zero-knowledge encryption protected by European Union laws.
- MediaFire allows users to expand their initial 10GB of storage up to 50GB by completing referral tasks and social media interactions.
- Proton Drive prioritizes security with end-to-end encryption and Swiss-based privacy laws, though it offers a smaller 2GB to 5GB free tier.

### Why it Matters
These free cloud services provide essential backup solutions for users who need to secure documents or photos without committing to monthly subscription fees. By choosing platforms based on specific needs like privacy, media playback, or storage capacity, users can effectively manage their digital data while avoiding the limitations of standard entry-level plans.
