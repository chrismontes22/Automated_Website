I reviewed the Lenovo Pro 9i Aura Edition, and this thing is absurd in every way
============================================================

The Lenovo Pro 9i Aura Edition Gen 11 is a high-performance 16-inch laptop designed for professional content creators, featuring an Intel Core Ultra 9 processor and NVIDIA RTX 5060 graphics.

### Key Points
- Features a 16-inch 3.2K Tandem OLED touchscreen with 120Hz refresh rate and VESA DisplayHDR True Black 1,000 certification.
- Powered by an Intel Core Ultra 9 386H processor, 32GB of LPDDR5X RAM, and an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5060 GPU.
- Includes a haptic Force Pad that functions as a pressure-sensitive drawing surface when paired with the included Yoga Pen Gen 2.
- Offers extensive connectivity with two Thunderbolt 4 ports, two USB-A ports, HDMI 2.1, and a full-sized SD card reader.
- Housed in a durable, carbon-neutral Thunder Grey aluminum chassis weighing 4.19 lbs with a 92.5Wh battery.
- Pricing starts at $2,799, with the reviewed configuration featuring the upgraded GPU and display retailing for $3,099.

### Why it Matters
- This device serves as a premium Windows-based alternative to Apple’s creative hardware, offering a blend of portability and high-end processing power for demanding AI and multimedia tasks. Its specialized display and integrated pen support make it a significant investment for professionals seeking a future-proofed workstation.
