DJI Power 1000 Mini review
============================================================

The DJI Power 1000 Mini is a compact, 1kWh portable power station designed for creators and mobile users, offering high-speed charging and a lightweight, highly portable form factor.

### Key Points
- Features a 1008Wh LFP battery with a cycle life of approximately 4,000 cycles to 80% capacity.
- Delivers 800W of continuous power and 1000W peak output, suitable for charging drones, cameras, and laptops.
- Supports rapid AC charging, reaching 80% capacity in about one hour.
- Includes a built-in retractable 100W USB-C cable and an integrated front-facing utility light.
- Weighs 11.5kg, making it significantly more portable than larger 2kWh-class power stations.

### Why it Matters
This unit prioritizes mobility and ease of use for professionals who need reliable power on the go rather than stationary home backup. By balancing capacity with a manageable weight, it offers a practical solution for vanlifers and content creators who require frequent, quick power top-ups.
