Everything New in iOS 27 Beta 3
============================================================

Apple has released the third beta of iOS 27, introducing enhanced Siri voice customization, new accessibility features, and refined interface updates ahead of the software's official September launch.

### Key Points
- Siri voice customization for pace and expressivity is now active on iPhone 17 Pro and iPhone Air models.
- The new Live Recognition accessibility tool uses on-device intelligence to describe surroundings and answer user questions via the camera.
- Photos settings now include a toggle for star ratings, while the Control Center displays cellular signal details even when Wi-Fi is active.
- AirPods users gain a new slider to adjust the balance between transparency and noise cancellation in Adaptive mode.
- Apple Intelligence features within the Home app now require a 2TB iCloud+ subscription for full functionality.
- The update adds 5G+ support for carriers in India and introduces a new wallpaper animation effect when accessing the notification center.

### Why it Matters
These updates demonstrate Apple's ongoing commitment to integrating on-device artificial intelligence into core system features like accessibility and voice control. By gating specific smart home capabilities behind premium storage tiers, the company is further incentivizing users to adopt its broader ecosystem services.
