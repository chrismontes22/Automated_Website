Enter a new era: Samsung Galaxy Unpacked confirmed for July, reservations begin
============================================================

Samsung has officially scheduled its summer Galaxy Unpacked event for July 22 in London, where the company will unveil its latest foldable smartphones and new wearable hardware devices.

### Key Points
- The event takes place on July 22 at 9 am ET/6 am PT and will be streamed on Samsung.com and YouTube.
- Samsung plans to showcase the next generation of foldable devices, specifically the Galaxy Z Fold 8 and Flip 8.
- The keynote will also feature the debut of the Galaxy Watch 9, which is expected to include new health-tracking features.
- Customers can currently reserve devices to receive a $30 credit toward pre-orders and enter a sweepstakes for $500 gift cards.
- The company is promoting the launch through a viral marketing campaign that involved clearing its official Instagram account.

### Why it Matters
This event marks a strategic push by Samsung to integrate advanced artificial intelligence capabilities into its foldable hardware to differentiate its products in a competitive market. By emphasizing personalized, adaptive experiences, the company aims to set a new industry standard for mobile AI integration.
