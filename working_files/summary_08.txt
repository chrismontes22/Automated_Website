Apple Wallet’s Digital ID feature could potentially have a major new use case soon
============================================================

Anthropic may utilize Apple’s Digital ID feature to verify user nationality and restore access to its Fable 5 and Mythos 5 AI models following recent US government export restrictions.

### Key Points
- The US government restricted access to Anthropic’s Fable 5 and Mythos 5 models for foreign nationals due to security concerns and reported jailbreaks.
- Apple’s Digital ID allows users to store passports and state-issued driver's licenses in Apple Wallet for secure identity and age verification.
- Anthropic previously integrated Apple’s age verification API, establishing a technical precedent for adopting Apple’s identity tools.
- Implementing Apple’s Digital ID could allow Anthropic to comply with export controls while avoiding reliance on third-party identity verification services.
- Current limitations include restricted support for Apple Wallet IDs to 14 states and the exclusion of users who do not own Apple devices.

### Why it Matters
Integrating Apple’s Digital ID could provide a streamlined, privacy-focused solution for AI companies facing strict government-mandated identity verification requirements. This approach highlights the growing intersection between consumer hardware ecosystems and the regulatory compliance necessary to deploy advanced artificial intelligence models.
