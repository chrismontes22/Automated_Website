Android XR's biggest hurdle may finally have a solution
============================================================

Qualcomm has unveiled the Snapdragon Reality Elite, a powerful new XR platform designed to enhance smart glasses and headsets with significant AI, graphics, and thermal efficiency improvements.

### Key Points
- The Snapdragon Reality Elite platform offers 60% faster GPU performance and 160% higher NPU performance than the previous XR2+ Gen 2.
- The chip supports on-device AI models with 48 TOPS of performance and displays up to 4.4K resolution per eye at 90fps.
- Devices powered by the new platform will benefit from 20% longer battery life and run 12°C cooler under sustained workloads.
- XREAL Project Aura and upcoming Play For Dream hardware are confirmed as the first devices to utilize the new chipset.
- Qualcomm introduced the Snapdragon START initiative to provide developers with pre-packaged hardware and software designs for faster wearable development.

### Why it Matters
This platform provides the necessary hardware foundation to bring advanced spatial computing, photorealistic avatars, and agentic AI directly to consumer-grade smart glasses. By significantly boosting on-device processing power, Qualcomm is enabling a new generation of wearable devices to perform complex tasks without relying on cloud connectivity.
