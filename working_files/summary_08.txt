AI is causing phone prices to go up, but you can beat the RAM crisis with this powerfully underrated Galaxy phone
============================================================

Samsung is offering significant Prime Day discounts on the Galaxy S25 Plus and Galaxy S26 Plus as consumers navigate rising smartphone costs driven by the ongoing global RAM crisis.

### Key Points
- The Samsung Galaxy S25 Plus is currently discounted by 34%, bringing the price down to $664 from its original $999 launch cost.
- The newer Galaxy S26 Plus, which launched at $1,099, is now available for as low as $759 during the Prime Day sale.
- The Galaxy S25 Plus features a Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, 12GB of RAM, 256GB of storage, and a 50MP primary camera sensor.
- Both models continue to receive software support, including One UI 8.5 features, AI editing tools, and ongoing security updates from Samsung.
- Rising smartphone prices in 2026 are largely attributed to industry-wide resource reallocation toward building data centers for artificial intelligence infrastructure.

### Why it Matters
These discounts provide a cost-effective alternative for consumers facing inflated hardware prices caused by the current RAM supply shortage. By choosing previous-generation models, users can access high-end AI features and robust performance without paying the premium associated with the latest flagship releases.
