You might not need the Galaxy S26 Ultra after seeing this record-smashing Prime Day deal
============================================================

Samsung has discounted the base Galaxy S26 to its lowest price of $670 for Prime Day, offering a significant $230 savings on the compact flagship smartphone.

### Key Points
- The base model Galaxy S26 price is reduced from $900 to $670 during the Prime Day sales event.
- The device features the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset and runs on One UI 8.5.
- Users receive access to Samsung’s latest Galaxy AI tools and seven years of guaranteed software support.
- The compact flagship offers higher peak display brightness than the more expensive Galaxy S26 Ultra model.

### Why it Matters
This price reduction effectively reverses the $100 launch-day increase, making the device a highly competitive option for consumers seeking a premium, compact Android experience. By lowering the barrier to entry, Samsung is positioning the base model as a more accessible flagship alternative for users who prioritize portability over the larger Ultra variant.
