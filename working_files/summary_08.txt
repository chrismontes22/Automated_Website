This new projector lineup is all about summer sports and outdoor viewing
============================================================

AWOL Vision has unveiled a new lineup of ultra-short-throw projectors and inflatable outdoor screens designed to enhance home theater and backyard sports viewing experiences throughout the summer season.

### Key Points
- The Aetherion Pro ultra-short-throw projector features 4K resolution, low latency, and high refresh rates for bright-room sports and gaming.
- The Valerion Max Theater Package utilizes triple-laser technology to provide a high-end cinematic experience for home theater enthusiasts.
- New red and purple color variants for the Valerion Pro 2 projector aim to integrate the hardware into diverse lifestyle and gaming room aesthetics.
- The new Valerion inflatable outdoor screens feature wrinkle-resistant, washable fabric and a silent, sealed-air construction that sets up in 10 minutes.
- Pricing begins at $2,699 for the Valerion Pro 2, $3,499 for the Aetherion Pro, and $4,199 for the Valerion Max Theater Package.

### Why it Matters
This product expansion positions AWOL Vision to capture consumer interest ahead of major summer sporting events like the FIFA World Cup. By offering specialized outdoor hardware and lifestyle-focused designs, the company is shifting projectors from niche home theater equipment into versatile, centerpiece entertainment solutions.
