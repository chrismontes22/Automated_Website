Top Stories: Apple Card AirPods Pro Promo, WWDC 2026 Schedule, and More
============================================================

Apple has announced the schedule for its 2026 Worldwide Developers Conference and launched a new Apple Card promotion offering customers free AirPods Pro 3 with specific usage requirements.

### Key Points
- Apple’s WWDC 2026 is scheduled for June 8 through June 12, with an in-person keynote event held at Apple Park.
- New Apple Card users can earn free AirPods Pro 3 via a $25 monthly rebate for 10 months, provided they use the card 10 times each month.
- Development of a foldable iPhone has reportedly stalled due to hinge reliability issues failing to meet Apple's internal quality control standards.
- The iPhone 17 Pro was ranked as the fastest-charging smartphone overall in recent CNET lab testing.
- Apple re-released the Hikawa Grip & Stand accessory in three new colors, priced at $54.95, to support users with mobility and grip challenges.

### Why it Matters
These updates highlight Apple's ongoing efforts to incentivize financial service adoption while managing complex hardware engineering challenges for future product categories. The focus on accessibility and charging performance reflects the company's strategy to maintain market competitiveness through both software integration and hardware refinement.
