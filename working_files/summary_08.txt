Weekend A La Carte (May 23)
============================================================

The latest edition of the A La Carte newsletter explores the intersection of technology, theology, and culture, featuring expert commentary on artificial intelligence, labor, and religious traditions.

### Key Points
- Chris Griswold examines how artificial intelligence is fueling anxiety regarding the future of work and middle-class stability.
- Brad Littlejohn analyzes the concept of techno-feudalism and the growing influence of modern technology companies on global structures.
- A Wall Street Journal article explores the parallels between Karl Marx’s economic theories and traditional Christian eschatology.
- Tony Costa provides a theological critique of Eastern Orthodoxy, highlighting key doctrinal differences for Evangelical readers.
- Michael Graham discusses the epistemological implications of relying on foundational AI models for religious and primary knowledge.

### Why it Matters
These articles highlight the growing tension between rapid technological advancement and traditional belief systems in modern society. Understanding these shifts is essential for navigating the evolving landscape of digital ethics, labor markets, and cultural identity.
