Avoid price discrimination with this 3-year VPN subscription for $68
============================================================

Surfshark is offering a three-year subscription to its VPN service for $67.20, allowing users to bypass regional price discrimination and enhance online privacy while traveling or browsing at home.

### Key Points
- The three-year Starter Plan subscription is available for $67.20 using the discount code VPN20, a significant reduction from the standard $430 retail price.
- Surfshark provides access to over 3,200 servers across 100 countries, utilizing 10 Gbps infrastructure to maintain high connection speeds.
- Security features include AES-256 encryption, support for WireGuard and OpenVPN protocols, and a Bypasser tool for managing app-specific VPN traffic.
- The service supports unlimited simultaneous device connections across platforms including Windows, macOS, Android, iOS, and Linux.
- This promotional offer is valid through June 30 at 11:59 p.m. PT via the StackSocial marketplace.

### Why it Matters
Using a VPN allows travelers to circumvent location-based price gouging for flights and hotels by masking their IP address. This tool also provides essential digital security and privacy protections for users accessing the internet on multiple devices.
