15.6-inch screen instantly mirrors any phone without cables, apps or Wi-Fi
============================================================

**Takolink is a new 15.6-inch portable wireless display designed to mirror smartphone content instantly without requiring dedicated apps, complex network configurations, or additional software installations for most mobile devices.**

**Key points**
- The 15.6-inch IPS display features 1080p resolution, 300 nits of brightness, and a 60-Hz refresh rate.
- It includes a 4,000-mAh battery providing three to eight hours of usage depending on the task and volume levels.
- The device supports wireless mirroring for iOS and Android devices, plus wired connections via USB-C and HDMI for laptops and game consoles.
- A built-in motion sensor automatically rotates the display between portrait and landscape orientations to match the connected smartphone.
- The Kickstarter campaign has exceeded its funding goal, with initial units scheduled to begin shipping to backers in June.

**Why it matters: **
Takolink simplifies the user experience by removing the technical barriers typically associated with screen mirroring, such as app requirements and network dependencies. This "plug-and-play" approach offers a practical solution for users seeking to easily expand their mobile viewing experience for work, media, or social settings.
