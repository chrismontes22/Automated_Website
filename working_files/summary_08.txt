I wanted an online store, so I turned to the CubeCart open-source solution
============================================================

CubeCart offers small businesses a free, open-source e-commerce platform that can be easily deployed on Hostinger’s web hosting services to manage online stores and digital product inventories.

### Key Points
- CubeCart is a free, open-source e-commerce solution featuring product management, multi-currency support, and SEO-friendly tools.
- The platform utilizes the Smarty engine and responsive templates to ensure store functionality across desktop and mobile devices.
- Hostinger provides optimized hosting for CubeCart, including LiteSpeed servers, SSD storage, and an auto-installer for simplified deployment.
- Security features on Hostinger include daily backups, malware scanning, and DDoS protection to safeguard customer data.
- Users can expand CubeCart functionality through a marketplace offering various plugins for shipping, marketing, and abandoned cart recovery.

### Why it Matters
Choosing an open-source platform like CubeCart paired with affordable hosting allows small businesses to launch professional e-commerce operations without expensive licensing fees. This combination provides the scalability and technical control necessary for growing companies to manage their digital presence efficiently.
