I didn't know Samsung gallery could edit photos like this — I haven't needed a separate app since
============================================================

Samsung’s built-in Gallery app offers a robust suite of AI-powered editing tools that allow users to enhance photos and remove distractions without needing third-party applications like Canva or CapCut.

### Key Points
- The Object Eraser tool effectively removes unwanted people or objects from images with accuracy comparable to Google’s Pixel devices.
- A Lasso tool enables users to cut out subjects for custom stickers, while the Styles feature applies artistic effects like watercolor or comic book filters.
- The Spot Color tool allows for selective color restoration, creating high-contrast, dramatic images by desaturating backgrounds.
- The Remove Moiré feature uses AI to eliminate distracting wavy lines and grid patterns often found when photographing digital screens or monitors.

### Why it Matters
Utilizing native editing tools streamlines mobile workflows by eliminating the need to export images to external software for basic adjustments. This integration highlights how smartphone manufacturers are increasingly prioritizing built-in AI capabilities to improve user convenience and creative control.
