How fast is the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 8 charging speed?
============================================================

The upcoming Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 8 is expected to feature a 5,000mAh battery and upgraded 45W wired charging when it officially launches on July 22.

### Key Points
- The Galaxy Z Fold 8 will feature a 5,000mAh battery, an increase from the 4,400mAh capacity found in previous generations.
- Wired charging speeds are expected to increase to 45W, a significant improvement over the 25W limit on the Galaxy Z Fold 7.
- Wireless charging capabilities will remain capped at 15W, matching the performance of the predecessor.
- The device is scheduled for an official unveiling on July 22 to compete in the increasingly crowded foldable smartphone market.
- Despite these hardware upgrades, the device faces stiff competition from the Motorola Razr Fold, which offers faster charging and a larger battery.

### Why it Matters
These incremental hardware improvements address long-standing user requests for better battery life and faster power delivery in Samsung's flagship foldable line. While these updates keep the device competitive, they highlight the growing pressure from rivals who are currently offering more aggressive charging specifications.
