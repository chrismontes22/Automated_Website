Verizon wants to give you a free Galaxy S26 Ultra AND a $100 gift card, just for kicks — here's the deal
============================================================

Verizon is offering a free Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra and a $100 gift card to new customers who add a line on the carrier's Unlimited Ultimate mobile plan.

### Key Points
- Customers receive $1,300 in credits over 36 months to cover the full cost of the Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra.
- The promotion requires no trade-in device to qualify for the full promotional value.
- An additional $100 gift card is provided for orders placed exclusively through Verizon's online store.
- The offer is tied to the Unlimited Ultimate plan, which is Verizon's premium tier starting at $90 per month for a single line.
- The Galaxy S26 Ultra features a 6.9-inch AMOLED display, Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 Elite processor, and seven years of guaranteed software support.

### Why it Matters
This promotion significantly lowers the barrier to entry for consumers looking to acquire a premium flagship smartphone without an upfront cost or trade-in requirement. By bundling high-end hardware with its most expensive service plan, Verizon aims to secure long-term subscribers while incentivizing digital sales through added gift card incentives.
