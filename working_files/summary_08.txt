Samsung's new teaser with Spider-Man all but confirms the wider Galaxy Z Fold 8
============================================================

Samsung has scheduled its next Unpacked event for July 22 in London to debut the Galaxy Z Fold 8 series, featuring a redesigned, wider form factor for its foldables.

### Key Points
- Samsung will host the Unpacked event on July 22 in London to unveil the Galaxy Z Fold 8, Galaxy Z Fold 8 Ultra, new Flip phones, and Galaxy watches.
- Promotional teasers created in collaboration with "Spider-Man: Brand New Day" confirm a shift toward a wider, passport-like device shape.
- The standard Galaxy Z Fold 8 is expected to feature a shorter, wider design intended to compete directly with future foldable smartphone offerings.
- Social media teasers show Spider-Man interacting with the new hardware, highlighting the upcoming Ultra model's design.

### Why it Matters
This launch represents a significant design pivot for Samsung as it attempts to refine the foldable user experience to better compete with emerging market rivals. By adopting a wider form factor, the company aims to improve usability and solidify its position as the leader in the premium foldable smartphone segment.
