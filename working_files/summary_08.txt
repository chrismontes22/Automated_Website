MacOS 27 Golden Gate: Top New Features
============================================================

Apple has unveiled macOS 27 Golden Gate, a major operating system update featuring the new Siri AI chatbot, systemwide artificial intelligence integration, and refined Liquid Glass interface elements.

### Key Points
- macOS 27 Golden Gate is exclusively compatible with Apple Silicon Macs, officially ending support for all Intel-based hardware.
- The new Siri AI, powered by Google Gemini models, offers systemwide integration, screen awareness, and advanced natural language processing.
- Safari introduces AI-driven tab organization called Topics and natural language-based custom extensions for improved browser management.
- Advanced Apple Intelligence features, including enhanced Voice Mode, require an M3 chip or newer and at least 12 GB of RAM.
- The developer beta is available now, with a public beta scheduled for release in July 2026.
- Regulatory concerns will delay the availability of Siri AI features in the European Union and China.

### Why it Matters
This update marks a significant shift in Apple's software strategy by embedding generative AI directly into the core macOS experience to compete with rival chatbot platforms. By restricting advanced features to newer hardware, Apple is also incentivizing a faster upgrade cycle for its professional and consumer Mac user base.
