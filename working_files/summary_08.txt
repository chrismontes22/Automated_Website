Conduent Incorporated (CNDT) Is A Top Stock To Buy According To Bill Miller
============================================================

Conduent Incorporated remains a notable stock pick in Bill Miller’s portfolio despite significant share price declines and a massive data breach affecting 25 million Americans in 2025.

### Key Points
- Conduent shares have dropped 44% over the past year and 20% year-to-date.
- A major data breach, first reported in October 2025, compromised the social security numbers and health data of 25 million individuals.
- The company announced the sale of its tolling business to a Canadian firm for $70 million on June 30, 2026.
- Management intends to use the divestiture to simplify operations and focus on the company's core business segments.

### Why it Matters
The company is currently navigating significant reputational and financial challenges following one of the largest data breaches in United States history. Investors are closely watching these restructuring efforts as the firm attempts to streamline its operations and recover from recent market volatility.
