Will Nifty, Sensex extend gains on Monday? Q1, oil among 5 major factors investors must track
============================================================

Indian equities Sensex and Nifty closed the week with gains exceeding 1% as market volatility declined, setting the stage for a busy earnings season and key macroeconomic developments.

### Key Points
- Sensex and Nifty extended gains for a second consecutive session, while the India VIX volatility index dropped 8% to 12.33.
- A total of 143 companies, including Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank, and Wipro, are scheduled to report June quarter earnings this week.
- Foreign Institutional Investors and Domestic Institutional Investors were net buyers, purchasing equities worth Rs 4,670 crore and Rs 8,280 crore respectively.
- Mutual fund SIP contributions reached a three-month high of Rs 31,780 crore in June 2026, reflecting a 2.7% increase from the previous month.
- Investors are monitoring geopolitical tensions in the Gulf, global technology stock performance, and the progress of the southwest monsoon for potential market impacts.

### Why it Matters
These factors collectively influence investor sentiment and liquidity, determining whether the current market rally can sustain its momentum. Monitoring institutional flows and corporate earnings provides critical insight into the health of the Indian economy amid shifting global geopolitical and inflationary pressures.
