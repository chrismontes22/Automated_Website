Walmart's ultra-cheap Google TV streaming stick just disappeared
============================================================

Walmart has removed its budget-friendly Onn HD Streaming Stick from its website, fueling speculation that a replacement model is imminent following a recent filing with the FCC.

### Key Points
- The Onn HD Streaming Stick is currently missing from Walmart’s online store, suggesting the product may be discontinued or undergoing a transition.
- A new device matching the outgoing model's specifications recently appeared in the FCC database, indicating it has been cleared for U.S. retail sale.
- The updated filing reveals the device is being produced by a new manufacturer, though the hardware remains identical to the previous version.
- Global shortages in RAM and storage components have recently driven price increases across the budget streaming device market.
- Walmart’s Onn line remains a primary low-cost alternative for consumers seeking a Google TV interface without the premium pricing of major tech brands.

### Why it Matters
The potential refresh of the Onn streaming line highlights the ongoing challenges retailers face in maintaining affordable hardware amid supply chain volatility. As one of the few budget-friendly options running Google TV, any shift in Walmart's inventory strategy directly impacts consumers looking for cost-effective alternatives to premium streaming devices.
