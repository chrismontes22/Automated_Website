Our new favorite budget phones
============================================================

The Verge has updated its list of recommended budget smartphones under $600, highlighting the iPhone 17E, Google Pixel 10A, and Nothing Phone 4A Pro as top market choices.

### Key Points
- The iPhone 17E is the top budget Apple recommendation, featuring the A19 chip and newly added MagSafe charging capabilities.
- Google’s Pixel 10A remains the leading Android option due to its reliable camera performance and extended software support.
- The Nothing Phone 4A Pro is highlighted as a competitive alternative, offering a distinct aesthetic and a high-quality display.
- All featured devices are priced under $600, with no further major budget smartphone releases expected until next year.
- The Nothing Phone 4A Pro marks a significant shift by becoming widely available for purchase within the United States market.

### Why it Matters
These recommendations provide clarity for consumers navigating a saturated smartphone market where recent hardware refreshes have concluded for the year. By identifying top-performing budget devices, the guide helps users balance cost with essential features like processing power and software longevity.
