Grimes Says She Made An Album Called Psy Opera
============================================================

Musician Grimes revealed in a new Interview Magazine feature that she is nearing completion of her upcoming album, Psy Opera, while discussing her evolving relationship with artificial intelligence.

### Key Points
- Grimes confirmed her next studio album, Psy Opera, is nearly finished and includes tracks titled “The Light Ages,” “Eve Is Online,” and “DeepSeek.”
- The artist utilized the Chinese open-source AI model DeepSeek to generate lyrics for the song of the same name.
- Grimes is currently developing a documentary titled First Contact, which explores the concept of machine consciousness.
- The singer previously parted ways with Columbia Records and now releases music independently through her own label, Nazgul Recording LLC.
- Grimes noted that a request to write a rap for a K-pop artist helped reignite her creative interest in music production.

### Why it Matters
This update provides clarity on the artist's creative direction following her departure from major label representation and ongoing personal legal proceedings. Her integration of AI-generated lyrics highlights the growing intersection between emerging technology and modern songwriting practices in the music industry.
