Samsung's wide foldable just got its first detailed specs leak
============================================================

Samsung is reportedly preparing to launch the Galaxy Z Fold 8, featuring a wider 4:3 aspect ratio, a thinner 4.5mm profile, and the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor.

### Key Points
- The device features a 7.6-inch internal display and a 5.5-inch cover screen, both utilizing 120Hz Dynamic AMOLED 2X panels.
- Internal hardware includes the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chip, 12GB of RAM, and up to 1TB of internal storage.
- The foldable is expected to weigh approximately 200 grams and measure 4.5mm thick when unfolded.
- Camera specifications include a 50MP primary sensor, a 50MP ultra-wide lens, and dual 10MP selfie cameras.
- The battery capacity is reportedly increased to 4,800mAh with support for Super Fast Charging 2.0.

### Why it Matters
This redesign signals a strategic shift for Samsung as it attempts to compete more directly with the form factor of Apple's rumored foldable iPhone. By prioritizing a thinner, lighter chassis and a wider aspect ratio, Samsung aims to improve the ergonomics and mainstream appeal of its flagship foldable lineup.
