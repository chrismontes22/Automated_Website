Your camera roll is a disaster — This app fixes it for $60
============================================================

The CleanMyPhone app utilizes artificial intelligence to help iPhone and iPad users manage storage by automatically identifying and removing duplicate photos, blurry images, and large, space-consuming video files.

### Key Points
- The app features a one-year license currently discounted to $59.99 from the original $99.99 retail price.
- AI-driven tools automatically detect and delete duplicate photos, screenshots, and redundant burst shots to reclaim device storage.
- Users can compress large video files and convert Live Photos into still images to further optimize available space.
- All scanning and data processing occur locally on the device to ensure user privacy and security.
- The application has achieved over 7 million downloads and maintains a 4.6-star rating on the Apple App Store.

### Why it Matters
This tool addresses the common consumer challenge of managing digital clutter on mobile devices without requiring manual review of thousands of files. By automating storage optimization, the app helps users extend the utility of their existing hardware while maintaining privacy through local processing.
