I always add these 6 quick settings tiles to my stock Android phone when setting it up for the first time
============================================================

Android 16 on devices like the Pixel 10 offers customizable quick settings, allowing users to optimize their mobile experience by adding essential shortcuts for improved daily device management.

**Key points**
- Android 16 allows users to add, remove, and reorganize quick settings tiles, including third-party options and adjustable tile sizes.
- Essential tiles missing from default stock settings include Location, Hotspot, QR Code Scanner, TV Remote, Song Search, and Battery Saver.
- Users can access the customization menu by swiping down twice from the top of the screen and selecting the pencil edit icon.
- The TV Remote and Song Search tiles are located under the system apps category, while others are sorted by privacy, connectivity, or utilities.
- The updated Now Playing feature is available as a standalone app tile for Pixel users running the Android 16 operating system.

**Why it matters: **
Customizing quick settings allows users to streamline their workflow by reducing the time spent navigating through deep system menus for frequently used features. By tailoring these shortcuts, Android 16 users can significantly improve their device efficiency and battery management.
