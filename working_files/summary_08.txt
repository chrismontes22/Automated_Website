I always add these 6 quick settings tiles to my stock Android phone when setting it up for the first time
============================================================

Android 16 on devices like the Pixel 10 offers customizable quick settings, allowing users to optimize their mobile experience by adding essential shortcuts for improved daily device management.

Key points
- Users can customize the quick settings menu by adding, removing, or reorganizing tiles through the edit pencil icon.
- Essential tiles missing from default stock settings include Location, Hotspot, QR Code Scanner, TV Remote, Song Search, and Battery Saver.
- The Location tile is categorized under privacy, while Hotspot is found in the connectivity section.
- Utilities and system apps categories house shortcuts for the QR code scanner, TV remote, and song identification tools.
- Android 16 allows Pixel 10 users to integrate third-party tiles and adjust tile sizes for a more personalized interface.

Why it matters: 
Customizing quick settings significantly reduces the time spent navigating through deep system menus to access frequently used features. By tailoring these shortcuts, users can improve battery management, enhance privacy control, and streamline daily tasks on their Android devices.
