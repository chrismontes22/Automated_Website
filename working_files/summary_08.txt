What's New in the iOS 27 Photos App
============================================================

Apple has introduced significant updates to the Photos app in iOS 27, featuring advanced generative AI editing tools, improved album organization, and enhanced cross-platform sharing capabilities for all users.

### Key Points
- New Apple Intelligence tools include Clean Up for object removal, Extend for image expansion, and Reframe for perspective adjustments.
- The Image Playground app allows users to add photorealistic accessories to photos using natural language prompts.
- Shared Albums now support contributions from Android and Windows users via link-based access and expiration settings.
- Users can now add star ratings and keywords to media, and save individual frames from videos as standalone photos.
- New Utilities folders automatically categorize images into "Captured by Me" and "Identity Documents" for better organization.
- AI editing features require an iPhone 15 Pro or later, while general iOS 27 updates support iPhone 11 and newer models.

### Why it Matters
These updates represent a major shift in how Apple integrates generative AI directly into core system applications to compete with professional-grade editing software. By opening Shared Albums to non-Apple users, the company is also lowering barriers to its ecosystem and improving the utility of iCloud for cross-platform households.
