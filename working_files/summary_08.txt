5 Places where Mature SOCs Keep MTTR Fast and Others Waste Time
============================================================

Security operations centers can significantly reduce mean time to respond (MTTR) by integrating behavioral threat intelligence directly into their daily workflows to eliminate manual, fragmented investigative processes.

### Key Points
- ANY.RUN provides threat intelligence feeds, lookup tools, and reports to embed real-time behavioral data into security workflows.
- The platform leverages data from over 15,000 organizations and 600,000 analysts who detonate malware and phishing samples daily.
- Integrating intelligence into SIEM and SOAR platforms enables automated containment of threats, reducing the time between detection and response to seconds.
- AI-powered search features allow analysts to query complex threat data using natural language, lowering the barrier for less experienced team members.
- Proactive threat hunting using updated intelligence feeds helps organizations identify emerging campaigns and adapt defenses before incidents occur.

### Why it Matters
Reducing MTTR is a critical business lever that minimizes the window for data exfiltration and operational disruption during a cyberattack. By streamlining workflows, organizations can improve their security posture and maximize the return on existing investments without the need for additional headcount.
