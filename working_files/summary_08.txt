Every Samsung Phone And Tablet Getting A Price Hike In 2026
============================================================

Samsung has quietly increased prices across its 2026 Galaxy smartphone, tablet, and laptop lineups, citing rising costs for memory components, global trade uncertainties, and broader economic pressures.

### Key Points
- Samsung raised prices for high-storage smartphone models, including the Galaxy Z Fold 7 and Z Flip 7, by up to $80.
- Tablet pricing saw significant increases across the Galaxy Tab S11 and Tab S10 series, with some models rising by as much as $280.
- Galaxy Book 6 Pro and Ultra laptop prices increased by $200 to $450 depending on the specific configuration.
- Industry analysts attribute these hikes to a global shortage of RAM and storage chips driven by high demand from the AI sector.
- External factors, including rising oil costs and potential trade tariffs, have further pressured the company to protect its profit margins.

### Why it Matters
These price adjustments reflect a broader trend of rising consumer electronics costs as manufacturers struggle with supply chain volatility and increased component expenses. Consumers may face higher entry points for premium hardware, forcing a shift in purchasing strategies toward waiting for promotional events or prioritizing base-model configurations.
