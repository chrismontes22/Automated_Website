Health-Tracking Pet Collar Acts Like a Smartwatch for Dogs and Cats
============================================================

Tractive has unveiled the Cat 6 Mini and Dog 6 XL smart collars, which utilize AI-powered sensors to monitor pet health metrics and provide real-time GPS location tracking.

### Key Points
- The Cat 6 Mini costs $79 and monitors feline respiratory and resting heart rates.
- The Dog 6 XL is priced at $89, features a scratch-monitoring system, and offers four weeks of battery life.
- Both devices require a cellular connection and a separate subscription plan, ranging from $120 to $300.
- The collars track activity, sleep, and health data, which can be shared with veterinarians to identify baseline changes.
- The Cat 6 Mini is scheduled to begin shipping on May 31.

### Why it Matters
These devices represent a growing trend in the pet technology market by applying human-grade wearable health monitoring to domestic animals. By providing actionable data on vital signs and behavior, these tools enable owners to detect potential health issues earlier and improve long-term veterinary care outcomes.
