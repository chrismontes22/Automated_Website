OpenAI hides Codex agent instructions behind encryption, leaving developers in the dark
============================================================

Former OpenAI CTO Mira Murati has launched Thinking Machines, a startup releasing a 975 billion parameter open-weights AI model designed to compete with existing large language models.

### Key Points
- Thinking Machines released its first open-weights model, positioning it as a direct alternative to Chinese large language models.
- The model features 975 billion parameters, marking a significant entry into the frontier AI space by the former OpenAI executive.
- The release emphasizes open access, contrasting with the closed-source development strategies currently employed by major industry players like OpenAI.

### Why it Matters
This launch signals a shift in the AI landscape as high-profile industry leaders move toward open-weights models to challenge proprietary systems. The move could accelerate innovation and transparency in the sector while providing developers with powerful alternatives to restricted frontier models.
