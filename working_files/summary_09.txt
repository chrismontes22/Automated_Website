The Chokepoint Economy: Seven Gates Between You and Your Audience
============================================================

Digital creators face increasing challenges as algorithmic gatekeepers and AI-driven search tools restrict audience access, mirroring the economic vulnerabilities of physical shipping chokepoints like the Strait of Hormuz.

### Key Points
- The Strait of Hormuz facilitates the transit of 20 million barrels of oil daily, representing one-fifth of the global supply.
- Organic reach on platforms like Facebook has plummeted from 16% in 2012 to approximately 1% by 2026.
- AI-generated search summaries significantly reduce click-through rates, with some sources receiving less than 1% of traffic from AI-quoted content.
- Over 50% of new web articles are now estimated to be machine-generated, leading to content saturation and reduced visibility for human creators.
- Creators are advised to prioritize direct audience ownership through email lists and private communities to bypass platform-controlled distribution.

### Why it Matters
The shift toward AI-mediated discovery and platform-owned audiences threatens the sustainability of independent digital publishing by severing the direct link between creators and their readers. By moving away from rented platform reach toward owned communication channels, creators can mitigate the risk of being silenced by evolving algorithms and automated content systems.
