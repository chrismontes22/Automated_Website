5 Discontinued Apple Products That Are Still Worth Buying In 2026
============================================================

Despite being discontinued, several older Apple devices remain functional and cost-effective options for consumers looking to save money on reliable technology in the current 2026 market.

### Key Points
- The 2018 iPad Pro 12.9-inch (3rd gen) features a 120 Hz Liquid Retina display and was the first Apple tablet to adopt USB-C connectivity.
- The 2019 13-inch MacBook Pro remains viable for basic tasks and is notable for being the final model to feature Apple's butterfly keyboard.
- The iPhone 11 Pro supports iOS 26 and offers a versatile triple-camera system, providing a budget-friendly alternative to modern 5G-enabled smartphones.
- The iPod classic has seen a resurgence due to its offline music capabilities and a modding community that enables modern upgrades like Bluetooth and SSD storage.
- The Apple Watch Series 5 includes an Always-On display and essential health tracking features, making it a capable entry-level wearable for fitness monitoring.
- These vintage devices are widely available on secondary marketplaces like Amazon, Best Buy, and eBay, with prices typically ranging from $100 to $300.

### Why it Matters
Purchasing discontinued Apple hardware allows budget-conscious consumers to access high-quality technology at a fraction of the cost of current-generation models. These devices demonstrate the long-term durability of Apple's ecosystem, offering continued utility for basic computing, media consumption, and connectivity needs.
