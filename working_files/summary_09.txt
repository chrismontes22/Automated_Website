Mark Cuban says you should ask 5 questions about your company and its leadership
============================================================

Billionaire investor Mark Cuban has released a five-question guide to help employees evaluate their company's long-term viability and career growth potential during the rapid expansion of artificial intelligence.

### Key Points
- Cuban advises employees to leave their jobs if their CEO lacks a fundamental understanding of artificial intelligence.
- Workers should assess whether their company is growing and if their current role provides sufficient intellectual challenges.
- Employees are encouraged to dedicate significant time to becoming AI-literate to better contribute to organizational goals.
- Cuban emphasizes that AI is a tool for smarter decision-making rather than a guaranteed shortcut for business success.
- He warns against using AI to outsource critical thinking, noting that workers should use the technology to accelerate their learning.

### Why it Matters
This framework provides a practical benchmark for professionals navigating the shifting labor market as companies integrate new technologies. By prioritizing AI literacy and leadership competence, employees can better position themselves for long-term career stability and professional growth.
