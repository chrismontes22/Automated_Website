SJCAM C400 review: affordable vlogging camera with all the trimmings
============================================================

The SJCAM C400 action camera offers budget-conscious content creators a versatile, accessory-packed vlogging solution for approximately $200, providing a cost-effective alternative to premium GoPro and DJI flagship models.

### Key Points
- The SJCAM C400 records 4K video at 30fps or 2K at 60fps and includes a 64GB SD card.
- The device features dual touchscreens, including a front-facing display for easier self-recording and framing.
- It comes with an extensive accessory bundle, including a remote control handle, mini-tripod, and a waterproof case.
- The camera lacks native water resistance and utilizes basic 6-axis gyroscopic stabilisation rather than advanced hardware-accelerated algorithms.
- Pricing is set at roughly $206 in the US and £185 in the UK, significantly undercutting major competitors.

### Why it Matters
This camera provides a high-value entry point for casual vloggers and social media creators who do not require the professional-grade stabilisation or high-frame-rate capabilities of expensive flagships. By bundling essential accessories, it lowers the barrier to entry for users seeking a complete, ready-to-use filming kit on a limited budget.
