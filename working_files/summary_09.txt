UGREEN DXP4800 Pro review: This is the best 4-bay NAS you can get in 2026
============================================================

The UGREEN DXP4800 Pro is a high-performance 4-bay NAS featuring an Intel Core i3-1315U processor, 10 Gigabit Ethernet, and advanced hardware transcoding capabilities for demanding media server users.

### Key Points
- Powered by a 6-core Intel Core i3-1315U processor with native AV1 hardware decoding.
- Includes 10 Gigabit Ethernet and 2.5GbE ports for high-speed network connectivity.
- Supports up to 96GB of DDR5 RAM and features dual M.2 slots for SSD caching.
- Priced at $719, offering a $63 premium over the existing DXP4800 Plus model.
- Features a durable metal unibody chassis with a toolless drive installation design.
- Compatible with third-party operating systems like TrueNAS for users requiring more software utilities.

### Why it Matters
The DXP4800 Pro provides a future-proof hardware solution for users managing extensive 4K media libraries or high-bandwidth data storage needs. By offering superior transcoding performance and 10GbE connectivity at a competitive price point, it stands out as a top-tier choice for home and professional NAS deployments in 2026.
