4 smart home projects you should never DIY (even if the subreddit says you can)
============================================================

Homeowners looking to upgrade their smart home systems should identify which complex installations, such as electrical panels and water monitors, require professional assistance to ensure safety and efficiency.

### Key Points
- Electrical panel upgrades and modifications should be handled by licensed electricians to avoid fire hazards and ensure compliance with building codes.
- Installing a Moen Flo smart water monitor requires professional plumbing expertise because it connects directly to the home's main water supply line.
- Hardwired security systems and cameras often involve complex cable routing and drilling that may be better suited for professional installers.
- Replacing ISP-provided modems and routers with custom hardware can improve network security but may lead to cable management issues if not installed correctly.
- Wireless smart home devices, such as standalone cameras, are generally user-friendly and do not require professional installation.

### Why it Matters
Recognizing the limits of DIY home improvement is essential for preventing costly property damage, fire risks, and potential insurance complications. Hiring professionals for high-stakes infrastructure tasks ensures that smart home technology functions reliably while maintaining the long-term safety and value of the residence.
