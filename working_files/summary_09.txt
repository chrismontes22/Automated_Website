2027's 'Tomb Raider' Remake: Unreal Engine 5 and AI-Assisted Assets 'Refined' By Humans
============================================================

Flying Wild Hog and Crystal Dynamics have announced Tomb Raider: Legacy of Atlantis, an Unreal Engine 5 remake of the 1996 original scheduled for release on February 12, 2027.

### Key Points
- The game is a full-scale remake of the original 1996 title, featuring updated puzzles, combat, and platforming mechanics.
- Development utilizes Unreal Engine 5, with Flying Wild Hog leading the project under guidance from Crystal Dynamics.
- Developers confirmed the use of AI-assisted tools for early exploration, stating all final assets were refined or replaced by human artists.
- The game will launch on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, PC, and the upcoming Switch 2 console.
- Initial community reception has been mixed, with significant player concern regarding the integration of generative AI in game development.

### Why it Matters
This project highlights the growing tension between major game studios adopting AI-assisted workflows and a player base increasingly skeptical of generative technology in creative media. The title's performance and reception will serve as a critical test case for how developers balance efficiency tools with the demand for human-crafted artistic integrity.
