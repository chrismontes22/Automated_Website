Galaxy S25 owners could see stable One UI 8.5 land in just a few days
============================================================

Samsung is reportedly preparing to release the stable One UI 8.5 update for the Galaxy S25 series this week, following a global rollout schedule beginning around May 8.

### Key Points
- The stable One UI 8.5 update is expected to launch in South Korea this week before expanding to North America and Europe.
- Canadian users are projected to receive the software update by May 9, according to recent leaks from tipster @theonecid.
- The release follows an extensive beta program that included 10 iterations to address development delays related to Android 15 and One UI 7.
- New features include refined user interface tweaks and upgraded agentic AI capabilities designed to better predict and automate user tasks.
- The update applies to the entire Galaxy S25 lineup, including the standard S25, S25 Plus, and S25 Ultra models.

### Why it Matters
This update represents a critical effort by Samsung to stabilize its software ecosystem after significant development delays hindered the initial rollout of Android 15. By integrating advanced agentic AI, the company aims to improve device performance and maintain the competitive relevance of its flagship S25 series in the premium smartphone market.
