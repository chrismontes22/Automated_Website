iPhone 18 Could Come With 12GB of RAM
============================================================

Apple is reportedly planning to equip the standard iPhone 18 with 12GB of RAM and a 2nm A20 chip to support advanced on-device artificial intelligence features starting in 2027.

### Key Points
- Analyst Dan Nystedt reports the base iPhone 18 will match the 12GB of memory previously reserved for Pro-tier models.
- The device will likely feature the A20 chip, manufactured using TSMC’s 2nm process for a 15% performance increase over 3nm technology.
- Increased memory capacity is intended to support intensive Apple Intelligence workloads expected in the upcoming iOS 27 software.
- Apple reportedly plans to delay the standard iPhone 18 release until early 2027, alongside the iPhone 18e and a new iPhone Air model.

### Why it Matters
Upgrading the entry-level hardware ensures that Apple’s full suite of AI features remains accessible across its entire future smartphone lineup. This shift reflects the growing memory requirements of on-device generative AI and signals a strategic move to maintain performance parity between standard and premium devices.
