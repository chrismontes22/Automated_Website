Google Cloud Next proves what we suspected: Everything is AI now
============================================================

Google Cloud Next showcased a heavy focus on artificial intelligence, featuring new custom chips and the rebranded Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform alongside discussions regarding recent cybersecurity model vulnerabilities.

### Key Points
- Google introduced two versions of its 8th-generation Tensor chips, specifically optimized for either AI training or inference tasks.
- The Vertex AI development platform was rebranded as the Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform to centralize the management of AI agents.
- Cybersecurity experts analyzed a recent security breach involving Anthropic’s Mythos model, which allowed for unauthorized access.
- Early results from Project Glasswing indicate that the Mythos model may perform below the high expectations set by initial marketing hype.

### Why it Matters
These developments highlight Google's aggressive strategy to integrate generative AI across its entire cloud infrastructure and enterprise development ecosystem. The security concerns surrounding models like Mythos underscore the growing risks and performance challenges companies face as they rapidly deploy AI tools.
