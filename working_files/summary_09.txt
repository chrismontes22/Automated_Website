There’s A Translucent Green Xbox Series X That Looks So Awesome I Don’t Want To Know The Price
============================================================

Microsoft unveiled the limited-edition Xbox Series X25 console featuring a translucent green design to commemorate the 25th anniversary of the original Xbox ahead of the 2026 holiday season.

### Key Points
- The Xbox Series X25 Limited Edition features a 1TB SSD housed in a translucent green chassis.
- A matching translucent OG Green controller will be sold separately, incorporating design elements from the original "Duke" gamepad.
- Both products are scheduled for release in November 2026 to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the Xbox brand.
- Microsoft has not yet disclosed pricing or specific preorder dates for the limited-edition hardware.

### Why it Matters
This release leverages nostalgia to drive hardware sales during the competitive holiday shopping season. By updating iconic design aesthetics for modern consoles, Microsoft aims to strengthen brand loyalty among long-term fans while maintaining interest in the current Xbox Series X platform.
