Gemini won't cut you off as much, and its reliability improved in Google Home
============================================================

Google is rolling out a July update for the Home app and Gemini, introducing improved AI conversational reliability, new automation suggestions, and enhanced device controls for smart home users.

### Key Points
- Gemini now features improved visual layouts for weather and general knowledge, alongside more accurate sports data for events like the FIFA World Cup.
- The "Continued Conversations" feature has been updated to increase reliability when processing follow-up questions and rapid-fire voice commands.
- Google Home version 4.20 introduces "Suggested Automations" for tasks like home security and energy savings, located within the app's Automations tab.
- New light control buttons are available for Walmart onn Outdoor Camera Plug-in and Floodlight Camera Hardwired devices.
- The update resolves several technical issues, including camera live stream refresh failures, thermostat fan flickering, and smart door lock status display errors.

### Why it Matters
These updates demonstrate Google's ongoing commitment to refining its AI-driven smart home ecosystem by prioritizing stability and user-friendly automation. By streamlining device management and improving AI responsiveness, Google aims to reduce technical friction for users managing increasingly complex connected home environments.
