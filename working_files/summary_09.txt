MagSafe battery banks: Best picks for your iPhone 17, iPhone Air, and more
============================================================

Apple has released a new $99 MagSafe battery pack for the iPhone Air, prompting a comparison with third-party Qi2 alternatives that often offer faster charging speeds and higher capacities.

### Key Points
- Apple’s official MagSafe battery for the iPhone Air is priced at $99 and limited to 12W charging speeds.
- The Anker Nano Power Bank offers 15W Qi2 charging and a 5000mAh capacity for $54.99.
- Baseus provides a budget-friendly 5000mAh option for $24.99, though it is limited to 7.5W wireless charging.
- UGREEN’s 10000mAh battery supports Qi2.2 for up to 25W charging, requiring an iPhone 16 or newer.
- The $99.99 Belkin 25W power bank features a 10000mAh capacity, a built-in kickstand, and a secondary MagSafe ring for accessories.

### Why it Matters
Third-party manufacturers are increasingly outperforming Apple’s official accessories by leveraging the Qi2 standard to provide faster charging and greater battery capacity at lower price points. This shift offers consumers more functional alternatives that better utilize the full charging capabilities of modern iPhone models.
