AI for Better Ad Creative: 3 Steps to Better Results
============================================================

Marketers can scale high-quality ad creative by using a three-step AI system to build brand-specific knowledge bases and generate targeted visual and written content for digital platforms.

### Key Points
- Conduct deep research using LLMs like Google Gemini to identify customer pain points, objections, and Reddit discussions.
- Create a dedicated Claude Project to store brand documents, customer reviews, and past performance data for consistent AI context.
- Use Nano Banana 2 Pro within Gemini to generate high-resolution, 4K product images based on specific, AI-refined prompts.
- Leverage AI to draft video scripts and ad copy, then refine the output manually to ensure human nuance and brand alignment.
- Utilize Photoshop’s generative fill for structural image adjustments to avoid the inconsistencies of regenerating entire AI-generated assets.

### Why it Matters
This systematic approach allows smaller brands to maintain the high volume of ad variations required by modern algorithms without needing massive production budgets or large creative teams. By grounding AI in proprietary brand data, marketers can produce professional-grade assets that resonate with target audiences while significantly reducing production time and costs.
