Bitcoin buyers add over 250,000 BTC between $59,000 and $67,000 as accumulation returns
============================================================

Bitcoin investors have aggressively accumulated over 259,000 BTC since June 5, signaling a shift toward broad-based buying across retail and whale cohorts as prices fluctuate between $59,000 and $67,000.

### Key Points
- Investors purchased a net 259,298 BTC over the past 10 days while prices remained within the $59,000 to $67,000 range.
- Glassnode data confirms the Accumulation Trend Score has reached 1.0, the highest possible reading for buying intensity.
- Accumulation is occurring across all wallet sizes, ranging from retail holders with under 1 BTC to large entities holding up to 1,000 BTC.
- This buying behavior marks a significant reversal from the period between March and May, when most cohorts acted as net sellers.
- The current peak in accumulation represents the strongest purchasing activity observed throughout the cryptocurrency's recent market drawdown.

### Why it Matters
This widespread accumulation suggests that market participants are increasingly confident in Bitcoin's value at current price levels following months of stagnation. The shift from distribution to aggressive buying may provide a stronger foundation for future price stability and potential recovery.
