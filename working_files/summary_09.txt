‘AI Should Serve People, Not Replace Them’: AI Founder Has a Plan to Empower Small Businesses and Communities
============================================================

Texas-based artificial intelligence company Lineage has introduced Bishop, a new AI platform designed to assist families and small business owners with daily tasks, currently available in private beta.

### Key Points
- Lineage launched Bishop, an AI tool focused on task execution rather than just providing information.
- The platform is designed to handle scheduling, email, research, and errands for individuals and small organizations.
- Former congressman Dr. Ron Paul has joined the company as an advisor to support its mission of individual empowerment.
- Bishop operates on a membership-based model that explicitly excludes data harvesting and advertising.
- Interested users can join the private beta waitlist at the company's official website, lineageco.io.

### Why it Matters
- By focusing on task completion rather than simple data retrieval, Bishop aims to provide small businesses and families with the operational capacity typically reserved for large enterprises. This approach challenges the current industry trend of centralizing AI power, potentially shifting the competitive landscape for independent entrepreneurs and local organizations.
