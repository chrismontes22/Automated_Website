“Still one of my favorites”: This dependable 2‑in‑1 is discounted again while modern laptops remain scarce
============================================================

Best Buy is currently offering the Lenovo Yoga 7 2-in-1 laptop at a discounted price of $799.99, providing a cost-effective alternative amid ongoing industry-wide RAM supply shortages.

### Key Points
- The discounted Lenovo Yoga 7 features an AMD Ryzen AI 7 350 processor, 16GB of RAM, and a 1TB SSD.
- The device includes a 14-inch 2K OLED touchscreen and an NPU capable of 50 TOPS for AI-driven tasks.
- Battery life is rated at approximately 13 hours and 20 minutes, supporting extended daily productivity.
- Additional configurations are available at Best Buy, with prices starting as low as $699.99 for the 512GB SSD model.
- The laptop is equipped with security features including a fingerprint reader and Windows Hello facial recognition.

### Why it Matters
This sale offers consumers a high-performance hardware option during a period where RAM availability has constrained the market and inflated prices for newer devices. By providing a reliable 2-in-1 alternative at a reduced price point, the deal helps users secure essential computing power without paying premium MSRP rates.
