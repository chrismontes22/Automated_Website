Prediction market traders bet bitcoin's selloff has further to run
============================================================

Prediction market traders are increasingly wagering that bitcoin will face further price declines this year, citing heavy U.S. ETF outflows and shifting investor interest toward high-performing artificial intelligence stocks.

### Key Points
- Prediction platforms Kalshi and Polymarket assign a 66% to 67% probability that bitcoin will fall below $55,000 before the end of 2024.
- Traders estimate a 50% chance that bitcoin prices will drop below $50,000 and a 31% chance of the asset dipping under $40,000.
- U.S.-listed bitcoin ETFs recorded $2.4 billion in outflows during May and an additional $1 billion in the first two trading days of June.
- Investors are increasingly rotating capital into stablecoins like USDT and USDC rather than exiting the cryptocurrency market entirely.
- Analysts at K33 Research suggest that bitcoin is losing investor attention to AI-related stocks, which currently offer higher perceived growth opportunities.

### Why it Matters
The shift in sentiment reflects a broader cooling of institutional demand for digital assets as investors weigh the opportunity costs of holding bitcoin against record-breaking equity markets. This trend suggests that while capital remains within the crypto ecosystem, market participants are increasingly cautious and waiting for more favorable entry points.
