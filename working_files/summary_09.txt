Apple reveals WWDC 26 in the shadow of Google I/O
============================================================

Apple has officially scheduled its annual Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) for June 8-12, 2026, while industry attention remains focused on Google’s upcoming I/O event occurring this week.

### Key Points
- Apple’s WWDC 2026 will take place from June 8 to June 12, featuring a keynote presentation on June 8 at 10 am PT.
- The conference will include the Platforms State of the Union, video sessions, developer labs, and the annual Apple Design Awards.
- Google is scheduled to host its I/O 2026 event on May 19 at 1 pm ET.
- Expected announcements at Google I/O include updates to Gemini Intelligence, Android 17, and the debut of the Android XR platform.
- Samsung may potentially showcase its first pair of smart glasses during the Google I/O presentation.

### Why it Matters
These back-to-back developer conferences highlight the intensifying competition between Apple and Google as both companies race to integrate advanced artificial intelligence and extended reality into their mobile ecosystems. The announcements will set the trajectory for consumer software and hardware standards for the remainder of the year.
