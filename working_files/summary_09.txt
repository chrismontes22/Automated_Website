Surfshark vs NordVPN: Which VPN service is better?
============================================================

This comprehensive comparison of NordVPN and Surfshark evaluates their security features, streaming performance, and pricing to help users choose the best virtual private network for their needs.

**Key points**
- NordVPN operates 8,300 servers in 126 countries, while Surfshark maintains 3,200 servers across 95 countries.
- Surfshark allows unlimited simultaneous device connections, whereas NordVPN limits users to 10 devices.
- Both providers utilize RAM-only servers and have undergone independent security audits by firms like Deloitte and PwC.
- NordVPN offers advanced specialty options like Onion over VPN and Double VPN, while Surfshark focuses on budget-friendly bundles including antivirus and data removal tools.
- Pricing for long-term plans starts at $1.99 per month for Surfshark and $3.49 per month for NordVPN.

**Why it matters: **
Choosing the right VPN is essential for maintaining online privacy and bypassing geo-restrictions on streaming platforms. Understanding the trade-offs between NordVPN’s advanced security ecosystem and Surfshark’s cost-effective, unlimited-device model helps consumers maximize value and digital protection.
