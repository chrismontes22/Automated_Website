9to5Mac Daily: May 19, 2026 – WWDC invites, iOS 27 expectations
============================================================

Apple has officially sent invitations for the upcoming WWDC26 keynote, where the company is expected to unveil iOS 27 alongside a new custom wallpaper feature and other software updates.

### Key Points
- Apple issued official invitations for the WWDC26 keynote event to showcase upcoming software developments.
- Reports indicate that the upcoming iOS 27 update will introduce new custom wallpaper features for users.
- The Department of Justice has reportedly requested that Apple and Google identify over 100,000 users of a specific car-related application.
- Apple announced more than 30 finalists for the annual Apple Design Awards to recognize excellence in app development.

### Why it Matters
These announcements highlight Apple's ongoing focus on software innovation and platform security ahead of its major developer conference. The legal request regarding user data also underscores the increasing regulatory scrutiny facing major technology companies regarding privacy and data access.
