The new ASUS ProArt PZ14 is a better Windows tablet, but far from a perfect one
============================================================

The ASUS ProArt PZ14 is a premium 14-inch Windows tablet featuring a high-quality OLED touchscreen and Snapdragon processor, though its detachable keyboard design limits its utility for traditional typing.

### Key Points
- The device is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon X2 Elite processor with 32GB of LPDDR5X RAM and a 1TB SSD.
- It features a 14-inch OLED touchscreen with 2880 x 1800 resolution, 144Hz refresh rate, and 98% P3 color gamut coverage.
- Included accessories consist of the ASUS Pen 3.0 stylus, which supports 4,096 levels of pressure sensitivity.
- Connectivity options include two USB4 ports, an SD Express card reader, Wi-Fi 7, and Bluetooth 5.4.
- The unit weighs 0.79kg and provides approximately 13 hours and 20 minutes of battery life.

### Why it Matters
- This device targets digital artists and creatives who prioritize a portable, high-fidelity drawing surface over a traditional laptop form factor. While it offers strong performance for creative applications, the suboptimal keyboard and Windows 11 tablet interface may deter users who require a primary machine for heavy typing tasks.
