Bitdefender Identity Theft Protection review
============================================================

Bitdefender Identity Theft Protection offers an affordable, beginner-friendly solution for individual users, providing essential dark web monitoring, credit reporting, and identity restoration services starting at $69.99 per year.

### Key Points
- Plans include the Standard option at $69.99 annually and the Premium option at $99.99 annually.
- Core features include dark web monitoring, SSN tracking, credit score simulation, and three-bureau credit reporting.
- Subscribers receive up to $2 million in identity theft insurance and dedicated expert assistance for restoration.
- The service lacks family plans, automated data broker removal, and the ability to execute credit freezes directly from the dashboard.
- The platform is designed for ease of use with an intuitive, lag-free interface suitable for cybersecurity beginners.

### Why it Matters
This service provides a cost-effective entry point for individuals seeking basic financial and identity monitoring compared to more expensive competitors like Norton LifeLock. However, the absence of family plans and advanced automation tools may limit its appeal for households or users requiring comprehensive, all-in-one security management.
