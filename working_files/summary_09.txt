Meta is reportedly working on smart glasses that would be recording all the time
============================================================

Meta is developing prototype smart glasses capable of continuous audio and image capture, potentially transforming the wearable device into an always-aware personal AI assistant for daily user support.

### Key Points
- The "super sensing" glasses would continuously record audio and capture images every few seconds to provide context for Meta AI queries.
- Meta is considering a system where raw data is processed as metadata on servers rather than stored directly by the user or the company.
- Internal discussions include potentially using the captured environmental data to train future AI models.
- The company plans to keep the LED recording indicator off during "AI Feature" use to prevent user desensitization to the light.
- Meta is currently implementing software updates to disable cameras if the LED recording indicator is detected as tampered with.

### Why it Matters
This technology represents a significant shift toward ambient computing where AI assistants maintain constant awareness of a user's surroundings. However, the project faces intense scrutiny regarding privacy, surveillance, and the ethical implications of recording public spaces without clear visual indicators.
