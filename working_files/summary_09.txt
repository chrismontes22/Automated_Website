Apple is working to incorporate AI agents on the App Store, per report
============================================================

Apple is reportedly developing a new system to integrate AI agents into the App Store while ensuring these autonomous tools comply with strict privacy and security regulations.

### Key Points
- Apple is designing a framework to regulate AI agents that perform complex tasks on behalf of users.
- The initiative aims to prevent unauthorized app creation and potential security risks associated with autonomous software.
- The company may unveil these App Store updates during the Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) next month.
- CEO Tim Cook recently noted increased demand for Mac mini and Mac Studio hardware to support AI agent workloads.

### Why it Matters
This development highlights Apple's attempt to balance the rapid growth of autonomous AI technology with its long-standing commitment to platform security and ecosystem control. Successfully integrating these agents could protect Apple's lucrative App Store revenue while providing users with safer, more capable automated tools.
