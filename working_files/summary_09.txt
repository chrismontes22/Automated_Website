Your Pixel can do 5 new things it couldn't do last month
============================================================

Google’s March 2026 Pixel Drop update introduces several quality-of-life improvements, including native flashlight brightness controls, enhanced location privacy indicators, and new customization options for the home screen interface.

**Key points**
- The QPR3 update adds a native slider for adjustable flashlight brightness within the Quick Settings panel.
- Users can now remove the At a Glance widget from the home screen while keeping it active on the lock screen.
- Adaptive Connectivity settings are now split into two independent toggles for more granular control over network switching.
- A new blue status bar indicator alerts users in real-time when an application is accessing location data.
- The update includes AI-generated icon packs available through the Wallpaper & style menu in the Pixel Launcher.

**Why it matters: **
These updates provide Pixel users with greater control over system behavior and privacy, addressing long-standing requests for more granular device management. By refining core features like connectivity and location tracking, Google continues to improve the daily usability of its smartphone ecosystem.
