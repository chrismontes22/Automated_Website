2024 iPad Mini vs. 2026 iPad Air Buyer's Guide: 20+ Differences Compared
============================================================

Apple’s eighth-generation iPad Air and seventh-generation iPad mini offer distinct advantages, forcing consumers to choose between the M4-powered productivity of the Air and the compact portability of the mini.

### Key Points
- The iPad Air features the M4 chip, 12GB of unified memory, and Wi-Fi 7, while the iPad mini utilizes the A17 Pro chip and 8GB of memory.
- iPad Air models are available in 11-inch and 13-inch sizes, whereas the iPad mini maintains a compact 8.3-inch display.
- The iPad Air supports the Magic Keyboard via a Smart Connector, positioning it as a more viable laptop replacement for productivity tasks.
- Pricing starts at $499 for the iPad mini, while the 11-inch iPad Air begins at $599 and the 13-inch model starts at $799.
- A next-generation iPad mini featuring an OLED display and A19 Pro chip is currently expected to launch in the second half of 2026.

### Why it Matters
- Choosing between these models depends on whether a user prioritizes high-performance multitasking and screen real estate or extreme portability for reading and travel. The $100 price difference makes the iPad Air a compelling value for those seeking longevity and laptop-like functionality, while the iPad mini remains a niche device for specific mobile use cases.
