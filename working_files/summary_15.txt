The rumored 20th anniversary iPhone design will be applied to both Pro models
============================================================

Apple is reportedly planning a significant design overhaul for its 2027 iPhone Pro models, featuring a quad-curved screen rather than a standalone 20th-anniversary commemorative device as previously rumored.

### Key Points
- The 2027 iPhone Pro and Pro Max models will feature a new quad-curved screen design.
- Apple is exploring the integration of under-display cameras and Face ID sensors for future Pro iterations.
- The rumored special 20th-anniversary iPhone model is not currently in development as a separate product.
- The upcoming design shift marks a departure from the flat-sided aesthetic established by the iPhone X in 2017.

### Why it Matters
This design shift represents a major aesthetic evolution for Apple’s flagship smartphone lineup as it approaches the two-decade milestone of the original iPhone. By adopting advanced display technology, the company aims to differentiate its premium hardware in a competitive market that has largely standardized the flat-screen slab design.
