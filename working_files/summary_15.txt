FOMC minutes, SpaceX joins Nasdaq 100: Crypto Week Ahead
============================================================

Cryptocurrency markets face a pivotal week as investors monitor Federal Reserve policy minutes, key macroeconomic data, and the inclusion of SpaceX into the Nasdaq 100 index.

### Key Points
- SpaceX joins the Nasdaq 100 on July 7, becoming the fourth index member to hold bitcoin with its 18,712 BTC reserve.
- American Bitcoin (ABTC) continues trading following a 1-for-15 reverse stock split designed to maintain its Nasdaq listing.
- The Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC) releases its latest meeting minutes on July 8, providing insight into future Federal Reserve interest rate decisions.
- Berachain (BERA) is scheduled to implement its PoL Next upgrade on July 7.
- Multiple decentralized autonomous organizations, including ENS, Frax, and Arbitrum, are currently conducting governance votes on budget allocations and protocol updates.

### Why it Matters
These events highlight the increasing intersection between traditional financial markets and digital assets as major corporations and economic indicators influence crypto sentiment. Investors are closely watching the yen's volatility and U.S. inflation data to gauge how macroeconomic shifts will impact bitcoin's price correlation and broader market liquidity.
