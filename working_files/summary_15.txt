Samsung appoints a new head for its TV business
============================================================

Samsung Electronics has appointed President Won-Jin Lee to lead its Visual Display division, tasking the marketing and content expert with driving growth for its TV and monitor business segments.

### Key Points
- Won-Jin Lee, formerly head of the Global Marketing Office, now leads the Visual Display division.
- The division oversees Samsung’s global production and sales of televisions, monitors, and soundbar products.
- Former division head Seok Woo Yong will transition to an advisory role under DX division leader Tae Moon Roh.
- Seok Woo Yong will focus on developing future strategies for artificial intelligence and robotics technologies.
- The leadership shuffle aims to leverage Lee’s market insights to identify new growth opportunities for the company.

### Why it Matters
This leadership transition signals a strategic shift toward integrating content and services into Samsung’s hardware ecosystem to maintain its competitive edge in the display market. By reassigning R&D expertise to AI and robotics, the company is positioning its Device Experience division to prioritize long-term technological innovation.
