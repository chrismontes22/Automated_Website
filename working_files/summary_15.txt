Healthcare IT solutions provider ChipSoft hit by ransomware attack
============================================================

Dutch healthcare software provider ChipSoft has taken its digital services offline following a ransomware attack that disrupted Electronic Health Record systems across the Netherlands and Belgium.

### Key Points
- ChipSoft disabled its Zorgportaal, HiX Mobile, and Zorgplatform services to contain the unauthorized access.
- The Dutch cybersecurity agency Z-CERT is assisting the company and affected healthcare institutions with recovery efforts.
- Multiple hospitals, including Sint Jans Gasthuis and Flevo Hospital, reported system outages following the incident.
- The breach has extended beyond the Netherlands, impacting several healthcare facilities located in Belgium.
- ChipSoft is a major provider of the HiX platform, which serves as a central hub for patient data in numerous hospitals.

### Why it Matters
This incident highlights the systemic risks posed by cyberattacks on centralized healthcare IT providers that manage sensitive data for multiple institutions. Such disruptions can severely impact hospital operations and patient care, underscoring the critical need for robust security measures in the medical software supply chain.
