Marvel Actor Still In Disbelief Over Disney’s Sweeping Layoffs, Claims Artists ‘Are Now Being Replaced By AI’
============================================================

Marvel actor Evangeline Lilly has criticized Disney for recent layoffs within its visual development team, raising concerns that the company is replacing human concept artists with generative AI technology.

### Key Points
- Disney recently laid off nearly 10 percent of its Marvel visual development team, which creates iconic character designs for the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
- Longtime concept artist Andy Park, known for his work on the Wasp suit and Captain America, was among those dismissed from the studio.
- Evangeline Lilly publicly condemned the downsizing, alleging that AI is being used to iterate on designs originally created by human artists.
- Disney previously entered a $1 billion deal with OpenAI to explore AI-generated video content using its licensed intellectual property.
- The visual development team was established by Kevin Feige to maintain a unified aesthetic across Marvel’s film, television, and streaming projects.

### Why it Matters
These layoffs highlight growing tensions between Hollywood studios and creative professionals regarding the integration of generative AI into film production. The shift threatens the job security of artists who define the visual identity of major franchises while raising questions about the future quality of studio-produced content.
