Connecting Your Car to Home Assistant
============================================================

The MeatPi WiCAN dongle allows users to integrate vehicle data directly into Home Assistant by connecting to the car's OBD-II port for real-time monitoring and remote diagnostics.

### Key Points
- The WiCAN device utilizes an ESP32-S3 microcontroller and runs open-source firmware to bridge vehicle data to local Wi-Fi networks.
- Users can monitor specific vehicle metrics including fuel levels, coolant temperature, and upcoming service intervals through the Home Assistant dashboard.
- MeatPi provides official documentation to assist with the configuration process, which requires car-specific adjustments to interpret ECU data correctly.
- Remote access to OBD-II data enables proactive maintenance by identifying potential engine or battery issues before they result in costly repairs.

### Why it Matters
Integrating vehicle diagnostics into smart home platforms provides a centralized way to track automotive health and performance metrics alongside household systems. This connectivity helps owners identify mechanical issues early, potentially preventing expensive failures and improving overall vehicle maintenance efficiency.
