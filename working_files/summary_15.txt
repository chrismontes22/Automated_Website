Apple CarPlay Just Got Three Popular iPhone Apps
============================================================

Apple has expanded its CarPlay ecosystem by integrating ChatGPT, Google Meet, and Audiomack, while simultaneously testing a significantly improved version of the WhatsApp application for vehicle dashboard displays.

### Key Points
- OpenAI’s ChatGPT now supports voice-based conversations on CarPlay, allowing users to interact with the chatbot safely while driving.
- Google Meet is available for audio-only participation in meetings and viewing schedules, with video functionality disabled for safety.
- Music streaming service Audiomack has launched a CarPlay interface featuring access to charts, playlists, and personal libraries.
- WhatsApp is currently beta testing a redesigned CarPlay app that adds dedicated tabs for recent chats, calls, and favorite contacts.
- Users must update to the latest versions of these applications on their iPhones to enable the new CarPlay features automatically.

### Why it Matters
These updates enhance the utility of the CarPlay platform by providing drivers with more sophisticated communication and entertainment tools while maintaining safety standards. As vehicle dashboards become increasingly integrated with mobile software, these improvements signal a shift toward more functional, voice-first interfaces for commuters.
