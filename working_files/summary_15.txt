Philips Hue and WiZ Launch Sports Live Feature for the 2026 World Cup
============================================================

Signify has introduced the Sports Live feature for Philips Hue and WiZ smart lighting, allowing users to synchronize home lighting effects with real-time match data during soccer games.

### Key Points
- Sports Live triggers lighting changes for specific events like goals, yellow cards, and red cards without requiring external HDMI hardware.
- The feature is available through the Philips Hue and WiZ mobile apps, supporting team-specific color schemes and neutral lighting during tied matches.
- Users can adjust broadcast delays to ensure lighting effects align perfectly with their specific television feed.
- The update is compatible with the 2026 FIFA World Cup, currently hosted across Mexico, Canada, and the United States.
- Philips Hue app version 5.69 also introduces a new Bridge zone feature to consolidate device management and automation groups.

### Why it Matters
This integration represents a shift toward software-based smart home synchronization that removes the need for expensive, dedicated hardware like HDMI sync boxes. By leveraging live data feeds, Signify enhances the immersive viewing experience for sports fans while increasing the utility of its existing lighting ecosystem.
