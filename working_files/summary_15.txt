Here’s everything new for CarPlay in iOS 27
============================================================

Apple is set to launch iOS 27 this fall, introducing significant CarPlay upgrades including advanced AI-powered Siri capabilities, native video app support, and refreshed interface design elements for drivers.

### Key Points
- Siri now features a glowing orb interface and improved conversational context, with all interactions automatically syncing to the iPhone’s Siri app history.
- Developers can now build dedicated video browsing apps for CarPlay, provided the vehicle manufacturer supports the new in-car video feature.
- The interface includes updated Liquid Glass app icons, new system wallpapers, and a redesigned MiniPlayer for media apps.
- Users can now perform audio scrubbing directly on the Now Playing screen using an enhanced progress indicator.

### Why it Matters
These updates represent a major shift toward making CarPlay a more autonomous, content-rich platform that mirrors the full functionality of the iPhone. By integrating advanced AI and native video browsing, Apple is increasing the utility of vehicle displays while deepening the ecosystem connection between mobile devices and automotive hardware.
