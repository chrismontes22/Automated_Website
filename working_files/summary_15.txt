This British farm is running AI on pig muck — and it could lead to a major windfall for farmers energy income, earning ten times more than the grid
============================================================

British farms are converting pig slurry into electricity via anaerobic digesters to power on-site AI data centers, creating a new revenue stream that significantly outperforms selling energy to grids.

### Key Points
- Easy Compute manages the Green Compute network, which installs AI hardware directly at agricultural sites in northwest England.
- Anaerobic digesters convert pig slurry into biogas, generating renewable electricity that is consumed locally for high-efficiency computing.
- Using electricity for AI processing generates 10 times more income than selling the same power to the national grid.
- On-site computing reduces the payback period for renewable energy infrastructure from 15 years down to approximately four years.
- Hardware can switch to mining cryptocurrency on the Bittensor blockchain to maintain financial productivity during periods of low commercial demand.

### Why it Matters
This decentralized model offers a sustainable alternative to massive, energy-intensive hyperscale data centers while providing British farmers with a vital new income source. By integrating renewable energy production with high-demand AI infrastructure, the project improves the financial viability of green technology investments.
