Notion shutting down its AI-powered email client, including Mac and iOS apps
============================================================

Notion has announced it will discontinue its AI-powered email client, Notion Mail, on September 22, shifting its focus toward automated email management through its existing AI agent technology.

### Key Points
- Notion Mail will officially shut down across web, desktop, and iOS platforms on September 22.
- The company is pivoting its strategy to prioritize AI agents that manage email workflows without requiring users to open a traditional inbox.
- Users can begin exporting their Notion Mail data starting June 25.
- September 21 serves as the final deadline for users to save or export any remaining data before permanent deletion occurs.
- Notion launched the standalone email service in April 2025, meaning the product will have existed for less than six months.

### Why it Matters
This decision highlights a strategic shift toward autonomous AI agents over traditional interface-based email clients within the productivity software market. By prioritizing agent-led workflows, Notion aims to streamline user tasks rather than simply providing another platform for manual inbox management.
