Beyond Siri: Here are the practical AI features coming to your iPhone in iOS 27
============================================================

Apple is integrating Apple Intelligence across its iOS 27 software suite to automate everyday tasks, enhance app functionality, and improve user productivity through seamless, background-integrated artificial intelligence features.

### Key Points
- Apple Cash will allow users to split restaurant bills by scanning receipts and automatically calculating individual shares, tips, and taxes.
- A new password-updating tool will automatically identify compromised credentials and navigate websites to upgrade security on the user's behalf.
- Messages will provide one-tap suggestions for reminders, calendar events, and photo sharing based on conversation context and keywords.
- The Call Context feature will surface relevant information, such as reservation codes, directly on the screen during customer service phone calls.
- Shortcuts will allow users to create complex automations by describing desired tasks in natural language rather than manual scripting.
- Safari will use AI to automatically group open tabs into relevant topics, such as travel or research, to improve browser organization.

### Why it Matters
By embedding AI directly into existing apps, Apple is shifting its strategy from standalone chatbot interfaces to invisible, utility-focused automation. This approach aims to increase user engagement with the Apple ecosystem by solving specific, real-world problems without requiring a steep learning curve.
