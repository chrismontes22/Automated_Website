The top content formats & types that earn AI search citations
============================================================

Marketers are increasingly adopting Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) to ensure their content is cited by AI tools like Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity through improved structural clarity.

### Key Points
- AEO focuses on structuring content so AI engines can easily extract, understand, and attribute specific passages to a brand.
- High-performing content utilizes question-led headings, direct-answer summaries, and explicit question-and-answer blocks to improve citation rates.
- Implementing semantic schema—such as Article, FAQPage, and Person markup—provides a machine-readable layer that clarifies brand and author authority.
- Strategic internal linking and topic clusters help engines crawl and associate related content, signaling topical authority to AI models.
- Key performance indicators for AEO include tracking citation frequency, mention quality, and assisted conversions to measure real-world business impact.

### Why it Matters
- As search behavior shifts from blue links to synthesized AI answers, visibility now depends on whether an engine can extract and attribute a brand's content. Businesses that fail to adapt their site structure risk losing traffic and authority to competitors who prioritize machine-readable, answer-first content.
