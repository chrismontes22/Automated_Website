HomeKit Weekly: The Qingping thermometer brings Thread and an E-ink display to Apple Home
============================================================

Qingping has updated its E-ink digital thermometer to include Thread support, providing Apple Home users with a more reliable and responsive way to monitor individual room climate data.

### Key Points
- The updated Qingping thermometer now features Thread connectivity for faster, more stable integration with Apple Home hubs like the Apple TV and HomePod mini.
- The device utilizes a high-precision, Swiss-made sensor to track real-time temperature and humidity levels.
- Its low-power E-ink display is powered by a single coin battery, which the manufacturer claims lasts for approximately eight months.
- Users can mount the device using an included magnetic sticker or place it on surfaces, with physical controls to toggle between Celsius and Fahrenheit.
- The companion Qingping+ app allows users to export up to 30 days of historical climate data for detailed analysis.

### Why it Matters
Localized temperature sensors allow homeowners to move beyond single-thermostat systems to create more efficient, room-specific climate automation. By integrating with Apple Home via Thread, this device enables users to trigger smart plugs and fans automatically, improving overall home comfort and energy management.
