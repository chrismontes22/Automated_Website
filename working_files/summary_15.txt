Binance outflows triple to $1.2B as ETH withdrawals hit 3-year high
============================================================

Binance recorded $1.23 billion in weekly net outflows as Ethereum withdrawal transactions reached a three-year high, signaling a significant shift in user activity across major cryptocurrency exchange platforms.

### Key Points
- Binance net outflows rose 207% to $1.23 billion for the week beginning June 29.
- Ethereum withdrawal transactions on Binance hit their highest level in over three years, exceeding 166,000 in a single day.
- Monthly net outflows for the exchange reached approximately $3.2 billion.
- Other major exchanges including Bitfinex, Gate, OKX, and Bybit also reported significant weekly net outflows.
- Analysts suggest the movement may reflect long-term accumulation behavior alongside regulatory uncertainty from the EU’s MiCA framework.

### Why it Matters
- This surge in exchange outflows suggests that investors are increasingly moving assets into private custody, potentially indicating a shift toward long-term holding strategies. The trend highlights how regulatory pressures and market volatility continue to influence liquidity patterns across the centralized cryptocurrency exchange sector.
