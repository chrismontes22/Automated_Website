Spotify, UMG To Let Fans Make Their Own Music With AI
============================================================

Spotify has partnered with Universal Music Group to develop generative AI tools for music remixes while simultaneously launching a new "Reserved" feature to prioritize concert tickets for dedicated fans.

### Key Points
- Spotify and Universal Music Group signed a licensing deal to enable AI-generated covers and remixes of songs by participating artists.
- The new AI music features will eventually be offered to Spotify Premium subscribers as a paid add-on service.
- Artists and rightsholders retain the authority to opt in or out of the generative AI program based on their personal values.
- The new "Reserved" feature aims to simplify ticket purchasing by setting aside concert seats specifically for an artist's most dedicated Premium listeners.

### Why it Matters
This partnership marks a significant shift in how major labels integrate generative AI into commercial music distribution while maintaining artist control over intellectual property. By combining these AI tools with a fan-focused ticketing system, Spotify is attempting to deepen user engagement and create new revenue streams for both the platform and its talent.
