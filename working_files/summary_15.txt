How AI Is Changing Lead Generation: 3 Key Things SEO & PPC Teams Need To Do Now via @sejournal, @CallRail
============================================================

AI search tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity are reshaping the marketing funnel by pre-vetting leads and driving high-intent traffic that requires faster business response times for conversion.

### Key Points
- ChatGPT currently dominates the AI-referred lead market with a 90.1% share, particularly in the healthcare and automotive sectors.
- Perplexity accounts for 6.3% of AI leads, showing significant influence in high-consideration industries like manufacturing and travel.
- Google’s Gemini holds 2.4% of the market, with growing adoption in business services due to its integration with Google Workspace.
- Claude captures 1.2% of leads, finding a specific niche in real estate and marketing agency services.
- Businesses can improve visibility by creating authoritative, structured content that directly answers the specific queries consumers pose to AI platforms.
- Implementing unified lead tracking and AI-powered voice agents can increase answered calls by up to 44% by capturing leads during after-hours or overflow periods.

### Why it Matters
The collapse of the traditional research funnel means that businesses must now prioritize real-time responsiveness and granular attribution to capture high-intent leads. Failing to track which AI platforms drive revenue leaves companies unable to optimize their marketing spend in an increasingly automated search landscape.
