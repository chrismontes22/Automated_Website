Samsung's 100-inch smart TV is now actually affordable at $3,000 off
============================================================

Samsung has discounted its 100-inch QN80F 4K QLED smart TV by $3,000, effectively halving the original retail price to make the premium home theater display more accessible to consumers.

### Key Points
- The Samsung QN80F features a 100-inch 4K QLED display with a 144Hz refresh rate.
- Gaming performance is enhanced through integrated support for AMD FreeSync Premium Pro technology.
- The television operates on the Tizen platform, providing access to major streaming services and AI-driven features.
- Samsung’s Vision AI enables real-time live translations and automatic optimization of audio and video settings.
- The unit includes built-in 30W speakers to support its large-scale cinematic visual experience.

### Why it Matters
This significant price reduction makes high-end, ultra-large display technology more competitive for home theater enthusiasts who previously found the MSRP prohibitive. By integrating advanced AI features and gaming-focused hardware, Samsung is positioning this model as a versatile hub for both entertainment and interactive smart home management.
