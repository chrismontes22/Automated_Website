I tested a $3,000 robot mower and now I finally get my work done without worrying about my lawn
============================================================

The Dreame A3 AWD Pro is a premium robotic lawn mower featuring LiDAR navigation and all-wheel drive, designed to automate lawn maintenance for busy homeowners with larger properties.

### Key Points
- The A3 AWD Pro series includes three models—2500, 3500, and 5000—priced between $3,099 and $3,499 based on maximum lawn coverage.
- Navigation is powered by the OmniSense 3.0 system, which utilizes 3D LiDAR and AI vision to map yards and avoid over 300 types of obstacles.
- The mower features an IPX6-rated design, all-wheel drive capable of handling 80% slopes, and a 40cm cutting width with adjustable heights from 1.2 to 3.9 inches.
- Built-in 4G connectivity allows for remote monitoring and anti-theft location tracking, with three years of complimentary service included.
- Setup requires a 2.4GHz Wi-Fi connection and may involve manual boundary adjustments, particularly for lawns with complex terrain or tall grass.

### Why it Matters
This device represents a significant shift toward full automation for outdoor chores, offering a high-end solution for users looking to reclaim time spent on manual lawn maintenance. While the high price point and initial setup complexity position it as a luxury investment, its ability to handle challenging terrain and provide remote oversight signals a maturing market for smart, autonomous landscaping tools.
