Apple’s iPhone Camera App Is Getting an AI Upgrade in iOS 27
============================================================

Apple unveiled iOS 27 at its Worldwide Developers Conference, introducing a new Siri AI camera integration and advanced generative editing tools to enhance photo manipulation and visual intelligence capabilities.

### Key Points
- Apple integrated Siri AI directly into the iPhone camera app to provide contextual assistance for identifying objects and text.
- The Photos app is gaining three AI-powered editing tools: Clean Up for object removal, Extend for expanding image borders, and Spatial Reframe for perspective adjustments.
- These generative AI features utilize updated foundation models to realistically fill in image gaps during editing.
- Apple is reportedly exploring the integration of this computer-vision technology into future hardware, including smart glasses and camera-equipped AirPods.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal Apple's strategic push to compete with Google’s Pixel AI features by embedding generative tools directly into the core user experience. By normalizing visual AI assistance on the iPhone, Apple is laying the groundwork for broader adoption of computer-vision technology across its future wearable product ecosystem.
