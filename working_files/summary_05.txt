Mark Cuban's job-search advice in the AI era: Look at small businesses
============================================================

Billionaire entrepreneur Mark Cuban advises job seekers to prioritize small businesses, arguing that AI adoption will drive significant growth and create new opportunities for recent college graduates.

### Key Points
- Mark Cuban stated on X that small businesses generate approximately 60% of new annual jobs.
- Bureau of Labor Statistics data shows firms with under 250 employees accounted for 51% of net job creation from 2020 to 2025.
- Cuban suggests that small companies lack internal AI expertise and will rely on new graduates to implement automation tools.
- The entrepreneur argues that small businesses use AI to expand their operational capabilities rather than to reduce their total headcount.

### Why it Matters
This perspective highlights a shift in the labor market where AI proficiency becomes a primary asset for entry-level workers seeking roles in smaller, agile firms. By positioning small businesses as the primary drivers of future employment, Cuban suggests that graduates may find more significant career growth and influence outside of traditional large corporations.
