tvOS 27 and watchOS 27 Second Public Betas Now Available
============================================================

Apple has released the second public betas for watchOS 27, tvOS 27, and HomePod Software 27, allowing users to test new features ahead of the official fall software launch.

### Key Points
- The watchOS 27 update introduces Siri AI integration, a redesigned app grid, and enhanced Smart Stack widget suggestions.
- New fitness features include standalone Workout Buddy functionality and improved distance tracking for treadmill runs and walks.
- The tvOS 27 update features a redesigned Podcasts app, support for Hi-Res Lossless audio, and faster application launch times.
- Users can access the beta software by registering through Apple’s official public beta testing website and updating via device settings.
- The update includes a unified Find My application for Apple Watch that now supports Precision Finding capabilities.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal Apple's ongoing integration of artificial intelligence and performance optimizations across its ecosystem of home and wearable devices. By expanding beta access, the company aims to refine user experience and software stability before the final versions reach the broader consumer market this autumn.
