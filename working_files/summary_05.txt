SpaceX Reportedly Has an AI Device Prototype
============================================================

SpaceX is reportedly developing a proprietary AI-powered handset device integrated with xAI technology, though Elon Musk has publicly denied the existence of such a project or prototype.

### Key Points
- Reports suggest SpaceX is testing a slim, handset-like device running a custom operating system to avoid reliance on Android or iOS platforms.
- The device would integrate technology from xAI, the artificial intelligence company founded by Elon Musk.
- SpaceX possesses significant manufacturing capabilities and chip access through its sister company, Tesla, to potentially support mass production.
- The project aligns with SpaceX’s broader ambitions to expand into the wireless sector, potentially leveraging its Starlink Mobile infrastructure.
- Previous market attempts at standalone AI hardware, such as those from Humane and Rabbit, have struggled to gain widespread consumer adoption.

### Why it Matters
This development highlights a strategic push by Elon Musk to create a vertically integrated ecosystem that bypasses traditional mobile platform gatekeepers. If successful, such a device could challenge established telecommunications providers and redefine how users interact with native AI interfaces on mobile hardware.
