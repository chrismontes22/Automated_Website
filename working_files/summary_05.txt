Don't buy a TV in 2026 without these features — you'll regret it within a year
============================================================

Modern television buyers should prioritize specific display and connectivity features, such as burn-in prevention, glare-free coatings, and HDMI eARC support, to ensure long-term performance and high-quality viewing experiences.

### Key Points
- OLED TVs now utilize pixel or panel shifting technologies to prevent permanent image burn-in from static logos or gaming badges.
- Mini-LED and emerging RGB LED backlighting offer improved contrast, brightness, and color accuracy for non-OLED display panels.
- Glare-free screen coatings, such as those found on Samsung’s S95D, allow for clear viewing in bright rooms by diffusing ambient light.
- HDMI eARC ports enable the transmission of uncompressed, high-quality audio to soundbars while simplifying home theater cable management.
- Choosing a TV with an intuitive smart operating system, such as Google TV or LG’s webOS, is essential for seamless content navigation.

### Why it Matters
Selecting a television with these advanced technical specifications ensures the hardware remains relevant and functional as media standards evolve. These features directly impact the longevity of the display and the overall convenience of integrating high-end audio and smart home ecosystems.
