Stop tap tap tapping on your Android keyboard and get the message through faster with this ancient but underrated feature
============================================================

Android users can significantly increase their typing speed by utilizing swipe typing, a feature available on Gboard and Samsung Keyboard that allows users to slide between letters instead of tapping.

### Key Points
- Swipe typing, also known as Glide Typing, enables users to input words by sliding a finger across the keyboard without lifting it between letters.
- The feature is compatible with Android devices running version 8.0 or higher and is natively supported by Gboard and Samsung Keyboard.
- Gboard automatically adds spaces between words and improves accuracy over time by learning user vocabulary and common word patterns.
- The functionality extends to multiple languages, custom dictionary entries, and Gboard-enabled smartwatches running Wear OS.
- Samsung Keyboard users must manually enable the swipe mode within their device settings to activate the feature.

### Why it Matters
Mastering swipe typing can drastically improve communication efficiency for professionals and students who rely heavily on mobile devices for daily tasks. As users become proficient, this method reduces the physical effort of individual key presses and accelerates overall text entry speeds on smartphones.
