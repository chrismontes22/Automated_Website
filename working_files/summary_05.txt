Where was tvOS 27 at WWDC?
============================================================

Apple omitted significant updates for tvOS 27 during the Worldwide Developers Conference, leaving the platform’s future uncertain compared to major announcements for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS operating systems.

### Key Points
- Apple featured iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27 at WWDC, highlighting new Siri AI capabilities and child safety features.
- The company provided no dedicated presentation segment for tvOS 27, mentioning it only briefly on a single summary slide.
- Apple previously announced upcoming accessibility updates for tvOS, including larger text support for users with low vision.
- Current Apple TV 4K hardware relies on the aging A15 Bionic chip, which may lack the processing power required for new Apple Intelligence features.
- Industry analysts suggest a new Apple TV model is necessary to compete with smart home integrations offered by Amazon Alexa and Google Home.

### Why it Matters
The absence of tvOS updates suggests Apple may be prioritizing hardware refreshes before integrating advanced AI features into its living room ecosystem. This delay leaves the Apple TV 4K trailing behind competitors in smart home functionality and software parity with the rest of the company's product lineup.
