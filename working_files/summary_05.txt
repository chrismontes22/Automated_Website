The 3 most important USB cables every phone owner should have
============================================================

Smartphone owners should maintain a versatile collection of USB cables, including high-speed USB-C, multi-connector universal adapters, and USB-C to HDMI cords, to ensure compatibility across all modern devices.

### Key Points
- A USB-C 3.2 Gen 2 cable supporting 60W or 100W power delivery is essential for fast charging and high-speed 10 Gbps data transfers.
- Universal multi-functional cables, such as the Raycon Magic Flex 6-in-1 or Anker Powerline II, provide flexibility for devices using Lightning, USB-A, and USB-C ports.
- USB-C to HDMI cables enable high-quality audio and video output for external displays, supporting features like Samsung DeX at 4K resolution and 60Hz refresh rates.
- Selecting cables with at least 6ft of length prevents physical strain and ensures convenient connectivity when using phones with monitors or televisions.

### Why it Matters
Understanding cable specifications prevents performance bottlenecks and ensures that devices charge at optimal speeds while maintaining hardware compatibility. Investing in high-quality, multi-purpose cables reduces clutter and provides a reliable solution for diverse charging and connectivity needs in professional or travel environments.
