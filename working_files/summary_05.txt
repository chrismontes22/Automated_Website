12th-Generation iPad: Everything We Know About Apple's Next Budget Tablet
============================================================

Apple is reportedly planning to release a 12th-generation iPad in early 2027, featuring significant internal hardware upgrades including a new processor capable of supporting Apple Intelligence features.

### Key Points
- The 12th-generation iPad is expected to launch in the first quarter of 2027 with a starting price of $449.
- New models will likely feature an A18 or A19 chip, enabling support for Apple Intelligence and requiring an upgrade to 8GB of RAM.
- The design will remain largely unchanged, retaining the 11-inch display, thick bezels, and current USB-C connectivity.
- Potential hardware additions include the N1 chip for Wi-Fi 7 and Bluetooth 6 support, alongside an Apple-designed C-series cellular modem.

### Why it Matters
Integrating advanced chips into the entry-level iPad ensures that Apple Intelligence features reach a broader consumer base beyond premium device owners. This update maintains the tablet's market competitiveness by balancing essential performance improvements with the established, budget-friendly price point.
