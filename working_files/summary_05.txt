Our favorite XR glasses just received a rare $170 discount during Amazon's Big Spring Sale
============================================================

Amazon is offering a $170 discount on the XREAL One Pro smart glasses during its Big Spring Sale, bringing the price down to $598.98 through March 31st.

**Key points**
- The XREAL One Pro features a micro-OLED display that simulates a 200-inch screen with a 120Hz refresh rate.
- Powered by the X1 Spatial Computing chip, the glasses convert 2D images into 3D in real time.
- The device connects via USB-C to most smartphones, laptops, and gaming consoles.
- Both medium and large versions of the glasses are included in the 22% price reduction.
- The hardware is limited to a 30FPS graphics cap, which may impact high-performance gaming experiences.

**Why it matters: **
This rare discount makes premium augmented reality hardware more accessible to consumers interested in portable, high-definition virtual displays. The promotion highlights a significant price drop for a top-rated device during a major retail event.
