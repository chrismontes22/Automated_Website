I found 7 Windows apps that use your PC's NPU to improve efficiency and performance with AI — You might be surprised at what's on the list
============================================================

Modern Windows PCs equipped with Neural Processing Units are increasingly leveraging specialized hardware to run AI-powered applications locally, enhancing performance, privacy, and efficiency across various software categories.

### Key Points
- Cephable uses the NPU to power adaptive, motion-based PC controls while keeping sensitive voice and video data off the cloud.
- Adobe Photoshop utilizes the NPU to accelerate resource-heavy tasks like background removal and subject selection, reducing battery drain and heat.
- Norton Antivirus employs the NPU for real-time, on-device detection of audio and video deepfakes and phishing scams.
- Algoriddim’s djay Pro features a Neural Mix tool that isolates vocals and instruments with lower latency than traditional CPU processing.
- BufferZone NoCloud AI and HP IQ demonstrate the NPU's role in enterprise-grade phishing protection and workplace intelligence workflows.
- CapCut leverages the NPU for efficient face tracking and background removal, offloading intensive video processing from the CPU and GPU.

### Why it Matters
The shift toward on-device AI processing marks a significant evolution in PC hardware, prioritizing user privacy and system efficiency by reducing reliance on cloud-based servers. As more third-party developers integrate NPU support, consumers can expect faster, more responsive software that preserves battery life and improves overall system performance.
