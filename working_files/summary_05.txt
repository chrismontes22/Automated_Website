New Company Hopes to Build Age-Verification Tech into Vape Cartridges
============================================================

**Ispire Technology and Chemular have launched Ike Tech, a new venture utilizing biometric data and blockchain to implement mandatory age-verification systems for disposable electronic cigarette cartridges.**

**Key points**
- Ike Tech uses smartphone cameras to scan IDs and facial biometrics, verifying user age through services like ID.me or Clear.
- Verified users receive anonymized tokens that unlock vape cartridges via Bluetooth connectivity.
- The system automatically disables the device if it moves out of range from the user's smartphone.
- Future updates may include geo-fencing capabilities to disable devices near schools or on airplanes.
- Developers plan to license this biometric technology to other e-cigarette manufacturers and potentially expand into firearm safety locks.

**Why it matters**
This technology addresses significant regulatory pressure regarding youth access to nicotine products by providing a potentially infallible digital verification method. If successfully implemented, it could set a new industry standard for age-restricted consumer goods and influence future FDA compliance requirements.
