Anker's ultra-compact 45W charger with a screen drops to its best price ever
============================================================

Anker has discounted its Smart Display Charger to a record-low price of $28, offering a compact 45W USB-C power solution featuring a real-time performance monitoring screen.

### Key Points
- The Anker Smart Display Charger is currently available for $28, representing a 30% discount from its standard $40 retail price.
- The device features a single USB-C port capable of delivering up to 45W of power to laptops, tablets, and smartphones.
- An integrated display provides users with real-time data regarding charging speeds and the capacity of connected devices.
- The charger includes a 180-degree rotating plug designed to improve orientation and fit within tight or awkward wall spaces.
- The product is available in three color options: black, white, and blue.

### Why it Matters
This discount makes a high-performance, data-capable charger more accessible for users seeking reliable power for expensive mobile hardware. By combining a compact form factor with real-time diagnostic feedback, Anker provides a practical tool for monitoring charging efficiency in a crowded peripheral market.
