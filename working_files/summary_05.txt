JMGO’s N3 Ultimate projector is the new portable 4K champ
============================================================

The JMGO N3 Ultimate is a high-performance 4K portable projector featuring a motorized gimbal and optical zoom, offering superior placement flexibility and brightness for a $2,399 sale price.

### Key Points
- The N3 Ultimate utilizes a triple-laser RGB DLP system to deliver 4K resolution and support for Dolby Vision.
- Its motorized gimbal and optical lens shift allow for "lossless" image adjustment without relying on resolution-degrading digital keystone correction.
- While marketed at 5,800 ISO lumens, the device performs best at approximately 3,000 to 4,600 lumens depending on color accuracy settings.
- The projector runs on Google TV with native Netflix support and includes a MediaTek MT9679 chipset for snappy navigation.
- Physical design drawbacks include the lack of an integrated carrying handle and a somewhat cumbersome menu system for Bluetooth speaker mode.

### Why it Matters
This projector represents a significant advancement in portable home theater technology by prioritizing physical optical adjustments over digital image processing. Its ability to maintain high-quality visuals from off-center angles makes it a viable, space-saving alternative to traditional, permanently mounted home theater installations.
