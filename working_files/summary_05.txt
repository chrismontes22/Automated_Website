Dyson’s powerful 360 Vis Nav robovac is down to $279.99 for a limited time
============================================================

Dyson’s 360 Vis Nav robot vacuum is currently available at Woot for a record-low price of $279.99, offering significant savings on a high-performance device through May 11th.

### Key Points
- The Dyson 360 Vis Nav is discounted by $919, bringing its price down to $279.99 with a two-year warranty included.
- The vacuum features 65 air watts of suction power and a D-shaped design optimized for cleaning edges and corners.
- It includes a 500ml dustbin and a low-profile build that allows it to navigate under furniture easily.
- The device lacks modern features such as AI-powered obstacle avoidance, a self-emptying dock, and has a 65-minute battery life.
- The promotion at Woot is scheduled to run through May 11th or until current inventory is exhausted.

### Why it Matters
This deal offers a substantial discount on a premium cleaning tool for consumers prioritizing raw suction power over modern smart-home automation features. While it lacks advanced obstacle avoidance, the significant price reduction makes it a highly competitive option for users focused on deep-cleaning carpets and pet hair.
