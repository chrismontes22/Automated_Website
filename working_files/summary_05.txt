Bosch and ECOVACS Built a Robot Vacuum That Hides Inside Your Kitchen Cabinet… Finally!
============================================================

Bosch and ECOVACS have unveiled a collaborative built-in robot vacuum system that integrates directly into kitchen cabinetry, utilizing home plumbing and electrical lines for fully automated floor maintenance.

### Key Points
- The system installs within a standard sink base cabinet, connecting directly to home water supply and drainage lines.
- The robot features 20,000 Pa suction, rotating extendable mop pads, and a low 84-millimeter profile for cleaning under furniture.
- Automated service modules handle dust collection, detergent dispensing, and mop pad cleaning with 75°C water and hot air drying.
- Users manage the device via the Bosch Home Connect app, which supports floor mapping, scheduling, and privacy-focused data storage.
- The hardware is scheduled for a European market release in spring 2026, following its debut at Milan Design Week.

### Why it Matters
This partnership marks a shift from portable consumer electronics toward permanent, integrated home infrastructure that eliminates the need for manual maintenance. By embedding cleaning technology into kitchen cabinetry, the companies are targeting the high-end renovation market and setting a new standard for seamless smart home automation.
