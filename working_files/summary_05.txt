Cannes Market Grows ‘AI For Talent Summit’ & Launches Creator Economy Summit As It Bolsters Innovation Strand
============================================================

The Cannes Film Festival’s Marché du Film will host its second annual AI For Talent Summit and debut a new Creator Economy Summit from May 12 to 20.

### Key Points
- The AI For Talent Summit expands to two days, May 15 and 16, focusing on production workflows, ethics, and education.
- A new 450m² immersive venue at the Carlton Hotel will host large-scale, multi-user experiences for up to 225 participants simultaneously.
- The inaugural Creator Economy Summit will explore the transition of digital content creators into long-form film production and cinematic storytelling.
- The Village Innovation will feature a 1,000 m² space equipped with a virtual production demonstration stage overlooking the old port.
- The Marché du Film Online platform is migrating to the newly rebuilt Cinando database to improve networking for remote participants.

### Why it Matters
These initiatives reflect a strategic effort by the film industry to integrate emerging technologies and digital creator models into traditional production and distribution frameworks. By fostering collaboration between tech executives and filmmakers, the festival aims to modernize business models and adapt to evolving audience engagement strategies.
