The LG Rolling TV That Went Viral Is Now 32 Inches, 4K, and $1,299
============================================================

LG has launched the StanbyME 2 Max, a 32-inch portable 4K smart display featuring a 4.5-hour battery and a versatile wheeled stand for flexible use throughout the home.

### Key Points
- The 32-inch 4K UHD display features a 144Wh battery providing up to 4.5 hours of wireless playback.
- Users can detach the screen from its wheeled floor stand and mount it on an included tabletop base.
- The device runs on LG’s webOS, offering access to streaming services, LG Channels, and touchscreen applications.
- Connectivity options include HDMI and USB ports, allowing for integration with gaming consoles and laptops.
- The product received a CES 2026 Innovation Award and is currently available for pre-order at $1,299.99.

### Why it Matters
- This device signals a shift in the television market by blurring the lines between a traditional stationary TV and a high-performance secondary monitor. Its portability and premium specifications offer a flexible solution for consumers who want a high-quality viewing experience in any room without being tethered to a wall outlet.
