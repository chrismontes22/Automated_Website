Walmart could be prepping a budget smart speaker with Gemini baked in
============================================================

Walmart is preparing to launch a new Onn smart speaker featuring Google’s Gemini AI, marking the first time the technology has been integrated into a third-party hardware device.

### Key Points
- A Connectivity Standards Alliance filing reveals the upcoming Onn speaker includes Gemini AI, Google Cast for Audio, and smart home control capabilities.
- The device features a 10W driver, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, and a physical hardware kill switch for the far-field microphone array.
- This release represents the first time Google has extended its Gemini AI platform to third-party smart speaker manufacturers since the technology's debut.
- While official pricing remains unannounced, the product is expected to be positioned as a budget-friendly alternative to Google’s own unreleased $100 Home Speaker.

### Why it Matters
- This integration signals a strategic shift for Google as it expands its AI ecosystem beyond proprietary hardware to reach a broader consumer base through affordable third-party devices. By partnering with Walmart’s Onn brand, Google can accelerate the adoption of Gemini in the smart home market while providing budget-conscious users with advanced AI features.
