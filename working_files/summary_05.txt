What It Will Take to Make AI Sustainable
============================================================

AI sustainability researcher Sasha Luccioni is launching the Sustainable AI Group to help companies quantify environmental impacts and improve transparency regarding the energy consumption of large-scale AI models.

### Key Points
- Sasha Luccioni, formerly of Hugging Face, co-founded the Sustainable AI Group with former Salesforce sustainability executive Boris Gamazaychikov.
- The venture aims to help businesses measure the energy and water usage of AI tools to better align with ESG goals and regulatory requirements.
- Luccioni advocates for UI-based energy reporting, such as displaying the carbon footprint of individual queries within tools like ChatGPT or Claude.
- The initiative addresses the lack of transparency from major AI developers regarding data center locations, grid sources, and supply chain emissions.
- Research focuses on identifying energy-efficient alternatives to massive general-purpose models, such as using specialized classifiers for specific business tasks.

### Why it Matters
As AI becomes a core business utility, companies face increasing pressure from employees and regulators to account for the significant environmental costs of their digital infrastructure. Establishing standardized transparency metrics could create a competitive market advantage for firms that prioritize renewable energy and efficient model selection.
