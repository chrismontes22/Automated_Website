Here are the tech leaders and ultrawealthy descending on rural Idaho as billionaire summer camp begins
============================================================

Tech and media industry leaders are gathering in Sun Valley, Idaho, for the annual Allen & Co. conference, a high-profile event dominated by discussions on artificial intelligence and strategy.

### Key Points
- The invite-only conference is expected to draw executives from major companies including Amazon, Apple, Meta, OpenAI, Warner Bros. Discovery, and Netflix.
- Sun Valley Friedman Memorial Airport expects between 300 and 350 private aircraft arrivals daily, more than four times the typical traffic volume.
- Artificial intelligence remains the primary focus of the event, with leaders like OpenAI’s Sam Altman and Palantir’s Alex Karp in attendance.
- Notable leadership transitions are represented, including Apple CEO Tim Cook’s upcoming succession and Disney CEO Josh D'Amaro’s first appearance in his new role.
- Strategic corporate shifts are a key theme, highlighted by Warner Bros. Discovery’s plan to split its streaming and studio businesses from its cable networks.

### Why it Matters
This gathering serves as a critical venue for the world's most influential executives to negotiate partnerships and align on the future of the global economy. The focus on AI and media consolidation suggests that these private discussions will likely shape major corporate investments and industry trends for the coming year.
