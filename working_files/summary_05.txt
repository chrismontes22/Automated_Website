WiiM Sound is the smart speaker upgrade I've been waiting for
============================================================

The WiiM Sound smart speaker offers high-fidelity audio, extensive connectivity options, and deep integration with Google Assistant and Alexa for a competitive retail price of $299.

### Key Points
- The WiiM Sound features a 4-inch woofer, two 1-inch silk-dome tweeters, and 100W of power.
- Connectivity includes Google Cast, Spotify Connect, Tidal Connect, Roon Ready, Wi-Fi 6e, and Bluetooth 5.3.
- The device includes a 1.8-inch front display panel for album art and playback control, with a $229 "Lite" version available.
- Users can create stereo pairs or integrate the speaker into 2.1 systems using the $449 WiiM Sub Pro.
- Future updates will allow the speaker to function as a rear or center channel for the upcoming WiiM Bar home theater system.

### Why it Matters
The WiiM Sound challenges established market leaders like Sonos by offering superior connectivity and customization at a lower price point. Its ability to integrate into existing multi-room audio setups makes it a versatile choice for consumers looking to build a flexible, high-quality home sound system.
