Birdfy Review: Smart Bird Feeders and Bird Bath Put to the Test
============================================================

Birdfy offers a range of smart, camera-equipped bird feeders and baths that utilize AI to identify avian visitors and record high-definition footage for home monitoring.

### Key Points
- Product lineup includes the standard Birdfy Feeder, the dual-camera Feeder 2 Duo, and the Bird Bath Pro, with prices ranging from $80 to $360.
- Devices feature 2K cameras, IP65/66 weather resistance, and optional solar panels to maintain battery life without manual USB-C charging.
- The Birdfy app provides AI-powered species identification, visit notifications, and cloud storage, with lifetime AI access available for a one-time fee.
- Unlike some competitors, Birdfy products are designed for pole or wall mounting rather than hanging, which may require additional baffles to deter squirrels.
- The Bird Bath Pro includes a solar-powered fountain and serves as a popular water source for both birds and local wildlife like foxes and raccoons.

### Why it Matters
These smart devices provide an accessible way for hobbyists to track local wildlife and automate bird-watching through digital notifications and video archives. While the setup and app interface are more complex than some alternatives, the availability of lifetime AI options offers a cost-effective alternative to recurring subscription models.
