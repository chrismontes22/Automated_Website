Walmart just doubled the price of its cheapest Google TV stick, and you already know why
============================================================

Walmart has increased the price of its Onn FHD Streaming Stick with Google TV from $15 to $30 for users who are not logged into their accounts.

### Key Points
- The Onn FHD Streaming Stick price has doubled to $30 for guest users on Walmart's website.
- Logged-in users may still see the original $15 price, suggesting a phased rollout of the increase.
- Rising costs for memory components, driven by high demand for AI infrastructure, are impacting consumer hardware pricing.
- Walmart previously increased prices for its 4K Pro and 4K Plus streaming models earlier this year.
- The company has not issued an official statement regarding the permanent price adjustment for the base model.

### Why it Matters
This price hike reflects a broader industry trend where surging demand for AI-related hardware components is forcing manufacturers to raise prices on budget-friendly consumer electronics. As profit margins on entry-level devices remain thin, consumers should expect similar cost increases across the streaming hardware market.
