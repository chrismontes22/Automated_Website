Five New iOS 27 Features You Might Have Missed
============================================================

Apple has released the iOS 27 developer beta, introducing expanded landscape mode support, new FaceTime dual-capture capabilities, integrated bill splitting via Apple Cash, and enhanced location sharing features.

### Key Points
- Landscape mode is now available in numerous built-in apps, including Apple Music, Podcasts, Fitness, and Home, often featuring a new left-aligned sidebar.
- iPhone 17 models gain a Dual Capture feature in FaceTime, allowing users to stream video from both front and rear cameras simultaneously.
- The Messages app now includes a comprehensive set of Markup drawing tools accessible directly through the app drawer.
- U.S. users can utilize Apple Intelligence and Apple Cash to automatically split bills and calculate tax and tips by scanning receipts with the camera.
- Find My location sharing now allows for custom expiration durations and the ability to pause sharing with specific individuals discreetly.

### Why it Matters
These updates signal a shift toward greater interface flexibility and hardware integration, potentially preparing the ecosystem for future foldable device form factors. By automating financial tasks and refining location privacy, Apple is increasing the utility of its core services to drive deeper user engagement within the iPhone ecosystem.
