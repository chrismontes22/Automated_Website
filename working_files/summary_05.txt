Apple Home recently got better in three ways, here’s what’s new
============================================================

Apple has introduced several enhancements to its Home ecosystem in iOS 26, including new thermostat automation, advanced smart lock technology, and streamlined setup processes for multi-pack accessory bundles.

### Key Points
- The new Adaptive Temperature feature uses on-device intelligence to automate thermostat adjustments based on user location and sleep schedules.
- The Aqara Smart Thermostat W200 is the first device to support Adaptive Temperature, functioning also as a Matter 1.4 hub.
- The Aqara Smart Lock U400 now supports Apple Home Key and Ultra Wideband technology for precise, proximity-based automatic locking and unlocking.
- iOS 26.2 introduced multipack accessory pairing, allowing users to enroll multiple smart devices simultaneously using a single setup code.

### Why it Matters
These updates reduce the technical friction associated with managing smart home devices by automating climate control and simplifying the installation process. By integrating advanced connectivity standards like Ultra Wideband and Matter, Apple is positioning its Home platform to be more reliable and user-friendly for mainstream consumers.
