CoreWeave (CRWV) to Join Nasdaq-100, Soars 9.67%
============================================================

CoreWeave shares surged nearly 10 percent on Tuesday following the announcement that the AI-focused cloud provider will join the Nasdaq 100 index starting on June 22, 2026.

### Key Points
- CoreWeave stock closed at $117.03 after a 9.67 percent rally, marking its fourth consecutive day of gains.
- The company is set to join the Nasdaq 100 index alongside Astera Labs, Nebius Group, Rocket Lab Corp., and Teradyne Inc.
- Nasdaq 100 index changes will result in the removal of Charter Communications, Cognizant Technology Solutions, Insmed Inc., Verisk Analytics, and Zscaler Inc.
- Inclusion in major indices often triggers share price appreciation as institutional funds adjust portfolios to mirror the new index composition.
- CEO Michael Intrator attributed the milestone to the company's early focus on building cloud infrastructure specifically designed for artificial intelligence workloads.

### Why it Matters
Joining the Nasdaq 100 serves as a significant validation of CoreWeave’s rapid growth and its strategic position within the competitive artificial intelligence infrastructure market. This transition forces institutional investors to rebalance their holdings, which typically increases liquidity and visibility for the company among major market participants.
