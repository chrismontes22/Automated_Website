Vivo’s X300 Ultra has the best cameras in any phone
============================================================

The Vivo X300 Ultra distinguishes itself from rival flagship smartphones by prioritizing a balanced, high-quality triple-lens camera system over the industry trend of pushing extreme telephoto hardware capabilities.

### Key Points
- The device features a 200-megapixel main camera with a 35mm focal length, a 200-megapixel telephoto lens, and a high-performance ultrawide sensor.
- Professional video features include 4K, 120fps, 10-bit Log recording, and support for custom 3D LUTs.
- Hardware specifications include a 6,600mAh silicon-carbon battery, a 144Hz 6.8-inch OLED display, and the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset.
- The phone carries an IP68 and IP69 rating for water and dust resistance.
- It is currently available in select Asian and European markets, including Spain, Italy, and Austria, with a starting price of €1,999.

### Why it Matters
This release signals a shift in the premium smartphone market, where manufacturers are moving beyond singular "hero" lenses to offer consistent performance across all focal lengths. By positioning the device as a modular, semi-professional camera system, Vivo challenges the industry standard of prioritizing flashy hardware specs over practical, everyday photographic versatility.
