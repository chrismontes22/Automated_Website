Dreame’s autonomous Lawn Mower packs more self-driving tech than most cars
============================================================

Dreame Technology has launched the A3 AWD Pro, an autonomous robot lawn mower featuring advanced LiDAR navigation and all-terrain capabilities that eliminate the need for perimeter wires or beacons.

### Key Points
- The A3 AWD Pro utilizes 360-degree 3D LiDAR and binocular AI vision to identify over 300 obstacle types without requiring pre-installed signal wires.
- Its four-wheel-drive system features independent hub motors, allowing the mower to climb slopes up to 80 percent and navigate vertical obstacles up to 5.5 centimeters.
- The device includes EdgeMaster 2.0 technology for precise border trimming and a floating dual-disc cutting deck to maintain consistent grass height on uneven terrain.
- Security features include 4G real-time location tracking, an integrated Apple AirTag slot, and PIN-protected operation to prevent theft.
- Available in four coverage variants, the mower supports dual-map scheduling for disconnected yards and is priced starting at $2,599.99.

### Why it Matters
This product signals Dreame’s transition from a home appliance manufacturer to a high-performance robotics company by applying complex mobility engineering to consumer lawn care. By removing the setup tax of perimeter wires and beacons, the company is setting a new standard for accessibility and intelligence in the autonomous outdoor equipment market.
