What to Expect From WWDC 2026: Gemini-Powered Siri, iOS 27, macOS 27 and More
============================================================

Apple’s 2026 Worldwide Developers Conference will showcase a major AI-driven overhaul for Siri and system-wide software updates across iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27 to enhance user productivity.

### Key Points
- Siri will gain advanced personal context awareness, onscreen interaction capabilities, and a standalone chatbot app powered by Google Gemini models.
- iOS 27 introduces customizable Camera app widgets, AI-powered photo editing tools like Extend and Reframe, and natural language shortcut creation.
- Apple is prioritizing privacy with on-device processing and Private Cloud Compute, while allowing third-party chatbot integration via new Extensions.
- The update includes system-wide accessibility improvements, such as AI-generated subtitles and enhanced VoiceOver descriptions for images.
- iOS 27 will drop support for the iPhone 11 series and second-generation iPhone SE, while macOS 27 ends support for all remaining Intel-based Macs.

### Why it Matters
These updates represent Apple's most significant push into generative AI, positioning the company to compete directly with industry leaders like OpenAI and Google. By deeply integrating these features into the core operating system, Apple aims to transform Siri from a basic assistant into a powerful, context-aware tool that manages personal data and complex workflows.
