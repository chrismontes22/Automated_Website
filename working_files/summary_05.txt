This RTX 5050 gaming laptop with an 18” FHD+ display, 16GB RAM, and 1TB SSD strikes a "happy medium between performance and price"
============================================================

The ASUS ROG Strix G18 gaming laptop is currently discounted to $1,349.99 at Amazon, offering high-end performance for users looking to play new titles announced at the Xbox Preview.

**Key points**
- The discounted ASUS ROG Strix G18 features an NVIDIA RTX 5050 GPU, AMD Ryzen 9 8940HX processor, 16GB of DDR5 RAM, and a 1TB SSD.
- The laptop includes an 18-inch FHD+ display with a 144Hz refresh rate for high-performance gaming.
- Users can upgrade the system to support up to 64GB of RAM and 4TB of total SSD storage via two available slots.
- Premium configurations featuring an RTX 5070 GPU and Intel Core Ultra 9 275HX processor are also on sale at multiple retailers.
- Potential buyers should note the device's limited battery life during intensive gaming and its tendency to reach high surface temperatures.

Why it matters: 
This sale provides a cost-effective entry point for gamers seeking hardware capable of handling demanding modern PC titles at high frame rates. The availability of multiple retailer discounts and upgradeable internal components makes this model a versatile choice for users prioritizing long-term performance.
