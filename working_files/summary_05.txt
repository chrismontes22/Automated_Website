X by Xreal is the first affordable pair of smart glasses that don't feel like a compromise
============================================================

The new X by Xreal smart glasses offer a high-end wearable display experience for $299, featuring advanced micro-OLED panels, modular frame designs, and innovative spatial tracking capabilities.

### Key Points
- Priced at $299, the X by Xreal glasses feature 1080p SeeYa micro-OLED panels with 120Hz refresh rates and 1600-nit peak brightness.
- The device includes industry-first 3840Hz PWM dimming and native 10-bit color support to enhance eye comfort and visual quality.
- Modular construction allows users to swap frames easily, with 3D-printing templates available for custom designs.
- The glasses weigh 62 grams and provide smooth-follow spatial tracking, a feature previously unavailable in the sub-$400 price segment.
- Connectivity is handled via USB-C, making the glasses compatible with most devices that support video output.

### Why it Matters
These glasses represent a significant shift in the wearable market by providing premium display technology and spatial tracking at a highly accessible price point. By eliminating the need for expensive hardware to achieve a high-quality virtual screen, Xreal is making portable productivity and gaming more attainable for the average consumer.
