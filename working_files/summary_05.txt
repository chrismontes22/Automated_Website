How to Watch Google I/O
============================================================

Google will host its annual I/O developer conference on May 19 in Mountain View, where CEO Sundar Pichai is expected to showcase new AI advancements and hardware integrations.

### Key Points
- The keynote begins at 10 am PT on May 19 at the Shoreline Amphitheatre and will be livestreamed via Google’s website and YouTube.
- Google plans to detail new AI-infused features for Android 17, including enhanced voice-to-text, smart autofill, and updated Android Auto interfaces.
- The company will provide more information on "Googlebook," a new line of premium AI-first laptops produced by partners including Acer, HP, Lenovo, and Dell.
- Presentations will likely include updates to the Gemini AI model, potential video generation tools, and a progress report on the Gemini-powered Google Home speaker.
- Google will offer a sneak peek at its Android XR smart glasses platform, developed in collaboration with brands like Samsung, Xreal, and Warby Parker.

### Why it Matters
These announcements signal Google's aggressive strategy to integrate generative AI directly into the core of its mobile, laptop, and wearable hardware ecosystems. By expanding its partnerships with major PC manufacturers and eyewear brands, the company aims to maintain its competitive edge against rivals in the rapidly evolving AI-driven consumer market.
