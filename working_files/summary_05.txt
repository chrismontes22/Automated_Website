Save $20 on This Already Inexpensive Wireless Mic Set
============================================================

The DJI Mic Mini wireless lavalier microphone kit is currently available on Amazon for $79, offering a $20 discount to help smartphone users improve their mobile video audio quality.

### Key Points
- The discounted kit includes a receiver, two transmitters, and a portable charging case.
- Transmitters feature magnetic or clip-on attachments for easy mounting on clothing.
- The system connects via USB-C for smartphones or a 3.5-mm TRS cable for digital cameras.
- Integrated features include manual gain controls, automatic limiting, and two levels of noise cancellation.
- The microphones offer direct compatibility with the DJI Osmo 360 camera for seamless audio integration.

### Why it Matters
This deal provides an accessible entry point for content creators looking to significantly upgrade audio quality without the bulk of professional recording equipment. By simplifying the transition from internal smartphone microphones to external wireless hardware, DJI is lowering the barrier for high-quality mobile production.
